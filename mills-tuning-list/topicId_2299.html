19-ET and 31-ET, being meantone tunings, use standard notation, where
the letters express the position on the circle of fifths mod 7, and the
sharps and flats correspond to shifts of 7 fifths. The five-line staff
predates 12-ET, which is only one possible meantone tuning. Here is the
standard notation for 19-ET:

0 C
1 C#
2 Db
3 D
4 D#
5 Eb
6 E
7 E#/Fb
8 F
9 F#
10 Gb
11 G
12 G#
13 Ab
14 A
15 A#
16 Bb
17 B
18 B#/cb
19 c

2 C#
3 Db
