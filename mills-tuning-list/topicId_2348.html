These are generated by the FasTrak utility, et. It uses Euclid's
algorithm to find the closest small number rational approximation
to a given interval.

By the way, in regards to the discussion about LCMs of periods of
chords, I wrote a program that generated a minimum chord pattern
based on minimum LCMs of periods, and then used FasTrak to
compose a small piece based on that minimum LCMs of periods
pattern. Then I used the automatic harmony feature of FasTrak to
convert the piece to a minimum LCMs of frequency harmonization.
I couldn't say that there was no difference between the pieces,
but, to my ear, it didn't seem that one was markedly more
consonant than the other.
