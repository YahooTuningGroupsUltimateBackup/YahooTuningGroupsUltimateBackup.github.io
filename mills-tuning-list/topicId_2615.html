>>>Even if you don't agree there's a difference between 300 and 19/16, it
>>>doesn't make the 19/16 worse than the 300.

It's an argument against: the farther in cents a tempered interval lies from
a just interval, the less consonant ("worse") it is. 19/16 is farther from
6/5 than 300 cents, but I think it's more consonant than 300 cents. In any
case, it's not "worse" than 300 cents.
