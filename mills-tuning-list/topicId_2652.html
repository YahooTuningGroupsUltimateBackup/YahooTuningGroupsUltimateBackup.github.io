Several people have asked me about a paper I
wrote a while ago in Experimental Musical Instruments
called "Relating Tuning and Timbre."

I have placed a slightly revised version of this paper
on line at

http://eceserv0.ece.wisc.edu/~sethares/consemi.html

for your hyperviewing pleasure.

Among other things, it gives a pretty complete
overview of the idea of "sensory consonance" that was
discussed earlier, and contains pointers to programs that
do the calculations.

Bill Sethares
