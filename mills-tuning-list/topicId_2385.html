I recently bought a 3CD box with organ music which I'm very content
with and will mention:
Orgelhistorie in Groningen (organ history in Groningen)
Syncoop Produkties, Slot Assumburgpad 54, 3123 RR Schiedam.
Produced with financial support of the Stichting Groningen
Orgelland, Groningen. I bought my copy in shop of the Groninger Museum
for f80,-
The box contains an 80 page booklet with a short history and stop list
of each organ in Dutch, German and English. It features 19 organs, the
oldest is from 1531. Very nice is the variety of tunings:

unaltered 1/4 comma meantone (3 x)
slightly modified 1/4 comma meantone
Neidhardt I
variant of 1/5 comma meantone
equal temperament (8 x)
Werckmeister III
variant of 1/6 comma meantone
tuning with six pure and six 1/6 Pyth. comma tempered fifths
Werckmeister I
Neidhardt III

The works are from many different composers, some of whom were unknown
to me. The music and recording are splendid.
