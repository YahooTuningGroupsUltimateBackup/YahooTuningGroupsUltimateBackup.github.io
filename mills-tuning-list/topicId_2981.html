The only microtonal instrument that I am playing these days is a set of
flutes based on a 17 tET scale. I made these flutes myself. You can play a
java version of these flutes yourself. Simply point your browser to
http://www.sirius.com/~touchles/poetry.htm then click on the graphic that
says "click here." What follows will be a little multi-media game. Every
time you click on either the animated triangle or the animated circle, you
will be either starting or stoping these instruments.
