On Fri, 13 Sep 1996, it was written:
> To generalize, an ET is level N consistent at the K-limit if all
> approximations of K-limit intervals vary from just by no more than

Your pardon. This is not actually the case for N=1. By definition,
the closest approximation will have an error less than 1/2 step, but not
all ETs are level 1 consistent, obviously.
