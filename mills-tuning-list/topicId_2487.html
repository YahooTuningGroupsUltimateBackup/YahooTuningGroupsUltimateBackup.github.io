For the record -- Egyptian music is very dear to me: all kinds, and of
course, significant. Shame on you Wolf for implying otherwise.

My philosophy of music has remained unchanged regarding previous
statements and I live my philosophy. It is okay to disagree with me.
This in itself, however, does not indicate rightness or wrongness on an
issue.

One last attempt to explain my non-acceptance of the microtone as _only_ a
discreet interval smaller than a semitone: perhaps it is the
reticence I have for doing mathematical calculations to provide
"microtone" remainders. Same problem I have with Ben Johnston's notation.
It's not that it is theoretically unsound. It is merely old fashioned to
my mind.
