We in San Diego had the pleasure of hosting
Harold Fortuin the last two days, on a visit
from Minneapolis.

Harold brought along his Clavette, an ingenious
and _very portable_ keyboard -- important words to
microtonalists with lots of notes! I want one.

He played some of his music for us, and I personally
really liked one called "Endangered Species" in
20-ET. He also jammed with us in an nice improvisation
tuned in the subharmonic scale.

Here's the URL for the Clavette:

http://www.wavefront.com/~hfortuin/clavett.htm
