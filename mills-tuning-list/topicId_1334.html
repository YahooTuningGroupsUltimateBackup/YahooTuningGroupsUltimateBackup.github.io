We hear people complaining about not being able to get xen music from
the web. Well, now you can get some .mid's. I have transcribed the 7
pieces by A.D. Fokker and Jan van Dijk, from Adriaan Fokker's book
"Recherches musicales" and have uploaded them to my site. These tunes
are based on 31TET approximations of Euler Generas. The titles are :
Preludium chromaticum Eu(3,5,7)
Improvisando Eu(5,5,7)
Tenacita Eu(3,7,7)
Canzonetta Eu(3,7,7)
Canzone Eu(3,3,7)
Cappriccio Eu(7,7,7)
Inventio Eu(5,7,7)

There is also some 12TET bebop, which I will soon detwelvulate.

The URL is http://www.interlinx.qc.ca/~kami/music.htm
