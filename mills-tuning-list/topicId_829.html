David Beardsley - Science Friction

Science Friction uses the ratios of just intonation tuning and
rhythm as compositional elements. The tuning used:
1/1, 17/16, 9/8, 19/16, 5/4, 21/16, 11/8, 3/2, 13/8, 27/16,
7/4, 15/8. The composition was realized at Endorphin
Computer Music Studios, Rahway, N.J.
