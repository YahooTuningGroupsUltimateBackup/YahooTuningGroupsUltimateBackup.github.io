right column, too.

It is important to remember though, that this scale is a matrix of harmonic
series (used as scales and chord materials) and subharmonic series (also
used as scales and chord materials). The matrix allows for close
relationships among the tonalities (individual series) for modulation among
them. The linear representation should be usefull for melodic instruments
(and perhaps harmonic as well) that would be expected to move fluidly among
the changes in tonality. Of course, numerous modes exist both within the
individual tonalities and the composit scale as well and various subsets of
these can be very useful.
