Microtonality Seminars in London

Dear Microtonalists,

The Music Technology Research Group of the London Guildhall University is
pleased to announce the following seminars:


21st OCTOBER: Brian Lee: "A new approach to just tuning"
*****Congratulations Brian!!! good feedback
from the students of the "Scales, Tunings and Temperaments" Unit
(Music Technology / London Guildhall University)

2nd DECEMBER: Jose Antonio Martin Salinas: "Julian Carrillo and the
Thirteenth Sound"


9th DECEMBER: Patrick Ozzard-Low (Alternative Tuning Projects) "A new
Chamber orchestra for the 21st century"


20th JANUARY:Lewis Jones & David Armitage: "A nineteen-note equal
temperament recorder"


They will take place at Room 100 (or 100A), 41 Commercial Road, London E1
1LA (close to Aldgate East Underground station). Seminars will start at
4.45 pm ending with a discussion by 6 pm.

Researchers that are interested in contributing to the series are invited
to contact Lewis Jones (ph/44-171-3201830 // Fax/ 44-171-3201830).

********

Tony Salinas

The Music Technology Research Group of the London Guildhall University
