Ed Dotson wrote:

>Paule, please pardon me,but WHY is the dominant 7th a dissonance? To me
>a tuned 7th is very consonant.

If it is tuned "in frequency ratios of 4x:5x:6x:7x where x=some fundamental
frequency," then it is consonant. If it is tuned according to a diatonic
scale with six consonant triads, it is dissonant.

>Do you mean with respect to the tempered scale?

Any such diatonic tuning is what I meant, not necessarily the 12-tone
tempered scale.
