movement.

In fact he wrote in the Ambient email list in Nov. '95,
in response to a thread about 4/4 grooves:

>From: bneill@interport.net (Ben Neill)
>Date: Thu, 9 Nov 1995 11:26:37 -0500
>Subject: Re: Ambient-Digest V2 #83
>
>My "Green Machine" record on Astralwerks avoids 4/4 altogether; the
>rhythmic parts are all in 6/4. The whole record is based on the harmonic
>ratios 6/7/8/9, therefore 6 is the fundamental rhythmic element throughout,
>and the 7, 8 and 9 are used as subdivisions of the 6.
>
>BN

And that's a better description then I could ever give.
