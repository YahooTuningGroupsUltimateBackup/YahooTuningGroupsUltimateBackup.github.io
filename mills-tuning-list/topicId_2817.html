Bob Gilmore's dissertation Harry Partch: "The Early Vocal
Works 1930-33," which is available from the British Harry Partch
Society has a bibliography listing several other dissertations or
theses.

Augustine, Daniel Schuyler. 'Four Theories of Music in the United
States, 1900-1950: Cowell, Yasser, Partch, Schillinger. ' Ph.D.
Dissertation, University of Texas at Austin, 1979.

Cameron, Janet. Transcriptions of Seven Li Po Settings by Harry
Partch from "Eleven Poems by Li Po."' M. Mus. dissertation, University
of Illinois, 1981

Hackbarth, Glenn Allen. An Analysis of Harry Partch's "Daphne of the
Dunes." D.M.A. thesis, Univ. of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. 1979.

There may be more in the bibliography at the Mills ftp site
(ella.mills.edu).
