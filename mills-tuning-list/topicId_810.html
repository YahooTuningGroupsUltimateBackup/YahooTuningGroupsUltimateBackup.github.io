Recently a reprint appeared of the book

Khinchin, A. Ya. (Aleksandr YAkovlevich), _Continued fractions_. 1935.
Translated from Russian. University of Chicago Press, 1964.
Dover Publications, New York, 1997, 95 pages, USD 6.95.

This cheap Dover reprint is certainly welcome since there are not many
comprehensive texts about continued fractions. The earlier mentioned
book of Schroeder (by Paul E.) also has a paragraph about them.
Convergents and CF's application in tuning lies in rational
approximation,
they are related to Farey fractions, mediants and are also useful in
getting
equal temperaments with closed circles of fifths.
