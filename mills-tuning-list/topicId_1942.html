The Amsterdam-based American composer and guitarist David Dramm has done
extensive work with guitar tunings where the frets remain 12tet, but the
open strings fall out of the 12tet set. He gives quite extraordinary
concerts with Ann LaBerge, the flutist and composer (herself well known for
her work with experimental flute tunings), of compositions and
improvisations.
