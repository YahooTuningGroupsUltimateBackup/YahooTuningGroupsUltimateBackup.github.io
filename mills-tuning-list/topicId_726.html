>conduct an experiment to test? What is the

>methods does the experimental composer use
>to analyze hi/r results--linear regression, chi

More importantly:

Don't these experimental composers realize that there are strict
guidelines for conducting research on human subjects?

How do they handle the issue of informed consent?

Should audience members sign release forms?

What about liability just in case something goes wrong? How much
insurance should a composer carry?

For a lot of the experimental music I've had to sit through, I would
have much rather been in the control group.
