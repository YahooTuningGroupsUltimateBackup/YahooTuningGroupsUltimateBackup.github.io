Date: March 29, 1997
News: It's Here! The New and Unproved Meadows!!

Yes, it's true: the redesigned Corporeal Meadows is online and waiting
for your visits, kudos and complaints! I got the revision up just in
time for our 1200th visitor, Mr. Octavio Commonus.

Why stop by now, if you've read it all before? How about "A Part of
Partch", where you can study some of the Boo part to Daphne, with not
only an explanation on notation, technique, etc. but the matching
sound clip? How about "The Garlic Highball", our mini-audio-docudrama,
where you can see the 1976 tour poster, read background on the same
tour and listen to Harry talk about "U. S. Highball" while the opening
plays in the distance?

Curious? I would be.

Actually, I am. Curious to see how many computers blow up from my
sound hacks, that is. Well, beyond the multimedia hoo-hah, you'll no-
tice we've cleaned up the look, tried to make things a little more
uniform and will continue to find ways to make the Meadows easier to
navigate. Take a look, let me know what you think (if you care) and
invite others. More stuff in the coming weeks. Til then, I'm pooped.
