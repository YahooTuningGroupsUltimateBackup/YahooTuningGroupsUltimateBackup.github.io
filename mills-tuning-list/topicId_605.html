I am very interested in the harmonica tunings, brought up by Pat Missin. I
used to play Harps in the 60's, but didn't know that they were tuned just at
that time (Didn't even know what Just was). all I knew was that I needed
different harps for each key.
Is it difficult to tune harps for custom requirements? I wouldn't
mind switching for keys and modes, if I could have my harmonics and
subharmonics too!
