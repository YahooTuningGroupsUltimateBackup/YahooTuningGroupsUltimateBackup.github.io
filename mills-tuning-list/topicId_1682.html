Maybe I *am* trying to "septimize" everything. What can I say, it is a part
of my musical exploration. Here is my latest discovery :

I was using the scale C D F Ab in many of my improvisations.

I asked the list if it could be 1 9/8 4/3 63/40. No one responded, because
the answer was no.

I found it this morning, it is 1 10/9 4/3 14/9.

This can be completed as 1 10/9 4/3 3/2 14/9.

Divide everything by 4/3 and you get 1 9/8 7/6 3/2 5/3 2/1.

C D Eb F G A C is used in some jazz music. Is there a name for this scale?
Synthetic minor pentatonic??
