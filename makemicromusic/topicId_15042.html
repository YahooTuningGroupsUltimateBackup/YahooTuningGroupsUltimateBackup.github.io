These 2 musics are uploaded:

Music based on jupiter scale proposed by Elsie hamilton <http://www.esnips.com/doc/0582de0d-378e-43b7-8f1c-e10d1a8520cc/hamilton-jupiter.mp3>

In Naked light <http://www.nakedlight.co.uk/Articles-00.htm> , web site of Brian lee which has some articles about Elsie hamilton <http://www.nakedlight.co.uk/Articles-00.htm> , we can see one of hamilton's musics named

"Steig hinauf zu höhern Kreise" , for soprano and alto lyre , in Jupiter scale <http://www.nakedlight.co.uk/pdf/articles/a-002.pdf> which has this scale : E F# G A# B C D E in 18-EDL with divisions based on 18:16:15:13:12:11:10:9 . I used 22-EDL to start scale from C but the Melodic trend starts from E with divisions based on 22:20:18:16:15:13:12:11 . in this serie we have 22:20:18 which is the same as 11:10:9.The overlap of this 2 scale is interesting , as if 18-EDL is a mode based on 22-EDL:

22:20:18:16:15:13:12:11
18:16:15:13:12:11:10:9

Second improvised microtonal music for setar with 3 strings in 96-EDO <http://www.esnips.com/doc/026b0d09-4ef4-4d00-8743-837c1264de2a/Second-improvised-microtonal-music-for-setar-with-3-strings-in-96-EDO-2.mp3>

For more about intervallic structure of these 2 , please click Shaahin's microtonal music folder <http://www.esnips.com/web/acousticsoftombak-Musicfolder%20> .
