Hi all,

"Penumbral" is a revision of a piece from several years ago called "Passacaglia & Fugue State". It uses a "scale" based on the pitches from one of La Monte Young's sine wave installations. Written (as usual) in Csound using blue.

It was just released yesterday (Aug. 7, 2010) as part of a massive 71-track 6-CD release on the Justnotnormal netlabel called "No-R-mal II" (see http://justnotnormal.wordpress.com/2010/08/07/jnn100-various-artists-no-r-mal-ii/).

My blog post about it is here:

http://mysterybear.net/article/50/no-r-mal-ii-penumbral

and the track may be streamed and/or downloaded from SoundCloud:

http://soundcloud.com/mysterybear/penumbral

Thanks for listening!

- Dave
http://mysterybear.net
http://soundcloud.com/mysterybear
