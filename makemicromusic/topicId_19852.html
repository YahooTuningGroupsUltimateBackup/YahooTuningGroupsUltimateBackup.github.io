but I think it's important enough to warrant it.

http://www.radioheadremix.com/remix/?id=1289

For those who don't know, Radiohead have invited anyone with
a computer to try their hand at remixing a popular track from
their album In Rainbows. Vistors can vote for their favorite,
and the band will audition the leading submissions.

I listened to the current top 10, and most of them are just
dumping some junk on top of the tracks. A couple of them were
good. But I think my favorite so far is the above, for
actually transforming the song in a meaningful way while
staying true to its emotion.
