MicroMusicMakers:

As an experiment, I've signed up for a weblog on xanga.com. My goal
is to post at least some fragment of a composition I'm working on
every day. It's kind of an motivational hammer to get the next piece
of music finished.

If you are interested in the process, visit
http://www.xanga.com/music1024 . It contains links to short (10-30
second) fragments of the day's progress on a piece called "Resolution
in Blue" for microtonal slide piano, in process. It will also have
references to the Csound code and tuning methodologies. Ten minutes a
day is all I ask...

Prent Rodgers
Mercer Island, WA
