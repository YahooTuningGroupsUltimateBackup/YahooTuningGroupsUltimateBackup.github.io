Hi Folks,
I've recently added modulation tuning to the Scala 2 Kontakt Microtuner.
Any tuning in the Microtuner can now be a source of modulation intervals to
any other tuning. So, for example, a five-limit scale can be modulated by
seven-limit intervals, or a diatonic scale can be modulated chromatically.

Also, I've made real-time scale and key change easier by developing a
companion S2K_Note2cc VST. Using the VST, multi-channel ensembles in
Kontakt can be retuned by simply playing notes on a reserved midi channel.

More info on the above at http://www.12equalboresme.com
