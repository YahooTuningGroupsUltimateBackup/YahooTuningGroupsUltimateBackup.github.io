Oblivion VSTi for Win supports real time changes in
the intonation. Comes with over 400 tunings (TUN and
SCL).

Here is the link:

http://www.oblivionom.com/

Demo is near fully functional for 11 days (LFO3 & LFO4
do not operate in demo).

Register and receive 200 more tunings (Gamelan and
Shruti, lots not found in Scala archive).

Couple more synths and ability to build your own
custom microtonal synth available soon.

Cheers,
David
