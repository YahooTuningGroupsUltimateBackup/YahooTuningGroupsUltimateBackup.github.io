precision 12
end
finish

sent to ir@...

Evaluating Linear Dependence:
Data: [ln(2), ln(3), ln(5),ln(7),ln(11)]

Method: PSLQ
Result (12): [265, -296, -308, 12, 256]

This tells us that 2^265 3^(-296) 5^(-308) 7^12 11^256 is a very small 11-limit interval, or comma.
