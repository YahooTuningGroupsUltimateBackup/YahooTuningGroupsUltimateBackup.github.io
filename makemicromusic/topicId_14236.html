On a silly note, I wrote some music appropriate for babies, inspired
by my 2.5 month old little girl, who we nickname 'babysmush' (she's
got a very round face with a ton of cheek!)

The tuning is the duodene, transposed to an A major tonic. The sounds
are entireley generated by ZynAddSubFX.
