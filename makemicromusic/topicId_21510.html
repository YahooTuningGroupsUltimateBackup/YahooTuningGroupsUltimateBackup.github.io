"Elegy, for Jon" is a JI retuning/rearrangement of a piece called "Solar
Midnight" from Complex Silence 4 (which used a non-rational Phi-based
tuning). Unbeknownst to me, I finished it on the day that my closest
friend Jonathan Gams passed away, and it is offered in his memory.

Made with Csound and blue using Kraig Grady's Centaur tuning.

- Dave
