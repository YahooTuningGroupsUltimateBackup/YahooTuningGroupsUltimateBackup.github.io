I finished the fourth piece in my attempt to make a CD for the RPM Challenge in the month of February. I finished four songs, which is less than the target of five cuts, but it is 35 minutes, which is allowed by the rules. And I really finished one of them at the end of January. But I had to build many of the instruments from raw materials to get the samples I wanted of long strings, balloon drums, and tube drums.

The latest is more melodic than the last ones.
