Here's another one, also made with Csound/blue, in which I continue to
explore a new (to me) way of generating a harmonic/rhythmic texture with
just intonation scales (this time, a pelog subset of Kraig's Centaur).
The scale is expressed in three different octaves at three different
rates of speed.

The scale: 1/1 21/20 7/6 3/2 14/9 2/1
