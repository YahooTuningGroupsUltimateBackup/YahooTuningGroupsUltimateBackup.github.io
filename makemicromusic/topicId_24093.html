A short (< 2 mins) harpsichord piece - there are some sections that obviously need revising but I don't have time right now. This started as an improvisation which I edited and then hit 3x on the tempo - I can't play runs like that!!

You should be able to play it online here
http://improvfriday.ning.com/xn/detail/4162021:Comment:22715

or

download it here

http://micro.soonlabel.com/hobbit_scales/daily201031--hobbit-archytas12.mp3

I used Gene's archytas hobbit tuning.
