Not that I know of, unless you want to try midi relaying with scala and midi yoke (I don't think ableton live works with it, the root note gets stray pitch bends). Eve One is a free microtuner:

http://dashsignature.nusofting.com/products/eve_one.htm

Also zynaddsubfx is great, even though is was made for linux originally. I often play with the standalone windows version 2.2.0:

http://sourceforge.net/project/showfiles.php?group_id=62934

Someone tried to make it a windows plugin, but it's not perfect:

http://personales.ya.com/mahtazz/ZynAddSubFX_Setup_Beta5.exe
