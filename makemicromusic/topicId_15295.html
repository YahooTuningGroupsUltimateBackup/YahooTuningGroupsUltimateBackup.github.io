One of my favorite podcasts is NonPop by Scott Unrein. Today he
released show #21, with some of Kyle Gann's music. I would recommend
that you subscribe if possible using this link and a podcast reader,
like PodcastReady, or iTunes: http://feeds.feedburner.com/Nonpop

http://nonpop.scottunrein.org/ is the web site where you can listen at
your computer.

Here's what he has to say about show 21:

1. "Custer and Sitting Bull: Sun Dance/Battle of Greasy-Grass River" 7:59
by Kyle Gann
Custer's Ghost
Monroe Street Music (MSM-60104)

2. "Soul Train" 17:41
by Mikel Rouse
Dennis Cleveland
New World (80506-2)

3. "Dream" 8:16
by John Cage
Piano Music by John Cage (Louis Goldstein, piano)
Greenseye (4794)

Is that a cool mix or what? While you're there, check out show #19. 1.
"Decasia - Excerpt One" by Michael Gordon is great.

Prent Rodgers
