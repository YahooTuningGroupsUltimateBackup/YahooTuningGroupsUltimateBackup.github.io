The synthesizer is my Fender Mustang + Gr-20 synth re-tuned on the fly to a tuning that approximates Bohlen - Pierce - each step in the scale is the 13th root of 3.
The drums are Sonar's SI-Drum kit driven by a Simmons SDMP1 which I got for a good price. Nonetheless - don't expect a groove - I'm not a drummer by any means!
