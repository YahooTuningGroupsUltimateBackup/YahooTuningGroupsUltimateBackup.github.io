Hello all

Its been a while since I've posted here (or anywhere for that
matter). We moved to Tucson at the end of last year, and now feel
settled in. I've been pretty busy with several projects.
One is I had Intervals of Motion remastered , added a new song and
spruced it up for general distribution. Several of you on the list
had asked me last year to announce when the remix was ready and it
now is. Its featured today on CDbaby, which was an enjoyable
surprise, along with some of Jacky's comments.

http://www.cdbaby.com/

I 've been busy here with new music having met up with some great
musicians. You can hear a little of what we are up to at

http://elucida.com/VECA_CHANT.html

(Veca Brouha is that magic 72, and Manu COdes is 81, 104, 109 and 131
ET) Jacky has agreed to help produce this stuff, and I think that is
just going take it over the edge. I've also become something of a JI
Mooney and have been working a lot with the very simple kring and
ekkring tunings. You can hear them if you wander through the elucida
site.

Any way I'm baaaack for a while :-)

Peace,
Mary
