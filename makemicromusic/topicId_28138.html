What You Forgot is an electronics / percussion piece that uses Absynth and Dimension Pro in two different harmonic series tuning (5 limit and 7 limit). The percussion is provided by Kontakt. The piece was preformed on an AXiS 49 in one pass while at the Xenharmonic Praxis Summer Camp. Consider it a celebration of starting feel like a human again after my hospital stay.
