Hi Jon --

> Apologies if you've posted details of your system before, but: how much RAM
> do you run?

No problem. I have 512 meg of RAM, 1.8 GHz CPU, Windows XP. I run VAZ
Modular as the VST host, with Rhino instances as "channel inserts" in the
mixing console. (Heh!) While running, it appears to require about 300 meg
of real memory. I do find that about 8 or 9 voices is really pushing my
system to 100%.

> do you ever run into latency issues when you are recording, or is this another
> one of your projects where you enter your notated score into Finale and
> play through Rhino?

Yes, it's Finale-to-Rhino. I don't have any problems with latency when
composing. I use a MIDI keyboard, input through Finale which sends MIDI
output via Midi yoke to VAZ Modular, and I don't experience noticeable lag.
Well, I don't play very quickly, but with chord/melody playing at moderate
speed, it doesn't seem like molasses or anything.

I use an Aureal Vortex sound card. It is full duplex, so I can record the
sound card's output with CoolEdit, which is sometimes useful. But for this
type of music, I have recently been capturing the audio output just by
using the "Capture" command in VAZ Modular and going to 44kHz stereo WAV
files. The synth output goes direct to disk and bypasses the sound card
entirely, so it's very nice. I use CoolEdit to adjust the overall volume
and clip the silent ends of the WAV files, then output an MP3 copy.

Rick
