I've got a new piece that uses the Wendy Carlos Harmonic tuning (and
my TG77/A5000) and is a celestially inspired processional. The title
comes from a photographic series by August Strindberg in which he made
photographs directly exposed to the night sky, without a lens. He
believed the lens warped the direct expression of the stars. Just
getting my feet wet for a new album to be called Celestographs. I used
my old TG77 piped into my A5000 for this piece.

http://www.harrington.lunarpages.com/mp3/celestographs/Jeff-Harrington-Celestograph_I.mp3

Here's an article about his work The Celestographs of August Strindberg

http://www.cabinetmagazine.org/issues/3/celesographs.php

Sorry haven't been around much recently; haven't had the opportunity
to do any new microtuning music. That's changing now that I'm living
under new circumstances! :)

Jeff
http://jeffharrington.org
