The Short Description: "In this society, the available means for music-making
are a product of the ruling musical system. To imagine anything different would
be to invite the (im)possible tasks of designing new instruments, new
orchestras, new human capacities, new concert formats. Microtonal design is a
seminar for unsticking the ear and everything attached to it."
