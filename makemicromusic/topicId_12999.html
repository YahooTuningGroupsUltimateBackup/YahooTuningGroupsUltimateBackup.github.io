Hello, everyone, and please let me begin by warmly thanking Prent
Rodgers for making me a part of MMMDay and the ongoing MMMYear even
before I learned about them by including _In Hora Observationis_ in
his podcast. Thank you, Prent, for helping make so much music from so
many people more easily available.

Also, before offering a first knowing submission for MMMYear, I'd like
to congratulate Christopher Bailey on _Balladei_, a title that might
suggest to me some 14th-century French ballade, especially given the
links to a neo-Gothic style. It's something fascinating, and I'm
trying to start by "mapping" the dynamics in different portions, so
that I can adjust the volume as needed to hear the quiet parts while
keeping the louder ones within an agreeable range for my imperfect
ears. What I have heard already intrigues me, and I'm looking forward
to getting to know it better as well as to your theoretical discussion.
