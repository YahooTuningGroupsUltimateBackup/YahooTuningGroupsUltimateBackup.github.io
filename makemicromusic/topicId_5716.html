Hi!

Been years since being in a Rat Shack, but they used to offer a factory
second from Crown on a PZM microphone that sounds ideal for your needs. It
is a battery powered condenser microphone with a nearly hemispherical
pattern. Remember, garbage in = garbage out, the quality of the microphone
will be nearly the sole determination of how good it is going to sound
otherwise, so break out a few bucks if you have to, it's important!

Also, check out this month's issue of Electronic Musician, mostly devoted to
making music using a laptop. The cover has a Neumann TL-103, I don't think
you need to spend THAT much!!

Bonnie *:>
