Hi there!

My name is Andrew Heathwaite, and I've been studying
microtonality/xenharmony on the internet for several months. I've recently
recorded a few very short keyboard improvisations in some equal
temperaments, and I'd like to share them with this list. They represent my
first creative experiments with xenharmony, and I think they came out pretty
ok. If anyone has comments/critiques, I'd be interested in hearing them.
There are only three pieces on the site right now, but I will add more as
they come. I've been very moved by the sounds and possibilities of
alternate tunings, and I plan to continue working with them. Thanks in
advance for listening.

-Andrew
