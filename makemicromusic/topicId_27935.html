Brand-New 16-EDO City of the Asleep Track on a "normal" Compilation!

cityoftheasleep <igliashon@...>

5/13/2011 1:32:45 PM

Hey gang, I got asked by my good friend Zach (aka Yojimbo Billions) to produce a cover/remix of a love song for a compilation on the lyfstyl.tumblr.com music blog called "Mad Love in Crazy Times". So I took an a cappella version of "I Am Stretched on Your Grave", transcribed the melody in 12-TET, re-tuned it to 16-EDO anti-diatonic, then used it to vocode the original vocal and wrote and epic Armodue-inspired dub-step/glitch/metal track around it. It's the 2nd track on the compilation, and you can hear it here:

http://soundcloud.com/lyfstyl/sets/mad-love-in-crazy-times-1/

or here:

http://lyfstyl.bandcamp.com/album/mad-love-in-crazy-times

The rest of the tracks are non-microtonal, but pretty good (IMO) if you like electronic music. Please give a listen!

-Igs
