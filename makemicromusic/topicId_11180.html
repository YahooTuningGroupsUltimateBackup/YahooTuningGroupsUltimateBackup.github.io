Make a beautiful score, with lots of detail about dynamics and articulation, then send it to one of the quartets that specialize in doing the difficult: the Ardittis or the Bozzinis, for example. With many groups, even if they decide not to play the piece, they'll give it a private reading and let the composer know why the piece doesn't work for them.
