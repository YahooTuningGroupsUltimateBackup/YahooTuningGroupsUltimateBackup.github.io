It's been an unusually productive weekend for me. Here's a set of two
short studies based on Wilson/Grady meta-slendro scales (in just
intonation, of course). These are basically experiments along one of my
interests, that of generating rhythmic textures that are directly
derived from harmonic structures. There are more (possibly hopelessly
obscure) details on the SoundCloud page.
