Chris wrote:

>Carl,
>Thanks! This software looks really interesting.

>What alternate tuning would you (or anyone else) suggest for a piece
>like this?

Good question. It's a long piece so it's hard to just audition
a bunch of tunings. It also gets around quite a bit, so it'll
probably wind up that something very close to 12-ET is preferred.
But I might take a few bars from different parts of the piece as
audition material and try things like

diminished[12]
augene[12]
dominant[12]
pajara[12]
stretchtone[12]
superpythag[12]

If none of those work (and they may not), go for a well temperament
like alaska7. See below for scala files for these.

-Carl
