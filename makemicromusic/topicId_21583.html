Hi all,

The ICMC has now set up the portal for submissions to the UnTwelve/60x60
April 27th project at the Museum of Contemporary Art in Chicago. Remember,
you microtonal work of 1-minute or less could not only be heard in Chicago,
but at the large retrospective of electronic music in 2010 at the ICMC! So,
this is a worthwhile project to submit to!

Submission details are at http://www.untwelve.org/60x60collaboration.html

The deadline for submission is Dec. 31st

We hope to hear some of your music in Chicago....good luck!!!

--

Aaron Krister Johnson
