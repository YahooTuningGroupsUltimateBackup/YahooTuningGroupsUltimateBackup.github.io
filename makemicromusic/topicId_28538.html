Yeni olarak dijital ortamda elden geçirilmiş "Impromptu Oyunhavası" bestemi aşağıdaki linkten dinleyebilirsiniz:

http://www.ozanyarman.com/muzikler.html

Bu eser "Mikrotonal Gitar İkilisi" için bestelendi, Tolgahan Çoğulu ve Sinan Cem Eroğlu tarafından, Uğur Keçecioğlu'nun ritimsaz eşliğinde MELM (www.mtonalist.org) kapsamında ilk kez seslendirildi.

________________

You may listen to the newly digitally edited "Impromptu Oyunhavası" here:

http://www.ozanyarman.com/musics.html

This work was composed for the "Microtonal Guitar Duo", premiered by Tolgahan Çoğulu & Sinan Cem Eroğlu rhytmically accompanied by Uğur Keçecioğlu within the context of MELM (www.mtonalist.org).

Dr. Oz.

Thanks Chris!

In response also to John Schneider's inquiry, commatic calibrations of sorts for a quick & dirty microtonal intonation effect was achieved in Logic Pro using the following offsets from 12-tET:

C +22 cents
C# 0
D +3 cents
D# -32 cents
E 0
F 0
F# 0
G +5 cents
G# -7 cents
A 0
A# -32 cents
B 0

This yields the following scale:

0: 22.000 cents
1: 122.000 cents
2: 225.000 cents
3: 290.000 cents
4: 422.000 cents
5: 522.000 cents
6: 622.000 cents
7: 727.000 cents
8: 815.000 cents
9: 922.000 cents
10: 990.000 cents
11: 1122.000 cents
12: 1222.000 cents

Slided over to C at 0 cents:

0: 1/1
1: 100.000 cents
2: 203.000 cents
3: 268.000 cents
4: 400.000 cents
5: 500.000 cents
6: 600.000 cents
7: 705.000 cents
8: 793.000 cents
9: 900.000 cents
10: 968.000 cents
11: 1100.000 cents
12: 1200.000 cents

The idea is to pronounce the Hijaz & Zirgule flavours (the middle solo guitar improvition part) on B among other things.
