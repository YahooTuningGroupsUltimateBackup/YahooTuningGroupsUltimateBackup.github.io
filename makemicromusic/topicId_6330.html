We all have tunings and scales we like. There may be abstract ways of
evaluating tunings for goodness to fit any number of criteria. But, in the
end, two things affect a tuning:

Timbre and creativity.

Let us all enjoy the freedom of the latter, and encourage the exploration
of the former.

Whatever tuning or scale we choose.
