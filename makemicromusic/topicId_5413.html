I muted every string on my piano with two towels and used two wedges to mute all
but one string of middle-C, sampled it over its entire decay and then fed it to CronoX.
The 'piano' patch I made actually sounds pretty decent--something like a lute stop on
the harpsichord and not at all unpleasant.

Then I did some vocal samples and made some wacked out patches that way. -Justin
