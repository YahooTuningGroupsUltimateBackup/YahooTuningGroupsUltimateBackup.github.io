I'm a subscriber to John Schneider's Global Village program from KPFK in Los Angeles. He always has something unique and wonderful on his Thursday program.

You absolutely must download the latest program from Thu, November 12, 2009, only available for another few days. He played a cut of Jon Catler that was out of this world. You have to advance to the 1 hour and 41 minutes in the program to get to the Catler tune (or listen to the whole thing if you want). The song is called 13 O'clock Blues by Willie McBlind on his album Bad Thing.

The program is available here:
http://archive.kpfk.org/parchive/mp3/kpfk_091112_100030gv_john.MP3
or here: http://64.27.15.184/parchive/ look for Global Village Thursdays (the other days have other people). They drop the shows sometimes very quickly.
