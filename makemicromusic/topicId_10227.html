Hi Jon,

Since you've got a PC, chances are your sound card, or on-motherboard
sound chipset, already has some effects including reverb and chorus,
built in. There is some degree of configurability in most I've seen - it
may not be what consider "good quality" tho. You can use those effects
to transform your replay from any sound source, including line in and CD.
Then record the result, and write it to CD.

That's a zero-cost option, and uses chip hardware rather than software,
which is a sight easier. Still, if you want more configurability, you'll
probably need software.

Regards,
Yahya
