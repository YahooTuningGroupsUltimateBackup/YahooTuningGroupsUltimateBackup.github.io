Jacob Barton and I are organizing a seminar in Microtonal Design with the School
for Designing a Society in Urbana, Illinois.

"In this society, the available means for music-making are a product of the
ruling musical system. To imagine anything different would be to invite the
(im)possible tasks of designing new instruments, new orchestras, new human
capacities, new concert formats. Microtonal design is a seminar for unsticking
the ear and everything attached to it."

We're assembling a crew of dreamer-doers to design together, with meetings
running February 7 to May 2 in Urbana.

News! We have scholarships available. We want to work with people who are
very excited about microtonal collaborations and social change. It needs you
to happen, so get in touch today!
