The Specter of Zombie Turkeys is an ambient piece with 12 edo sampled
guitar + effects + voices and 12 of 31 edo tuned synthesizers (Zeta+
2.1 and Dimension pro) plus performed 22″ wind gong, 6″ Tibetan
Singing bowl, and various china, crash and ride cymbals. Mastered in
Sonar X2.
