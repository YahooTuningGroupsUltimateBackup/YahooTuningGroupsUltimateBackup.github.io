Gathered music-makers,

No fair when the starter of the list goes on and on and never posts
music, huh? But while I haven't started in on my new round of
writing, I've looked again for little moments of past microtonality,
and so there is one online for a short time.

I've uploaded a short excerpt from a dance score I did, best I can
recall, back in 1986 or 87, called "Fallen Angels". At one point in
the dance, the motion slowed considerably and seemed to float in
space, and it called for a very different (and fairly oblique) change
in the music. So this is the chunk that it happened in.

Early drum machine, live percussion, and Prophet 5. No particular
theory or pre-set tuning, just floating out of 12-tET for a moment...

Cheers,
