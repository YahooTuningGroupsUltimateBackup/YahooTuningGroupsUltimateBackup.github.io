Arabic keyboard proposals seem to have two catagories of solutions:

1) Have all tones available by providing more keys.

2) Double (or triple) loading of 12 keys through switching.

With most oriental music a selection of scale occurs. In Carnatic
music for example 72 scales are created from 23 tones(if memory
serves me well). Each scale has 7 tones. With few exception
Arabic scales have 7 to 9 tones (assending and desending differences
accounting for the extra notes}.

I would like to point out that I have drop the multiple key approach
from my current thinking. The post "Arabic Tone Box" reflects my
current thinking and current Arabic practice. Please weave these two
threads together.
