List,

It's been a few days, and quite a few emails. Lots of good input and a lot of heart-warming personal msgs to me. Profoundly thank all the people that have expressed their thoughts on this forum, past, present, and future.

So.

I need to separate myself, and I also wanted a clear break from what *was*. But I mistakenly thought it really wasn't a big deal. I don't see many people post, I certainly haven't gotten a lot of feedback on past requests for projects, etc. Nonetheless, you're out there, and you know what you want.

So MMM isn't going into deep-freeze.

At some point in the very near future, Ownership of the list (these are Yahoo! terms, not grandiose entitlements) is going to be transferring to Prent Rodgers, and Moderator status will be given to Rick McGowan. It will be up to them to add other moderators if they wish.

Prent and Rick have been around since the beginning, and I have the utmost respect for them, both in terms of music-making as well as their level demeanors and technical skills in internet matters. The list will be in good hands.

When I have a couple extra moments, I'll write up a little post-script and send it to the list when we change ownership and a few details. To those of you that choose to stay, you'll still have the resources of the list as microtonal music moves further into this millenium. For those who might leave, all I hope is that you leave to make more music somewhere, anywhere else.

Until the stagehands drop the curtain,
Creating new scales,
Soon to be ex-ListMom,
Jon
