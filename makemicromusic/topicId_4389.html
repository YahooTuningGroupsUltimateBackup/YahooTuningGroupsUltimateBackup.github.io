In case any of you microtonalists haven't followed things since last night... Rhino, the Big Tick newest soft synth, has been released.

http://bigtick.pastnotecut.org

I highly recommend you look at it. Special price is 80 euros until April when it goes up to 100 euros. Well worth it.

I have been able to emulate a few patches from my TX802, and this is a solid FM implementation that goes well beyond the TX802 capabilities.
