Sorry to anyone that tried to downloaded "A Great Crossing" from my
website, as the files were corrupted. I've fixed that, as well as
uploaded some other things. Please download away, and write me with
your reactions! I've pasted descriptions below.

A Great Crossing - This is a 30-minute piece of pure electronic music.
Please enjoy the download, but you may also be able to purchase a
surround-sound dvd! If you are interested, e-mail me.

Beresheit - This piece was written for choir, string quintet, and a
pair of microtuned keyboards. It's the largest ensemble I've ever
worked with! The text is from the first few sentences of the Torah.

Orestes Variations - This is a trio for violin and two 'cellos. It
begins with a melody from ancient Greece (I think circa 5th-century
b.c.), which is reworked in successive variations. Can you hear the
theme throughout?
