HI There,

Here is a piece I did after travelling in a coach with a
beautiful tampura type drone in the sounds it made as
it travelled along.

http://www.mp3.com.au/track.asp?id=47823

Thought you might like it especially Joseph? Do you
remember suggesting I listen out for coach music.
It has rather transformed some of my journeys
into a most pleasurable experience - you never know what
kind of a music it will make.

Using the good old FM7 for the voice, tweaked
one of them to sound as much as I could make it
coach sounds. But you need to imagine really a
lot of other notes and sounds from the engine
going on as well and this was quite a subtle
effect on top of it, quite quiet and gentle
- it was most beautiful :-).

I don't think I'll try to do the rest of the noises
though!

Robert
