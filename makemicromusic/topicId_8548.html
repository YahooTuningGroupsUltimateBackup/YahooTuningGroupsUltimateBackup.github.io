Hey everybody, I just uploaded a new rock song in 31-tET. It's
called "Obrundul, or the Thromboid Parallel". Listen to it here:

http://www.soundclick.com/bands/1/cityoftheasleepmusic.htm

It's rather sandy-sounding, with a pounding groove in 7/4 meter.
Mostly based on a Gb septimal minor scale, but there are plenty of
microchromatic embellishments. This one is much less of an improv
and much more of a structured song.
