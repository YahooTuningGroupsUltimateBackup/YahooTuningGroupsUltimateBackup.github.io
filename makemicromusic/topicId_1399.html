John,

JICalc is not compatible with G3 Macs as you have
probably discovered and it is also unable to work with
USB Macs as it uses the old Serial Manager only.

My Li'l Miss Scale Oven is the only software I know of
that will work for you. It runs on 9.1 and is fully
compatible with OMS. It uses its own file format, and
also imports Scala format files. It's a robust
commercial program, and even displays the scale
graphically. Scales can be edited in cents, ratios or
equal-divisions and converted among the various
representations. Contact me if you are interested.
