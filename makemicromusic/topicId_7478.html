I've started to upload some of my microtonal piano music. I think
piano music is one of the hardest things for me to be happy writing;
in other words feeling like I have something meaningful to say with
it in my own way. As it turns out the Sovittaja Park Preludes wound
up being some of my favorite pieces that I've written, but I'm not so
sure how their sparse textures and subtle, personal language will
translate in an mp3 format... so with that in mind I've included two
other piano pieces that probably project better.
