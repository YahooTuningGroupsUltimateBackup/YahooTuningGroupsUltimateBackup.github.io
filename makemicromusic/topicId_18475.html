Im a big fan of the galbanum architecture pack. More waveforms than you could ever dream of and really cheap.

The universal and CE packs are just .wav files so you can load them into Kontakt 2 or loads of other samplers / synths.

http://www.galbanum.com/products/architecture-universal/

and

http://www.galbanum.com/products/architecturewaveforms-ce/

im not sure what the difference in content is. the universal says there are 1,800 waveforms and CE says 2,500 so maybe its best to get the CE version (which is the one I have)
