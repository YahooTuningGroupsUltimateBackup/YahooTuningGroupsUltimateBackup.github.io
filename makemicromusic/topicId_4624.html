Dear Group,

The way Arab keyboardist get 3/4 tone steps is a QUARTER TONE BOX.

"THE BASIC IDEA OF A QUARTER TONE BOX IS THAT IT HAS 13 SWITCHES (12
ASSIGNED TO THE 12 KEYS ON A KEYBOARD, AND A 13TH BUTTON AS A CANCEL
BUTTON). WHEN PUSHING A BUTTON (OR FLIPPING A SWITCH) OF A CERTAIN
KEY, THAT KEY (ON ALL OCTAVES OF THE KEYBOARD) GETS LOWERED BY A
QUARTER TONE (-1/4). SO IF YOU WANTED E AND B TO BE LOWERED YOU WOULD
SIMPLY PUSH THE TWO RESPECTIVE BUTTONS, AND SO ON...THE CANCEL BUTTON
IS SIMPLY THE SWITCH THAT CANCELS ANY ACTIVE QUARTER TONE BUTTONS."
