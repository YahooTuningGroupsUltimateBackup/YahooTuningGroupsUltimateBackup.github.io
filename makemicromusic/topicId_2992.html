>No softare sampler will give you the polyphony of a hardware sampler and no hardware sampler will give you as much sound design flexibility as a software sampler.

From NI website:
"KONTAKT's advanced design and dynamic resource allocation ensure that its audio engine is always running at optimal efficiency, for up to 256 stereo voices per instance on a standard computer."

The Akai S5000/6000 has 64 voice polyphony and can be upgraded to 128 with a $500 expansion board. Kontakt has 256 _per instance_ with the number of instances limited only by total CPU/RAM.
