For your Valentines day pleasure:

/makemicromusic/files/sethares/Dream2Beat.mp3

is a 19-tet folksy microlove song "Dream to the Beat".
Like "Signs", this was written in a microtonal frenzy
sometime in the summer of 1991. At the time I wrote:

********************

The nature of relationships is that they change
continuously. Each word and each smile sends a
message. The ones we love echo back the word
and the smile... And then we hear the word,
see the smile and re-echo our own reply...
each action causes an infinity of reverberations
that change us. In changing you, I change myself.
In moving you, I move myself.

********************
