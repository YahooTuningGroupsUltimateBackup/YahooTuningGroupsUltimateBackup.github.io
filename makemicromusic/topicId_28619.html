Hi Caleb,

Thanks for the listen and comment. In this case the feedback was created using an "ebow". Ebow is a device that senses string vibration and then injects a magnetic pulse back into the string. It sometimes brings out harmonics and certainly sounds like feedback if yoy want. (There are many ways to use it). They cost about $100usd and if you play electric guitar I recommend the device highly. I've had one since about 1980 and still can find new sounds.

Chris
