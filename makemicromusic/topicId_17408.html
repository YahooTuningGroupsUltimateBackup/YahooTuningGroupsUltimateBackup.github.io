It is meta Slendro,and was done on a pump organ. yes the unevenness is in the volume of the chord members. there are 5 notes in each chord 2 of which are30-70 cents away from others.
I have played a vibes version too for fun and might use that next week.
In a month i play with a trumpet player Kris Tiner and i through this up so he could play with it. Something quick in the interest of time.
So that's why i shared it here. A tabla player Cory Beers rounds out this trio- The Emperor of Ice Cream
