Hi all,

I have a new piece up called "Gyre". It's built on La Monte Young's Magic Opening Chord and uses binaural beating where the beat frequency is proportional to the pitch, creating a kind of psychoacoustic polyrhythm. I made it with blue and Csound and a bunch of sine waves, and no effects or additional processing. Headphones strongly recommended for full effect. I hope you enjoy it.
