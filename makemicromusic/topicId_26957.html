Really glad to see that Chris put the "Eartha" CD online. After 13 years, I think it holds up very well. To folks who don't know...it's basically a compilation of music sent to me by the artists who wanted their music on the CD....we had a list discussion about who wanted to participate, and what you hear are the results. I simply took the DAT tapes I was sent, and assembled them into the CD, got the art done, and pressed it. Each artist also sent their own liner notes, which I think are superb...one can learn a lot just by reading them. And I played guitar on Starrett's two pieces, and Ernie's song too. Sj Taylor's monster composition is a masterpiece, and Starrett's "Citified Notions" is another personal fave...also a bitch to play.
Glad ya'll are getting a chance to hear it, and boy, big thanks to Chris for putting it out there...best...Hstick
