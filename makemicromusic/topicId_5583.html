Carl,

Oh I wish I had saved the tuning either in mind or head. (... searching ... ) I did! It was
1/1 17/16 10/9 5/4 6/5 4/3 3/2

I used an Alesis QS 6.1 - not retunable - and MIDI relay (FTS). I was just playing entering some values in FTS and checking the sound coming out.

Very often I just improvise and if some feeling or chord feels fine, I record. Later I ask myself about the tuning. I should get used to write it down somewhere (and remember, where ;) ...

There are two new pieces, if you feel like them - one is in the Japanese Koto Scale, [www.ichbinamleben.de/music/the entertainers resumee.mp3] and the other, [www.ichbinamleben.de/music/juggling the clown.mp3] is in ... well, I am doubting about the tuning of this one. Maybe... well, it must be an ancient tuning the Europeans used times ago... 12-TET? ... i am not quiet sure. I was not busy a lot with the tunings of this time.
