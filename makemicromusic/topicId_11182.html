Divide by Pi's latest release, 'Ice Ritual', is now available at

http://www.cdbaby.com/dividebypi2

I think it's a great release, and musically and sonically well in advance of
our last. A prevelant obsession on this disk is Gene's 'aaron.scl', a 3-and-7
harmonic based scale. Background on this Fokker-block scale Gene discovered
is here:

http://launch.groups.yahoo.com/group/tuning/message/53059

Of course, there are many aspects to Divide By Pi's music that are of interest
besides the tuning---primarily, timbre is hugely important. And the element
of the surreal, of course.

We hope you will buy and enjoy...

Thanks,
Aaron.
