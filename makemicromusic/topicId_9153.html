I listen to a lot of Podcasts, since I'm a serial podcaster myself. I recently heard a wonderful microtonal bell piece by John Maxwell Hobbs on Cinema Volta, at http://www.cinemavolta.com/daily/ . The one from 2/17 was microtonal: http://homepage.mac.com/john.maxwell.hobbs/dailyambience/Cinema.Volta.2005-02-17.mp3

He wrote to say the following:
--------------------------------------------------------------
This was just a standard scale, tweaked a little bit. The adjustments are:
