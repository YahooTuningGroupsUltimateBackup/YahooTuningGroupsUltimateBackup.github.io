Boston-area micromusicmakers:

Probably unbeknownst to most of you, I moved to the
Boston area from Minneapolis last winter. I've
certainly enjoyed the richer cultural offerings here,
but do miss my ol' musical collaborators back there
like guitar builder/guitarist Kris Peck and instrument
builder Dixie Treichel.

As I prepare for my participation in the El Paso
Micro-Hoot in November, I think I'll be ready by
mid-Sept. through October to demonstrate my Clavette
microtonal MIDI keyboard controller with 3D pedals,
and my soon-to-arrive 890-key Microzone from Starr
Labs.

What I don't know is--WHERE & WHEN might I do such a
demo? A classroom or church basement should be just
fine. I can cart around my own 140W amp & my
equipment, which should all fit well enough in my
Hyundai subcompact. I think that 1-2 hours should be
enough time.

Any ideas?
