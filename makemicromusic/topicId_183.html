In Praise of CoolEdit

John A. deLaubenfels <jdl@...>

8/1/2001 12:27:28 PM

I came across CoolEdit somehow, years ago, and immediately found it _so_
much easier to use than some other programs. I spent $49.95, or
something like that, to register it, and have added a few bucks now
and then to upgrade.

I now have a home studio for under $100.00, with full duplex ability
to layer track upon track in the digital realm, and as soon as I get
the courage, and a high-speed connection, I'll upload a song with my
very own almost-adequate voice, all done on CoolEdit. (somehow, at
least, I feel more comfortable here than on that nasty_music list;
wonder why?).

I do believe we should all, poverty-stricken as we are, try to
contribute a few reasonable bucks to the companies who provide such
wonderful software.

JdL
