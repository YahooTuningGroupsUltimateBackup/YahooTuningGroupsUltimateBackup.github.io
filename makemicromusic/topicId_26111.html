You know, while the whoopin' and hollerin' about "songs," pieces," whatever was occurring on this list, there were hundreds of folks here who were hearing microtonal music for the 1st time, and having a pretty favorable reaction. A 12 year old boy who plays guitar told me his mind was blown, and another young guitarist said he had never heard anything like it...maybe these are the future microtonalists? Many other people told me they really liked what they heard...the sound man was so inspired he said his band rehearsed till 3 AM...plus sold/gave away 20 CD's, and got killer press. Wish more folks here were out playing and spreading the concept of micro music...off to Denver...Hstick www.microstick.net
