Hi Jake

Modplug can do this and supports alternate tunings last I looked if you use samples.

Its free but not at all perfect. It is open source. www.modplug.com should get you there.

A side note; I think all tracker software supports playing on a pc keyboard. Modplug is just the only one I know that supports alternate tunings.

Chris
