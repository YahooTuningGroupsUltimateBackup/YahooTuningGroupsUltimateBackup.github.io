Well, I've had these microtonal guitars staring at me for a while now,
and it's slowly become clear to me that I am not doing them justice.
I do not play them nearly enough, and I have not even remotely
followed through with the grandiose plans I initially had for them.
So I have decided that I need to pass them on to someone who will make
better use of them.

I have 2 Fender Strats, one with a Catler 12-tone Ultra Plus neck, one
with a Carruthers 22-EDO neck, both of which intonate fabulously and
sound great. Both necks cost me around $500 or so each, and the
guitars that they were grafted onto were both about $700 new,
so...make me an offer?

I also have a 17-EDO acoustic guitar that was made custom for me by
Lukas Brunner in Switzerland. It is a deep resonant instrument meant
to be tuned low and played loud, and I spent a pretty penny on it--a
cool $4000. To get $3000 for it would be sweet, but I'll entertain
other offers.

I'm going to cross-post this to the Tuning List just in case, please
e-mail me off-list if you're interested.
