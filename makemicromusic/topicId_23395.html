10/9/2010 7:01:14 AM

Hi all,

I just released version 2.7 of the Sruti/Drone Box.

Changes in this version:
- all the pitch controls can now be changed in real time now;
- the base pitch can now be tuned to conventional (Western) pitches like
A using note names or pitch-class notation (8ve.num);
- the main oscillator for each drone now plays even when all effects are
leveled off.

As always, this is free and available under a Creative Commons
Attribution-Noncommercial license. Requires Csound and QuteCsound.
