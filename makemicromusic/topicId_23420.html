Heh, I have never seen a Silverlight 4.0 website before...

Short criticism after quick reading:

- Naming the new note "H" makes sense from the point of view of english note names, but will bring trouble to native german speakers, since in german "H" already has a meaning (it is used for the note that in english is called "B").
- As for notation, I think there is no need/use for a new proposal. The tuning of your scale, after all, is 24TET, which has been around for quite a while, and for which there exist well-established standards.

I would be interested to listen to music based on your scale - is there already music around? I think examples of actual music demonstrating the possibilities (preferably great music, of course) are one of the best means to propagate new musical systems.

Good luck in any case!

Hans Straub
