Hey all,

Just thought I'd give a notice about a wonderful post that Margo gave on
non12comp, for those of you who still check that group out....she responded
to my recent post about taking that group down, and wrote eloquently about
what a potentially unique position it could occupy as a somewhat "middle
ground" between MMM (practice) and the old tuning list (anything, but tends
to be mostly theory), where one could get more descriptive and in depth in a
smaller group about what specific techniques of counterpoint, timbre, style,
etc one might use in a given tuning or temperament.
