Gertrude Stein meets Picasso in 7-tet

Hi All,

A while ago I had put Gertrude Stein's poem
"A Completed Portrait of Picasso" to music -- it
is in 7-TET, and is sung/read by the author.
Last year when I was in Paris, I took lots of
photos at the Picasso museum.
It occurred to me that it would be fun to illustrate
the song with the photos -- the result is called
"An Incomplete Portrait of Gertrude Stein," and
you can hear/see it:

http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gL9ccnZOfOk

Comments/questions/critiques are welcome...

Bill Sethares
