Hello Alison!
bows can be seen here
http://www.eee.org/bus/omega/acc.htm
easy links to the instruments

I also have looked at the harmonization material reproduced in Chalmers book and
haven't noticed a particular easy code to break besides trail and error .
I will try to ask Erv.
The tetrachords you have are quite unusual which makes me think the numbers
will be high. I notice that the chord second from the bottom alway includes a
notes from both upper and lower tetrachords. Often though if the 3/2 is held the
4/3 is not included in any of the chords!? which means not all the tones are
harmonized or you need to use both the harmonic and subharmonic solutions to get
them all.
