This is a piece for 3 software synthesizers tuned to Gene Ward Smith's
chromatic pair tuning Indium 17 plus drum samples and fretless guitar.

Online play, the details and a bunch of links and a picture of the element
Indium are to be found here:
http://chrisvaisvil.com/?p=1532

A direct download can be found at this link:
http://micro.soonlabel.com/chromatic_pairs/indium17/daily20111101-indium17.mp3

Have a great day!
