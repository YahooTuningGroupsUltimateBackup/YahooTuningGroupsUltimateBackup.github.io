Judas Iscariot Recants His Betrayal exclusively uses Sonic Couture's EP73
Deconstructed and is an ambient leaning piece performed live. The piece
uses 3 simultaneous tunings, 7 limit JI, Carlos Gamma, and unoctave reduced
harmonic series and five EP73 sample sets (normal, mallet, bowed, plucked,
and effects). The retuning was accomplished with Sonic Couture's
Xenharmonium script.
