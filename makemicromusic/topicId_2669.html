I don't know how you ever stumbled upon the page of Christos Hatsis!

I went to school with Christos at Eastman.

It's nice to see that he became such a sucess as a composer! Large
works, tenured position in Toronto, Canada!

Actually, it's great because when he was at Eastman he was the
subject of *much* scorn.

He was unconventional, and wrote fairly avant garde works and was
subjected to a lot of disdain and mockery.

Now, where are all those people? He's triumphed over them!

Christos won in the end! Huge works, an amazing website.

It's heartening...
