Kraig asked a few days ago about how to play Real Audio files without the Real Audio player.
I'm not sure if there are any other devices out there that can decode the .ram files, though there
is a stripped down version of the Real Player called "Real Alternative". Basically it is the RA
player without all the bullshit spyware installed. I swear that my computer operated *everything*
30% faster once I got rid of the Spawn of Satan, aka Real Audio Player. Do a Google search on
"Real Alternative" to find out how to download it. I don't know if there is a Mac version...
