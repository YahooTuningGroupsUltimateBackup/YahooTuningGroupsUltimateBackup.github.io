Kraig Grady <kraiggrady@...>

10/17/2008 7:38:17 PM

Some of my favorite music on this planet and something i would play allot of on my radio show. I put this up on my blog which i thought might interest some. Their music is general is highly developed. I have quite a few recordings of these people and each regional group tends to have there own tuning quite distinct to others ( despite what has been written in journals) . This is explainable by being on different islands, island areas.
