Hi there,

Just to mention an idea I've been exploring.

Give it a whirl to see what we are missing :-).

http://www.robertinventor.com/microtonal_firebird_scripts/discus.cgi

You can post as "anonymous"

As you will see, it does have lots of advantages in
the way of display formatting - things you can't
do with yahoogroups.

The pro verison will let you upload any kinds of attachments.
The free version only lets you upload images.

One big disadvantage for a high volume list like
this one - it doesn't yet let you send
the e-mails in digest form. That is something
the company are thinking about adding in the future
but there are practical difficulties involved
apparently.

You _can_ opt to only be sent notifications about particular
threads.

This is the free version. The pro version will
also let you edit and delete posts after you
post them and has other useful features.

It is also possible to send e-mails to the
forum via e-mail rather than through the
web page but I would need to contact
my web host to set that up so it
doesn't do that yet.

Maybe it isn't quite what we need now
because of the digests problem,
but I wonder if it might be useful
for something or other?

Robert
