Cinema Volta: Daily Ambience
Fresh Ambient Music Composed Daily
a new tune every day, free of charge
at: http://www.cinemavolta.com/daily

Daily Ambience, a project where I record and release a new ambient
tune daily has reached the final two weeks of it's run.

I find it hard to believe that a year has gone by and I have created
and released almost 365 tracks. I'm not sure what comes next. I'll
take a bit of time off, then plan the next project. I've really
learned a great deal from creating in this fashion, and it's not
something I want to give up. I will probably continue to create on a
regular basic like this because it's a good way to stay on form.

Daily Ambience is available for free on the web at: http://
www.cinemavolta.com/daily

It is also available as a podcast. It can be found on iTunes, simply
by searching for "Cinema Volta."

Take a listen, I'm sure you'll find it interesting.
