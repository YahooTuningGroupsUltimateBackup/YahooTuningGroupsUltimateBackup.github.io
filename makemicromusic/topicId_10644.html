A while ago there was discussion of the lack of
ogg vorbis support for quicktime --
it has (finally) arrived... you can download it at:

http://zskl.zsk.p.lodz.pl/~skali/oggvorbis.html

In QT, you can read and write oggs,
and programs that use quicktime
(like iTunes) can also play them
(just drag them to a playlist).

In celebration, you can find some aliens singing
in a single harmonic series built on a root of
65 Hz. It's called Fumeax (hey, I'm in Paris right
now) and is in ogg form here:

http://www.cae.wisc.edu/~sethares/Fumeax.ogg

or mp3 here:

http://www.cae.wisc.edu/~sethares/Fumeax.mp3

Comments/questions/criticisms welcome!

--Bill Sethares
