Hi all,

It's been nearly a month since I last uploaded files to my soundclick.com
site, so I thought I'd post to let ya'll know there are 6 new ones there as
of this evening, Tuesday, February 10.

http://www.soundclick.com/andrewheathwaite

I moved and had problems getting the internet to work in my new apartment
until tonight. I've recorded many improvisations in non-twelve equal
temperaments since then, so I'll probably continue to upload them at my
leisure. I think they're getting better, but let me know if you disagree.

I'm currently student teaching with a couple of music teachers in Syracuse.
It's going very well, and I'm having much more fun than I expected. I have
decided to focus more on student teaching than xenharmonics for a few
months, so if I record more improvisations they will be few. I was doing
both, but I'm finding it's a lot of new stuff for me to have in my brain at
once, and it's sometimes overwhelming.

Tonight is also the first chance I've had to listen to the pieces by "pfly"
and Prent Rogers, both of which I think are great. Before I started doing
any of this stuff myself, I found music by both of you and it inspired me.
These pieces have been a treat for me tonight. Thank you.

Merry musicmaking to all,
Andrew Heathwaite
