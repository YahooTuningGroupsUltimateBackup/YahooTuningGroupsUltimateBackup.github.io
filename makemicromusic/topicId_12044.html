There are lotsa ways that you can import various sample formats
(incl. Akai S1000 and S3000, giga, SF2, ReCycle, & Vienna into .exs24
in Logic Pro 7.
(but it's expensive and only for Mac OSX - about $1000);

Cameleon 5000 is an amazing plug-in for microtuning samples, synths,
effects etc. for Mac & PC and relatively low cost (look out for group
buys/student discounts etc.)
I paid <$100 about two years ago, and they provide regular free
upgrades.

It microtunes via read and play .ana.tun files. (see recent posting
in various tuning related list for conversion advice from .scl files),
and can read, resynthesise and play almost any audio file format.

It also offers lotsa free user constructed patches and effects, from
its community pages, and has a readable and comprehensive manual

All its very flexible features are not immediately obvious, so it has
quite a steep learning curve,
yet if you have experience with old analog hardware synths; you'll
appreciate the possibilities and be in familiar territory.

Details are here:

http://www.camelaudio.com/cameleon5000.php

More details and tuning codes are here:

http://www.lucytune.com/midi_and_keyboard/pitch_bend.html

Have fun with it!
