I am happy to say that my piece "Free Sue" was selected for the 2011 Vox Novus International mix. The piece is a microtonal piece written in 18 note per octave tuning. You can play the piece at http://chrisvaisvil.com/?p=360 or download it here: http://micro.soonlabel.com/18-ET/60x60-2010-try2-free-sue2.mp3

More information about Vox Novus 60x60 can be found here:

http://www.voxnovus.com/

Have a great day,
