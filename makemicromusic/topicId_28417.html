For the major chord, it has the ratio 4:5:6 just for the fundamentals [to
overtones], first order.

4:5 gives 1 and 9

4:6 gives 2 and 10

5:6 gives 1 and 11
So you have the tone 1,2,3,4,5,6, 9,10,11 with just the fundamentals.
