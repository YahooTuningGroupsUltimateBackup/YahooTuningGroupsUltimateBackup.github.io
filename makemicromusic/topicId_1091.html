Greetings Tuners, music making microtonalists and crazy musicians,

enclosed below concert info on upcoming NY event of significant interest
this weekend:

Michael Harrison's Revelation is based on 3/2 and 7/4 relations, Michael
being a protege and heir to LaMonte Young's line of tuning and music, the
tuning is a Pythagorean cycle of fifts for the white notes with the further
5 at a 7/4 to some of the white notes. I am hoping to bring this piece to
the Planet Tree Music Festival in London in 2002.

I am likely to play a concert in NY the second week of January, I will keep
you posted.....

very best wishes to all pioneers, geniuses, head scratchers, human
spreadsheets, composers, musicians, sages and therapists thereof.......

your friendly and notorious semi-lurker and all of the above,

go well

Lawrence Ball
