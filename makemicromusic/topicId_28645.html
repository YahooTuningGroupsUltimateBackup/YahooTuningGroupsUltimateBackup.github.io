17 Dynes <http://micro.soonlabel.com/17-ET/STE-005-17-dynes.mp3> is an
improvisation with a 17 note per octave electric guitar that was heavily
modified in Melodyne Editor 2.0 DNA. A Dyne is a unit of measure for
surface tension <http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dyne>.

http://chrisvaisvil.com/?p=2235

19th Ide of March
<http://micro.soonlabel.com/19-ET/19th_ide_of_march.mp3>is an ambient
/ glitch synthesizer piece using Absynth 5 in 19 equal notes
per octave performed on an AXiS 49 and Korg nano Kontrol.
