My piece 'Toivo' for 15-string kantele (= Finnish zither) and electronic soundtrack can be
heard here (at 43'40"):

http://www.wnyc.org/stream/ram?file=/newsounds/newsounds012309.mp3

(WNYC New Sounds 2891 / 'New Music from Finland')

The kantele, played by Eija Kankaanranta, is here tuned to the natural harmonics of the
accompanying drone, up to the 13th.

Also the other work of mine heard on the show, the piece for clarinet and kantele, uses a
microtonal tuning for the kantele.

Juhani Nuorvala
http://www.fimic.fi/nuorvala
