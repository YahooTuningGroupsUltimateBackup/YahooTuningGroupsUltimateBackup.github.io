Four additions to complete this list.

Scale twelve: 0-100-150-550-650-700-800-1200.
Chords: I=(0-150-650)
IV=(550-700-1200)
V=(650-800-100)
Intervals: 2-1-8-2-1-2-8.
Altered tetrachord:(8-2-1)=(0-400-500-550).

Scale thirteen: 0-100-200-550-650-750-850-1200.
Chords: I=(0-200-650)
IV=(550-750-1200)
V=(650-850-100)
Intervals: 2-2-7-2-2-2-7.
Altered tetrachords:(2-2-7)=(0-100-200-550)
(2-7-2)=(0-100-450-550)
(7-2-2)=(0-350-450-550).
Comment: This scale is a modal displacement of Scale fourteen.
It is also theoretically interesting.

Scale fourteen: 0-100-450-550-650-1000-1100-1200.
Chords: I=(0-450-650)
IV=(550-1000-1200)
V=(650-1100-100)
Intervals: 2-7-2-2-7-2-2.
Altered tetrachords:(7-2-2)=(0-350-450-550)
(2-7-2)=(0-100-450-550)
(2-2-7)=(0-100-200-550).
Comment: This scale is a modal displacement of Scale thirteen.
It is also theoretically interesting.

Scale fifteen: 0-100-500-550-650-1050-1150-1200.
Chords: I=(0-500-650)
IV=(550-1050-1200)
V=(650-1150-100)
Intervals: 2-8-1-2-8-2-1.
Altered tetrachord:(1-2-8)=(0-50-150-550).
