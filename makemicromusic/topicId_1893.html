Recently I decided to make a little project of digitizing and
archiving a bunch of old 4-track cassettes done over the past few
years, since I tend to misplace them for months/years at a time.
This has been a good way to go back and listen to things I did 5-6
years ago and hear them more objectively. Unfortunately the few
microtonal pieces I've recorded on tape are pretty underdeveloped.
The most interesting items tend to date from before I got distracted
by all this microtonality stuff, simply because in those "single
days" I had more available time to sit down and work things out.

Anyway I've posted up a couple of files for your enjoyment, or
amusement, or whatever. These are probably 3-4 years old. I might
put up more over the next few days. As always, comments, praise or
ridicule are welcome.
