Since I get the list in digest form, I only just received this post, and it was the first I had heard about Tim's untimely passing. Not long after I started MakeMicroMusic, I spent a lot of time in the KVR forums, tracking the progress of the soft instrument world, trying to prod developers into including microtonality, and it was there that I met Tim.

He epitomized the qualities we would want in a musician within a community: endless sharing of work and information, boundless enthusiasm, and what appeared to be a life-long fascination with the sheer magic of creating music. He was one of the most positive people I've ever met in years on various forums, and it was reflected in his helping others. On top of all that, he was a uniquely gifted sound designer, and there are an awful lot of instruments that owe their success to his initial patch designs.

Thanks you for remembering him in music, and may his spirit be free to do as it pleases.

With respect,
Jon
