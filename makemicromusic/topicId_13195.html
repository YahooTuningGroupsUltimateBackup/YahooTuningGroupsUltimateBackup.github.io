I noticed your microtonal website ( http://diapason.xentonic.org/cm/ ) thanks to a mention on the Make Microtonal Music Yahoo groups mailing list ( /makemicromusic/ ). We share information on microtonal music being made by contemporary microtonal composers and performers. We recently collected performances by 20 different microtonal composers and put them together in pair of podcasts. These can be found at http://podcast1024.libsyn.com/ .

If you'd like, please join us at the mailing list, or listen to a show or two.
