Hi everyone. I have begun updating my microtonalists list at
http://infohost.nmt.edu/~jstarret/microtonalists.html
So far, I have gotten through the E's, and it takes a lot longer than
I thought it would to update broken links. I haven't been paying
enough attention to what you have been doing, and I see a lot of new
names here. If you would like to be included in this list, just send
your URL to me at jstarret@...

Also, if you have a separate page where your compositions can be
heard, I would be glad to link to them on one of my Listen pages,
which should be next in line for update. Next to be fixed will be the
pages on writings about microtonality, then the microtonal instruments
pages, but if you send me something for these areas, I will put them
in right away.

John Starrett
