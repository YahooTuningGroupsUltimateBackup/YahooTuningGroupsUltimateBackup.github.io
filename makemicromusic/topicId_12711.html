MMM day is going to happen, and it's going to happen around April 7,
or thereabouts. A celebration of what happens every day.

So we want to get lots and lots of music recorded for a
giganto-podcast. You're all invited to submit, of course, and if you
can get your friends to MMM for a day too, all the better. (And hey,
technically you don't have to do it that day if you're going to be
busy, just submit it by then.)

I want to get an idea of how many / who many can participate, so if
you're interested, go to http://xenharmonic.wikispaces.com/MMMDay06
and add your name to the list. Just click the "Edit" button and go,
it's that simple. (And if you like the idea of a microtonal wiki,
stick around!)

Maybe I should cross-post this to tuning, huh?
