Thanks for listening. I use a lot of indeterminacy. Each instrument
has many choices for what to do next. They get lists of note strings
to choose from, and have constraints over how much repetition they are
permitted at any given point in time. I make a number of takes and
then choose the one that sounds most interesting.

Prent
