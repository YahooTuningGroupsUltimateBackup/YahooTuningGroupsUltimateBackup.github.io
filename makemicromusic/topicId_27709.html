Hi folks!

Tonight, Tuesday April 17, we are kicking off the Microtonal Design UnConference
(which runs through Saturday and is chock full) witha Meet and Greet in the
Family Room of the Independent Media Center at 8:00 P.M.! This will be an
opportunity to get involved with Microtonal Design Projects in the days ahead.
We'll be making a schedule for the week and assigning ourselves roles as
designers, performers, hosts, guests, composers, organizers, collectors,
showers-off, collaborators, conductors, audience, participants, listeners,
presenters, answerers and questioners. Then we will have a Pantonal Jam!

Please come by if you're interested. It's free and there will be snacks!

And if you can't make it out tonight (or even if you can), consider yourself
invited to edit the UnConference Wiki Page with your desires and contact info,
so we can keep you in the loop:

http://xenharmonic.wikispaces.com/Microtonal+Design+Unconference+2011

See you tonight?

~Andrew Heathwaite
