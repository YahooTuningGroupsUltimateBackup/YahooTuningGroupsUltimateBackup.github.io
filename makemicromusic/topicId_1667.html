Guys,

Actually the music that *I* imagine while dreaming at
night and then promptly forget in the morning is *much*
more accomplished and beautiful than Erlich's dream
music. I've been to his dreams and listened and it's
just not as good as he thinks it is unless you like a
lot of screeching and banging on tin cups and such.

Jacky's dream music is twice as good as his CDs.

Pehrson's dream music is really amazing, as is Rick's
and Jon's.

I only visited Zelenz and Stearns' dreams once and am
scared to go back -- the rhythms almost pumped me up to
another level.

Perhaps I'll write more dream music reviews later or in
more depth if there is an interest.

-- Dream Master X -- in your dreams
