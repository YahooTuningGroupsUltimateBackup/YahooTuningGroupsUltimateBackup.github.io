****Hi Monz!

Well, when working with this piece I was *always* fully conscious of
the "close to Just Intonation" sonorities because I had the lattice
in front of me, and developed the harmonies, even the more extended
ones, while referencing the lattice.

My earlier xenharmonic pieces were mostly done only by "ear" but this
one was a lot different (hopefully I used my ears, too! :)

This piece was "plotted out" almost as much as a "serial" piece would
be... (but I hope it doesn't *sound* like that... :) )

So, naturally, I was coming as close to Just Intonation intentionally
as Blackjack would permit. Of course, the pitches are about 3 cents
off from just, and since I was using a keyboard they weren't "bent"
in that direction. However, in the recent trombone piece that I
wrote also in Blackjack, I wouldn't be too surprised if the live
player wouldn't make those small adjustments going along...
