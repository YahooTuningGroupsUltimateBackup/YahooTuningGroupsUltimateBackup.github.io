At the top of every message window you'll find a box to enter text, with a button next to it titled "Search Archive". And it will do a moderately OK search of all the messages in MMM. Not perfect, but if you put in something relevant it should return something of value.

Another thing to note is that you can go to a particular message number, and if you enter 1 you can start at the beginning of MMM (about a year old now, I think!) and see what you can find.

Give that "Search Archives" button a try - I bet you find interesting stuff!
