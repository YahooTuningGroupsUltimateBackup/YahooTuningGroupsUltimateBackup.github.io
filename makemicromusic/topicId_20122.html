Chris,

> 24 notes per octave done in December 2001 (apparently on
This sounds very consonant, but IMVHO lacks a bit in character due to either extremely obvious motifs or overly jazzy parts with little sense of motif. If sounds like 12TET with a tad of bending to introduce a tad of "in between" character as if modulating in between two normal 7 note scales.

> 22 EDO french horns - with some chorus and reverb
Sounds like it would be less acceptable to a major audience, but much more interesting an exotic in tonal color: this gains in personality more than what it loses in consonance.
Probably the best 22TET piece I have heard from you so far: epic and slowly creeping at the same time...and even though it sounds off at times you've found many very cool chords and sweet spots in this tuning: keep it up!

-Michael
