skiks (Bruce Hamilton) has released his new album "compulse" yesterday...

http://split-notes.com/009

compulse is a collection of (mostly) pulse-driven microtonal/xenharmonic tracks produced in 2010-2011.

Quirky infectious hooks, fields of distortion, ambient soundscapes, and chamber music textures rub shoulders in an adventure with some surprising turns. Tunings are often mixed, producing a kind of heterophonous pitch space; other times one tuning or scale is explored with focus. The track list is designed for album style listening (headphones recommended).
