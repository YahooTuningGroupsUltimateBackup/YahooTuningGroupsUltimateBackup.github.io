11 Dimensional String Theory

Chris Vaisvil <chrisvaisvil@...>

This is an electronic piece that explores a bit more the capability of
recording via midi data streams my live adjustment of the sound of my Korg
MS2000 synthesizer.
The piece is in 11 notes per octave - I repeat the 11th note to fit nicely
into the Korg's User tuning capability.

Online play of this piece can be found here: http://chrisvaisvil.com/?p=1864
