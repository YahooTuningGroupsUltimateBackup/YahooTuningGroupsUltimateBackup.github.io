Anybody use or tried Ketron keyboards? Apparently they have really
flexible and easy to use retuning functions, though limited to 12
notes/octave.

class like on the GEM Orientals, but the XD9 and SD1 also has tuning
functions accesible. They even have FM synthesis... the built in
samples are pretty good too.
