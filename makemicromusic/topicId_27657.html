Yesterday was Carl's birthday. I wasn't aware it was anyone else's. I
have a tradition that when it's one of my IRL friends' birthdays, to
call them up and play some sort of jazzed up reharm of the happy
birthday changes to them over the phone. In this case I thought that
porcupine would do the trick, and also help me learn how to navigate
around in these porcupine MODMOS's we've been talking about.

The joke, which I guess nobody got, was that the title of this thread
makes it seem like I'm going to engage in some kind of discussion
about psychoacoustics, which I am prone to do. Then, the guy whose
birthday it is clicks the file, and surprise! Happy birthday in
porcupine temperament, not an argument about psychoacoustics at all!
How pleasant.

Now that I've explained it, it is the least funny joke ever told, and
I feel all the more depressed for having made it.

-Mike
