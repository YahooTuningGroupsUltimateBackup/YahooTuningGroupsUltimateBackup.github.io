greg,

Just to clarify, *what* program are you referring to? Is this a function of Cubase SX (the "Micro Tuner"), or is it also a VST plugin?

I myself haven't used Cubase, so I'll just have to hope someone on the list has first-hand knowledge. If Cubase can alter the pitches 'on the fly' with pitch bend data, then you are going to have to do single-line tracks on different channels (i.e. the pitch bend method would affect all the notes the same way if you played a chord). If, instead, it is trying to send a sysex or other control to retune an entire instrument (hardware or software), then that instrument would have to support that function, and few hardware boxes do.

Also, if internal synths show the tuning working but external ones don't, make sure there aren't global midi setting on the box that have control information filtered out in the incoming data stream...

hth,
Jon
