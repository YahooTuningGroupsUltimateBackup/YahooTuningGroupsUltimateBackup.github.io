Hi Joseph,

Thanks for the inquiry.

Actually, we released Infinity's second service pack today which
includes new objects and enhanced DXi and VST plug in features.

In terms of micro tuning, even the original release of Infinity was
capable of micro tuning. To make it easier, we have implemented two
methods of permanently setting micro tuning values for audio sources
(oscillators, KarplusStrong, and Sampler objects).

You can provide 128 frequency values, one for each MIDI note. Or you
can specify an octave and provide 12 note values and the object will
automatically generate the values for the other octaves.

If you have any other questions, please let me know.

Michael Lambie
Sound Quest Inc.
