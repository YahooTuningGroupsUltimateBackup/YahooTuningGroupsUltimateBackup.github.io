This is the recording of my last performance of the year, from a couple
of nights ago. As usual for my live pieces, there is no computer or
keyboard involved (and in this case, no synths either), so there is no
specific tuning used. However, in this piece I "play" a Deluxe Memory
Man in a manner that essentially exploits harmonics/overtones, so in
that sense it is in a form of just intonation.
