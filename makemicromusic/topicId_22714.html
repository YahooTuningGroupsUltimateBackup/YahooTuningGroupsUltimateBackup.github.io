Various Artists - Crack My Pitch Up (new album)

sevishmusic <sevish@...>

7/12/2010 12:34:15 PM

I'm very honored to present the work of Jacky Ligon, City of the Asleep, Scratchy Dubplate Crew, Carlo Serafini, Flao YG, Fature, Cameron Bobro, Paragon, Microdub and myself, in this collaborative album 'Crack My Pitch Up!'

Download and listen free: http://split-notes.com/spnt003.php

The album is in various styles, and uses various tunings. Liner notes are included with the album download, for those of you who like the gritty details!!

Please enjoy and spread the word. :)

Sean "Sevish" Archibald
