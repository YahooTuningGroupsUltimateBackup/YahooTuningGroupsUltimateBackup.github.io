List,

If one desired the opportunity to hear a 4-hour radio special devoted to the life and work of Harry Partch, one could be satisfied in a few hours. Tonight, from 10:00 PM to 2:00 AM (PST), radio station KFJC (out of Foothill College in the California Bay area) will do just that, including interviews (some taped, some live, like the one with me) with people who knew and worked with Partch, and/or were influenced by him. Details at:

http://www.kfjc.org/promotions/index.php

Note that you don't have to be in the area with an antenna - they stream their shows on the net:

http://www.kfjc.org/netcast/index.php

Check it out. Or sleep. Up to you.

Cheers,
Jon
