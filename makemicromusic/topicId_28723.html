*Fantasy for Piano in 25 Note per Octave
Tuning<http://micro.soonlabel.com/25edo/fantasy_for_piano_in_25_edo.mp3>
* is a short piece I composed in Sibelius 7 using Pianoteq 4 in 25 note per
octave (edo) tuning. This composition was created per a request in the
Xenharmonic Alliance as a response to my piece in 32 edo tuning. This is my
first piece in 25 EDO which is an interesting tuning. My approach to the
composition was to develop melodic material first and then fill out the
harmonies. After about half way through I started writing both together
though there is application of some contrapuntal techniques like imitation.
The score is also
available.<http://micro.soonlabel.com/25edo/fantasy_for_piano_in_25_edo%20.pdf>
