==================================================
Summoning, 21 January 2002 by X. J. Scott
Neutral 3rd MOS tuning on bamboo xylophone.

The primitive instrument breathes in and out violently;
it falls back and forth, precipitously. Rumbling, it
tears through the thin, cold evening air. Plucked, it
resonates, calling forth the yellow-orange presence
unseen, yet felt, heard and tasted.
