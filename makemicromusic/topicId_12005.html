AFMM Concerts March 25 and May 26 in NYC

1/24/2006 7:04:49 PM

Thanks to microtonal tuner Marc Weinert, the American Festival of Microtonal
Music has 2 Saturday concerts on its schedule for the Spring. They will be
presented at St. Luke in the Fields Church on Hudson Street in New York City’s
Greenwich Village.

March 25, 2006 – featuring Joshua Pierce, piano in Kirnberger III
Johann Philipp Kirnberger SONATA FOR PIANO No. 3
Joshua Pierce, piano in Kirnberger III
Georg Phillip Telemann SONATA FOR BASSOON IN F MINOR
Johnny Reinhard, bassoon
Rebecca Pechefsky, harpsichord in 1/6th comma meantone
Ludwig van Beethoven MOONLIGHT SONATA
Joshua Pierce, piano in Kirnberger III
-intermission-
William Friedemann Bach SONATA FOR 2 HARPSICHORDS IN F MAJOR
Rebecca Pechefsky and Joshua Pierce, harpsichords in 1/6th comma
meantone
Tui St. George Tucker SONATA No. 2 “HYPERTONIC”
Johnny Reinhard, alto recorder in quartertones
Johannes Brahms SONATA FOR CLARINET AND PIANO
Gilad Harel, clarinet
Joshua Pierce, piano in Kirnberger III

May 6, 2006 – featuring Joshua Pierce, piano in Werckmeister III
Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart PIANO QUINTET IN Eb (k 452)
Johann Sebastian Bach DUETTO #2
Joshua Pierce, piano in Werckmeister III
Joel Mandelbaum WIND QUINTET NO. 2 in Just Intonation
-intermission-
Josef Haydn LONDON TRIO IN D MAJOR FOR FLUTE, BASSOON AND PIANO
John Eaton TRIO IN X FOR FLUTE, OBOE AND BASSOON (premiere)
Mikhail Glinka TRIO FOR CLARINET, BASSOON, AND PIANO
Jennifer Grim, flute
Bram Kreeftmeijer, oboe
Gilad Harel, clarinet
Johnny Reinhard, bassoon
Rheagan Osteen, horn
Joshua Pierce, piano
