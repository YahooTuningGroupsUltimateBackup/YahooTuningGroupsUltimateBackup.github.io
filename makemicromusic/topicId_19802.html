Greetings-

I have posted a video of a piece I worked out on harmonic series guitar. All
notes on this guitar can be considered partials of the same series, in which
case the strings are tuned to partials 4,6,8,12,16,24 and the frets for each
string are placed accordingly. I like the simplicity of this setup.

http://www.vimeo.com/1829615

theres a bit more about the tuning on my page:
