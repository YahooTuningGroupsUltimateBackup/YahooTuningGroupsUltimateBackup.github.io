H-Pi is also making Microsynth http://www.h-pi.com/microsynth.html which is an amazing virtual synthesizer.  I use the deluxe (only 20 bucks) version.  I can run 16 different midi channels in and have them all play in the same scale, HAVE YOU EVER TRIED THIS ON A MAC?  It accepts System Exclusive Midi Messages (which a Mac's AU synth doesn't) for retuning, alternate scales, etc.  Now you can Jam with your friends in a 41 tone scale, individually allocating voices, transposition, etc.

It's awesome. enjoy,
