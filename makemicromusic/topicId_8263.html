For anyone who might be interested I've uploaded two new microtracks:

http://zebox.com/daniel_anthony_stearns/
Oldworld_1
Otherworld_1

At one point in my life there were a lot of diverse and occasionally
overlapping projects going on at the same time, and I sometimes found
myself composing ahead of my collaborators' contributions. And that's
certainly the case with these two cuts. The first one started as an
intended collaboration with Scott Dakota, but he was unable to
participate at that time so I ended up just finishing it. The second
cut is the unfinished instrumental parts to an intended collaboration
with vocalist Debra Shea for the band AzayKoÂ—-see clang on this site
for an example of a finished cut with this project. Though both are
different from their intended ends, and one is incomplete, I think
both hold up very well and represent a style or sound I call
oldworld/otherworld.
