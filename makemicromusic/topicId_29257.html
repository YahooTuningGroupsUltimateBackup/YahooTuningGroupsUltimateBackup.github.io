To Craunch the Marmoset
<http://micro.soonlabel.com/simultaneous-tunings/20130919_poetry_to_craunch_the_marmoset.mp3>is
an electronic ambient poly-tuned piece – Albino in 16 edo, Z3TA+ 2.1 in 8
edo, Absynth in 43 harmonics series segment reduced to an octave, Cameleon
5000 in 22 edo, and DIY Erhu in roughly just intonation on D about middle
and harmonics thereof. Phrases from “English as She is Spoke” by Pedro
Carolino are read by Google (who sounds a bit like Siri actually.)

http://chrisvaisvil.com/to-craunch-the-marmoset/
