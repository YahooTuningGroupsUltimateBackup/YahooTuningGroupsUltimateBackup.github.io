We have some new sound files up on the Web for the Composers
Concordance, including extracts from such pieces as Gene Pritsker's
art-rap masterpiece, _Poetic Subjects Eternal_, Frank Oteri's
microtonal woodwind quintet in quartertones, _circles mostly in
wood_, David Macbride's fusion of Eastern and Western styles with
violin and temple bells, _In Common_, Martin Bresnick's novel work
for violin and vibes, _Songs of the Mouse People_, Hayg Boyadjian's
pairing of the mighty doublebass with the mighty flute, and my own
work for cello in the near-just-intonation "blackjack" scale of 21
notes. These are all pieces that were performed during our last 2002-
2003 concert season. Hope everybody is having a nice Summer!

http://artists.mp3s.com/artists/443/composers_concordance.html

[P.S. don't worry about having to "sign up" to hear sound files:
mp3.com sends *very little* spam...]
