The regular 72-ET Sagittal notation is like this:

0 C
1 C/|
2 C|)
3 C/|\
4 C||)
5 C||\
6 C/||\ D\!!/
7 D!!/
8 D!!)
9 D\!/
10 D!)
11 D\!
12 D
etc.
