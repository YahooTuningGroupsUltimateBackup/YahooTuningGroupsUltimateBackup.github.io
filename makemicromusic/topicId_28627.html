Hello, I propose Bach's Ricercar a 6 from the Musical Offering BWV 1079, tuned into:
19 edo, 31edo, quarter comma meantone, Fokker's second alternate septimal tuning.
16 notes used in the piece: Ab, A, Bb, B, Cb, C, C#, Db, D, Eb, E, Fb, F, F#, Gb, G
Fokker's second alternate septimal tuning, restricted to the above notes:

F 4/3
F# 45/32
Gb 10/7

Bb 25/14
