Some of my ideas can be traced back to D'Arcy Thompson's "On Growth and
Form" and Joseph Schillinger's System of Musical Composition and even
further. I don't care who is assigned credit for my musical ideas just
so long as they can be put into practise and not scattered amongst the
dusty tomes of a long lost library.
