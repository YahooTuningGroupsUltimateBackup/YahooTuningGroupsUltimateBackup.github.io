Some of th exact details i don't remember (being 15 years ago)
It i was done with Brad Laner.
i took a subharmonic flute and made a recording of a two octave cluster.
every note or close to it. Brad put that on a sampler so the whole chord be be moved up and done. I think i stayed with 4th and 5th. maybye a second.
so something like a subharmonic cluster over a pythagorean chain.
the percussion track was made out of the inside of grandfather clocks i had mounted on a board.
brad used an eventide to proce the whole thing, a dual delay at 4 and 5 sixteens respectfully
like i said most of what is up is quite quite old
