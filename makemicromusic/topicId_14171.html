Verses One One (Confession)
Forasmuch as many have tried to tell the story
of what God has done among us:
In the beginning, God created the heavens and the earth.
In many parts, and in many ways,
God of old spoke to our fathers in the prophets:
Blessed is the man who has not walked in the counsel of the wicked,
nor stood in the way of sinners, nor sat in the chair of pestilence
The Word of Life existed from the beginning.
We have heard it. We have seen it. We have touched it.
Not of men, nor by man, but by Jesus Christ and God the Father,
who raised him from the dead;
Truly I love you all, and not I alone,
but also all who know this truth:
In the beginning was the Word,
and the Word was with God,
and the Word was God.

Cheers,
