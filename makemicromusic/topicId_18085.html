I expect to be composing rabidly for the next 25 days at an artist
colony, I-Park in Connecticut. My multi-faceted self-assignment
involves microtonal (especially 31-tone) pedagogy, microtonal music
about microtonal music, utopian false statements, chiptunes, and
perhaps some entanglements of cybernetics. I'll be talking to myself
and maybe you, if you're bored or interested, at

<http://udderbot.blogspot.com>

I am specifically interested in starting a discussion on microtonal
pedagogy. I made of page of questions:

<http://xenharmonic.wikispaces.com/Pedagogy>

As always with "the wiki", participation is welcome (and moreover is
kind of the whole point of having a wiki). If there is none, I'll go
the inbox-pollution route and post the questions here.

Be on the lookout for a 17-edo/JI round I wrote last week.
