Dear friends,
few weeks ago I finished retuned version of my song Vitr (Wind) from 1986. The new version is in just intonation (harmonics 14 to 27)
14/14 - c
15/14 - c#
16/14 - d
17/14 - d#
18/14 - e
19/14 - f
20/14 - f#
21/14 - g
22/14 - g#
23/14 - a-
24/14 - a
25/14 - a#
26/14 - b
27/14 - c-
In verses the flute goes from 16/14 to 26/14 over the bass 16/14. In refrains the chords are Am=12:14:18 and Cdim=14:17:20:24.
That's all.

It can be found at http://www.traxinspace.com/profile/aum
or
http://www.last.fm/music/Flao+YG/The+Forgotten+Works

Enjoy!
Any comment welcomed.

Milan
