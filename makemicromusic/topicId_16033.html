Long-lost recordings of a concert in spring 2004! A mostly personal
failure of a piece, but in that good character-building way.

Apparently for violin, 2 cellos, bass, clarinet, otonal organ,
percussion, and kazoo! Notated in HEWM 72tet ( v < - + > ^ ) (the <
and > were confusedly mixed up, if I recall, because placed to the
left of noteheads, they suggested their opposite in meaning) but
really only hangs around low harmonics of G. Much more confusedly
notated than it needed to be. Originally intended as a first section
to a sonata-allegro form. For what it's worth:

two takes:

<http://www.owlnet.rice.edu/~jbarton/rough/exposition1.mp3>

<http://www.owlnet.rice.edu/~jbarton/rough/exposition2.mp3>

Featuring lots of good ole 5/4's, most intelligibly from otonal organ...
