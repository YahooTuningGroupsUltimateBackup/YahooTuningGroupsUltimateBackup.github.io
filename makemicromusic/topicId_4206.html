I have an Big Briar etherwave theremin. It's a pretty nice intstrument. If you're looking for a MIDI interface for it, there's a company called Wavefront that makes one for about $675. It's a Theremin to MIDI converter unit in its own box. So you can get a theremin and a MIDI converter for about $1000 insteaed of $5000...
