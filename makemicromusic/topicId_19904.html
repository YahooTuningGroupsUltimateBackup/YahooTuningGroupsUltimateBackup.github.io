1/6 saturation freeze rhyme

It's the day for bagels,
shits an' giggles fried up
splits can Rome torrential
quits pan-dromedary
writs Genoa spurring
fits when throe occur, yoke
pits ran Yoga blur broke

----

aide ride aire

loot foam foil foot form foal fool
root form rift foal ride fool aire loot firm riot foam rife foil aide foot fire
