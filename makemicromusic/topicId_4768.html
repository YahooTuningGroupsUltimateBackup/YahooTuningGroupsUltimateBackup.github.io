Hi, I've set up and invited a few regulars on the Internet Experimental
Scene, (Joe Benzola, et al) to help me write a blog about new
electronic music.

I'm interested in how the current electronic music scene is
experiencing a blurring between academic and popular forms (as
evidenced by David Horne's recent concert and another recent one at
the All Tomorrows Parties festival.)

We plan to especially focus on recent music available on the internet
for download and hope to curate and make it easier to get the good
music to the interested listener. Reviews, general commentaries,
some tech talk, etc., will be found at beepSNORT.

Recent articles cover Squarepusher's achievements, a Xenakis
realization, is academia really embracing the young experimentalists.
