6/7/2010 6:22:56 PM

This is a little piece dedicated to the lost world island of Socotra that grabs a lot of instrumentation from across Asia with little discrimination - the melodic instruments use Afshari with the exception of the Jews harps which use 9-tET Sorog. I am submitting it as the 6th demonstration piece for the Mark of the Unicorn demonstration piece competition.

http://notonlymusic.com/board/download/file.php?id=396

If you wish to listen on line and see details of the tuning used go to:

http://notonlymusic.com/board/viewtopic.php?f=23&t=336&start=0
