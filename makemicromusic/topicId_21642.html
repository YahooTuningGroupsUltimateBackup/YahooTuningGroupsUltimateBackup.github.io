When I played in Urbana IL last December I was able to take a video of one of my performances as well as midi record it. This performance at the Oddmusic Convergence used the same tuning (19 ET), equipment and software as GR-20 Hexaphonic 19-ET Guitar Improvisation with the exception that Session Drummer 3 was added.

Using that video and the original midi I combined video effects plus the output of "Marktxt" to the original performance

I applied that program to a large number of lyrics by Evan Harrington and myself and used the output as the basis of the text
in this video http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3ROb89b1Cr4
