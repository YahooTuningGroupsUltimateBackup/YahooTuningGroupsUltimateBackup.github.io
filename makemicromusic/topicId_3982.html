Hi there,

Here is an mp3 of my minor subminor piece.

http://mp3.com.au/harmonicflyght

Played on the Harmonic Flyght and Poly Rose instruments of the
FM7, and with the dream scape reverv of the SB Live!
so I call it Harmonic Flyght Dream Scape.

Since the FM7 is monotimbral, I did it with two copies of FM7 running at once,

Uses MTS sysexes to retune both copies of the FM7 (relaying to the
two copies of the FM7 via the out menu using Midi Yoke Junction).

I did another experiment later also adding
an SB Live! oboe voice playing Part 1 again, using pitch bends.

This shows one can use pitch bends and MTS sysexes simultaneously
in FTS, also MTS sysexes simultaneously on several devices at once.

This is new - you will need the latest update of FTS to
try this out.

Before the MTS sysexes would only retune one device at a time
(that was a bug) and couldn't be used simultaneously with pitch bends..
