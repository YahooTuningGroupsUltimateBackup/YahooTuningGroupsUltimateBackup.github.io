This is juvenalia, from shortly after my first visit with Erv Wilson in Spring of '78. It's for piano and ensemble, in the "double hexany" tuning. I've spared you the rest of the piece, which is basically more of the same, but the opening horn chords over the pizzicato contrabass is still pretty cool for a sixteen-year-old.
