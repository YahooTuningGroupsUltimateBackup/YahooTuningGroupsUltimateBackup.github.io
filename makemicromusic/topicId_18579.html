Hi Kraig

Very nice and beautiful.
I tested your scale and have played something in 96-EDO version of
your scale by my PC and scala:
http://240edo.googlepages.com/Amahourin96-EDOverofdrone1986scale.mp3

your music isn't out of tune and this terachord :1/1 8/7 9/7 4/3 or
its 96-EDo version 0 237.5 437.5 500 is a kind of major or mahour
tetrachord to me.
It shows that combination of "Lower semiaugmented tone of 237.5
cent" , "Tone of 200 cent" and a "Upper quartertone of 62.5 cent"
Have a taste of mahour tetrachord.
another 96-EDO version can be 0 225 437.5 500 with these intervals as
"Acute tone of 225 cent"+"Upper tone of 212.5 cent"+Upper quartertone
of 62.5 cent".
Best wishes for you
