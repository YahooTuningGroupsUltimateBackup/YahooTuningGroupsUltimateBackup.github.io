It's on for February. Record an album in a month. I've done it for the past three years, and it's really effective in getting music to plastic in a hurry. You can always go back and refine after the end of the month.

More here: http://rpmchallenge.com

Hello RPMers past and present!

The time has come! Registration for the 2011 RPM Challenge is now open! Whether you're a returning stalwart or a past participant, we invite you to mosey on over to http://rpmchallenge.com, sign in and re-join our zany family.

It's been a long, strange year and what better way to ensure 2011 gets on the right track than by taking 28 days to do nothing but produce music? Probably there isn't one. At least, not one we can think of right now. Anyway, the point is, January is sign-up and gear-up time. So get to it!

Prent Rodgers
