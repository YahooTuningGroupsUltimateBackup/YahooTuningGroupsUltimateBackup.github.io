Hi all,

New music posted. There's a 12-EDO MIDI version, for flutes, at:
/makemicromusic/files/Yahya%27s%20music/

Jon L Smith retuned this for me in the intended 5 limit scale, which I've labelled "D minor tritonic hexatonic", which has some interesting interval properties:
D = 1/1, E = 10/9, F = 6/5, G# = 25/18, A# = 125/81, B = 5/3
and performed it with four different orchestrations. I've uploaded the version for Descant, Clarinet and Viola.

Enjoy!
Regards,
