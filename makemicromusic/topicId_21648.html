Hi all,

I am putting together a compilation album of microtonal music for release on the record label "Split Notes". Split Notes is an online label, digital releases only, of free microtonal music.

The album has an open submission. Composers of all kinds are encouraged to submit a track or song (or as many as you like) to be considered for inclusion on the compilation. We'll try to be as inclusive as possible, and of course "popular music genre" is a relaxed-enough restriction that we can get a good variety of music going.

Once made, the album will be released and be spread far and wide across the 'nets. If you share the same view as I do, which is that microtonal music deserves to be on more people's ipods, then I'm eager to collaborate with you. Please consider getting involved! :)

All the details for submission are at the following web page: http://split-notes.com/compilation2010.php
The final deadline is April 30th.

Cheers,
