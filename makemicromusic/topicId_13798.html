Comparing these two makes for a good example of what difference a
tuning for a given temperament makes. The piece is in Meantone[12],
and so can be performed in either 19 or 31, but 31 makes it sound
bright, whereas 19 makes it sound more dreamy and introspective. Since
it was conceived in 19, it makes sense the tuning fits the mood.
