Hey tuning gang...I just became a Seymour Duncan endorsee last week...Seymour is a legend in the field of guitar/bass pickups, and this endorsement means I get good deals on their gear, and they have the option of using me in future promo stuff...which is the whole point for me. I can also tell the mags/writers I'm a Seymour guy, and that can't hurt either. The tuning angle is I'm gonna put Duncans on my fretless 7 string, and 19 tone Starr axe as well ($$$ permitting). I'm imagining I am the only cat playing fretless/micro guitars on their roster, so hoping that will interest them...we'll see, hoping for the best. Jeez, Jeff Beck gave Seymour the Fender Esquire he used with the Yardbirds...it's now in the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame...and I hear Seymour is thinking of buying Roy Buchanan's old '53 Tele for a mere $80,000...if I had the $$$, I'd do it myself...best from sunny Denver...Stickman

and..."Fretless" is available at CDbaby.com...nice review at ww.somethingelsereviews.com
