OK list, a bit of history you may not want.

One day in 95', I was driving Lou Harrison around Joshua Tree because he and
Bill Colvig had come down to visit me, and when Lou got here, he liked the
place so much, he thought about building a retreat here. As we were driving
around and talking, there was a small silence of about thirty seconds. Then,
Lou said out of (almost *) nowhere, "Harry Partch had no balls. He lost them
as a child to the measels." I ( H.P. is a HUGE influence on my person)
choked a bit. "Uh...oh really?"

"It's true. He had no balls".

Gosh, thanks Lou. Harry had (metaphorically) the largest balls in 20th
century music as far as I'm concerned. Anyone who followed the cattle-run
off the cliff into serialism surely doesn't stand beside Harry.

Wow, I just wrote a micro-diatribe. At least it was micro.
