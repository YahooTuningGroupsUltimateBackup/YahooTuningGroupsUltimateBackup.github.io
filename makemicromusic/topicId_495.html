Jacky Lignon (and other MMMers),

Thanks for your most excellent frog-song, but I must say that
the most consonant frog scale depends crucially on the particular
species of frog. Please dont lead people to believe that
one scale fits all frogs... as you can hear in my piece
"Frog Planet", frogs from Sumatra (I was in a rice field
somewhere outside Bukatingi) sound very different from
other frogs, and hence the scale in which they sing most
consonantly is also different.

You can find Frog Planet at:

http://eceserv0.ece.wisc.edu/~sethares/mp3s/Frog_Planet.mp3

Enjoy!

Bill Sethares
