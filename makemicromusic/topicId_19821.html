Sorry to butt in, but what it says there is not actually wrong.
Although there's no consensus reached regarding these issues,
most of the books I've been through counts the harmonics similarly.

For example, say we're investigating C1's overtone series, then G2:

-is the 3rd partial,
-has 3:1 ratio
-yet it's the 2nd overtone and 2nd harmonic.

So, I think we should distinguish the difference between "overtones
and harmonics" and "partials".

Have a sunny day.
