I've been using the rgc:audio z3ta+ synth, which tunes using .scl files, for a while and I like it. Rene, the developer, is quite open to ideas, but has not made any firm commitment to adding microtuning to sfz. I don't know if it is more problematic than something more synthesis oriented, but I'll mention it again in the rgc forum over at KVR-VST. Worth a try, anyway. Hell, I'm still looking for a moderately advanced sampler that can microtune (a step or two above simple ones like TobyBear's "Helios", or even the limited sample usage in LinPlug's "CronoX")
