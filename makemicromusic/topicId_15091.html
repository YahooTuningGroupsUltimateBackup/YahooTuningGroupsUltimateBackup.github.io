Hi Jacob-

> question regarrding such wide spaces between low frets - how far away
> from the fret can your finger be and still have the fret work?

like always, best is right behind the fret. With those wide frets- if you
press more than an inch or so behind the fret, you are then playing fretless
guitar and getting the finger note rather than the fret note.
