there are many fine scales in 22 . . . another is herman miller's
porcupine scale, which i had used in my piece "glassic" -- on the
black keys there are four 5-limit triads and an 8:9:10:11:12, nice
chromatic links through the 7-limit:

w w w b w w b w w b w w b w w b w w b w w b

also graham breed's magic scale is pretty loaded with good
stuff . . . magic works in 19, 41, 60, etc. as well as 22. this has
the major third (7/22 oct.) as generator -- see if you can work out
what b/w arrangements this implies.

but one is probably going to rearrange the keys on one's keyboard
only once, or at most once for each tuning system . . . but hell, i
don't want any screwdriver-happy musicians to be discouraged from
daily rearrangements of their keys!
