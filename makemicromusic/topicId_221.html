Hello all,

I've just signed on to this list, like I'm not on enough tuning lists
already :-). Every now and then, I have to wonder why I subscribe to ALL of
these lists, what with high bandwidth and little time. Last night I had the
sort of experience that makes sifting through the emails all worthwhile.

Through the tuning list, I'd met overtone singer Jim Cole, whose cassette I
already had acquired a number of years ago. We'd corresponded offlist a few
times, and planned a visit when he was going to be on vacation here in New
Hampshire where I live.

Last night we met up in person for the first time at the park next to the
music store where I teach, we talked for a while and watched the almost full
moon rise over the river. When it got buggy, we went inside the store with
our instruments. We tuned up and improvised together for a while, Jim
playing his backpacker tanpura and singing, and me with my recently acquired
fretless acoustic guitar. Magical MicroMusicMaking at its absolute best!

This is the truly wonderful thing about the internet, and on lists such as
these, to be able to find and build a community of artists and friends with
similar interests all over the world. Here's to much more micromusicmaking
with new friends!

Seth
