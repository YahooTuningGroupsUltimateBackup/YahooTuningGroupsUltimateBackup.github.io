Btw, this for anyone who might be interested and happens to live in the
Boston area, this Sunday night will mark the 36th anniversary of Mark
Harvey's Aardvark Orchestra's Christmas concert at the Emmanuel church :

http://calendar.boston.com/boston-ma/venues/show/17596-emmanuel-church

As a teenager, I used to go to Boston all the time to hear the likes of
David Moss and other ultra-out avants, and these Christmas concerts
always were the highlight of the year--a kind of modern and local Duke
Ellington-like Sacred Concert--and never failed to light me up with
some of the special magic I felt in this season as a kid.
I hope to be there this Sunday with my wife and son. So if any list
members might also make it let me know, and maybe we can share a hello
and a Merry Christmas or other non-denominational seasonal holiday
