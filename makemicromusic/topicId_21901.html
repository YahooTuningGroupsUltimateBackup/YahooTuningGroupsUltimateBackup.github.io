MicroFest 2010 has been announced!

The 12th year of the festival which celebrates the beauty that lies in between the piano keys begins in Southern California this month. You can find all the details at www.MicroFest.org. This year's festival includes nine concerts:

Trio Scordatura Tuesday March 9 Electric Lodge, Venice

Jack Quartet: In the Dark Monday/Tuesday April 19/20 Neighborhood Church, Pasadena

Quartertuned + Saturday April 24 Steinway Piano Gallery, West Hollywood

Party for Betty! Wednesday May 5 REDCAT Theater, Los Angeles

Modern Ancient Instruments Sunday May 9 Pomona College, Claremont

Giant Quartertuned+ Sunday May 16, Rubenstein Hall, El Segundo

Partch@REDCAT: Even Wild Horses Wednesday/Thursday June 2/3 REDCAT Theater, Los Angeles

Just Brass Sunday June 13 Church in Ocean Park, Santa Monica

Partch@LACMA Tuesday June 29 Los Angeles County Museum of Art, Los Angeles

If you'd like to be put on the MicroFest snail mailing list, send me a message, and I'll get a poster to you.
