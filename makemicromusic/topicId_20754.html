Custom Scale Editor (CSE) is for standard MIDI keyboards and Tonal Plexus Editor (TPXE) gives the experience of having a virtual Tonal Plexus keyboard at your fingertips. They are both free software applications for Mac and Windows which give you microtonal control over pitch.

Some new features in these updates:

- TPXE customizable keyboard colors
- Enhanced CSE exporting options
- TPXE Shapes drawing, playing, transposing, and inverting chord and scale patterns on the keyboard
