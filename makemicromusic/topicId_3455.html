I've been exploring half comma meantone,
and found it's pretty interesting.

Here is a gamelan like fractal tune exploring
a near seven equal subset (but then I
repeated it at the eleventh degree
instead of the twelth so it digresses
into other less equal subsets).

http://homepage.ntlworld.com/robertwalker/half_comma_mean_tone.mid

Also been enjoying improvising in it and particularly
the two six tone scales, each of which has a wide gap
which you can put a surprise seventh note into from
time to time. It's a nice melodic scale I find.
