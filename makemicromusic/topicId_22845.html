OPA!

Raw pretty performance files of a pretty raw performance:

http://www.archive.org/details/AnUdderbotRecital

I'm indebted to JI composer & bassist Paul Kotheimer for doing the
recording, and to everyone else for everything else they did.

I'm thinking, this was a big milestone, and MAN, I need to practice more.
Or some competition. It's lonely at the top. So, make udderbots! & learn
to play if you're so possessed. Also, write music for me or someone to play
on it, like and unlike the music you hear here! There is now a Composer's
Guide to the Udderbot audio file I have prepared, thanks also to P.K.:

http://udderbot.wikispaces.com/composers+guide

Here's to "keeping" "it" "real",
Jacob A. Barton
