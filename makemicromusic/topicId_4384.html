> ----> Event Description
> <-----------------------------------------------
>
> CCM Composer-in-Residence Ann Warde presents her piece
> "Omong-Omong" for gamelan, singers, and electronics with the
> participation of Mills student performers. The composition
> engages vocalists and instrumentalists in a conversational
> investigation of traditional Javanese vocal musics and
> multiple tuning systems. Surrounding the instrumental
> texture, real-time computer-based electronics (using MaxMSP)
> transform the vocal and instrumental sounds and create a kind
> of "sonic commentary." Ms. Warde holds degrees from the
> University of Michigan, Wesleyan University, and the
> University of Illinois. She currently directs the gamelan
> ensemble at Cornell University.
