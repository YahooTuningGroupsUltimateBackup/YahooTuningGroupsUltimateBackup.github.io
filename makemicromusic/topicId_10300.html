Micro Musicians,
I love all the new music the list posters have all been creating. In the past I've been able to keep up with all the new works and create a podcast for each one, but I've falled behind. Can I get everyone's permission in an email, or post to the list, to Podcast the works you point to on the MMM list? I have programs ready to go of the following pieces, and just need your permission:

Christopher Bailey - "Post-industrial Nuclear Thrash Mutated Smurfette JumpFest"
Kraig Grady - Crickets (so good I play it twice)
Tidal Falls by Jonathan Szanto
Bill Sethares - Maple Manytet

I've got a bunch more that are older I'd like to do as well, by Aaron Johnson, Hans Staub, Margo Schulter and others. If you have one you'd like me to do next, send me a note or post to the list. I won't podcast the music without your permission. I have about 100 subscribers, who will automatically download the next podcast, if I just get a second to post. I've been dormant so long that when I finally get around to posting, they might be surprised by the sounds on their iPod's or MP3 players.

Prent Rodgers
