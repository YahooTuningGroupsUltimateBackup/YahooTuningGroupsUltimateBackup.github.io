Hello, amigo! Welcome to the forum.

In the interest of helping you get some microtonal stuff up (so you
can show these folks what you can do), allow me to offer some tips to
uploading music:

Tip #1: you need a site to upload to. I would suggest
soundclick.com, audiostreet.net, or musicianmp3.com (in order of my
personal preference). All of these are free.

Tip #2: have your audio files in the correct format. All the sites I
listed accept mp3's compressed at 128kbps (the default). Make sure
whatever program you are using to get your music onto your computer
(iTunes or MediaPlayer, etc.) is ripping the tracks as these kind of
mp3's.

Tip #3: once you have your songs ripped and a site set-up, just
follow the directions on your website to upload the tunes. If you
use soundclick, just go into the mp3 edit area, click "add new song"
and then click the "browse..." button to select the song on your
computer that you wish to upload.

Tip #4: well, this isn't really a tip, more of a suggestion:
upload "Hair Eaters" first. I think people here will really dig that
one.

Happy uploading!

-Igliashon
