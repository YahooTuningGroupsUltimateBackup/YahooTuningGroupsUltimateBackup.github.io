Yes it is completely diatonic, as one falls off of metaslendro. and admit that it s pretty flat.
Since my back has been forcing me to play with such alien creatures to myself, i have played with more extreme things but haven't really felt like any of it is good enough to let out.
I am quite interested in pitch though and when i get to a certain point of electronics, pitch really doesn't matter at all.
on the other hand we can't concede to staying with sine waves forever. So i am trying to find a point where i can still contribute something to the exploration of pitch along side such an interesting possibilities in timbre.

I take you prodding into that direction though as a well informed and understanding of what it is i throw out
