I have uploaded a music based on bihexany of gene ward smith

olin.mp3

please see the steps:

0: 22/21 80.537 undecimal minor semitone

1: 35/33 101.867

2: 11/10 165.004 4/5-tone, Ptolemy's second

3: 15/14 119.443 major diatonic semitone

4: 56/55 31.194

5: 15/14 119.443 major diatonic semitone

6: 11/10 165.004 4/5-tone, Ptolemy's second

7: 35/33 101.867

8: 22/21 80.537 undecimal minor semitone

9: 21/20 84.467 minor semitone

10: 80/77 66.170

11: 21/20 84.467 minor semitone

12: 22/21 80.537 undecimal minor semitone

We have a beautiful symmetry in steps from o to 8 .

This is mainly based on 66-ado with 5/4 and 7/4 but we can also consider
132-ado to have 7/4 and 5/4.
