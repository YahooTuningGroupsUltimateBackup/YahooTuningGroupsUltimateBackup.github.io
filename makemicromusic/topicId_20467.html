-------- Original Message --------
Subject: Dax's Return Stimulates Mega-Reunion, Show with Jason Webley

Ladies and Gentlemen,

On Tuesday, March 10th, at the Anno Domini Gallery,
Corpus Callosum is reuniting for a spectacular concert!

After 30 months of near-hibernation, we are exuberant to
announce the first performance of Corpus Callosum with their
long-lost accordionist and songwriter Dax Tran-Caffee, recently
returned from Chicago. Our anticipation is certainly as great,
if not greater, than yours, as we prepare a performance of
new songs, old favorites, and a variety of other wonders
including Dax's Portable Picturebox, which has been amazing the
inhabitants of Chicago for the last two years. We have more
members and more new instruments than ever before. Ladies and
Gentlemen, please join us in this tremendous affair at the Anno
Domini Gallery, also featuring performances by the inimitable
Jason Webley of Seattle Washington, and the Foundry Field Recordings
of Columbia Missouri. Admission is unforgiveably cheap, and
no one will be turned away on basis of age.

Tuesday, March 10th
Corpus Callosum, Jason Webley, Foundry Field Recordings
at the Anno Domini Gallery
366 S First St, San Jose, CA
7PM, all ages, $5

Sincerely,
Corpus Callosum
www.corpus.cc
