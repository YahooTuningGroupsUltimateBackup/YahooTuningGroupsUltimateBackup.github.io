*Pretty Industry<http://micro.soonlabel.com/24et/20130420_pretty_industry.mp3>
* is a re-composition of four Benjamin Smith piano improvisations created
by manipulating and re-orchestrating pitches extracted from the
performances using 24 edo (24 et, quartertone) tuning. The new
orchestration consists of Harp, Solo violin, viola, cello, contrabass
strings, french horn, and bass drum, snare drum, and tamtam.
