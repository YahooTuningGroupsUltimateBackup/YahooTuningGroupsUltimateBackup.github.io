So that other list members aren't in the dark, and to make it clear this means something for MMM, Carl is referencing the last of the Philip Blackburn / Innova series on the life and work of Harry Partch, entitled (collectively) "Enclosures".

Enclosure VII: http://www.innova.mu/artist1.asp?skuID=263

Includes (for those not following the link) a newly mastered/edited transfer of the film "The Dreamer That Remains", a newly transferred film version of "The Delusion of the Fury", a slideshow of his instruments to accompany the original spoked text descriptions Partch did on the instrumental demo LP in the original "Delusion" package, some footage from the original production of "Revelation in the Courthouse Park" and a little bonus out-take material. Dreamer is a great introduction to Partch, something we always showed people when they became members of the ensemble.

The entire Enclosures series is a remarkable project all on its own, and a very complete take on one man's life-long journey to create a very individual artistic vision.

And it wasn't in 12tet.
