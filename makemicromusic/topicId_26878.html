Well...happenstance, mostly.

It happened that I was looking at 52et after working on "Nigel-38"; it
also happened that I wanted -- for "Empathica III" -- a hexitonic scale
built from unequal steps (gamelans & gagaku were on my mind). Right
under my nose, 52et happened to have some very nice variants in small &
large tones. I selected 10 pitches for 2 scales:

This looks really interesting, but I haven't had a chance to listen

course, but it's interesting that 52 = 2^2 * 13, so there's a flavor of

resources as well. So again, interesting stuff....
