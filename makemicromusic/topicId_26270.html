Carlo <carlo@...>

3/4/2011 12:13:48 AM

Hi Jake,

in case of 19EDO/Carlos Beta my reasoning has simply been to translate the original 12EDO score of "Over the Rainbow" using those 19 notes per octave (or stretched octave in the case of Beta) as 7 natural, 5 sharp and 7 flat notes so the harmonic structure is quite similar to 12EDO, without the enharmonic equivalence of sharps and flats though.
I hope I am making myself clear,

Best,

Carlo
