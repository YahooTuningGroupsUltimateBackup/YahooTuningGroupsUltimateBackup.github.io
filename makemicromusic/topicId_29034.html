Here are three new pieces I just made. I was trying to meet the RPM Challenge, in which you record an album of six tunes or 35 minutes of music during the month of February. I made three and finished on March 3 this time, late and incomplete. But definitely worth doing.

In the pieces I take a bit of text and try to tease some music out of it. All in good fun. They are scored for winds, percussion, and strings, plus the speech part.

You can listen here: http://ripnread.com/?page_id=547

Or download them here:

1. Principle: http://ripnread.com/listen/drum14-t10.mp3
2. Barely Make It: http://ripnread.com/listen/drum15-t5.mp3
3. Rules: http://ripnread.com/listen/drum17-t9.mp3

Prent Rodgers
