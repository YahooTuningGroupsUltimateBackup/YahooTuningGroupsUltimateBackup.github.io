The closest to neoprog may be Charles Carpenter. He has two cds out of
heavy King Crimsonish music in the Bohlen Pierce scale: Frog a la Peche and
Splat.

If you want something purely classical flavored then the Easley Blackwood
Microtonal Etudes cd is very nice.
