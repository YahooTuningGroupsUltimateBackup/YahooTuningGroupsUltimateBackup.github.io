I'm enjoying ZIA a lot!

In my escapades searching for microtonal audio, I enjoy finding incredible
style differences. It's an interesting thing that there's a unified
microtonal internet community because the thing that we all have in common
is really such a small part of music. Our credo is "Say no to 12 equal,"
but beyond that, we can choose to do anything, so many different kinds of
music are all connected. Rock guitarists, synth-pop bands, college
professors, symphonic composers... all unified by the simplest little idea,
that we like to use tunings other than 12tET. I think that's awesome!
