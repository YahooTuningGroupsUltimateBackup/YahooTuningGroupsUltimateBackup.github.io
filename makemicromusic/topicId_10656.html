I have put up the recorded remnants of my junior recital last Friday
to my soundclick site:
http://www.soundclick.com/bands/pagemusic.cfm?bandID=145852

There is music for 1, 2, 4, and 8 bassoons, with all the multiples
being copies of me. There is also a three-movement slide bottle sextet
performed by "Bottlestar Galactica." I hope to talk more about slide
bottles over at new_instruments (my comatose Yahoo group) and would
love to see more people playing them and/or writing for them.

Also featured on the recital were the hit single "foum" and an old
(twelvulated) Theme and Variations for Trumpet and Piano.

It was a messy, ill-prepared, more starting than finishing recital,
and these are all pretty rough recordings. But the realism is nice
about them I think.

Enjoyt,
Jacob b
