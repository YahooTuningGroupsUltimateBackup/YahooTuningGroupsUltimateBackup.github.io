As an exercise in futility, I have used the midi file provided by Marcel, and LucyTuned it to 3b2s i.e CC#DEbEFF#GAbABbB.

This gives us a rather strange chord of CEbF#A in bar 20, yet it actually sounds right.

I suppose you could consider it to be F# diminished 7. (F# A C Eb) three steps of bIIIrds.
