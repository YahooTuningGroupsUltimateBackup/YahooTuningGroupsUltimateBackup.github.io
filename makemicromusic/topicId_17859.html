Even Ptolemy mentioned this as you can find in his writings. He suggested that the interspersing notes in the lower tetrachord be higher than those found in the upper. My thought on this i think was to increase the feeling of mode in the sense that only certain tones would be perfect concords so one could tell easier what the mode is.
