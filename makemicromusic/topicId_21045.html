A radio broadcast of the first concert performance of my recent work 'Boost' for cello and analogue synthesizer can be heard at http://areena.yle.fi/audio/426857 until Oct 17.
The piece starts at 17'25''

It is preceded by an orchestral piece by Jouni Kaipainen. Both works are introduced in Finnish by Olli Virtaperko, another composer.

'Boost' is a virtuoso toccata written in extended just intonation (11-limit, mostly 7-limit). The cellist is Juho Laitinen. Jouko Laivuori plays the synth part, using a Dave Smith Mopho re-tuned with the pitchbend method.

Juhani Nuorvala
Finland
