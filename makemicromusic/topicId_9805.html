Cinema Volta: Daily Ambience
Fresh Ambient Music Composed Daily
a new tune every day, free of charge
at: http://www.cinemavolta.com/daily

On January 30th, I began a project to release a new ambient track
each each day for a year as an exploration of ambient music and the
limits of my own creativity.

Today the project reached the one quarter point - 91 songs. I can
barely believe it myself. The project has been a lot of fun and hard
work and I'm really glad I started it.

For those of you who have been following along, thanks for
listening. For those of you who haven't - this is your chance to get
in on the fun.

Podcasting is starting to get a great deal of attention and from day
one, this site has supported that technology. If you have an iPod or
another portable music player, you can have the music automatically
downloaded to your player as it comes out. You can find more
information on the site
