Today must be the day for posting music... Chris Bailey posted the cool
Little Piece for Lou Harrison, which I enjoyed... and here's something else
for you all...

I finally made a little piece of music with the latest Rhino beta, just as
a demo. You can get it here:

http://rm-and-jo.laughingsquid.org/Music/RhinoPrelude-2003-02-22.mp3

It's about 1 minute long, piece for quintet of instruments in 10
tones/ocatave. Patches are all ones I've designed over the last couple of
weeks. I used 5 instances of Rhino inside VAZ Modular host, and used a
little bit of reverb.
