Hello everyone!

Here are 4 tracks from the Hickman/Pancini
Project.
I played my "out of tune" piano and Mike
"went around the 12 tone pitches" in stanard
tuning.
I would have to state this is not really
microtonal music. But, we did try to add
different colours with different articulations,
dynamics, and Mike's glissados in his playing.

I thought it was an interesting idea and I wanted
to share these tracks with the group!
So, give them a listen when you have the chance
and let me know what you think!

Mike and I had fun doing this project!

Thanks!
