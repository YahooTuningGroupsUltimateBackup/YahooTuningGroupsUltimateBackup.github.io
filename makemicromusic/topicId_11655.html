There's a good article on fretless guitars in the new GP mag, and the second in as many months where the topic of tuning is discussed. I'm hoping this will become a trend; and I'm trying to get them to let me write a more full blown article on tunings, but it's slow going. Any mainstream press is a good thing, and perhaps the guitar community will become a leader in the recognition of non 12 tunings. Go to unfretted.com for a look at a large group of fretless guitarists, most of whom are good to excellent players...HHH
