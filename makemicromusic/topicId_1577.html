List/friends,

I've got the basic parameters of a site up - point your browsers to
http://www.microtonal.org/

What you see is a framework of the place; the main reason it exists
is to have somewhere to upload mp3 files, and if no one ever *visits*
the site, fine (we can just post links to the files). One thing that
will be there, and you can test it out, is streaming of a RealAudio
file so even a casual browser to the site can hear one of the pieces.

However, I'll fill it out as much as people want, so just take a
quick look, and then give some feedback. Essentially what I would
like are your recommendations for:

- sites that should be linked to (tools, info, resources, personal
sites)

- if you would like to be listed in the members page (including links
to your own site, if you have one)

- any other information you would like to see there (within reason!)

- and, obviously, any problems with the site.

This was a quicky, and I've got other duties to attend to, but it's
in place and awaiting the first mp3 file to upload (which will prod
me to put together a page of instructions for uploading!).

Cheers,
ListMom
