The North American Embassy of Anaphoria Island has added a page to our
site featuring some Harp compositions by composer Larry London.
http://anaphoria.com/london.html A student and collaborator of Lou
Harrison who was the 2012 artist in residence at Anaphoria's own Radio
Nabu Congul. Excuse the informal picture of London's instrument, yet we
feel it gives one a good idea of the scale. The original design is from
Colvig and Harrison. Selected scores can be found in issues of Xenharmonikon
