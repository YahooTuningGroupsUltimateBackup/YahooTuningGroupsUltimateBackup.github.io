This setting of Psalm 4 was commissioned by John O’Sullivan. The commission
was for a composition in Dorian mode of Raven V2 tuning. (For more on
John’s Raven tuning follow this link <http://www.johnsmusic7.com/>) I
present here three versions, piano only and one with my vocal added times
three and a capella. On the last version I did tweak my vocals with v-vocal
editor (bot NOT to 12 equal) to take care of my weak ability to sustain a
pitch accurately since here I can’t hide behind a piano.
