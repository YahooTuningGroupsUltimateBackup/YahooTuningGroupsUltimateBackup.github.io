(Kleismic) Joy. Udderbots, humming, singing, gong. The tuning has
been called Kleismic[11] out of 19-edo.

The "udderboxing" uses samples made on the udderbot, plugged into ChucK
code that arranges them into ever-morphing Moment-of-Symmetry rhythms.
The generator begins at 3/16 then plunges down through 2/11 (marked by
the reverse gong) to 3/17, then, at the end, 1/8 folding into 0/1.

The udderbot is a slide woodwind instrument capable of up to a 5 octave
range. I currently can't afford a server for www.udderbot.com, but I
would direct you to that in better times. A whole album "An Udderbot
Xmas" was begun but not finished this season; maybe next year!

Enjoy!
