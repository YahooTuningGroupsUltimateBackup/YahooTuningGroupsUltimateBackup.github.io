Hi there,

I've just done an improv. in a spiralling
hexany that I've been playing around with:

1/1 8/7 12/7 96/35 12/5 32/15 4/3

Non octave, repeats at 4/3.

Pitches go up and down in waves.

Has some pure octaves spanning > 6 notes.

Any consecutive three notes play a triad.
So do four of the six possible sets of
non consecutive triads with steps of
two degrees such as 0 2 4,
and the remaining "crunchy" chords
are quite interesting too.

Improvisation page says a bit more about it.

http://members.tripod.com/~robertinventor/tunes/improvisations.htm#spiralling_hexany
