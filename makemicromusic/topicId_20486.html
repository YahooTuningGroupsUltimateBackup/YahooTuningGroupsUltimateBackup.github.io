for anyone who might be interested, i've been working on some collaborations with French Canadian vincent Bergeron, whose music, artistic commitment, and integrity i've long admired:

http://www.yousendit.com/download/U0d3SU5BMm1ENlJjR0E9PQ

my contributions here, while decidedly microtonal, are also minimal and completely absorbed into that particular thing that is Vincent-world. Anyway, I just love the Copland-like hoedown/Irish dance interludes, and Viveka Eriksson (female vocals) and Jorg Fischer(traps) really shine here as well IMO.check it
