Please excuse the cross-posting.

I've just released a revised and renamed version of the piece I originally called "Symmetrical Melodic Variation on the Romantic Symmetry". It's now called "Resonant Palindrome", and although the notes and structure are the same, the timbres, textures, and pacing have changed.

The piece is a variation on La Monte Young's "The Romantic Symmetry (over a 60 cycle base) in Prime Time from 144 to 112 with 119".

Commentary, Csound score, and audio files (MP3 and Ogg-Vorbis) can be found at http://mysterybear.net/article/7/resonant-palindrome.

As always, comments are welcome.
