Dear MMM,

I just finished reformatting and re-updating the definitive Microtonal
Listening List. Because I just care like that. And I'm
procrastinating. And I think that educationally it's an extremely
important resource.

http://xenharmonic.wikispaces.com/MicrotonalListeningList

This list was started by Andrew Heathwaite, but he's not currently
working on it and his Angelfire webpage is good and frozen, so update
your links, mkay?

I know a lot of pages full of links are flying around everywhere, and
I would like this one to be useful to novices. I tried to make the
short descriptions better, we could really use a page of recommended
microtonal listenings; specific pieces that have affected you, and
how, and why. And the list needs to distinguish between people with
full-blown corpera of microtonality and the one-hit wonders.

If you can make it better, or more complete, or less offensive and
label-dependent, please do. The existence of this wiki is intended for
widespread, collaborative editing.

Happy Birthday one and all,
Jacob
