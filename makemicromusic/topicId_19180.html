This is again a piece featuring Carlos Alpha, a non-octave scale based on the division of a
perfect fifth into 9 equal parts (each step 77.995 cents).The song has been composed,
recorded and mixed with Reason 4.
This piece uses a new feature X.J.Scott added to a beta version of his LMSO: "spectrally
matched waveforms" (see the blog for more informations).
