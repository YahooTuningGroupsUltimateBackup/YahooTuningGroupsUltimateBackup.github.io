Just because a post did not receive any responses does not necessarily
mean you are "un-welcome". There could be a number of reasons why
people did not respond. One time I posted an announcement for a
musical piece that I had posted on my SoundClick page. To the best of
my recollection, no one responded to that message here on the forum.
I noticed, however, that the traffic to that song I mentioned had
increased noticeably for the next few days thereafter. So, I know
that people did at least take the time to listen to it, even if there
wasn't a specific written response of any kind. My song even got a
fairly significant ranking in its genre for a while because of the
traffic so at least enough people listened to it and marked enough
"stars" on it to work it up the ranks.

Have fun and I hope you stick around.
