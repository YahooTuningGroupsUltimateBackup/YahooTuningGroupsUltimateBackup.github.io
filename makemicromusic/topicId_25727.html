Hello all,

I've prepared the *score *of the *Cyprus Piano Concerto* for those
intersted. It's at the musics section of my website:

http://www.ozanyarman.com/musics

or for Turkish speakers:

http://www.ozanyarman.com/muzikler

The employment of the Hüzzam and Neveser maqams with the microtonal
accidentals of yonder days can be scrutinized in the pertinent pages of
the work.

The score still needs to be expanded via a more thorough orchestration
for a grander effect. I had had commenced re-writing the Concerto a
couple of years back, adding new material in the process, but stalled
due to a lack of motivation. I may get around to it again sometime in
the future.

You can also find therein the *scores & **music* (check the flash
applet) for freshly re-mastered two symphonic pieces of mine - although
completely non-microtonal - titled "*English Anthem*" (2004) and
"*Balkan Carnival*" (2003). These are supposed to be Movements for a
projected "European Symphony" of mine, which I never got around to
finishing.

Enjoy!
