I've posted another podcast of microtonal music on
http://podcast1024.libsyn.com. This one is almost 45 minutes long, and
weighs in at 41MB in size, so it will take a while for my dial up
friends to receive it. As always, you can subscribe at
http://podcast1024.libsyn.com/rss with a podcast client, like
PodcastReady or iTunes.

It includes lots of pieces posted recently on this list, including
work by Aaron Johnson, Jon Smith, Margo Shulter, Dante Rosati, Hudson
Lacerda, and one by me. The theme is Neo-Medieval Music, and a lot of
it could have been written in 1300, in a kind of alternative history
way. There's also a clip of Partch talking about the how important it
is that "the tongue must couple with the cavity." Sexy beast.

Thanks for listening!
