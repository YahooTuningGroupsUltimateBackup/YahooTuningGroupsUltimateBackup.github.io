***Thanks, Jeff! Nice to see you there. I have to say that Carson
Cooman has a particular talent for setting up dramatic registrations
on the organ.

Speaking of microtonality... in doing the program notes I realized
that this work was done when I was in the midst of many specifically
microtonal works... Although the *organ* is not a particularly
convenient instrument for microtonality, I was mentioning to Carson
that I thought I was getting halfway there through the use of
clusters, which are used to offset other elements several times in
the piece...
