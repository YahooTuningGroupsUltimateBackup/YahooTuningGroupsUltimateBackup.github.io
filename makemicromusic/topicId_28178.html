There have been some discussions about various micronal conrollers on this list. You can check, e.g.,

http://launch.groups.yahoo.com/group/tuning/message/76190 http://launch.groups.yahoo.com/group/tuning/message/76549

and the follow-up messages.

But you can also play microtonal scales on a standard MIDI keyboard - this is actually how I am doing it currently. All you need is a synthesizer, expander or software synthesizer that supports retuning. Nowadays many synthesizers support this; you can get information about his on http://www.microtonal-synthesis.com .

Welcome on this list and good luck!
