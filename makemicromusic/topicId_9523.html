Harvey Starr recently posted on the ztar group that he is still
working on a Ztar that will use 6 ribbon controllers (per "string").
This seems like a good time to contact him if you are considering
buying this for your microtonal work with questions/comments if you
have specific features in mind. I would think a "quantize position"
feature wherein your finger position is quantized to the nearest pitch
of your desired tuning, to facilitate chords, would be one of them.
