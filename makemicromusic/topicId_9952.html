I've just posted a new piece called "Drift Dhikr" to http://mysterybear.net/article/12. As usual, there are MP3 and OGG versions for download, as well as the Csound orchestra/score.

From the web page: "Pitches start at the perfect fifth and simultaneously glide up to the octave and down to tonic, and an everchanging chord emerges from the reinforced difference and summation tones. A slowly changing ambient landscape of relative consonance and dissonance."

Comments are welcome.
