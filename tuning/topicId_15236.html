In 12-tone equal temperament, going up n semitones means multiplying
the frequency by

2^(n/12).

Going down n semitones means multiplying by 2^(-n/12), or
equivalently, dividing by 2^(n/12).

In m-note equal temperament, the formula is 2^(n/m).
