Herein is the info for the Microthon 2000 event.

DATE: November 11th (Veteran's Day), Saturday between
noon and 9 P.M.

LOCATION: In Manhattan (New York City) at the Quaker Meeting House located at 15 Rutherford Place (between 2nd and 3rd Avenue and East 15 and 16 Streets. The performances take place in the Quaker Meeting Room.

DESCRIPTION: The Quaker Building House is a red brick 19th Century structure.

ADMISSION: $12 for the entire day. Hands will be stamped with a Kokopelli insignia (the AFMM logo).

REFRESHMENTS: There will be an hour (or more) for dinner. And just outside the entrance there will be a 10 X 10 refreshment stand for coffee and fresh lemonade, among other things. The neighborhood offers much in the way of food, including Indian and Ukrainian delicacies.

Hope to meet some of you at the event.

Best, Johnny Reinhard
