Hi Carl,

it's the Yamaha QS-300. I keep using it because it's fully XG-compatible, including the XG tuning messages. I used the samples in a totally different way than they are expected to be used -- i.e. to immitate the reeds of old organs, I've sped up the saxophone sound, to immitate some old bowed instruments, I've sped up the double bass sound.

BTW: Except from the variations of 2009, all the others are tuned (in theory) to 50-equal (of course, one never knows how much the samples deviate from the proper pitches).

Petr
