Good generators for this are a sharp fifth, "f", and and even sharper
supermajor third (9/7), "t". That 245/243~1 means that (9/7)^2~5/3,
which is basic to this system.

We have 3 ~ 2f, 5 ~ 2ft^2 and 7 ~ 2^2f^2t^(-1), and the 7-limit least
squares fit has the curious feature that the 7 is pure. We have a
fifth 2.92 cents sharp and a major third 0.417 cents sharp, again
quite nice values. Here is a rectangle of notes of this system:
