I've spend many a year trying to figure out what system of accidentals to use for JI as well as 72 and other ETs, and I'm settling on Helmholtz-Ellis, at least up to 13-limit.

http://newmusicbox.com/article.nmbx?id=4191

The reason: I'm already used to using Tartini-Couper accidentals for quarter tones, and these are used for raising and lowering a note by 33/32. The 27/26 third-tone flat looks a little strange to me, and I might want to use Persian koron instead, but either way. I'm not sure I like the use of diagonal marks, pluses and minuses and arrows on the side for 17-limit and up. I wouldn't need those for 72 ET anyway.
