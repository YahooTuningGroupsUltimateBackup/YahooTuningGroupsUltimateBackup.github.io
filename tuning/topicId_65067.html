There are new releases of the OSX and Linux versions of Scala.
The OSX version now can also play midi-files without the need
for an external player, like the Windows version. Use PLAY and
PLAY/STOP.
Tuning support for NI Kontakt and Korg X5DR has been added.
Thanks to Nick Dan, Steven Miller and Bill Sethares for their
help.
http://www.xs4all.nl/~huygensf/scala/downloads.html
