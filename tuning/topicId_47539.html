*Online interview with Young & Zazeela NOW in October NewMusicBox*
The long awaited 4+ hour conversation with La Monte Young
and Marian Zazeela by Frank Oteri on the release of the DVD
of /The Well-Tuned Piano in The Magenta Lights/ and many
other subjects is now online in the October issue of
http://www.newmusicbox.org, the American Music Center's
award-winning magazine. With 27 sub-sections, live streaming
video clips of La Monte and Marian speaking, and many informative
URL reference links, it is the featured interview in /In The First Person/. Highlights include: * Studying with Pandit Pran Nath in the /guru-shishya /tradition
(Section 3. /The Guru-Disciple Relationship)/
* La Monte as disciple and as mentor (Section 9. /How to Learn)/
* The development of Marian's light designs (Section 11.
/Alternative Concert Venues)/
* The DVD format as a platform for Young-Zazeela works (Section 15.
/DVD)/
* Releases and plans for the Just Dreams label (Section 16. /The
Tamburas of Pandit Pran Nath)/
* On The Theatre of Eternal Music Big Band (Section 17. /Working
with Musicians and Orchestras)/
* Why La Monte avoided the major third (Section 23. /Choosing
Intervals)/
