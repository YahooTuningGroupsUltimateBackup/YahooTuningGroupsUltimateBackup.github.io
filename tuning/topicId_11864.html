musical notation but really it wasn't according to Monzo) said that the number of syllables of

the very elaborate games that writers play in their books to hide certain things for their
desired audience of readers. Berg did something like this too. Except that his was more for
the humor. He would construct melodies from the names of his friends using the letters as
notes.. all that music that has influenced the world so was merely letter games for Berg.
