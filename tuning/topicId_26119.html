Hi Dan,

It's been far too long since I've written anything here about
my reactions to your music. I *love* the warped weirdness
of it! :) _Out in the Street_ was great, and _Silvered
Alar Rise!_ is massaging my ears right now as I type this.

One of the things I admire most about my idol Mahler (whose
141st birthday was yesterday) is the way he created a whole
universe in sound, of such a deep complexity that one really
believes it mirrors the outer universe of our everyday lives.
If you allow yourself to be taken in, the music takes you
somewhere else, far away from your ordinary reality.

I hear this in your pieces too.
