Haresh asked:
>Can we have the pitchbend data on 5-limit, 22-shruti Indian system?

The format is semitones followed by pitch bend offset (pitch_bend
attribute in Scala).
For the actual MIDI pitch bend number, add 8192.

0: 1/1 0. 0
1: 256/243 1.-400
2: 16/15 1. 481
3: 10/9 2.-721
4: 9/8 2. 160
5: 32/27 3.-240
6: 6/5 3. 641
7: 5/4 4.-561
8: 81/64 4. 320
9: 4/3 5.-80
10: 27/20 5. 801
11: 45/32 6.-400
12: 729/512 6. 480
13: 3/2 7. 80
14: 128/81 8.-320
15: 8/5 8. 561
16: 5/3 9.-641
17: 27/16 9. 240
18: 16/9 10.-160
19: 9/5 10. 721
20: 15/8 11.-481
21: 243/128 11. 400
22: 2/1 12. 0

Manuel
