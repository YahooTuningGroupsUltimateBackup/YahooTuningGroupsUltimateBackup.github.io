A big thanks to David Beardsly for putting up Real Audio files of his
all microtonal Juxtaposition Net Radio broadcast
(http://www.virtulink.com/immp/jux/radio.htm). The first time I tried
to access one of these files it seemed to take upwards of a billion
years to download (though this was probably a result of my own lack of
computer savvy), but for whatever reason that is no longer the case! A
click of the mouse and you've got instant access to many many hours of
microtonal music... Up until tonight I had never heard anything by
Ezra Simms, Johnny Reinhard, Jon Catler, Kyle Gann, Rod Poole... what
a JOY.
