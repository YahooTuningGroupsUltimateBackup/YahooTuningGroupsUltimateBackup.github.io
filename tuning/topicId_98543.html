Re the English Cadence, I've been listening to pre-tonal music a lot, both Medeivel and Renaissance (and this list was a key in turning me on to a lot of
It). It was obvious there was some momentary 'spiciness' in the Tallis I was listening to. It sounds a lot better with a choir than an mp3 piano. Harmony
(whether consonant or dissonant) was a byproduct of melodies, leading tones came before Vmajor-->I! So the 'augmented octaves' is just a harmonic
collision between the 'rising' melody and the 'falling' melody. The amount of spiciness may have a lot to do with how a choir tunes it. For a "sharps are
higher than flats" kind of tuning, it will be a milder dissonance than a meantone.
