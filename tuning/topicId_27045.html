It's well known that unfinished Fugue in "Die Kunst der Fuga"
has a palindromic theme. D-A-G-F-G-A-D; melody in Gematria:
4-1-7-6-7-1-4

> "wohltemperirt"

1 Tempering fifth B-f#
7 perfect fifths.
