Hi everyone,

Nihavent, the microtonal score editor, completed its beta phase and is out
now as Mus2 with the backing of Istanbul-based software house Data-Soft Ltd.

I wanted to take this opportunity to thank everyone who provided feedback,
bug reports and suggestions, both on this list and through private mail, to
help us gradually build a tool that hopefully many will find useful.

The new website for Mus2 is now at www.mus2.com.tr/en/. As always, we'd love
to hear your thoughts, questions and suggestions.

Best,

Utku Uzmen
