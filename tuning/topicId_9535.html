Hey, so far so good! Here's problems I've encountered thus far:

1. The "Ratio Calculation" (on the bottom-right) appears to ignore the
second ratio entered, and just squares the first ratio. For example, 2/3 x
5/7 should yield 10/21, but incorrectly yields 4/9 instead.

2. The "balloon help" for the "Sound Off" button (displayed when the
cursor hovers over the button) says "Enable Sound". This is misleading; it
should probably say "Disable Sound".

3. I'm not a "Mac" person; I have never used JI Calc before. You'll
probably want more than just an "About" command in the "Help" menu. Maybe
at least a link to online help.
