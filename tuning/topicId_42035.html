Hi Graham,

I have to disagree with every point you tried to make. I won't go on at
length here on the list, but for every argument you propose /for/ I can
find at least three against.

MIDI is the tyrrany of twelve. If you want to make music: all the SW is
stacked against the microtonalist. Not /one/ commercial product includes
microtonal support as an /inherent/ component of the possibilities for
music making. Add-ons that some provide are second-rate, friday jobs, not
worth the characters they're written with.

And so on...

I really can't waste my time explaining - there are plenty on this list who
know the /real/ position. Anything else is just self-delusion.

It's like being told that a dog can walk on its hind legs - I'm not
suprised it can be done, but would be suprised that it can be done well.

And I see no dogs walking well.
