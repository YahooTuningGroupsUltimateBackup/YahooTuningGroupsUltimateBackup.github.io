This is an updated version of "Yulegu Island at Twilight", using sounds based on the gangsa and reyong instruments, but adapted to the tuning. For the top two parts I started with the imitation gangsa sound, changing the envelopes and filtering to get more of a wooden sound like a marimba. The bass part has more of a metallic sound, with a tremolo effect added to simulate detuning. I added a fourth part which mainly doubles notes from the other three parts, which is played by the reyong.

Compare the original version:
