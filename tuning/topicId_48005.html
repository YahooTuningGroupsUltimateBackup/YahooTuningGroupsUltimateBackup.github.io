The American Music Center now has Michael Harrison's _Revelation_ as
performed by Joshua Pierce on their website:

http://www.newmusicbox.org/webcasts/revelation/index.nmbx

It's a "streaming" item, so it's important to have a fast connection
to get the most out of it...

The video was well done, with some great shots of Josh and the inside
of the piano...
