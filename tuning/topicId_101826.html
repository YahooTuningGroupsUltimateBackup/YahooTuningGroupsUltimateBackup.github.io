Yeah! This guy is awesome!

This is a great example of someone who writes music in at least 7-limit JI (in practice it might be schismatic temperament or something, point is it's quite accurate and not meantone), with more or less familiar-sounding tonality, just "decorated" with xenharmonic sounds, but I'd describe it as *breathtakingly* beautiful.

In this respect I'd put him in the same category as Eivind Groven, another of my favorites (check out this CD: http://www.musikkonline.no/shop/displayAlbum.asp?id=24480 ). Can anyone recommend any similar artists?

I remember finding his website ( http://eufonia.de/ ) many years ago, wishing I could read German, and having my mind blown by things like "Sonnenwind" and the other examples on this page:
http://eufonia.de/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=105&Itemid=179
Does anyone know how I can buy a CD or something that has these complete tracks??

Another great piece is http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mD8bI4hNQSE , and also http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6gxuySP08Is which I've just heard for the first time. I'm so glad this stuff is on Youtube now.
