That is the reason why I have spoken (written) a lot of him in my book (with Costeley, Huygens, Sauveur, Euler, and many others). There is a photo of him in page 74, at the end of section VIII (Temperaments). This leads us to Micro-tonality, which is present almost everywhere in ï¿½Histoire de lï¿½Acoustique Musicaleï¿½. Section XII is devoted to Just Intonation Temperaments of more than 12 notes (19, 31 and 43), and section XIV is on Micro-Tonality in general, including 24-ET. Micro-tonality is also treated with the arabic scale of 24 quarter-tones and indian 22 shrutis. Just Intonation 12-ET Tuning has also a great part of this book.
