Ellen Fullman has a new release of early works for the Long String Instrument
out on Anomalous Recordings. Stunning music in JI.

Tunings include:

Staggered Stasis (1987)

1. Pythagorean
2. Seven limit overtone series
3. 5/4s (major and minor thirds)

Duration (1986) - 11 limit harmonics

...and if you haven't, be sure to check out the
other Long String Instrument releases: Body Music (Experimental Intermedia),
Change of Direction (New Albion) and the collaboration with the
Deep Listening Band, Suspended Music (Periplum).

Those three days with DLB and LSI at Columbia U. a few years
ago were amazing...
