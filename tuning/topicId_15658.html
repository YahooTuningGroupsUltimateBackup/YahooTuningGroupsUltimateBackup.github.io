Here's a concert announcement I thought I'd pass along should there be
any interested Boston area folks. (Scott Dakota plays extended JI on a
wide-range fretless guitar of his own design.)

Scott Dakota of The Moors
Plays Boston Loopers Collective As One Of Many
Crazy and Wonderful Looper Musicians
Heavy on the Guitars
Monday November 27 Middle East Down
8:00 pm (no kidding, early show) doors 7:30
18+, $7/$5 with printout of this or any other e-mail promo
*
Boston Loopers Collective #6, an ongoing project helmed by David
Kirkdorffer (UNDO, Little a) featuring area and beyond musicians that
use real time looping delays delays to facilitate self accompanying
sound construction.

This is a special six guitar, six gun edition, as David has been
threatening. Each will perform solo sets, followed by small
spontaneous ensembles, then the irresistible attraction to a six
guitar finish.

Featured Fretted Artistes (Scott Dakota will have no frets) include:
UNDO (aka David Kirkdorffer) * rosS Hamlin * T.G. Noyes * 2KJB (aka
Plastic Razor Protector) * Frank Gerace (of Dreamchild)

Also Live Video Mixing, and artsy underground ambience. A cool scene.

it out. Feel free to pass along to any friends who might dig it.
