--- In tuning@yahoogroups.com, "Mohajeri Shahin" <shahinm@...> wrote:

> 1- what is exact meaning of temperament , has every arrangement of
> intervals a temperament?

Temperament means that intervals are tempered, or adjusted, away from
the theoretical just value so they can play more than one role. For
instance, a whole tone might be employed both as a fifth above a fifth
reduced to an octave, or as a minor third below a fourth.

> 2-In edl or ado , we can have 12-tone scales with 12 different fifth ,
> what about this?

Different sizes for 7 scale steps out of 12 is usual, and may involve
tempering or may not.
