i am usually just a lurker, but I really like The Bali Sessions produced by
Bob Weir. Its a 3 disk set that runs about $20. It includes 2 disks of
traditional gamelan with the third being 'new' compositions that take the
traditional gamelan into a modern composer setting. Might not be the best
or something, but I am very pleased with it!
happy holidays
erik
