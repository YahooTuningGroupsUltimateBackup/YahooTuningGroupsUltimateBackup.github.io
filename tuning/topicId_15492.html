Thought it would be a good time to write of the recent Vicentino
performance on the past AFMM concert. We are presently awaiting a mastered digital
recording...any day now.

The intonation in the performance was good over all. I might wish
entrances to be "thinner" or less rich. A particular solo is heard quite
differently than I would have designed, for example. But the microtonal movement,
from a just intonation model of tuning, remained intact.

As soon as our video is wedded to the new mastered sound, I will release
the recording for viewing. Alas, we did not have the power of singers who
have devoted themselves to their voices, at 100%. Our soprano described the
performance as "soulful."

Anyone out there see the performance?
