BeOS internet radio (run by enthusiasts of the "little operating
system that could" that wouldn't die even when they unplugged the
respirator) is looking for original material to play on their 24 hour
a day radio show. Check it out at http://www.beosradio.com or write to
submissions@beosradio.com for information on making your music
available for airplay (wireplay?). I did, and they put "Dance of the
Mermaids" in rotation. I wonder if it's their first microtonal music?
