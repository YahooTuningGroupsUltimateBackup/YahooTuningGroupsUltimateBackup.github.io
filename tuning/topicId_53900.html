Well, some of them are tuned with Midiconv and some of them are tuned with Scala. I wonder if that has anything to do with it? Scala adds an extra track and puts all the pitch bend messages in that track. But they should be getting sent to the proper MIDI channels. Maybe your MIDI player gets confused with having messages from more than one track sent to the same channel, and messages from one track sent to more than one channel?
