Here's a list of CD's of various styles with interesting non 12 tunings:

Ahmed Mukhtar...The Road to Baghdad
Wendy Carlos...Switched on Bach 2000 & Beauty In The Beast, (a real masterpiece)
Village Of The Unfretted (unfretted.com)
Enid Katahn...Beethoven In The Temperaments (great job on the liner notes, Ed)
Mei Han...Outside The Wall
Catler Bros...Crash Landing
Naseer Shamma...The Lute of Baghdad
Ivor Darreg...Detwelvulate
Mayumi Tsuda...Microtonal Works
John Schneider...Just Guitars
Easley Blackwood...Microtonal
David Fiuczynski...Kif
And, check out guitar9.com to hear samples of some of my music...HHH PS...not sure if this got through a while back, but pictures of my guitar collection are up at microstick.net...
