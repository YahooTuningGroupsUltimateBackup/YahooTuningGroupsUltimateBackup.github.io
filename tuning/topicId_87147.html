Hello everybody this a reminder that at the start of May I'll begin compiling the Split Notes compilation album with the help of Tony Dubshot and Michael S.

The album will be released for free online download, and will be a valuable tool in promoting the goodness of microtonal music. The theme of the album is simple - microtonal scales in popular genres.

We're accepting track submissions until April 30th, so you still have plenty of time.

You can send in your submission by email, following the guidelines on this information page: http://split-notes.com/compilation2010.php
