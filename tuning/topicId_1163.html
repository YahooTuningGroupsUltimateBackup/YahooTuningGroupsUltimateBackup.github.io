Dan Wolf wrote:

> Is there any meaningful distinction between
> periodicity and finity/infinity here?

Well, now that I've become familiar with Fokker's
ideas and terminology, I suppose not. Most probably,
my mystical side was waxing poetic when I thought
up the ideas myself.

Still, even acknowledging the similiarities between
my theories and Fokker's (and Erv Wilson's), a
nagging voice in my head keeps telling me that
there's more to the finity/inifinity aspect that hasn't
yet been uncovered - I'm thinking particularly of
the ways different parts of the brain process
information differently, in that there may be a
subtle difference between what we experience
thru analysis and what we hear/feel directly.
To my way of thinking, both types of
perceptual-cognitive operations are at work
when we listen to music (and speech).
