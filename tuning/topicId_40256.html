those things that james mccartney called bingo cards . . .

i made them hexagonal (they mean exactly the same thing as the
triangular lattices i always draw around here). now the unison
vectors point in exactly the same direction as the corresponding
lines in my ET graph (xoomer.gif).

for example, if you go three hexes east and one hex southeast, you've
moved by a syntonic comma -- draw a line connecting your starting
point and your ending point -- and on xoomer.gif, the ETs where the
syntonic comma vanishes (i.e., meantones) all fall along a line
pointing in exactly the same direction as the line you drew!
