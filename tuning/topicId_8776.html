I feel it only fair to extend a warning to you all as to the inherant
dangers of altering you're brain's chemistry with chemicals.

These can permanantly change your personality, and you're psych.

Even so called "harmless" substances such as marijuana can cause both brain
damage, lack of motivation, and to the very suseptible, flashbacks can occur
with the use of marijuana.

A close friend of mine injested a muffin laced with hashish oil, some time
back, and was very nearly institutionalised,-it completely freaked him out.

He thought he was going insane....

I would not recomend any experimentation what-so-ever to try to enhance
you're pleasure and awareness of music, microtonal or otherwise.

A word of advice to you all, on the tuning list,-I see you all, well, for
the want of a better word, "percieve" you all as friends, and I would not
wish on any of you, such a terrifing experience that psychadellics and
hallucinogens can give you.

----Sarn.
