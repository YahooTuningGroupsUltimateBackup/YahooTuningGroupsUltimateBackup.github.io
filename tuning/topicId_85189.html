tracks to a fair composition contest on the internet. 19 interesting
pieces entered this contest titled the "SoOn 2009 Compo" organized by

continue until 7 November 2009 11:59:59.

Voting procedure:

Just send an e-mail (JUST ONE) to 2009vote@... containing a
text like this:

Best (5 points) is: Entry #XXX by XXX
2nd place (3 points) is: Entry #XXX by XXX
3rd place (1 points) is: Entry #XXX by XXX

That is to say, give 5 points to the track that you think deserves the
first place, 3 points to the track that you believe deserves the
second place and 1 points to the track that you feel deserves the
third place.

For example:

Best (5 points) is: Entry #17 "The Saba Storm" by Ozan Yarman,
2nd place (3 points) is: Entry #10 "Freaks we are" by dj Alces,
3rd place (1 points) is: Entry #19 "Behind The Sun" by solo.

Use the bold text and modify the underlined text to include your track
of choice and its composer and then send it as an e-mail to 2009vote@...
.

*

The microtonal pitches heard in my composition "The Saba Storm" (Entry
#17) are those established in the Yarman-36a tuning scheme. For
details (in Turkish, but with video demonstrations) navigate to:

http://www.ozanyarman.com/yarman36.html

*

"The Saba Storm" appears to be the only entry in the SoOn 2009 Compo
that is composed intentionally microtonally. It embodies not only Saba
aires characteristic of Turkiye, but also cleverly melds these
traditional Middle Eastern melodies with the trance-disco style and
beat. I await your support for entry number 17, "The Saba Storm"!

I thank you all for sharing a moment of your precious time to vote.
