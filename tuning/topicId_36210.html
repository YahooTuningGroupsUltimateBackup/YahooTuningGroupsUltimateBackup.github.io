The Pulse Ensemble Theatre will be producing a second run of
Shakespeare's _The Tempest_ directed by Alexa Kelly and using the
music of Joseph Pehrson. This music consists of original pieces, as
well as repertoire items from the Renaissance. All of it is in the
tuning of Shakespeare's day, "quarter comma meantone."

Linda Past (Pehrson) is in the production as the "goddess Juno."

_The Tempest_ runs from April 17 through May 12, 2002. It's
Wednesdays through Saturdays at 8 PM, and Sundays at 3PM.

Please note the address. It's *not* at the Pulse Theatre, but down
in the next block:

531 W. 42nd St. NYC

All shows (except opening night) are $18

Reservations: 212-695-1596

Or you can order online at the Pulse Website:

http://www.pulseensembletheatre.org/

The Pehrson music for the production is also online at:

http://artists.mp3s.com/artists/299/joseph_pehrson_tempest.html
