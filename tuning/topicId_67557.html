Yes, Xenharmonikon 18 has finally been published. Because of the size of
this issue and the fact that my time is rather limited these days, I
printed only enough copies for the contributors and myself. However,
copies will be available from Frog Peak Music, P.O. Box 1052, Lebanon,
NH 03766,
and on the WEB at http://www.frogpeak.org/ .

Frog Peak Music, "A Composers' Collective," was founded as by Larry
Polansky and Jody Diamond when they were at Mills College in the 1980s
and subsequently accompanied them when they moved to Dartmouth. Among
its first publications were James Tenney's "Meta-Hodos" and my
"Divisions of the Tetrachord." Frog Peak also sells an extensive set of
CD's of experimental music, including microtonal and xenharmonic, in
addition to books and scores. Check them out..
