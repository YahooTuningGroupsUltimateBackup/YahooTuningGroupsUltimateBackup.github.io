I have updated the Microtonal Synthesis Web Site with new information on
microtonal synths. The most significant discovery is that the new Emu
Audity 2000 and Proteus 2000 synth modules support 12 user key tunings (full
keyboard range) and that they can be assigned per patch, or preset in Emu
terminology, not globally as was the case in earlier Proteus modules. User
key tunings can be changed realtime by MIDI continuous controllers, so
modulations in just intonation can be easily accomplished.

The Microtonal Synthesis web site is now located at
http://home.att.net/~microtonal/ . The pointer from the old location will
soon be removed, so be sure to update your bookmarks.
