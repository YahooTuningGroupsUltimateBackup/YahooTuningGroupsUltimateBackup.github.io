The answer is in your QY70's manual or additional material. There should be a section called »MIDI implementation«.

I have a QY300, and there's nothing you can do beyond the regular ›master tuning‹. I also had a look at the QY70 MIDI implementation -- same here.

So the answer is: no, you probably won't be able to do microtuning on your QY.

Which, on the one hand, is a pity, considering the great tuning features of other Yamaha products, even in a lot older ones (DX7 etc.). But on the other hand -- the QY is a mainly sequencer, not a sound generator. That's what you'll use the other devices for that, hopefully you've got slaved to the QY :)
