Hi Haresh and others.

A few days ago, Haresh has sent a notice about the "Yousendit" site. This is
the first time I've tried it out and now you can hear some music, if you
like. I've made an almost improvised piece of music yesterday and I've tried
to retune it in various ways. I've stored it all in a regular PKZip archive.
There you'll find MIDI files and then some scales. The MIDI files starting
their names with "m" contain the music from yesterday, the ones starting
with "c" are just some chords, scales, and short harmonic cadences. The ones
with a "12" in their names are in 12_EDO, the ones a "ji" in their names are
5-limit JI versions retuned with "euler.scl", the ones with "jimin" are
retuned with "mmswap.scl" (5-limit JI with major and minor harmonies
swapped), and the ones with "odd" are versions retuned with "odd12.scl" (I
was inspired by the discussion on BP you were leading some weeks ago).

OK, here's the link:

http://download.yousendit.com/98C497C2A711E1D9

Petr
