Hi there.

Most of you probably won't know the melodies of the particular carols but I'll post the composition anyway.

The first melody occurs in its original form many times throughout the piece. The very first occurrence, interestingly, is rather an exception because there the theme is inverted unlike all the subsequent repetitions.

For the final part towards the very end, another carol is quoted twice, first with a soft drone added, then extensively harmonized.
