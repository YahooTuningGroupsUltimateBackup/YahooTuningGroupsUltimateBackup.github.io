Bandwidth hogging I am, but I just wanted to send the briefest of msgs to
clarify my last point from earlier today: that it is interesting to see the
variations on music-making, music-studying and music-listening, and not (in
this case) that I'm saying any one particular way is wrong or right.
Tunings can be (and many times must be) very precise, duplicable in
different settings and situations, but what moves one in music is not, nor
should it be, a universal and/or singular motivation -- including whatever
might move me.

uprooted for a while looking good.
