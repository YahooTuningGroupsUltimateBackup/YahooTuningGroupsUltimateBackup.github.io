Some valuable information here. On Todd McComb's Medieval website
(which actually contains CD recommendations all the way up to the
Baroque period):

http://www.medieval.org/emfaq/cds/

There are DIRECT links from the CD recommendations to online retail
outlets, such as Amazon.com, for the particular CD for which one is
searching.

In other words, NO MORE standing in line at the "special order"
counter at your favorite record store, with the clerk squinting over
the fine print trying to find something THEY feel is pretty obscure.

It couldn't be simpler this way.... I just ordered a Medieval CD
sitting here...
