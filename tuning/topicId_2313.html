lots of updates - among them:

- many more definitions in the dictionary

- a downloadable zip file of the entire HTML dictionary
including my lattice article and diagrams
(and a few webpages too, if i recall)

- lots of new MIDI files
some of my latest microtonal tunes
and some of my best work in 12-Eq
including a couple of good pieces from College days

- the complete Mahler 7th, 1st movement MIDI file

- weird and wonderful new lattices John Chalmers made
on some of my most recent ideas

- slightly expanded coverage of Marchetto and Hendrix
and MIDI for Marchetto

- charts of Arab lute frettings

- charts of ETs plotted against tonality diamond pitches
(in the dictionary)

- charts of pitches on Poole's keyboard

- a much better version of the Webern 'Piano Variations' MIDI

- And the Euler translation is finished.
I'm waiting for prof. Bailhache's permission to upload it.

already under way in addition:

- an HTML version of Jan Haluska's highly technical
mathematical paper on schismatic temperament
(anyone out there know how to do HTML equations?)

- MIDI files of the musical examples (and pieces by van Dijk)
from Fokker's 1949 book 'Just Intonation'

- an HTML hyperlinked version of Ivor Darreg's translation
of Vyshnegradsky's 'Manual of Quartertone Harmony'
with MIDI files of the examples

- a webpage about Fabio Colonna (c. 1618) and his
38-tone-per-octave ~JI keyboard the 'Sambuca' (great name!)

- an HTML version of Schoenberg's 1927 essay and 1934 article
'Problems of Harmony', with MIDI music examples

Of course, I eventually plan to
fill all these webpages with lattice diagrams.
I'm lacking serious graphics software right now.
