10/22/2012 1:37:54 PM

Somebody on the Csound list said that MusicXML is a better
format for microtonal music than MIDI. I decided to find
out what the microtonal support is. Somewhere down the
search results, I found a tuning list conversation
including me. But there was also this:

http://lists.common-lisp.net/pipermail/gsharp-devel/2007-June/000357.html

In short: eighth-tones, with fine tuning. But who knows
how widely implemented?

What's this gsharp, I then thought? Here's the home page:

http://common-lisp.net/project/gsharp/

I don't know if it's of any use relative to the things I
already know about.

The hottest free score editor now is MuseScore, and here's
its microtuning project:

http://musescore.org/en/project/microtune

It looks promising for those who want to do some
programming.

Graham
