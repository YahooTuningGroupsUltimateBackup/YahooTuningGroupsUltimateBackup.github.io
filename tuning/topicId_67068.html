Ozan Yarman wrote:
> Page alignment issues and time. Let's automate the procedure. Why not make
> it more printer-friendly?

The only automatic way I have to generate a PDF is from the Print dialog in Safari. Printing that's naturally going to give exactly the same results as printing it from Safari and I have no reason to assume it'll be better than printing it directly from your web browser of choice.

Converting it to LaTeX first might give better results, but it's also a significant amount of work, and perhaps you'll have finished reading it by then. If you, or anybody else, want to convert it I'll consider hosting the result if you think it'll be easier for others to read.

Anyway, you can find the probably useless output of Safari at http://microtonal.co.uk/TheRegularMappingParadigm.pdf

Graham
