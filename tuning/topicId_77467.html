"Xenharmonic" by itself is not a good title because it's an adjective.
The title says what the article is about, and it can't be "about
xenharmonic". See
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Manual_of_Style#Article_titles
.

However, "Xenharmony" isn't ideal either, because it's much less
commonly used than the adjective form and sounds weird.

Therefore, I changed the title to "Xenharmonic music", which seems
better all around.

No, normal users are allowed to "move" (i.e. change the title of)
existing articles. The problem is that if there is already a page
under the new title (even a redirect), you have to delete that page as
part of the moving process. Only admins can do that. See
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Help:Moving_a_page#Moving_over_an_existing_page
