Tonight adds clarinetist Perry Robinson, and Cuban microtonal bassist Daniel
Stabile. Daniel composes for microtonal oboe and saxophone in Havana.
Featured is Lew Soloff, trumpeter.

Tomorrow has Philip Anderson singing the Webern (with its 3 microtones). Ben
Hume will play his Sumerian Aulos replicas, as well as a tambur in Phrygian
tuning (according to Ptolemy). As bassoon soloist, I will be playing "Solo
per fagotto" by Vladislav Shoot.

Hope to see some of you tonight and tomorrow. Johnny Reinhard
135 West 4th Street at 8 PM Friday and Saturday
