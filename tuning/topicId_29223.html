I just got permission from Jay Williams to post a selection of his
pieces, and I picked two of my favorites:

. The Mishandled Blacksmith (Jay09.MP3). Actually the third of three
cuts which comprise the Music History Suite, this is my favorite of
the 12 tracks in the CD Jay recently sent me.

. Slide Trombona (Jay06.MP3). A hilarious parody of My Sharona by
The Knack.

Important disclaimer: I play a small role in the first of these: Jay
entrusted me with the tuning of the piano part in 7-limit adaptive JI.
I think it's lovely! But mostly this is due to Jay's composing and
execution of the piano and trombone parts.

I uploaded both pieces to

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/tuning-challenges/files/

which should be accessible whether or not one is a member. You'll see
the files in the root directory. Enjoy!!
