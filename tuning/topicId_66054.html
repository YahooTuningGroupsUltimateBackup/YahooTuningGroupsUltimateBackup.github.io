I would describe that as: a single interval class with exactly two
sizes: 3/2 and 4/3. For the scales referred to, with 8 tones, ic4 is the
central interval class. Perhaps the number `n' in (3^x/2^y)^(1/n) is
related to the number of steps the scale should have to show that property?
