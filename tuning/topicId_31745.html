Two weird and degenerate temperaments

Since we are trying to approximate 5-limit harmonies, I don't think it makes sense to try to temper out 6/5,5/4,4/3 or 3/2; that leaves
10/9 and 9/8 as things to consider if we want to go past my cut-off.

10/9

Map:

[ 0 1]
[-1 2]
[-2 3]

Generators: a = .339; b = 1.

The generator is a sharp (by 20.5 cents) major third, which makes it a better third than the "official" third given by the map.

badness: 211
rms: 74.5
g: 1.414
errors: [91.2, 0, -91.2]

This is not making any sense.

9/8

Map:

[ 0 2]
[ 0 3]
[ 1 4]

Generators: a = .2794, b = 1/2

The generator is a sharp (20 cents) minor third which once again is better than what the map gives.

badness: 314
rms: 72.1
g: 1.633
errors: [-102, -51, 51]

Again, this may not have much to do with what someone who actually used this tuning would do with it.
