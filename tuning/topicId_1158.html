One of my very favorite 12-tET chords is the 1-3-#5-7 (@3+3+b3)... which to
my ear[s] 'needs' a >15/8 (or 13/7) major seventh to retain its (personally
desirable) 12-tET character... So I prefer a +19/15+5/4 for the augmented
triad with a +6/5 on top; +19/15+5/4+6/5 @ 1/1, 19/15, 19/12, 19/10.
