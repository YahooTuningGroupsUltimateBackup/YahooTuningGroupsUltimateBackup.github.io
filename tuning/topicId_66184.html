Hello all,

The Sonic Arts Gallery will be open as part of the quarterly
South Park Walkabout on Friday evening, April 28, from 6:00
to 10:00 pm.

The front of Sonic Arts is an antique shop, and in the back
Jonathan Glasier, Brink, and myself will be improvising
microtonal music, them on keyboards and me on a laptop
equipped with Tonescape. In between jam sessions, i'll be
giving demos of Tonescape to those interested.
