Hello Tuning Listers:

I wanted to report the good news that my two "seminal" (or at least
possibly "most significant"... at least to myself :) microtonal
scores are now available commercially.

_Blackjack_ for trombone and electronics and _Blacklight_ for cello
and electronics, both in the "Blackjack" scale, that we devised
mostly on this list over a period of a couple of years, are now
available from Subito Music, Inc.:

http://www.subitomusic.com/

I should also mention that _Blackjack_ as some may already know, is
available in recorded form as played by the wonderful Chris Washburne
on Johnny Reinhard's American Festival of Microtonal Music CD,
entitled, ELECTRONICAL.

It is available though CD BABY, Amazon.com and iTunes. Just
search "Joseph Pehrson" any of those places...
