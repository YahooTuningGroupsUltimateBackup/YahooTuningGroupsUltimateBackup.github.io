This one is a little less straightforward to write down, but I think
musically better since the variance of fifths away from pure is less
while the number of pure thirds is the same and there is one more pure
septimal interval. Eb-Bb and C#-G# are now pure and C-G / E-B very
slightly flat. The sequence of fifths is still symmetrical about D-A.
The 'wolf' G#-Eb is now 32/21 (729.2c or 27.3c sharp). F-G# and Eb-C#
are 75/64 (7.7c sharp of 7/6) whereas Bb-C# is pure 7/6.
