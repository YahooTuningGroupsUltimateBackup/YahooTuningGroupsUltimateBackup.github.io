Most hammer dulcimers are indeed set up for diatonic scales. Some larger instruments have additional strings for some accidentals, but I have yet to see a hammer dulcimer that covers all accidentals of 12-edo.

As for scales, I would shoot for JI based scales. You are correct about the left bridge giving the 5th. I have to check about the right bridge being a major 3rd -- I don't remember. Look up some websites that talk about standard tunings of the hammer dulcimer and it should be pretty obvious -- then translate to the scale of your choice. Occasionally, you will hear of hammer dulcimer players tuning a string here or there to a different note than normal.

Anyway, I am so glad to hear you have a hammer dulcimer. Some day I will spend some time with mine!

Chris -- really enjoy your music and your prolific work. Keep up the good work!

Jay
