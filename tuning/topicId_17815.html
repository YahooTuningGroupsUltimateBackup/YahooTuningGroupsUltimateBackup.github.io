Well, let's forget about trying to put the present on film... how
about seeing it in person?! Somehow my co-director Bill Rhoads

Concordance concert Friday, January 26th at Miller Theatre of

coup that he was able to negotiate with him over dinner.... Again,
the Miller Theatre is at 2960 Broadway at 116 Street and the tickets
are $17, available at the Miller Box Office, 212-854-7799.
