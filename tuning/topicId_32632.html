As many of you probably know, Jacky Ligon's webzine has a new
edition, #3 that just hit the press-web!

It's really great, as they all have been.

Wonderful interview with Neil Haverstick by Dan Stearns.

An engrossing article by Margo Schulter about 13-tET with sound files
that *sound great*... (probably thanks to Jacky's magic)

Jeff Scott chimes in and stretches the mind with stretched tunings...

My friend Anton Rovner comments on a recent quarter-tone piano
concert in Moscow and the origins of the ANS synthesizer and other
microtonal goodies.

Jeremy Gill presents his mermerizing Unidalamandala-lala

Jacky Ligon makes a great sound file through the *super* particulars
of superparticulars.

Did I leave anybody out?

Oh.. myself... my report on last May's Microfest, and some great
reviews of CDs.. Prent Rodgers new release, and much much more.

And it's all right here:

http://tma.asgarddesign.net/TMA_vol_3_index.htm

Way to go, Jacky!
