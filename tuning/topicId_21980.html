It's a surprise that all these 1-3-5-7-9 dekanies reside in an MOS scale with only 31 notes.

Just to review, each dekany contains

one 1-3-5-7 hexany
one 1-3-5-9 hexany
one 1-3-7-9 hexany
one 1-5-7-9 hexany
one 3-5-7-9 hexany

five 9-limit tetrads (as above -- tetrads in 2)5 dekany are otonal; those in 3)5 dekany are utonal).
Are there any saturated 9-limit tetrads too (10:12:15:18 or 12:14:18:21)?

thirty 9-limit triads (twenty otonal and ten utonal in 2)5 dekany; ten O and twenty U in 3)5
dekany)

thirty 9-limit dyads

and, of course, ten notes.

This is true of dallesandro which although has 36 tones is mapped over a 31 tone keyboard. this
contains two 1-3-7-9-11-15 eikosanies. Working with this material for so long i don't find it
surprising but i guess it could be.

BTW the 1-3-5-7-9 double dekany is a 14 tone CS -my find. I have found only one useful 14 tone just CS.
kinda rare

When you think that the hebdomekontany has 28 different eikosanies each occurring twice. Likewise
there are 56 different 2)5 dekanies each occurring 3 times. The same with 3)5 dekanies. thus you have
112 different dekanies each occurring 3 times. That's 336 dekanies!
BTW Gending Boehme (From the interiors of Anaphoria CD) is based on the 1-3-7-11-15 3)5 dekany where
the melody (vibes) is on the tetrad with the complementary hexany on the marimba. Uses all five
possibilities, each 79 beats long, repeated once. It is one of my favorite structures in the eikosany
