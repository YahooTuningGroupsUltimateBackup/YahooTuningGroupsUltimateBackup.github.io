New webpages have been added to the H-Pi website which explain the musical system forming the basis for the Tonal Plexus keyboards and Megascore notation:

<http://www.h-pi.com/theory/huntsystem1.html>

Details of the structure of the 205ET scale, pitch naming, and interval naming are given. Online Hunt system calculators are also available.

A new forum has been added to H-Pi forums for discussion of the Hunt system. Please note that I am unable to be involved in discussions of the system (as well as anything H-Pi related) outside of the H-Pi forums, so I cordially invite everyone who is interested to please join and participate:
<http://www.h-pi.com/phpBB2/viewforum.php?f=8>

Please note that measures were recently undertaken to prohibit robots and spammers from ruining the H-Pi forums.

Note also that Altavista language translation has been added to the website, so that pages are now available in English, French, Dutch, Italian, German, Spanish, and Portuguese.
<http://www.h-pi.com>

The Hunt System pages will be expanded in the future, as will H-Pi Instruments documentation for the non-English speakers of the world.
