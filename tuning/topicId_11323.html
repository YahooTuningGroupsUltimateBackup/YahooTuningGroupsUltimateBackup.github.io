> > Hello, I am trying to edit the pitch table on my EPS sampling keyboard

> > deviations from equal temparment?
> In a practical way, you could add an equal amount of cents to each of the
> cents figures in a tuning so that the lowest "minus" deviation is assigned to
> a "normal" note. All the other notes are now sharper.

????? Just add 100 minus the deviation flat to the next-lower 12TET pitch.

For example, if you want B to be 5 cents flat of a 12TET B, tune it to Bb + 95
cents.
