hello all,

tonight in the car on the way home from work,
i was listening on the radio to KPBS, my local
San Diego affiliate of NPR.

the announcer advertized a program that will be
aired Sunday at 7:00 pm, called "American Mavericks".

well, as soon as i heard that title, you can imagine
who i thought of right away ... our old friend Harry.
in my mind, those two words *immediately* conjure up
his cranky old bearded face.

but then of course, the very next thought in my head
was, "no way ... a radio station this big would never
play Partch".

well, guess what? yep. the announcer said,
"featuring Charles Ives, Aaron Copeland, Harry Partch,
and many others ...".

so is Partch really making the big time now?
or is San Diego radio merely being hip enough
to pay homage to a local hero?

... you be the judge. ;-)

-monz
