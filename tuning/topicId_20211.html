Hi Jacky, At www.SoundOfIndia.com, click on 'Performances', to
listen to a 27-second tanpura sample. I obtained two loops (for two
tanpura-s), but neither was clean enough. So the output is a little
muddy, wobbly, and hollow. Any shortcomings in the loops have not
only come through, but also got magnified. I hope to get better
loops in the future. And then, the converting software has its own
personality and preferences!
The tanpura is tuned in the key of C.
