Mike.

The answer is yes.

Since it is an endless set, there is not a final cell. The cell can stay at position 2 of the octave or return to the 1 base. Similar to the earth orbit where the permanent sum of the translation lenght, measured from a point in the space that corresponds to note C = 1, homologously gives the cell number.

By now, the only application of the progression of cells was to solve a 4 variables equation to determine the K and P semitone factors of the Piagui scale system. Full information is given in my book titled "The Piagui Musical Scale - Perfecting Harmony". Keenan Pepper has bought the book
