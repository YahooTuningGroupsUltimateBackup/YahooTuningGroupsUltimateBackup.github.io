While most rank three temperaments are planar, there are exceptions, with jubilismic, tempering out 50/49, being one of them. As an example to show that hobbits are defined for non-planar temperaments also, I give Jubilismic[10] below, and by way of comparison, or something, also Archytas[12], tempering out 64/63. All scales are strictly proper.
