This is his diamond, a harmonic construct, without the added pitches Partch used to complete his scale. Without the added pitches it fails to be a constant structure ( or as good of one that can be done incorporating such a structure). This melodic integrity seems to be lost on a whole segments of JI personas many who claim to be based on his work. It favors harmonically based grids while failing to even suggest that a concept of a scale might exist. Partch's addition of the other 14 tones scales are of as great importance as this structure and without them represent a mere half statement.
