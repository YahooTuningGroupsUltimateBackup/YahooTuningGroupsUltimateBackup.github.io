Hey listers. Just got back from GB, where I met up with Graham Breed and
Robert Walker. Had dinner with Graham and played some of his instruments.
The tuning of the meantone guitar was especially interesting. A lot of
cool sounding intervals. Had dinner with Robert Walker and talked about
his Fractal Tune Smithy, which makes fractal music with microtonal scales.
I know many of you have heard Hans-Andre Stamm's enharmonic organ, but if
you missed it, see it at
http://www.eufonia.de/enharmfr.htm
and hear it at
http://www.eufonia.de/enhcompe.htm
Just a short note before I wade into all the back issues. Ciao.
