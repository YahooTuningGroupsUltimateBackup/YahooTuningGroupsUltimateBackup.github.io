A number of the quarter-tone pieces listed in that link is available on YouTube, e.g. works by Alois Hába, Ivan Wyschnegradsky, Charles Ives. See here:

http://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=quarter-tone&oq=quarter-tone&aq=f&aqi=g-C2&aql=&gs_nf=1&gs_l=youtube-reduced.3..0i33l2.3378.3378.0.4699.1.1.0.0.0.0.85.85.1.1.0.

It's not directly downloadable MP3, though.

A few MP3 links are available on the XenWiki:

http://xenharmonic.wikispaces.com/24edo#Compositions

The recent Untwelve competition ( http://untwelve.org/2011_competition.html ) also had several 24edo contributions, downloadable namely Hickman, Hollier.

And there are sure much more, if you search a little over on the MakeMicroMusic Yahoo list.
