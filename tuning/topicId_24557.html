It's all coming thick and fast folks. Here are both pages of the 22 tet
guitar reduction of Just Vespers. It's tricky in parts but the
counterpoint works well and is quite effective if all the voices, in
particular where there is contrary motion, can be kept alive. Despite
the copyright I'm happy to let people play or perform these pieces.
They'll only be up for a short time as I've some others in the pipeline.
