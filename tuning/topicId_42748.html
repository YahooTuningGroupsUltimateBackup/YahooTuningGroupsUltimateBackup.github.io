Should anyone be interested I've recently updated my ZeBox site and
there are now 16 pieces up.

The most recent microtonal upload, One Summer Sunday, consists mostly
of little homemade instruments and tape treatments. This is primarily
a quiet music that works best in headphones:

http://www.zebox.com/daniel_anthony_stearns/

OSS was inspired by Ives' Universe Symphony plan in The Memos and
sound-art groups such as Cranioclast and Soviet France. It's also an
ode to the occult resonance of the natural world and a particular day
in my life when I was a young boy.
