UC Santa Barbara Presentation Nov. 29

Aaron Andrew Hunt <aahunt@h-pi.com>

11/6/2007 3:16:46 PM

I have been invited by Curtis Roads and Clarence Barlow to give a presentation to students and faculty of UC Santa Barbara on November 29, 2007. I will demonstrate my systematic extension of traditional Western music theory with corresponding instruments and notation supporting the composition of music using expanded pitch resources, and will demonstrate the Tuning Box TBX1 microtonal MIDI keyboard converter, the Tonal Plexus TPX2s microtonal keyboard synthesizer, and MegaScore microtonal notation and sequencing software.

WHEN: Thursday, November 29, 2007 at 6 PM
WHERE: UC Santa Barbara Music Building, room 2215

I have been told that the room will be open to anyone who wants to attend, so if you are in the area, I hope you can make it.
