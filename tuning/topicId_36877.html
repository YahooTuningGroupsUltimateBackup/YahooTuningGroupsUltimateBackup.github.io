Here is a symmetrical JI scale related to Blackjack, obtained by
detempering Blackjack by what I call "Euclidean reduction", where I
choose the scale step nearest to unity in the symmetrical 7-limit
octave-equivalent lattice. In the one case where there was a tie I broke
in in favor of 49/40 and 80/49 rather than 60/49 and 49/30; the latter
giving an alternative version.

1, 49/48, 15/14, 35/32, 8/7, 7/6, 49/40, 5/4, 21/16, 4/3, 7/5, 10/7, 3/2,
32/21, 8/5, 80/49, 12/7, 7/4, 64/35, 28/15, 96/49
