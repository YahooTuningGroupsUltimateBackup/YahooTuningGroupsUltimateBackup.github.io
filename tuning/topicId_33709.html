Theoretically the factors are 3 3 3 15 15:

7/6 7/4 21/16 63/64

5/4 15/8 7/5 21/20

4/3 1/1 3/2 9/8

The point of this tuning is the harmonic bridges, that is, the "middle row" of pitches are related by 5-limit intervals to both the upper and lower rows,which are related by the 7-limit.
