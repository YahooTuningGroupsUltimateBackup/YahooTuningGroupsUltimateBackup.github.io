This week's winner:

John Chalmers, for publishing Xenharmonikon, the World's
coolest fringe periodical!!

I work in publishing, and I can tell you that most
periodicals aren't worth the pulp they're printed on.

Not so with Xenharmonikon! For over thirty years, John
Chalmers has published (with some help including that of
Daniel Wolf) the de facto standard journal of what is
probably the most esoteric pursuit ever undertaken by the
human race: purpose-built musical tunings.
