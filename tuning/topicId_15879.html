Shows connections of octony with CPS set. (Clavinet, broken chords)

First, the two red triangles are 3, 5,7 and 3*5, 5*7 and 7*3
I.e. 1)4 and 2)4 for 3, 5 and 7 as factors instead of 1, 3, 5.

Then, the two red triangles together with the white lines of the cube joining
them to each other make the 1, 3, 5, 7 hexany. (It's squashed slightly to fit
the cube shape)

Red triangle or spheres play the triads of the 1, 3, 5, 7 hexany.
Magenta spheres play the diads of 1, 3, 5, 7 hexany.
