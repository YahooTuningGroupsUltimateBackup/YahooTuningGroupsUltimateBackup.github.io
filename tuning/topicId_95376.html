Just two details I would suggest, Mark, in your scala presentation lines of ovovo10 and ovovo27 :
Eronyme in itself is not, strictly speaking, a "temperament", even if it could be experimented as such ; it is, more exactly, a specific equal-beating algorithm you applied to your tuning.
Same with the Natté algorithm (and in addition I made a typing mistake it should be "Pisot", but anyway the Pisot-Vijayaraghavan numbers are not known as "temperaments", and you could just mention this eq-b algorithm is "based on P-V number #1" if you wish.

Cheers !
- - - -
Jacques
