If one desired the opportunity to hear a 4-hour radio special devoted to the life and work of Harry Partch, one could be satisfied in a few hours. Details at:

http://www.kfjc.org/promotions/index.php

Note that you don't have to be in the area with an antenna - they stream their shows on the net:
