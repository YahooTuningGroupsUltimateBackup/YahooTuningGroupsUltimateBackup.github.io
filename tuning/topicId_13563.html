Last month I posted some reflections on the lack of JI/Microtonal music
at the Edinburgh Festival. Several people showed an interest in playing
in Edinburgh at some point for which I am grateful. However they were
all USA or mainland Europe based which is very beautiful but I would be
interested to hear from any British performers who feel that they might
be able to offer interesting JI/Microtonal performances. I am about to
negotiate with various individuals and institutions and I feel that they
will want to see some interest from within the UK to get the ball
rolling. It might be important if funding becomes an issue.

Again, no promises, but it will have to happen one day.
