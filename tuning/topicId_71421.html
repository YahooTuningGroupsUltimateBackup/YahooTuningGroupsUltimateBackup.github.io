Hello all,

We at Tonalsoft have gotten tired of dealing with the
problems potential users have been having with Microsoft's
DirectX, which is an essential component of Tonescape.

So we've decided to invite Linux programmers to help us
port the graphics engine to Open GL, and probably ultimately
to port the whole program to Linux.

I've created a new Yahoo group exactly for this purpose:

http://tech.groups.yahoo.com/group/tonescape-linux/

If anyone's interested, please join that group so that
we can keep the discussion focused on the task. Thanks.
