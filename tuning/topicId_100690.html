I suspect the scale is a smaller version of the 12-tone scale called "wilson_helix.scl" in Manuels archive. The scale itself is ingenious, I've used it a couple of times in my compositions as well. It also offers a very "distant sounding" mode of "1 3 2 1 1 3 2" steps.
