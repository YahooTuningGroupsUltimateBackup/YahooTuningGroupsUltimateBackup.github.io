A couple of friends of mine have an interesting show in Denver,
Sunday nights at 10 PM...it's called Jazz Tales, and features a creative
mix of poetry and jazz. The cool thing is, they're starting to do more
original music to accompany the poetry, and they're using a program
called Acid to construct some interesting tunes of their own. The
microtonal slant? I am starting to record on some of their tunes, and I
just did a session last night where I used the 34 tone electric to lay
down a long track...it came out rather well. Also, they've used my piece
"Swing 19" a couple of times, and I foresee more involvement on my part
in the future. It's on KUVO, and go to KUVO.org to pick up their
streaming signal...you might like it...Hstick
