This is perhaps of some interest for tuning experiments on guitars with
fixed frets: after the last Microthon in New York I bought a Third Hand Capo
at Matt Umanov Guitars in Bleeker Street. While a normal capo stops all six
strings at a fixed position, the Third Hand Capo (14$) only stops the
string(s) you really want. So you can create any open tuning and conserve in
the same time the standard fingering on the fretboard.
