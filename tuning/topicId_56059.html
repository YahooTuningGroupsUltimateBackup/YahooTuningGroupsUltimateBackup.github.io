Regarding

www.hermode.com

and "Flash":

Flash is at nearly all systems installing itself automatically.
But at an public computer it may happen that the Admin has
forbidden the installing of plugins.

I am sorry, but actually we have no text-basing sites.

Nevertheless one of our informations can be downloaded.
It is a diagram, written for developers, which shows how the
HMT function is working.

You will find it at:
http://www.hermode.com/download/HMT_data_flow_diagr.pdf

Regarding "Mutabor": They offered a tuning programme, basing in pitch bend
messages.

(Splitting the different notes and tuning messages on different MIDI
channels).

I don't know whether it will be still available.

We also had such a programme for Hermode Tuning

and PC-DOS. I believe that deep in my computer it is

still stored. But we don't more publish it.

Best

Werner Mohrlok
