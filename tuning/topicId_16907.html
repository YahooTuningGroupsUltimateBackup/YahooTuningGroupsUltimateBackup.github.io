New Age is a grab bag of different styles, but some of it shares with
_Beauty in the Beast_ the quality of being inspired by musical traditions
from around the world. I can't think of any specific New Age music that
sounds anything like BitB, but it's been a while since I listened to New
Age music often. BitB might almost fit better in the Jazz section, but it
doesn't easily fit into any of the established categories.
