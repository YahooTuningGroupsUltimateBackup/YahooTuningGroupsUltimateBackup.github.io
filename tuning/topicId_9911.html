For anyone who might be interested, this is a 1987 interview with
Pierre Schaeffer that was conducted by Tim Hodgkinson and originally
appeared in the Recommended Records Quarterly magazine, volume 2,
number 1:

<http://www.cicv.fr/association/shaeffer_interview.html>

The interview really gets off with quite a bang(!), as Schaeffer
grimly ends his very first response with, "seeing that no one knew
what to do anymore with DoReMi, maybe we had to look outside that...
Unfortunately it took me forty years to conclude that nothing is
possible outside DoReMi... In other words, I wasted my life."
