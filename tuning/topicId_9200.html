Finally, my new CD "Other Worlds" is ready to go...let me make a few
comments about it. It is electronic space music, and features the Roland
GR 300 and Korg X 911 guitar synths, as well as Boss and Digitech
delays, a Korg A1 effects unit, and Alesis Microverb. There are 4
tracks: 1. "Other Worlds" features a 34 tone Telecaster, and was
recorded live in 1998 at Microstock 4...2. "The Spider" uses a Starr 19
tone guitar...3. "Didgeridon't" utilizes a Roland GR 300 guitar
(fretless), and features John Starrett on Starrboard...4. "Nebulae" uses
a Cort 12 tone axe. There are two tunes at about 17 minutes, and two
around 11.
