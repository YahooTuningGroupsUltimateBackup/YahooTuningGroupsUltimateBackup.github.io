Hi everyone,

I did one more listening test. This time, I started with a flat-fifth
mavila type thing, and then moved the third up to a neutral third. You
can all tell me if you think the neutral third sounds "major" at the
end. (Yes Carl, this means you!)

http://launch.groups.yahoo.com/group/tuning/files/MikeBattaglia/flatdiatonic.mid

It should sound like this:

C-Eb-G
C-D-Eb-F-G
C-D-Eb-F-G (sustained)
C-G-D-A-E (octave equivalent, sustained)

Looking at the MIDI file to see the tuning is punishable by death.
