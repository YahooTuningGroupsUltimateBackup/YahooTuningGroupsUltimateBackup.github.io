> has interval of 3^(-41) which is the difference between pyth. And
mercator comma (19.845 cent)any name and function?

Huyghens-Fokker has it listed as '41-tone' comma, which is the only
name I know of for it. How do you say 41 in farsi?

and what about difference between this 3^(-41) and mercator comma
(which is 16.23 cent)

That would be 2^149/3^94, so maybe the 94-comma or something.
