Dutch Microtonal Music Festival

John Chalmers <JHCHALMERS@UCSD.EDU>

10/17/2002 10:39:00 AM

I just received a flyer in the mail from the Netherlands about on-going
series of lecture/classes in microtonal composition in Amsterdam by the
Nieuw Ensemble of Utrecht and a festival, "Het verfijnde oor," next
February in Amsterdam and Utrecht.
The next lecture is by Clarence Barlow at the Amsterdam Conservatory on
October 24 (there was a workshop today by the Nieuw Ensemble, in the
Musiekcentrum Vredenburg Utrecht).

Some of the composers featured in the February concerts are
Wyschnegradsky, Carrillo, Ferneyhough,Scelsi, Feldman and Murail. New
microtonal works and compositions by Baroque composers will also be
featured.

For more details see these web sites: www.cva.ahk.nl and
www.nieuw-ensemble.nl
