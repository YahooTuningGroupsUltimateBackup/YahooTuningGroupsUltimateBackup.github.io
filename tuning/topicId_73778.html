I've just added a score to my first String Quartet (three movements, ca. 6'30", in just intonation; from 1978, about the same time I first met Erv Wilson) to my webpages. I've decided to publish the score without the original microtonal accidentals because (a) as each movement is limited to seven pitches, it's easy enough to play without them, and (b) any of the competing systems floating around today can be added in, if needed, by hand.

the score is here: http://home.snafu.de/djwolf/QuartetI.pdf

The piece originally had a fast fourth movement; like many other things from the '70s, it's gone walkies and is probably not missed.
