The reference of 0.0005 determines how sensible is the selection of acceptable consonances. Should this value is reduced to 0.0002, the acceptable consonant ratios diminish. The numbered elements are cells of the progression.

M 139 1.17055326935 CELL
M 140 1.171875
J 141 1.17320082553
J 142 1.17452815104
M 143 1.17585436996
M 144 1.17718208638

47/40 = 1.175 - 1.174528151= 0,000471 < 0.00 05 Then 1.175 is almost consonant.
