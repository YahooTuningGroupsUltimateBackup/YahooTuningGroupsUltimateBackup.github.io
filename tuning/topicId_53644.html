Late notice, but I thought I'd mention it anyways. I'll be playing my (not Jazz,
but improvisational) slow microtonal music with a group of modern dancers who
move very slow - Motion Sculpture. We'll be playing in a store front venue in
the shadows of Corp. America and folks can come in, sit down and take it all in.
Details:

“Cilla_Vee Movement Projects” presents:

MOTION SCULPTURE MOVEMENT INSTALLATIONS
Festival of performance, sound & visual arts

CHASHAMA storefront theater & gallery

Each afternoon June 21st – June 25th.
1pm – 6pm. (“peak times” 2pm – 5pm)
A NEW INSTALLATION EACH DAY.

The audience is invited to view the installation from the street & also to come
inside,
sit down, see art work exhibited in the gallery & enjoy refreshments.

Tuesday June 22nd
“Between MA & a Soft Space”
Microtonal guitarist David Beardsley [http://biink.com/db].
Art work by Valerie Mendelson and Lara Hanson...more here: www.hhproduction.org
[http://www.hhproduction.org]

Motion Sculpture performers:
Director – Claire Elizabeth Barratt & Jill Frere, Pedro Jimenez, Marianne Giosa,
Kaz Kuken,
Emi Kato, JoyDawn Williams, Sam Humphries, Alisa Philips & others.

Art work by Hisayasu Takashio will be exhibited from June 22nd – July 5th

Chashama, 217, East 42nd Street at 3rd Avenue.

Contact Claire Elizabeth Barratt – director “Cilla_Vee Movement Projects”
(917) 650 7321 www.hhproduction.org [http://www.hhproduction.org]
