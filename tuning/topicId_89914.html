Another litte tune using MOTU Ethno2:

http://dl.kibla.org/dl.php?filename=FrogAndCobra_BobroEthno2demo.mp3

Thanks for the very nice comments and compliments on the last demo! Been working with Ethno2 quite a bit, most interesting to me is its nature as a sampling synthesizer for new musics. I think it is superb in this way. And, I've been testing how it works with acoustic recording, an acid test for any synthesizer or sampler, for I have found that over the years that any pheaux-phat, that bloated "wow" sound that's so easy to do in digital, just vanishes into a distant whimper when mixed with acoustic recording, unless the acoustic recording is digitally processed to the point of becoming itself a synthetic performance. The Ethno2 is doing great, its sound quality is solid.

There's have been many interesting posts here recently, have to catch up!

-Cameron Bobro
