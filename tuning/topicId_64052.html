Hi, I'm a big Robert Rich fan. All his solo cd's starting with
'Numena' are in just intonation. His 'Lithosphere' collaboration,
which just came out last October, is in JI as well. There's some
lengthy sound samples of 'Lithosphere' here:

http://robertrich.com/rrlithosphere.html

I'm also looking forward to 'Electric Ladder', which will come out in
a week or 2. No sound samples online as yet:

http://robertrich.com/rrladder.html

Also, if you're having trouble sleeping, there's over an hour of sound
samples from his 7 hour sleep dvd 'Somnium' here:

http://glurponline.com/audio.php

Anyone know of any similar JI artists?

Woohoo. 'Electric Ladder' just became available for order.

http://robertrich.com/rrladder.html

Robert tells me he mostly uses 5 limit tuning with a 7/5 tritone, plus
some harmonics:

(this was in the jacket for 'Geometry')
1/1 16/15 9/8 6/5 5/4 4/3 7/5 3/2 8/5 5/3 9/5 15/8

Nothing too radical for you guys, I guess.

I just wanted to add that he runs different modes on a scale like
that. So he ends up getting other septimal intervals like minor 7th
(7/4) & subminor third (7/6).
