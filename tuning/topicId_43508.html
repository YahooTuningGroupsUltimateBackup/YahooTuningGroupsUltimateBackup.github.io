From the Yahoo Jazz Guitar Group (YJGG) Moderators

Hi YJGG Members

Well as you may know we ran out of space in our files
section and so we started another Yahoo group to archive our
files - jazz_guitar_files.

Over the week we've been hard at work to free up more file
space and have moved over more files from the file section
to our new file archive group. Our new File archive group
can be found here:

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/jazz_guitar_files/

We encourage all our members to join this new group which
acts as our file store (you will get no additional messages
from this new group). Some of the files we've moved over
include, the Pat Metheny lessons, W.Leavitt's Chord Melody
Arrangements, Ike Isaacs Guitar Columns.

In moving files over we found out that Yahoo has recently
split file storage between Files and Photos. Files gets 20
MB, Photos 30 MB. Therefore where possible it is best to
post Photos rather than Files. Photos can be JPEG (.jpg) or
GIF (.gif) file formats. Yahoo has also a new ActiveX
component or a plug-in which allows you to upload and
download multiple photos making the process much easier. So
if you have transcriptions or files and can make them into
images it would be best to place them in the photo section.
To convert and print images we recommend the program
Irfanview http://www.irfanview.com/

New Files and messages should be placed in the existing group:

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/jazz_guitar/

Thanks to everyone for making the group one of the best (and
free) jazz guitar community destinations on the web. Keep up
the great posts and continue to make this one of the best
places to find out about everything related to jazz guitar.

YJGG Moderators
Alisdair, Andy, David, Tom, Vaclav, Anon.
http://www.jazzguitargroup.com/
http://groups.yahoo.com/group/jazz_guitar/
