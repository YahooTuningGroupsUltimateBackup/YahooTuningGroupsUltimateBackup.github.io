Hi folks,

Here are the various musical events we have scheduled for this summer.
Please check the website for details and last minute additions.

7/6/01 Beverly Woods appears with the NH Music Festival Orchestra Pops
Concert, Silver Hall, Plymouth NH, 8 p.m. for Tales of the Vienna Woods.

7/7/01 Contradance with Seth Austen, Beverly Woods, Jerry Weene and caller
Byron Ricker at the Tamworth Town Hall, Tamworth, NH. 8 PM

7/14/01 World Fellowship Center. Klezmer music concert with Seth Austen and
Beverly Woods; mandola, fiddle, tsimbl (eastern-European hammered dulcimer)
and guitar. This concert is part of the Jewish History & Culture Week,
however, it is possible to attend the concert separately. 8PM, Chocorua, NH.
Call (603) 447-2280 or <http://www.worldfellowship.org> for details.

7/22-7/27/01 National Guitar Summer Workshop. Seth will be teaching an
Acoustic Guitar Intensive with special guest Don Ross in Toronto, Ontario,
Canada. Fingerstyle, flatpicking, open tunings, music theory, slide and
more. Call 1-800-234-6479 or <http://www.guitarworkshop.com> for details.

8/3 and 8/4, Beverly Woods will be playing at the Yankee Homecoming
Festival in downtown Newburyport, MA. Performances between noon and 5 p.m.

8/4/01 2nd NH Tailltean Games. Seth Austen and Jim Dalton play Celtic music
on fiddle, pennywhistle, bouzouki, mandolin, tenor banjo and guitar at 1 and
3PM. Visit <http://homepage.fcgnetworks.net/pmather/tailltean/index.html>
for more details. Festival events all weekend in Franklin, NH.

8/10/01 Contradance with Seth Austen, Beverly Woods and caller Byron Ricker
at the Holderness Town Hall, Holderness, NH. 8 PM

Hope to see you there!
