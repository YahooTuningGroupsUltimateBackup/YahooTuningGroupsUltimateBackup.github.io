Hey all!

Thanks to all who proffered advice and pointers on the
Relaxing Reperio piece I posted a few days past.

I was a little trepiditious at the whole idea but I
actually found that the advices given were all quite
worthwhile and interesting.

Anyway, worked with them a bit and they led to other
things and such and so here is a new version. In the
end, it doesn't seem it directly implement any of the
advise straight-out but it has ended up influenced in
some way by all of it.

So thanks again and if you have the time check out the
new version. It's a little bit longer and the tempo has
been slowed down a notch and a (etheral electronic)
choir singing 4 part harmony, and a couple other
instruments have been added.

All voices are still in the Reperio tuning which I
think works especially well with the choir voices.

As before, it is at:

http://geocities.com/nonoctave/

And as before all comments and reactions, good or bad
are most welcome.
