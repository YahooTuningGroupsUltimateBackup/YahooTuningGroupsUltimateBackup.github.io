I'm playing an organ concert on Sunday evening 10/30, including a couple of newly-composed pieces for this temperament. The concert includes music by Bach, Froberger, Frescobaldi, Attaingnant, Bull, Fischer, me, Burkhart, and Brahms.

Program and venue details:
http://www-personal.umich.edu/~bpl/2005oct30.htm
It's at Goshen College, in northern Indiana: about an hour's drive from either Notre Dame U or Kalamazoo, and about halfway between Chicago and Toledo.

This organ had its dedication ceremonies in May and has been in regular use there at the college since about January. The recordings on it, from March, are due for release in November.

Another new organ with this temperament is in Helsinki.
