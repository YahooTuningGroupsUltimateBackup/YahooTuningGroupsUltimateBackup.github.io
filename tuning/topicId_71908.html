Next in the series of Tonescape files for all of the
temperament families described in Paul Erlich's
"Middle Path" paper (published summer 2006 in
_Xenharmonikon 18_):

A zipped archive of Tonescape files for TOP augmented,
5-limit. I've included:

* a .space Tonespace file showing the tempering-out of
the [7 0, -3> = 128:125 enharmonic-diesis;

* .tuning files of the 6-note (3L+3s), 9-note (3L+6s),
12-note (3L+9s), and 15-note (12L+3s) augmented DES,
as shown in Erlich's horogram, and also the 27-note
(12L+15s) DES;

* sample .tonescape Musical Piece files, illustrating
all of the modal rotations of the 6-note (3L+3s) and
9-note (3L+6s) DE augmented scales.

http://launch.groups.yahoo.com/group/tuning_files/files/\
monz/tonescape-top/tonescape_top_augmented_5-limit.zip

(You have to be a member of the tuning-files group
to access the file. I used Windows XP's 7-zip program
to zip the archive, but hopefully any unzipper will
work.)
