Here are a few more well-temperaments I've found. These all have maximum
errors less than 20 cents in the thirds (both major and minor), and 6 cents
in the fifths.

"Butterfly I" (from the shape of the graph)

C-G, E-B tempered 3/20 Pyth. comma
G-D, A-E tempered 1/5 Pyth. comma
D-A, C#-G#, Eb-Bb tempered 1/10 Pyth. comma
B-F#-C#, Ab-Eb, Bb-F-C just

"Butterfly II"

G-D, A-E tempered 1/4 Pyth. comma
D-A, C#-G#, Eb-Bb tempered 1/10 Pyth. comma
F-C-G, E-B-F# tempered 1/20 Pyth. comma
F#-C#, Ab-Eb, Bb-F just

"Bug I"

C-G, A-E tempered 3/20 Pyth. comma
G-D-A tempered 1/4 Pyth. Comma
F#-C#, Eb-Bb tempered 1/10 Pyth. Comma
E-B-F#, C#-G#-D#, Bb-F-C just

I like this one. The C-E third is less than 3 cents sharp, it has lots of
just fifths, and a nice progression from "good" to "bad" thirds.
