> Are there freeware or shareware utilities that will record .wav
> or .mp3s let's say 5-7 minutes in length??

"CoolEdit 96" from syntrillium.com is available on the web as a demo-ware,
and is only $40 or so for full version. The demo can record as much as you
like.

You can use MPEGSuite, also share/demo-ware, to turn your WAV files from
CoolEdit into mp3 files.

Rick
