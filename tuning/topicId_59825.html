Hi,
I am soon releasing 2 new pdf download guides at my site.
- How to make your own drumbooth (step by step construction indications
on how to make, construct and build a home made drumbooth)
I have been building this the past few weeks and recording each step on
tape of what I do!

- Build a small recording studio for recording drums and percussion
instruments

Any suggestions or ideas from other drummers that have been looking for
information on these 2 topics? I'd like to hear from you to be able to
get new ideas. For example, what you would like to have included, etc.

Check out www.drumrhythms.com for these new releases.
