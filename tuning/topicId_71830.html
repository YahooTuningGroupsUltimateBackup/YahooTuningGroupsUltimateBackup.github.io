Next in the series of Tonescape files for all of the
temperament families described in Paul Erlich's
"Middle Path" paper (published summer 2006 in
_Xenharmonikon 18_):

A zipped archive of TOP hanson, 5-limit. I've included:

* a .tonespace file showing the tempering-out of the
kleisma,

* .tuning files of the 4-, 7-, 11-, 15-, 19-, 34-,
and 53-note DES scales, as illustrated in Erlich's
horogram,

* sample .piece files, illustrating all of the
modal rotations of the 4-note (3L+1s), 7-note (4L+3s),
and 11-note (4L+7s) DES hanson scales.

/tuning/files/monz/tonescape-top/tones\
cape_top_hanson_5-limit.zip

You have to be a member of the tuning-files group
to access the file. I used Windows XP's 7-zip program
to zip the archive, but hopefully any unzipper will
work.
