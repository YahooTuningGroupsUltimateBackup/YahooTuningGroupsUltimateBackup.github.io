TX81Z - spotted for sale in San Jose

Rick McGowan <rmcgowan@apple.com>

1/18/2000 9:59:34 AM

Mainly for Bay Area tuning enthusiasts... Over the weekend I spotted two
Yamaha TX81Zs for sale. I believe they are $75 each. They're at the Guitar
Showcase consignment shop, 3090 S Bascom Ave, San Jose (California).

As you might recall from previous discussions here, the TX81Z is a nice
little 8-voice polyphonic FM box, really super for anyone doing anything with
tunings. If you have a PC and download Scala free from the web they're
incredibly easy to tune, full keyboard.
