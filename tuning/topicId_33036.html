Date: January 22, 2002
News: Events and site additions

Hi, all!

As promised recently, I've put final info on the site for the
Feb. 2 Harry Partch Centennial gathering at San Jose State U.
You'll find a listing of the days activities, directions, a press
release (with photo) for downloading, and all pertinent informa-
tion. I hope to see some of the Meadows correspondents there,
and I hope you'll introduce yourself!

Also, we've got news and content refreshing: two CDs to note,
a new collection from award winning poet Janet Holmes that in-
cludes her poem "Partch Stations", and if you didn't make it to
the UCLA Centennial bash, we've got the presentation that Bob
Gilmore gave that afternoon, "Reconstructing Harry".

Hope that's enough for now - plan your trip, read some stuff, and
I gotta go get the photo exhibit ready!
