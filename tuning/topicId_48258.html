Microtonal trumpet ideas that come to me (which you've probably had) are:
Marco Blaauw, 2-bell 1/4 tone trumpeter: marcoblaauw.com
Lois Vierk's Cirrus for 6 trumpets uses enough continuous glissandi to
qualify
Patrick Ozzard-Low as a resource you must already know
