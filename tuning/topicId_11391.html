Hello all,

I've made some additions to my website, chiefly in adding
a lot of definitions of Greek terms from John Chalmers's book
(he graciously donated them to the cause) to the Dictionary.
Most of the new words are under D, E, F, and G.

The 'Tutorial on ancient Greek tetrachord-theory' therefore
has many new links into the dictionary:
www.ixpres.com/interval/monzo/aristoxenus/tutorial.htm

There's also a picture of Woolhouse that someone sent me,
which I've added at the top of that page:
www.ixpres.com/interval/monzo/woolhouse/essay.htm

And I've expanded 'A Century of New Music in Vienna'
so that it now includes information on Tanaka's JI 'Enharmonium'
which wowed Austro-German musical circles 120 years ago:
www.ixpres.com/interval/monzo/schoenberg/Vienna1905.htm

Work continues apace on my explorations into Sumerian tuning,
but I've been quite sidetracked into studying other aspects
of Sumerian culture, so there will soon be lots of new webpages
on non-tuning issues relating to mythology, writing, the Bible,
etc., in addition to some really new stuff on ancient music.

Stay 'tuned'.

-monz
