Hello folks!

Thank you to all the participants of the Motu-Ethno2 microtonal competition, whose compositions give a good idea of what microtonality can be, at least in traditional or neo-traditional ways (Ethno2 is not done for exploring the great diversity of synthesized sounds and experimental musics, but his microtonal implementation and the easiness to use it are exceptional).

So, i propose to let the DropBox open (dedicated exclusively to Ethno2) so that everyone who wants to share a composition could open there a personal folder, by just asking to me (or any of the participants to the context) for codes and instructions. I think this will be helpful to everybody.

I understand your position. But to use the dropbox you don't need to have it installed on your computer: you can load files (up and down) directly from the dropbox site. So, just uninstall the dropbox application from your computer, then delete the associated folder .

To let or not your files in this common dropbox is (of course!) up to you.
