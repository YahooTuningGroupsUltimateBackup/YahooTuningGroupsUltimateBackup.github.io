The key to good fret placement is to measure very carefully and check
your measurements. Then if you are at all coordinated, you can cut the
fret slots accurately. The rest is surprisingly self correcting. I have a
tutorial on refretting your guitar at
http://www-math.cudenver.edu/~jstarret/guitar.html
