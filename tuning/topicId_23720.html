Since Dave Keenan asked for it, I have scanned a page of Ezra Sims'
72-tET notation and placed it in the "files" section.

This particular page has all the "glyphs" used...

This is from Joe Maneri's book: _Preliminary Studies in the Virtual
Pitch Continuum_ and is part of a 2-part harmony study.

Please note the inclusion of standard cents values above the staff to
facilitate ear training and learning. He uses this throughout the
book.

You will find this scan in the "Files" section of

egroups, in my personal directory:

/tuning/files/Pehrson/

The file is called:

Sims.GIF

They're pretty "wacky" and, not being in a computer font, are not
necessarily "elegant"... but here they are!
