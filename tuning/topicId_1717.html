Yes, Im not claiming any innovation. Basically what I have done is lined
up three octaves of the 8 X 8 matrix and removed the pitches of lesser
frequency than the 1/1 and greater frequency than the 4/1. One reason I
have done this is that I have 122 solenoids to work with. I could
manufacture more, although I find it helpful to impose certain limits on
the project so that it does not remain in the status of vaporware ad
infinitum. Also, the keyboard layout provides for 128 switches -- one midi
channel. I could go to two midi channels, but this again provides a
limiting factor to keep the project down to earth.
