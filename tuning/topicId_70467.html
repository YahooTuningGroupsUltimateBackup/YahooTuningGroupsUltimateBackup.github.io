Hi everyone.

This is an experiment I made in september 99. I just tried to make a recording without using any sequencer or any metronome. I just played the parts and layered them using an 8-track MD audio recorder. Unfortunately, as a result, my experiment showed to have its significantly dark sides as well because I was still fairly unexperienced in playing fast passages at that time and that's why there are a few moments when the music falls somewhat out of beat. But I believe you'll understand I just wanted to give it a try and this was what came out. As far as the used tunings go, I even don't think I'll have to say what they are -- someone gonna guess? - Here it is: www.yousendit.com/download/T2dmRkJkOW5TRTQwTVE9PQ
