The Vogue MK2 free microtonal synthesizer version 1.0 (for Mac Intel only) is now available for download at the following URL:

http://tinyurl.com/24fq7xm

The Vogue MK2 is a standalone eight-voice synthesizer fully compatible with Max Magic Microtuner and currently distributed as free-license software.

This is a follow-up to the Vogue Quattro four-voice synthesizer released earlier this year, with doubled polyphony and additional features: be aware the MK2 is more processor-intensive than its predecessor, so you'll need a latest generation Mac to avoid performance problems.
