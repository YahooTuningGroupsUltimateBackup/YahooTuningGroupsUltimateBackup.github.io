Dear Microtonalists

It is with deep sorrow that I feel urged to communicate to you that
Eduardo Sábat-Garibaldi, a member of this community, passed away last
February 25th at the age of 71. I'm one of his nephews, and he usually
shared with me his thoughts and develpments in music and
microtonalism. I'm not a musician, but learnt to enjoy Eduardo's
enthusiasm towards microtonalism and its active community -within
which he has found friends-, the "Dinarra" and the "Gama dinámica".

His death was the consequence of a failed surgery, which irremediably
hit his health. His agony lasted one month.

At least I want this information to be shared with John Chalmers, and
with Johhny Reinhard, two of the nice friends of my uncle, if not to
all who will miss him as I do.

Best regards

Haroldo
