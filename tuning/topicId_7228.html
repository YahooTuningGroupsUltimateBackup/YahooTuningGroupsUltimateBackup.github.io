Use a computer to generate a C at 240hz for C or 272 for C#
and Just tune up from there
or consult Alain Danielou books , Ray Tomes' website has a neat graphic
60 hz is usuallty the base though
but it differs of course from gharana to gharana

The Korg MT-1200 is a multi-temperament electronic
tuner. There's 8 presets but also room for 2 user
defined tunings. Between the two you should have enough
room for the 22 tone Sruti scale of Indian music.
I paid $300 for mine at the local Guitar Center.

As for learning JI, I always recommend David Doty's
Just Intonation Primer. Check it out here:

http://www.dnai.com/~jinetwk/
