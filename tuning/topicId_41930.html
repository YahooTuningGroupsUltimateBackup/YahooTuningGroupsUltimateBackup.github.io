Come join Zeitgeist and Philip Blackburn for our latest Lowertown
Listening Session!

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

Zeitgeist presents
Lowertown Listening Session
The Dreamer That Remains: The Music of Harry Partch

Tuesday, February 4th , 2003 at 7:30 p.m.
Zeitgeist Studio
Northwestern Building - 275 E. 4th Street, St. Paul
Please call (651) 755-1600 for information or disability access info.
Admission is Free

In this latest installment of Zeitgeist's Lowertown Listening
Sessions, Special Guest speaker Philip Blackburn of the American
Composers Forum will lead an insightful and entertaining discussion
about one of America's most revered experimentalists, Harry Partch.
With a plenty of audio, video, and even touchable samples,
participants will get an in-depth look into an amazing and beguiling
figure whose unique musical journey has inspired countless other
creative artists.

Zeitgeist's Lowertown Listening Sessions, held monthly at Zeitgeist's
studio, is a series of informal and intimate events designed to
demystify contemporary music and deepen the listening skills of
participants. Through this series, the ensemble and our audience
explore new works-in-progress, works from our repertoire, and various
trends and issues in new music.

Heather Barringer
Artistic Co-Director
Zeitgeist
