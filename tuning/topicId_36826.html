Jose,

SACD uses 1-bit delta-sigma samples, at a rate which I
remember as being higher than 196KHz., but which are
anyway not comparable rate-for-rate to traditional
16-or-more-bit PCM samples. It's debatable, and somewhat
academic, whether SACD is better than 96KHz PCM.

Being fully programmable, Kyma should be able to do all
of the things you asked for; I don't know if it does them
out of the box.
