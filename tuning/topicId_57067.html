1) It's spelled "Temperament", not "temperment". :)

2) Temperaments, including 12-tone equal temperament and meantone,
can be derived from JI by positing certain equivalencies between
ratios, resulting in tuning systems less complex than JI. The paper
that I will send you explains this and shows how many of the
resulting temperaments are *not* spirals of fifths. I've also been
told that the new paper is far clearer than _Tuning, Tonality, and
Twenty-Two-Tone Temperament_. So I think you should read it!

3) If you still have any confusions concerning any of my older
papers, please allow me to clear them up for you.

4) I will make comments on what you have when I get a chance. But I
feel this will be more productive/helpful after you have read my new
paper, whether you choose to modify anything on your webpage because
of it or not. At least you'll have a better sense of where I'm coming
from with my (forthcoming) comments!

Until later,
