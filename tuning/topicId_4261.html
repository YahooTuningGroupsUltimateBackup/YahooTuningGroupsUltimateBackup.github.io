Sorry, I wrote too fast. Finale can't handle long sys-exes. Instead, I do a
standard midi dump from Finale, then use the programs MF2T and T2MF
(available on the csound website) and introduce the sys-exes as text.

MF2T and T2MF are two programs by Piet van Oostrum for manipulating standard
midifiles. MF2T is a program that reads a standard midifile (format 0 or 1)
and writes an ASCII representation of it that is both compact and easily
parsable. T2MF is the companion program that reparses the text representation
into a midifile.
