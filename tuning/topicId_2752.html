Hi Kraig-

If you have a PC download nexencode studio, which you can get at (among
other places):

http://zapcom.softseek.com/Home_Family_and_Leisure/Music/Audio_Encoders_and_
Utilities/Review_22383_index.html

this will enable you to put a CD in your CDrom drive and "strip" a track off
it as a wav. file. Unless you have humungous storage on your server, next
download Ra encoder:

http://www.thinice.com/tic/realaudio.html

so you can convert it to Real Audio. This will reduce a multi megabyte wav.
file to a couple of hundred K real audio file which you can then upload to
your server.

If you have a power mac, try mp3.com or Harmony Central, or search for "cd
ripper mac" and "real audio encoder mac"

good luck
