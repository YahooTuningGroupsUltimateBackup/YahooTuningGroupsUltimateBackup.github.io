These are 5-tone sub-scales of the 8-tone
12EDO blues mode 8-26 (scale step pattern
1-2-2-1-2-1-1-2) of 12EDO pitch class set
theory:

http://solomonsmusic.net/pcsets.htm

The "nesting" concept of mine is that the
prime and inversion forms of each blues
pentachord are aligned/sandwhiched next
to each other in the minimum possible
pitch/registral space, and the scales are
then played in the manner I outline in the
diagrams I sent you.

I also sent you invites to my 12EDO blues
nested sub-scales Yahoo groups, each of which
have MIDI file archives.
