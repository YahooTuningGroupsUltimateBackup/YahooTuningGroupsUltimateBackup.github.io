Friends,

Harry Partch: the dreamer that does, indeed, remain.
Born: June 24, 1901
Died: September 3, 1974

One of Harry's favorite sayings was the one he saw written in appreciation on a wall:

Once upon a time
There was a little boy
And he went outside.

Today, in appreciation for whatever Harry did for your life, go outside. He did it for as long as people can remember; you can do it for one day.

Respectfully,
