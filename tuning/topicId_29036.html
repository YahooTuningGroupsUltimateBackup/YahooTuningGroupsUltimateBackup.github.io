I really love this Monz webpage:

http://www.ixpres.com/interval/dict/eqtemp.htm

I know Monz intends this to be totally serious, but, curiously
enough, I found some humor in it... The idea of adding incrementally
by, generally speaking at least in the beginning, one note, and then
listing the practicioners I found slightly humorous.

No offense to Monz, I hope. There's nothing wrong with a little
humor, especially in these times. I particularly like when we
finally get to 12, and then *in bold* "1900-1999 the predominant
tuning of the 'developed' world."

Now, isn't there something a little funny there, or am I finally
losing my mind?.. which is quite possible these days.

I was delighted to see this page updated and, in fact, was surprised
to see my *own* accomplishments listed on it.

Frankly, I was unaware that I had done anything, so I was, naturally,
delighted to learn that I had...

What a great webpage!
