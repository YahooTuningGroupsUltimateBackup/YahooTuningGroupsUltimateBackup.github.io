I had been asked to list my scale for Harmonic 17 Tuning which I used for my
quartet "Trespass" for soprano, violin, viola, and cello (performed in
Amsterdam 3 years ago).

It's a beautiful, alluring tuning with little connection to the whole or
semi-tone ordinarily associated with it in its combination of exoticism and
normality in chordal function. This tuning, like the Harmonic 13 Tuning
which requires all ratios to include a 13 in its ratio or its multiple, is
different from the just intonation limit typically used to define tunings.
There is a different qualitative result as well for the basic 5-limit
intervals are avoided, changing its effect.
