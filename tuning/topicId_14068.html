For fans of my music (but off-topic, because these pieces are
still in 12-tET... but I hope to retune them into JI someday...),
I've added MIDI-files of the two most significant of my very
early compositions:

http://www.ixpres.com/interval/monzo/worklist/sym2.mid

and the revised version of my _1st Quartet_ [1977], at:
http://www.ixpres.com/interval/monzo/worklist/strqt-rv.mid

I was 14 imitating Mozart, and 15 imitating Beethoven,
repectively.
