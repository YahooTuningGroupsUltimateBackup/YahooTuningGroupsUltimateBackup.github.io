Dave,

And a good read it was! I'm sure the method will be of great
practical value in creating more playable microtempered
fretted instruments. I was surprised at just how few partial
frets you can get away with, which makes bending the notes
practical on almost all frets. Also, because your method
maximises the number of full-width frets, it also increases
their spacing, which helps articulate notes cleanly without
buzzing.

I'm impressed!

And glad you made it available without waiting any longer.
