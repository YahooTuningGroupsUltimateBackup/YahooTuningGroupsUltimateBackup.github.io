Here are the key strokes for Finale's quarter-tone accidentals:

Tamburo font:
i = 0.5 flat
shift-i = 1.5 flat
option-m = 0.5 sharp
shift-option-n = 1.5 sharp
shift-b = backward flat

Personally, I like the 0.5 and 1.5 sharp symbols, but am not found of
the 1.5 flat symbol, but use it out of convenience in Finale'ed scores.
