Hello all,

I've been bugging Dave Hill for a long time to
allow me to upload his work to the Sonic Arts website,
and he has finally consented.

I've made the first webpage of Dave's work out of
a CD (converted to mp3's) and his accompanying notes:

"Ten Musical Passages in Just Intonation"
http://sonic-arts.org/hill/10-passages-ji/10-passages-ji.htm

This includes one piece by Ivor Darreg, an excerpt
from the "l'Homme Arme Mass" by Guillaume Dufay (1450),
and 8 compositions by Dave, including his wonderful
"Venusian Cataclysms" and "The Second Swan".

This page also now has a link on Dave's main page
at Sonic Arts, which has finally been updated after
11 years!

http://sonic-arts.org/hill.htm
