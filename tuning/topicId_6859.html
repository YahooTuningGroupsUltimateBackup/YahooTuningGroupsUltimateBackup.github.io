I thought, before anyone in the Szanto/Grady/Wolf camp
(sorry about the generalization) really did get angry
with me over this, that perhaps I could provide a bit
of defense:

I was making a MIDI-sequence of the beginning of _Barstow_
*IN PARTCH'S TUNING*, and for the purposes of my own study
only. But I had to make a 12-eq version of the sequence
first, and then add pitch-bend to get the proper tuning.

I simply kept the 12-eq version, and thought it would be
interesting to listen to in comparison from time to time.
