You've gotta be joking Stafan, even "backward companies like Apple provide microtuning nowadays as a given feature e.g. Logic. Also Reason, Absynth, CuBase etc. etc. have begun to understand and provide microtonal capability.
Since you already have the code etc. in other versions of Melodyne for user defined pitch correction, it must be possible to add a subroutine onto the DNA beta to get the pitch snap to go to user defined pitches as well as an old ubiquitous 20th century tuning system like 12edo.
Obviously it would be possible to repitch each individual note to the nearest cent or 1/10th cent, as the beta is at present, but having the tuning table capability makes microtuning a "simple snap", and saves hours of tedious work for those thousands of users who are nowadays microtuning their creations. Those thousands are also increasing day by day, as more musicians appreciate and exploit the advantages of microtuning.
