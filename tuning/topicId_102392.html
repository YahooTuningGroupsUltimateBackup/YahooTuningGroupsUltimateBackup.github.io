Okay folks, I think I've finally finished my project of looking at all the rank-1 5-dimensional pentadic subgroups of the 13-limit from 5-ET to 36-ET. Here's a spreadsheet of the adjusted error for the best (lowest-error) mapping of each ET for each subgroup (so the mapping for a single ET may vary from subgroup to subgroup, to minimize the error):

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/tuning/files/IgliashonJones/5D%2013-limit%20Subgroup%20ETs.pdf
