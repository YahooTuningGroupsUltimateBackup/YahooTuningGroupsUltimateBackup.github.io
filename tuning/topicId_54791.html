Instructions for LucyTuning Spiral

Display note names: Move mouse near note.

Sound notes: Click on note

Select Chords from Pulldown menu

Add/remove notes from chord: Toggle notes on/off by clicking on notes whilst holding Shift.

Sound notes/chords: Hit Return

Zoom In/Out: Use Up/Down Keys

Comments/gripes/suggestions etc. appreciated.
