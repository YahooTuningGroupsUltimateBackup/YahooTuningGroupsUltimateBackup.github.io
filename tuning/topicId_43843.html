If you are like me, you've been ignoring the kleismic temperament
since it seems too closely wedded to the 5-limit. However, there's a
5-limit + 13 point of view which expands its possibilities quite a
bit. With a good 5-limit and a good 13, we can make use of the
8:10:13 chord as an alternative to 8:10:12, for an interesting effect.
These chords sound quite nice when well enough in tune.

Below I analyze the 19-tone kleismic scale, showing the kinds of
chords which turn up in it.
