Dear Folks

Long time no chat,flame or otherwise.
i am still looking forward to exchanging some Max/Msp files with any and all
Max user interested in JI.
I have created a nice patch called "teh" ~ the internet word replacing
"the". It is a generative patch that Justifies any input using Phase
Vocoding resynthesis objects created by Christopher Penrose and Eric Lyons
in their wonderful FFTease package (also available for JMax-linux). "Teh"
received it premiere at the 11th annual Florida Electroacoustic Festival in
March with Paul Lansky of Cmix fame as our CIR.
http://emu.music.ufl.edu/femf/fest11prg.html

I have begun playing fretless JI guitar & amplified strings with a pop band
we will be playing a festival in Brooklyn "come the freak on II" October
26th
http://www.screwmusicforever.com/bound~
the page is in its infancy but does include a cheesy streaming vid and some
pretty sissy images courtesy of yrs truly.

about 17 pitches per octave randomly in a quasi-ambient textural splash of
freely evolving lovies.
