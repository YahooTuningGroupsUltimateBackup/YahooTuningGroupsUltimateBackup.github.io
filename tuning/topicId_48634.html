At the MaMuX Seminar on november 8, 2003 I have given a talk on "Microtonality and limited
transposition modes in n-tet". You will find all the details on the Web site :

http://www.ircam.fr/equipes/repmus/mamux/documents/ResumesNov03.html

in particular you could download three documents :

1) the slides (LimitedModespar6.pdf)
2) a complete discography on microtonality (disco.pdf)
3) a table of limited transposition modes in n-tet (tablellmited.pdf)
