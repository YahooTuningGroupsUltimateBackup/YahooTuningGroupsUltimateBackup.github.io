Sorry to use you as an example Judy, but this is really addressed to
everyone:

If you have a concert which is in any tuning other than 12tet*, please post
all the information a few weeks in advance of the event. Include the date,
time, and address of the establishment, ticket price, and information number
so that other micro-enthusiasts at least have a chance of showing up! It's
not egotistical, just practical. This list is my primary link to the
microtonal community, and I imagine it's the same for many others. If I
don't find out through this list, chances are I won't find out at all.

Footnote: *If you live in a country where 12tet is not the norm, you should
post those concerts as well.
