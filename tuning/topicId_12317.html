Of course, in the context of 5-limit harmony, the "comma problems" are that
the two minor thirds and one major third are supposed to be consonant, but
they are off by a comma. Perhaps you are coming from the context of 3-limit
harmony (well, I know that you are, but newer members might not, so you
might want to say so), in which case I agree, this is a perfect solution
when the consonant harmony is constructed entirely from fourths and fifths.
