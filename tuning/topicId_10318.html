Bradley Lehman has a very nice MP3.com Clavichord site... "The
clavichord used in these recordings is a Carl Fudge instrument (Hubert
model) built by Brian Joyce. Tuning: A=440 Hz, well-temperament
similar to Werckmeister III":

<http://artists.mp3s.com/artists/70/bradley_lehman.html>

These are all selections from his pretty interestingly varied
collection "The Domesticated Clavichord" (I personally thought that
the Pass arrangement, "Sultry," was particularly effective):

<http://www-personal.umich.edu/~bpl/domesticnotes.htm>

I also found his keyboard temperament analyzer/calculator (which
might've been mentioned here at the TD before?) at:

<http://www-personal.umich.edu/~bpl/temper.html>
