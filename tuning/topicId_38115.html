We've got a term "wafso-just" which is like what you are proposing. When
I say "effectively just" I mean something stronger than that; within a
1/2 cent of just at least. Hence I've called 118 "effectively just" in
the 5-limit,
and 171 or the ennealimmal temperament effectively just in the 7-imit. I
also don't call something a "microtemperament" unless it gets closer to
just than 72 does--octoid or the 224-et I call microtemperaments, but I
don't yet have the courage to call them "effectively just", despite how
hard it is to find any difference.
