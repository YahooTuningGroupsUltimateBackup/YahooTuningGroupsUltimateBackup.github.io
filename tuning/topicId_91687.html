On the surface, the Ab is sour but everything else (from the root) looks great.

But
1) Again, you have an unusable 5th around 1.48 from 1.5 to 1.1 on the next
octave.
2) You have a bunch of thirds around 1.2656. That's a massive 21 cents or so of
error which make it, in my mind, unusable.

So yes, you have a lot of perfect 5ths, but that makes most your thirds
unusable and makes the last fifth unusable. In fact it makes many more of the
thirds unusable than your last scale did.
My advice: try to keep most of the thirds at least under 1.257 or over 1.243
or so and make all the 5ths between about 1.49 and 1.515 (or allow a fifth or
two around 22/15 or 13/9...which really don't sound all that bad and certainly
much better than anything near 1.48).
