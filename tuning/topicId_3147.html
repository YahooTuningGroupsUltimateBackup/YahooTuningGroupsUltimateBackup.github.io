Last night I was on Perfect Sound Forever's
streaming audio/net radio program. Here's what I played:

Harry Partch - Ring Around the Moon
Ben Johnston - Sonata for Microtonal Piano
Terry Riley - Across the Lake of the Ancient Word
John Cage - Dream (performed by John Schneider & Amy Schulman)
John Cage - In a Landscape (performed by John Schneider & Amy Schulman)
Birdhouse - Summons to the Birds
Birdhouse - Chorus d'Oiseaux
Ornette Coleman - Free (Performed by the Catler Bros.)
Johnny Reinnhard - Trio on the Cuff 6
Shree Swifty [Pat Pagano] - Isis Unveiled
La Monte Young - the Well Tuned Piano (excerpt from the opening)
David Beardsley - Ttoet

Not many suprises here.

I only had two hours, hopefully there's more
programs in the future.
