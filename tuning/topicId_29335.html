Partch's book "Genesis of a Music" actually has quite a bit of detail
in the descriptions of his canons (well, most all of his
instruments), including many of the dimensions of the sound boxes,
number and tuning of strings, etc. While not schematics for building
per se, they have been clear enough for at least two people who built
similar instruments:

http://www.corporeal.com/art_inst/showinst/inst96.html
http://www.corporeal.com/art_inst/showinst/inst96.html

I'm sure there are many more out there, as well, but maybe this would
be one starting point...
