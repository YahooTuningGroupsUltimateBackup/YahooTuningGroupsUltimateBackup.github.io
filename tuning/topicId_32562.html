Here's a link to an interesting article on the tree
of Mr Stern's and Mr Brocot's independent efforts

There is no sign that either man knew of the others work. After the fact,
however, connections between them are easy to see; they are doing the same
thing but describing it differently.
There is even a connection between Brocots algorithm and the Fibonacci
series, where this whole quest began. To see the relation, just try using
Brocots method to find ratios approximating the constant known as phi, or
the golden ratio, an irrational number with a value of approximately 1.618.
The series of approximants begins 1/1, 2/1, 3/2, 5/3, 8/5, 13/8,. Hidden
within these ratios is the complete sequence of Fibonacci numbers.
