Hello, there, and I would like strongly to express my own view that
Jacky Ligon's scale using some beautiful ratios of 23 and 37 is indeed
just intonation as I define it: a system of tuning based on integer
ratios.

While Jacky has emphasized the melodic qualities of these intervals, I
would add that many of his choices have what look to me like they have
very beautiful vertical applications also.

The dialogue we have seen here from many able contributors has
convinced me, at least, that the term "JI" has such different
associations and premises for different people that the best we can do
is to acknowledge and indeed cherish this diversity.
