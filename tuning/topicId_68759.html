Actually, the full title is "Creepy Critter" in English, and "KIRKAYAK" (milipede) in Turkish. The piece is supposed to be funny until the critter is squashed underfoot at the end of the piece.

I had composed it in Encore. And yes, it was done originally in 12-edo. It is possible to save it as a midi file too, but what's the use when I am still confined to 12-tones?

Glad you enjoyed it!
