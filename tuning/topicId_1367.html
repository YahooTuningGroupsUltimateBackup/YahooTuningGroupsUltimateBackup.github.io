Many thanks to J.Smith for his excellent music(For Shaahin, Prelude and Rhythmic Study), a new language for modal music.
A jovial music <http://www.esnips.com/doc/5b2b21b8-42ac-4cb2-a418-1e5dd265083c/A-jovial-music.mp3>
This music is composed in a mode of Reverse baran scale ( Bold degrees): <http://240edo.googlepages.com/arithmeticirrationaldivisions(aid)>
