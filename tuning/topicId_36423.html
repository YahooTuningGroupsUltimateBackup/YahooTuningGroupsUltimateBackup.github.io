Well, my "Jerry 10" post isn't appearing, and since it's such a
crucial, crucial post, I'll post it again, just in case

In essence, I listened again to "Jerry 10" and *this* time, no matter
how I listened, the third went up (seemingly about 14 cents :) ) when
the 12-tET triad came in.

So, while this is not encouraging to the "high third" camp, I must
still confess that I'm a "believer" (Santa Claus is not far behind).

Seriously, though, I really believe that a "barenaked" JI third
without a fifth sounds *lower* than the third in a full triad.

However, in *this* example, with the 12-tET triad coming after, it
doesn't work for me. The third goes *up...*

At least, that's what happened *today!* :)
