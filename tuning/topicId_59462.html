Here's a MIDI file of the beautiful
"Cavatina" movement (complete) from Beethoven's
13th Quartet in Bb, op. 130, tuned in a 15-tone
chain of 1/6-comma meantone, generated from a
Tonescape file:

beethoven_cavatina_15-tone_1-6cmt.mid

in the "monz" folder of the Files section of
the Yahoo tuning_files group:

http://launch.groups.yahoo.com/group/tuning_files/files/monz/

complete URL (delete the line-break):

http://launch.groups.yahoo.com/group/tuning_files/files/monz/
beethoven_cavatina_15-tone_1-6cmt.mid

The piece is in Eb, and the meantone chain, which
uses only the notes notated by Beethoven, extends
from Fb to F# and includes the three enharmonic
pairs Fb:E, Cb:B, and Gb:F#.

(The lucky group of Tonescape alpha-testers also
get to see the Lattice notated with Beethoven's notes,
and the uneven steps in the Pitch-Height notation,
when playing the .tonescape file.)
