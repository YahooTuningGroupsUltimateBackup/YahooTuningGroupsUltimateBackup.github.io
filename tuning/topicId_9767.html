A while back, John Link reported that the headers from individual posts were missing. I decided to subscribe from another account, non-digest mode, to see for myself. What I get are properly formed messages, with full headers (i.e. From, To, Subject, etc.).

Tracked it down and had it fixed in a day, so maybe the solution is downstream a bit.
