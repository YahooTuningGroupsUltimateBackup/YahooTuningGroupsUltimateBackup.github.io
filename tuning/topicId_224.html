A while back astute readers of this list might recall that I pointed to a
12-of-17 tET piece that's part of the Midsummer Night's Dream music I did for
City Lights here in San Jose. The run ended June 30, and was apparently
well received.

For microtonal enthusiasts, here is one more tidbit. I managed to get the
two lovely fairies who sang for this production to record the "Fairy Lullaby"
from Act II. Words by Billy "The Bard" Shakespeare; music by me.

The file is available at:
http://rm-and-jo.laughingsquid.org/FairyLullaby.mp3

The vocalists are Nina Breton and Lilly Small. As mentioned, the
(electronic) ensemble is tuned in 12-of-17tET.
