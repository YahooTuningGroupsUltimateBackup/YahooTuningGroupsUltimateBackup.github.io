The text of Paul's Erlich's Tuning List postings on Harmonic
Entropy, with graphs and additional commentary by me (based
on Paul's own explanations to me) is available at:

http://www.ixpres.com/interval/td/erlich/entropy.htm

There is also a link to the original, unedited texts.

A version with additional graphs is also available at Dave
Keenan's website (I haven't had the time to import it to my site.
Sorry, Dave, if you can hear me...)
http://www.uq.net.au/~zzdkeena/Erlich/index.htm

There's also a briefer definition of it in my Tuning Dictionary:
http://www.ixpres.com/interval/dict/harmentr.htm

Regarding some of the other formulae considered in the Harmonic
Complexity topic, Dave Keenan has also made some beautiful Excel
spreadsheet graphs of different measurements discussed in the
several-week-long complexity thread (see the Onelist Tuning List
Archives), downloadable here as a zip file:
http://www.uq.net.au/~zzdkeena/Music/HarmonicComplexity.zip
