For those of you in the Austin, TX, USA area:

There will be a Dhrupad concert this Saturday night at UT. Here's the
info. (from http://www.icmca.org/):

------------------------------------------------------------------------

Gundecha Brothers
Live, in Concert!

Umakant and Ramakant Gundecha
Saturday, September 9, 2000
8:00 p.m.
(Please note the revised time)
Jessen Auditorium, UT-Austin
(21st Street & University.
Jessen Auditorium is located in the Old Music Building,
on West 21st Street, immediately west of the Littlefield Fountain)

Tickets: ICMCA members - free
Regular - $20
Students/Seniors - $10
You may purchase your tickets online or at the door.
(Please note, online ticket purchases are non-refundable.)

The Gundecha brothers are renowned artists of the Dhrupad style of
Hindustani Classical music. Dhrupad is an ancient style of Indian
Classical music which traces its origins to the melodic chants of the
Vedas.
