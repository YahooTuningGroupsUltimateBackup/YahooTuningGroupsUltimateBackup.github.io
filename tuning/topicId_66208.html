Oblivion VSTi for Win comes with over 400 tunings (TUN
and SCL), and support for real time changes in the
intonation. True stereo reverb, tempo sync digital
delay, lightning fast VCA, realtime performance
control, multimode filter, and four tempo synced LFO's
are included.

Here is the link:

http://www.oblivionom.com/

Demo is near fully functional for 11 days (LFO3 & LFO4
do not operate in demo). Register and receive 200 more
tunings (Gamelan and Shruti, lots not found in Scala
archive).

Couple more synths and ability to build your own
custom microtonal synth available soon.

Cheers,
David

Hi Mohajeri Shahin,

Please try to download again. Simply drag drop the .dll file in the
vstplugin directory (often located in the Steinberg directory).

The SCL files intentionally have repetitious intervals. Scales with
less than twelve notes have the octaves at every twelve notes, cool
for the keyboardist new to alternate tuning, easy to play.

www.Oblivionom.com

Go to the scala archive for scales where the octave does not repeat
every twelve notes. There is a command where one can convert the
complete SCALA archive into TUN files in just a few minutes. How are
the SCL files in the RAR corrupted?

It should take less than four minutes to get the Oblivion instrument
working, and it sounds smooth, amazing resolution for the tunings.

Perhaps you got a corrupt download. I verified the rar posted is
good. There is not a setup file. Simply a DLL, the TUN & SCL files, a
readme, and documentation in .PDF. Can you read the PDF from the file
you downloaded?

Thank You,
David Frost
