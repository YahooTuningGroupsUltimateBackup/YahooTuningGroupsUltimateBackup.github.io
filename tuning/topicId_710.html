Folks:
Tony Conrad's name was mentioned on this list. If you don't know abourt
this guy take a moment ot visit his site at http://www.tonyconrad.com/tote/

He was an essentioal part of LaMonte Young's early work and has a lot to
say about what went on in those early years. As for Slapping Pythagoras- it
is a bit "edgy" but OK if you are into audio meatgrinders. I do, however,

guarantee you will enjoy this very much-
