Here's some of the 12-note tuning table spec for the Akai S5000 & S6000.
It seems to have 7 tables :

0=user, 1=even-tempered, 2=orchestral, 3=werkmeister,
4=1/5 meantone, 5=1/6 meantone, 6=just, 7=arabian.
