Hi, all

I'm currently working on some recordings (nothing to do with
alternative tunings), and I came across a freeware MIDI synthesiser
called SynthFont (I got it as a host for SoundFonts).

The thing that amazed me is that the options menu includes an option
for tuning. It allows individual tuning of each scale step (in cents
above or below ET), and it offers 75 preset scales. I had a quick
look, and those presets cover every 12-note scale I've ever heard of
(and a lot that I haven't).

The website is http://synthfont.com
