Do we have some Swiss members? Just received the program of the 2nd Art
Festival in Switzerland. Apart from a shakuhachi concert, there is one
*hard* microtonal concert, for two pianos :

Time: 30 August 2000 at 4 pm
Place: Musikerhaus am St. Moritzersee, 7500 St. Moritz, Switzerland
Action: Gertrud Schneider and Tomas Bächli, piano, play quartertone
compositions by Ives, Jordi, Werder, Wyschnegradsky (with Christoph
Schiller, viola), Zimmerlin and others.
Slogan: Ein feineres Tonsystem schafft andere Ohren (a more refined tone
system creates better ears) (Wim's translation)
Information: http://www.fdk.ch/Seiten/Text/mu15.html
