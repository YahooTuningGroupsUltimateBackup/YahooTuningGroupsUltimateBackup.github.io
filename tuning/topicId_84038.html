for my last will and testament.

his keen awareness of how people who have
something to contribute to the general fund for humanity

that when i go, i will leave a vast legacy of unfinished projects.

just to house my collection of books and papers.

i want to ensure that in my will, my estate
goes to someone or some entity that will make the
best use of what i leave behind.

so who wants it? or which university libraries, etc.,
does anyone think would get the most value from my stuff?
