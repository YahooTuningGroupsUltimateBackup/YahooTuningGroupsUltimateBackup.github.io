*) the augmented second in Pythagorean tuning is 19683/16384

**) Only regular tuning systems (e.g. meantones, Pythagorean) have definite
values for conventionally named intervals. Just intonation will have several
values for intervals with any conventional name. 9/5 and 16/9 are both minor
sevenths that occur in 5-limit just intonation, and context will usually
help to determine which should be used. 9/5 is the usual seventh of the
"just minor scale". As a dyad, 9:5 is more consonant, but 16:9 occurs in a
pretty consonant triad, 16:12:9.

***) The augmented sixth in Pythagorean tuning is 59049/32768.
