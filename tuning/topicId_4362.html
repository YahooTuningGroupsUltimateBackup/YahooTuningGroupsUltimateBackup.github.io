the 65 note scale is a scale devised by Alain Danielou
as detailed in Music And The Power of Sound:
The Influence of Tuning and Interval on
Consciousness, revised edition 1995, Inner
Traditions, ISBN 0-89281-336-9

Danielou called it a "Scale of Sounds" (53 harmonic
or cyclic intervals + 12 quarter tones = 65 notes to the octave,
66 total)

by system of harmonic proportions & that of a scale of 5ths
a division of the octave into 53 intervals is accomplished...
the addition of 12 quartertones bring the total division to
66 DISTINCT sounds...

music." To which I add.... anything beyond this is just
