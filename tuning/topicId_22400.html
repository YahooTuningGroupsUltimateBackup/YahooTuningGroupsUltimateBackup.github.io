Since I have so many "-"s in the lattice, I'm modifying Monz's notation as
follows:

72-tET notation will be 12-tET notation with the following new accidentals:

v = 1/4 tone flat
< = 1/6 tone flat
{ = 1/12 tone flat
} = 1/12 tone sharp
> = 1/6 tone sharp
^ = 1/4 tone sharp

So the blackjack scale is

C< 70 11&2/3
B{ 65 10&5/6

Bb< 58 9&2/3

Ab} 49 8&1/6

Gb} 37 6&1/6
F#{ 35 5&5/6
F 30 5
F< 28 4&2/3

Eb< 16 2&2/3
D> 14 2&1/3
