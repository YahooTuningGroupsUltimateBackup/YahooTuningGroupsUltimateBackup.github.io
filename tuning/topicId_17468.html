I thought I would point out that a video of mine is available online this
week -- it has been syndicated to windowsmedia.com. You can view it from
http://windowsmedia.com/mediaguide/middle/movies/moviesalt.asp, scroll down
to the CinemaNow.com line and choose your connection speed -- 100k or 300k.
Though WindowsMedia leaves out a lot of the detail (it can't really keep up
in the very fast parts) and the audio quality leaves a lot to be desired,
even at the higher bandwidth, you can at least get an idea of what it's
like.

The piece, Hiway 70, was written in freely modulating JI with Csound. In
fact, the files used to generate it are on the CD-ROM accompanying the
Csound book. The audio was also on the tuning list tape swap some time ago.
If I can be permitted one more act of self-promotion, you can buy the video
(with others) online at http://www.iotacenter.org/. The JI network should
be selling it too, but I haven't seen it on their website.
