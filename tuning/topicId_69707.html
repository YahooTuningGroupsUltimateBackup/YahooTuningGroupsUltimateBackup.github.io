Paolo, I am very pleased to learn of your interest in kanun music. Though I
listened to no solo kanun CD, I'm sure you will enjoy the tone of the
instrument in these CDs:

Turkish Art Music Compositions - Instrumental Works Collection
http://www.ideefixe.com/muzik/tanim.asp?sid=WDUUORJMUS3SWTMSOI82

"Life is a song" Huzzam Collection
http://www.ideefixe.com/muzik/tanim.asp?sid=M4I4IJT8CT5TDSPDJ5IF

Turkish Art Music Selections, Kurdili Hijazkar Collection
http://www.ideefixe.com/muzik/tanim.asp?sid=FEC1SV7D5H7HXLESIK6T

Songs loved by Ataturk:
http://www.tulumba.com/storeItem.asp?ic=MU940910AO765

This seems to be dedicated to kanun music, but I haven't listened to it
myself:
http://www.tulumba.com/storeItem.asp?ic=MU938952QL461

For the 79-tone music, I beg a little more patience. Once I set the theory
in place, a new school of music can be started in Turkiye featuring this
model.

Oz.
