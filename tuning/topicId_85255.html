The just intonation Harvey Mudd College American Gamelan will perform at

Music Recital Hall, Building 24
Cal Poly Pomona (http://www.csupomona.edu/)
This Friday Nov. 20 at 4:00 pm

We will be performing Lou Harrison's Gamelan 2, A Cornish Lancaran (with Linda Silva, clarinet), and Threnody for Carlos Chavez (with Cynthia Fogg, viola). We will also be performing Bill Alves's Imbal-Imbalan, Breath of the Compassionate (with video), and Elegy for Lou Harrison (with viola). For more information on the HMC American Gamelan and Bill's pieces, see http://www.billalves.com/hmcgamelan.html.
