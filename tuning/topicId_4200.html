CDs of Ivor Darreg's music are available from Brian McLaren,
who can be reached at:

Brian McLaren
2462 SE Micah Pl
Corvallis, OR 97333-1966

Brian has been hard at work on 'Microtonal Bookshelf',
a whole series of CDs of microtonal compositions and
improvisations, mainly featuring west-coast microtonalists.
(I was lucky enough to hear and see many of them when
I was at Sonic Arts last year. Some of the illustrations
on the covers and in the catalog are hilarious.)

Ask him about it. He's usually quite generous in writings
and packages to people who express an enthusiasm for
microtonal music.

We have several of Darreg's guitars at the Sonic Arts Gallery.
Anyone in the San Diego area is welcome to come by for a visit
and an improvisation session on the instruments. Write or call:

Jonathan Glasier
Sonic Arts Gallery
PO Box 620027, San Diego, CA 92102

619 231 3673
