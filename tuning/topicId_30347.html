I just tuned in from the netherlands a couple of days ago.
Y'all might know that the dutch do their essential reading at

http://www.xs4all.nl/~huygensf/index.html

This is the website of the Huygens-Fokker foundation, named after two dutch
pioneers of the 31tone-system. It has a lot of stuff happening, like MP3's,
theories, bibliographies etc, and alot of links.
Check it out, & Do The Just Thing!
