Thanks for listening and i myself confess that i often don't get to everything posted which should never be taken as a comment in itself. If you heard the piece before this Gendhing Canright it is a remix of this which were all samples of my instruments. Which doesn't have much bass in fact most was taken out cause it became too much in this context. Here ran through envelopers. software guitar amps with reverb EQ and then scattered through 18 tracks. a final ending or deconstruction of my own music. meta slendro again.

Named after my friend Tom Recchion who even Terry Riley has referred to as one of the kings of loops. It sounded like one of his pieces to me.
