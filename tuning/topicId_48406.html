I have uploaded a new piece at: -

http://myweb.tiscali.co.uk/slowtones

It's called Slow Kotekans Breaking. Kotekans are short interlocking
rhythmic/melodic patterns used in Balinese gamelan. Here they are presented
in slow motion using the 1.3.5.7.9.11 Eikosany.
