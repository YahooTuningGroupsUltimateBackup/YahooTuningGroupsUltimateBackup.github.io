Interesting list. Many of these are not unexpected, as for instance dominant, sharptone, and flattone (alternative 81/80 temperaments, not as accurate as meantone). Similarly there are those that are more associated with 5-limit temperaments (garibaldi, diminished, mavila, ripple, sycamore) or with higher-limit temperaments (orwell, rodan, unidec, buzzard, kwai).

Probably the most unexpected one on the list is pajara; although the 5-limit srutal is also important, and higher-limit versions do exist, pajara is most often thought of as a 7-limit temperament.
