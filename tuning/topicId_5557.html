For inspiring insight into how a master 20th century composer works,
visit Karlheinz Stockhausen's official website. There is atleast one mp3
(nearly 6 minute bit from the Helicopter String Quartet), lots of articles,
interviews, etc.

URLs :

http://www.stockhausen.org/
http://www.stockhausen.org/whats_new.html
http://www.stockhausen.org/helicopter_intro.html

James Ingram, Stockhausen's copyist since 1974 :
http://home.t-online.de/home/j.ingram

For more good stuff, including samples of scores :
http://www.u.arizona.edu/~jkandell/music/stockhausen.html

Karlheinz Stockhausen's New Morphology of Musical Time :
http://www.music.princeton.edu/~ckk/smmt/
(this paper contains much discussion of microtonality)
