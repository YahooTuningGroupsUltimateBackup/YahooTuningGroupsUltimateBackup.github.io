In terms of tetrad lattice coordinates, the comma pump goes to [14 14 14]. When I take all the notes of all the chords from [0 0 0] to [14 14 14] and map the result by 224 equal, which tempers out the akjaysma, I end up with 224 notes. That's one approach, I suppose, but don't let me stop anyone from using 1848et or even 20454et.
