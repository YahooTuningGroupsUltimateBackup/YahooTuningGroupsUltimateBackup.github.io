Hi- I'm in band called the Gongs, and we are going to be touring the country
this summer with Momus, and a few others on the American Patchwork label.
Although not blatently so, all our music demonstrates a complete
lack of 12tet- the instruments are all self-made, and tuned to various
things- a lot of 7tet, but including just intoned intervals and a little
bit of straight out arbitrary.

To get more info, and to look at all our instruments that we will be
using, check out
http://www.oberlin.edu/~chlatham

We'd appreciate attention from the tuning community, since we are trying
to bridge many styles and philosophies with our music...

Thanks,
Peter
