Finally, the Tuning Box TBX1 is back in stock and ready to ship, with a new feature, and a new low price of 299 USD.

The Box now features a Bypass switch in place of the old Resolution switch, allowing easy switching between Tuned mode (any tuning via pitch bend on multiple channels) and Bypass mode (12ET MIDI THRU), good for keeping TBX1 plugged into in your setup and using it just when you want it, without having to swap cables. Resolution options are still available using TBXE software.

The bypass feature improvement was requested by TBX1 owner Jeff Berg of
<http://www.unfretted.com>
who uses TBX1 with GR series Roland guitar synthesizers -- thanks, Jeff!

TBX1 is a portable hardware retuning device for multi-timbral synthesizers supporting pitch bend. It stores 128 tunings in 4 layers = 512 tuning tables of 128 notes each, all user programmable using TBXE (featuring Scala file import), all immediately accessible at the push of a button.
