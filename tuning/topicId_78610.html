Those of you who have expressed an interest in performances of the HMC American Gamelan
at MicroFest and elsewhere may be interested to know that a new CD of this just intonation
gamelan playing my works is now available. Information along with sound clips, scores, and
program notes is available at http://www.billalves.com/imbal-imbalan.html. The CD is
available at CD Baby and Amazon and also features performances by Roger Lebow (cello),
Rachel Vetter Huang (violin), Bill Shozan Schultz (shakuhachi), and Linda Michelou (tingklik).
