http://anaphoria.com/burmacries.mp3
6 lines all the same part, but staggered and quantized (or not) differently, played through different logic instruments, in an quasi enharmonic scale found in MetaSlendro.
Compositionally can be thought of of applying the idea of a 'nuclear melody' in this medium.
