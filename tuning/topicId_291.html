I would like to add some more info to the guitarists out there who are
into microtuning. I have been working on a website that is dedicated to
myself (Jeff Collins), Dan Stearns, and Ken Rubenstein. All three of us are
into microtuning. Dan is probably the most into the new world, having up to
five different tuning systems in one piece. Making for some music that needs
to be heard with certain restrictions to get the full effect of his music.
Ken does music in Quarter tones and just intonation systems. And I mostly
work with bending of an already unnatural sounds (which sounds like Musique
Concrete, but actually comes from a single prepared guitar).

I do hope this can interest any of you into checking out our worlds of
sound at...
http://www.geocities.com/SunsetStrip/Stadium/1429/STRANGETONES.html

Hope you can find something that catches your ears.
