For those of you not closely reading the MMM (Making Microtonal Music) list these days,

I threw out the idea of having a MMM day sometime in April, where we would all make an
effort to do exactly that. The event is, of course, what you make of it. There is the idea of
collecting many recordings of people's doings and mixing them into one giganto-podcast
by the end of it. Of course, it is not limited to the internet, what better excuse to spread
the microtonal gospel (you know, the one thing everybody here agrees on? right? oh,
forget it) in a more forward way.

Anyhow, if you're interested, you have a free hour to record some music between now and
next Saturday (April 8th), and you'd like to contribute to the podcast bit, do visit http://
xenharmony.wikispaces.org/mmmday06 and add your name to the list.

Thanks fer hearin' me out,
