If we take a septimal schismic tuning of the fifth of 11264^(1/23), or
702.231 cents, and tune 23 sucessive fifths, we get a wolf of exactly
16/11. The fifth is poptimal, and between the 94 and 41 fifth; it is
quite close to the 270 fifth, which among its other talents can do an
excellent version of 7 or 11 limit schismic despite the fact that the
schisma itself is one step of 270-et.
