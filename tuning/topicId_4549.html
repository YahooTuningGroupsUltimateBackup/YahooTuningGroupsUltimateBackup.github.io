Fokker published a book in English in 1949 called _Just Intonation
and the Combination of Harmonic Diatonic Melodic Groups_.
I wrote a pretty thorough synopsis of it in a Tuning List posting
back around February or March: check the Archives.

I've been MIDI-sequencing the musical examples in the book,
to eventually appear on a webpage on my site. Here are three
of the 4-part examples composed by Jan van Dijk:

http://www.ixpres.com/interval/fokker/59-c.mid
http://www.ixpres.com/interval/fokker/60-61-d.mid
http://www.ixpres.com/interval/fokker/77-t.mid
