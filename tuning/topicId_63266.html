Hi all,

I normally host a classical music program on KGNU the
fourth Sunday morning of each month (9-11am Mountain
time), but this month I swapped shifts and will host
the Present Edge show (contemporary classical music).

excerpts from several of the CDs he released in 2005
on Sunday, January 8 from 1-3 pm Mountain Time. The
interview is very introductory, but this is also a
chance to hear the music he and his ensemble play.

KGNU is at 88.5 FM in Boulder Colorado, 1390 AM in
Denver, or on the web at www.kgnu.org. Hope you can
tune in to hear us!
