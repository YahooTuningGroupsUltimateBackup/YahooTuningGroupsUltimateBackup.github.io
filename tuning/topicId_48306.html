spurred on by the work i did on Dave Keenan's list of "kommas"
(on the tuning-math list), i've made a huge list of all the
11-limit intervals where the <3, 5, 7, 11>-monzo exponents
are <-15...+15, -10...+10, -4...+4, -2...+2>, arranged in
order by cents.

it takes quite a while to download ... there are over
28,000 intervals in the list. i hope it will be very
useful as a reference.
