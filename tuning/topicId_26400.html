This is a *really* good point about static/dynamic!, in light of
the discussion here regarding time as an element of music.

I sent a post this afternoon discussing the fact that harmony
itself, at least when considered as ratios, is also time-dependent.

I'd also like JustMusic to be able to analyze harmonic timbres
on lattice diagrams. And hey, why not rhythms or meters too?

A lattice can be useful for understanding *any* type of rational
or proportional relationship, and thus for examing any type of
periodicity that can be found in music. And periodicity plays
a role in a *lot* of different aspects of music! Look in my
Dictionary for some examples:
