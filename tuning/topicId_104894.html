This actually sounds very slighty weird and un-barbershop-like because everything is in such precise JI and there's no vibrato or anything. Even when a really good barbershop quartet is ringing a chord and perfectly locked in, there's still some kind of modulation, at least on a multiple-second time scale - if not from slight mistuning, then from vibrato. So this sounds slightly alien or mechanical or whatever.

Still awesome though.

I ought to have left the vibrato a little wider. It was a quick recording relatively speaking. The mechanical type sound is certainly more due to the artifacts of the tuning adjustment than only to the tuning itself.
