The old timers already know this, but for any new guys/occasional
posters, I wanted to mention that I have 3 CD's of Microtonal guitar
music available..."The Gate" is very hard rock, and features 12 tone, 19
tone, and one fretless piece; "Acoustic Stick" is 19 and 34 tone
acoustic guitar music, and features John Starrett on bass, and Ernie
Crews on percussion; and, my latest, "Other Worlds," is electronic space
music in 19, 34, and 12 tone eq; there's also one fretless piece, which
has Starrett on Starrboard, a fascinating tapping instrument of his own
invention. They are $15, postage paid, from Box 150271 Lakewood, CO
80215. I also have a 19 tone music theory book available for
$10....Hstick
