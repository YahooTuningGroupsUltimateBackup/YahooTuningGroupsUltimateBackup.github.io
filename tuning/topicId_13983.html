Hello, there, and here is a corrected version of a diagram in my
article remedying two errors regretfully found after posting. These
errors involve weighting factors for intermediate ratios between 5:4
and 9:7.

In this diagram, the y/x ratio for 19:15 should be 1/2, not 2/3; and
for 33:26 it should be 2/3, not 3/4 -- the latter of which would give
(4*5+3*9):(4*4+3*7) or 47:37, ~414.163 cents, a point on the continuum
a bit on the other side of 29-tET.

The emended diagram follows along with the introductory paragraph from
the original post, and with an added point for x/y=3/4 as 47:37: since
I got it wrong the first time, I might as well include it and get it
right this time.
