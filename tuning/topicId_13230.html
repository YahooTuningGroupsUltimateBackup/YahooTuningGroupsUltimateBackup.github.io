Just taking a break between my 22-tET
session with Ara and my funk jam . . . anyway,
we just noticed that some of the decatonic
key signatures in my 22-tET paper (the last
page) are wrong. In particular, the key
names for the 2nd, 4th, 6th, 8th, and 10th
signatures in the left column should be
switched with the key names for the 2nd, 4th,
6th, 8th, and 10th signatures in the right
column, respectively. Since no one's noticed
this before, I take it no one is likely to get
confused by it soon . . . but I'll be sure to get
a note into the Xenharmonikon Errata page,
and hopefully Carl will help me get a
corrected .pdf or .html version of the paper
up on Starrett's site.
