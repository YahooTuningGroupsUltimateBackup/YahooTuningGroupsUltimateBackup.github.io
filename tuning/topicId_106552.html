Hello friends,
I am glad to inform you that the Semantic Daniélou website is open, in both French and English :
http://www.semantic-danielou.com/ http://www.semantic-danielou.com/
from where you can download, for free, the Semantic virtual instrument in the tuning system originally devised by Alain Danielou, along with its french and english manuals, as well as the scala files of its 72 tunings.
Enjoy and have fun composing Just Intonation music in indian ragas and other microtonal semantic compatible scales up to 53 notes per octave. - -
Semantic Microtonal Music International Competition -
Know also that AEH (Atelier d'exploration harmonique) and FIND (India-Europe Foundation for new dialogues) invites you to a microtonal music challenge for Semantic Danielou-53 users, ending on the 31th of january 2018.
Selected pieces will be presented in various places in France and Italy, and broadcasted in the Semantic pages of the Alain Danielou website (full rules and prices details in the same FIND pages).
