This is very funny so, unfortunately, I had to post it.

Jeff Scott noticed the little line I generally write under my name
and wrote this to me privately:

>Anyway, after seeing the following many
times:
_________ ____ _____ ___
Joseph Pehrson

I counted and found:

123456789 1234 12345 123
_________ ____ _____ ___

And I thought -- "I wonder if Joseph is sending
us a secret message that he is using some tuning
like: 9/4,9/5,9/3,4/5,4/3,5/3 ?"

>I felt sure you would confirm my suspicions and
congratulate me for being the first to notice
ever since you started doing that 19 odd years ago.

>But since your response I noticed that you don't always
use the same pattern. :<

That's pretty funny, Jeff. I guess I started using that little
"dividing line" to separate my "signature" after I first started on
the list... It didn't mean much, but I guess I WAS thinking a little
of string lengths and proportions for sound, and that's about all it
meant... Glad to see you have found more significance in it.

Well, back to tuning...
