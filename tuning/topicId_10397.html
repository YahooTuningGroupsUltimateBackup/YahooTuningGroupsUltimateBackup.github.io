I'd like to announce the opening of a new site and CD at
www.elyrialights.com.
The music is all in 22 tone equal temperament, and stylistically is a blend
of country, electronic, and rock. You can hear for yourself; the site has
a lot of mpegs from the cd, from live concerts, and from b side recordings.
So please visit it. It's the fist time I've been on major booksellers'
lists. Although I've sold a lot locally, I would like to also reach
communities with similar interests, especially the tuning community.

Pardon me if the site is still a little under construction in the "redlight
band" section. It won't be under construction for long. I will probably
also be posting more theoretical information on how I used 22 tone equal
temperament, as time passes.
