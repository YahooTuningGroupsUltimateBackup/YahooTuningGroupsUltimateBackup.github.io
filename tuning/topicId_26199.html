P. S.:
Calculation of beats for those who like to check:
*
(Beats of tempered intervals occur between nearly co-inciding harmonic
overtones).

Let within the triad be
f0 be the fundamental,
fT be the frequency of the third ang
fF the fifths frequency.

The beat frequency of the fifth will the be :
f0*3-fF*2

The beat frequency of the third will the be :
f0*5-fT*4

The crucial ptoportion of beat-ratio - absolute value, of course - in the
B-major triad,
between (B-d#) and (B-f#) is 6 : 1. Approximately, but by far sufficient to
assure accurate tempering. Proof:

Kellner, H.A.: Das ungleichstufige, wohltemperierte Tonsystem. In
"Bach-stunden", Festschrift fï¿½r Helmut Walcha, Hg. W. Dehnhard
