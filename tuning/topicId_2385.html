YP Circular
YP Circular, a Mac program by Yves Pelletier, simulates an object in
uniform circular motion and/or an object in simple harmonic motion.
Both motions (circular and harmonic) may be animated simultaneously
to show that the uniform circular motion is a combination of two simple
harmonic motions. Be sure to look at Yves' many other programs.
<http://www.kagi.com/pelletier/circular.html>
