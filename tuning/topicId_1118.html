I agree about the augmented triads, but (being a 31-toner) I kind of
like the way full diminished 7ths come out in 31TET, with the "augmented
second" going to 7:6 and the tritones going to 7:5s or 10:7s.

> Other problem chords, such as those
> with four or more notes forming consecutive fifths, may also be tuned in
> 12-tone, in which fifths aren't too bad, and thirds are at least
> preferable to those generated in 3-limit tuning!

I think most people would prefer those in something meantonish, in the
1/4 or 1/5 comma range, though IIRC (and I'm not positive that I do)
Paul Erlich has advocated something closer to 2/7 comma. I lean towards
1/4 myself--again, me being a 31-toner, that's probably not surprising.
