2. >Tuning Dump for what? Please expound.

>DBurgess

The midi tuning dump is the protocol used to map, store and transfer
individual note tunings between instruments
e. g. Proteus etc. Yet as far as I know, no existing midi instruments
will play the tunings any closer than 1/64th of a semitone. The
resolution of the tuning dump is specified to the nearest 1/16384th of a
semitone.
The accuracy of the tuning is particularly significant to generate more
precise beat frequencies between simultaneously sounded notes.

see http://lehua.ilhawaii.net/~lucy/lsd/pitchbend.html
