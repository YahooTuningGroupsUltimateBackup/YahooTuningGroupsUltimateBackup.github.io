Hi tuning people: if anyone's interested, BBC radio 3 aired quite a good programme on Vicentino this morning. They played some of my retuned recordings of the pieces from his treatise, and they also played live recordings of a group of singers that the BBC put together especially for the programme, who did very well with the 1/5th-tone shifts after just two days' practice.

I don't know if this link works everywhere in the world, but here in Canada it says the programme will be available online for the next 7 days:

http://www.bbc.co.uk/iplayer/console/b00rptxb
