Jacky,

Glad to be a help. If you play clearly, the tracking is not that bad.
Sometimes it can be kind
of funky, when you set it to play any microtonal note, but it's minimal
as long as you adjust
to it's feel.

It should play your micro fretted notes fine because it's tracking any
note within a 100
cents. There should be no slide from note to note. When I play within a
half step or a non et
interval, there's no sliding unless I purposely slide. This is with a
fretless and I assume it
should treat your guitar the same, since it treats bends on ET guitars
to non bended notes
without a slide. You could always double check by playing it at a store.

Good luck.

Al
