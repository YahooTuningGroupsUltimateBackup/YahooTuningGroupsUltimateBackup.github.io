Yes, the title of the book has changed, and apparently I got a listing
under the old title. The correct title is _The Csound Book_. The full
contents and text of some chapters is available at:

http://mitpress.mit.edu/e-books/csound/fpage/pub/csbook/csbook.html

My chapter (editorially retitled, unfortunately, to fit into the MIDI
section) is on the CD-ROM, as is the complete score and orchestra of a
freely modulating JI piece of mine, Hiway 70. More information about Csound
is now at:

http://www.csound.org/

>I'm probably not alone in being very interested in CSound but anything
>that would help get me over the learning curve could be the thing to
>make me take the plunge. ("curve? plunge?...")

The CD-ROMs that come with the book are helpful in that regard in that it
has hundreds of ready-made instruments. Many more along with their sounds
are available on the Csound Catalog CD-ROM (available separately).
