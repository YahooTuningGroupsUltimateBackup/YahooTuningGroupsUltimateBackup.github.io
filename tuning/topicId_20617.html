MicroFest registrants:

Some of the participants in MicroFest have asked me if it would be
permissible to bring along some CDs, books, or other items to sell at the
conference. Insofaras this is a opportunity for attendees to purchase items
that might otherwise be difficult to find, I have decided to dedicate a
table near the conference registration desk for a small display of CDs and
other items for sale. When a student assistant is working at the
registration desk, she can take care of transactions, which will later be
settled with the owner. When there is no one there, we will probably have
to pack away those items for security's sake. Since I have told some people
that this is OK, I feel that I should open the opportunity to all
participants, but keep in mind that space is limited and the student
workers will at times have other priorities.

Thanks, and I look forward to seeing you here!

Bill
