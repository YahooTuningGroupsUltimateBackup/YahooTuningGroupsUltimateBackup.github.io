There was an error that no one caught (I was surprised :) ) in a previous
post: Fb would be lower than an E in Chromatic Pythagorean. The correct
ascending order from A would be:

A Bb A# Cb B C B# Db C# D Eb D# Fb E F

E# Gb F# G Ab G#
