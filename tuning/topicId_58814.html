every memorial day for the last couple years I've posted a link to At a
Day Job:

meowing.memh.uc.edu/~chris/micromp3s/suite.html

At a Day Job is made up of various American patriotic tunes the most
notable of which are America the Beautiful and Battle Hymn of the
Republic. At a Day Job also uses a 22-tone set that is built on two
overtone series, 10 - 20 and 14 - 28.
