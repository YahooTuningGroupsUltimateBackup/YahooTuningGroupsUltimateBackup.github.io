My Tuning Dictionary is at:
www.ixpres.com/interval/dict/index.html

All words that are in the dictionary are hyperlinks wherever
they appear, so you can just roam around in circles until
you understand it all!

Seriously, the dictionary is still under development, and
there's a lot that I still need to add to it. (Chiefly, the
rest of the glossary from John Chalmers's _Divisions of
the Tetrachord_, which he graciously allowed me to incorporate.
I've only gotten as far as 'H', and have not included any
of the separate 'Glossary of Greek Terms' yet.)

I've been very busy with other projects that I need to finish,
and have had to put dictionary entries on hold for a while.
But there's a lot in there already. Hope it helps.

We've just in the past few days discussed creating a FAQ
for the Onelist Tuning Digest Archive webpage. Paul Erlich
and myself are both interested in doing it, but neither of
us has the time right now. Any other volunteers?
