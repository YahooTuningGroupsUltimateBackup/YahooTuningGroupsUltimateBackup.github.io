AK-1 ORIENTAL SCALE CONVERTER is a MIDI device that converts wide range
of synthesizers to a specific Scale Tuning. It can adjust the pitch of
each note in the octave (C to B) to -50 cents.
AK-1 Key Benefits
* AK-1 can be connected simultaneously to two synthesizers via the
two MIDI_OUTS * AK-1 works on most MIDI synthesizers i.e Yamaha ,
Roland, Korg, Ketron, and Kurzweil * AK-1 chip is upgradeable
