An Equal Temperament Tuning System Is NOT Actually...

Bill Flavell <bill_flavell@email.com>

12/8/2005 10:51:44 AM

...a specific tuning system, but a generalized
matrix within which can be inscribed both symmetrical
and asymmetrical subscales/tunings.

For instance, each transposition level at which an
inversion is played results in a possibly different
superscale tonality when applied to 12TET asymmetrical
subscales.
