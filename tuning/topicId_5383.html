For those who are interested,

I'm currently working on the 4th edition of my _JustMusic_
book (the previous three have been small self-printed
affairs; this one probably will be too).

I've made the text of the 3rd edition available online
on my website in a zip file (291 K) in Microsoft Word format
(212 pages):
http://www.ixpres.com/interval/monzo/book/JustMusic3ed.zip

This is the text only, with no musical illustrations and
no lattice diagrams of the type I make now. It does have
my more primitive (< 1998) rectangular lattice diagrams,
which I was calling matrix diagrams at the time, for 3-
and 5-limit systems which make up the bulk of this edition.
