I have just added this paper
http://anaphoria.com/secondaryrecurrents.html
to this page http://anaphoria.com/journal.html

The chart has a variety of uses.
!. It shows what recurrent sequences coincides with others in converging on the same ratio
2. It shows recurrent sequences that are close. This provides potential resources in using as secondary chords that might have certain musically interesting possibilities.
3. It shows spacings of triads that might be useful within a system that are not intended in it conception.

Already the Meta Slendro tuning i gain insights. I was aware of two such proportional triads which now i know of two more. This is also true of my Copan scale where two more recurrent sequences were found bring 4 possibilities there.
Also with Meta Slendro there are also recurrent sequences that converge on the same interval plus an octave.
