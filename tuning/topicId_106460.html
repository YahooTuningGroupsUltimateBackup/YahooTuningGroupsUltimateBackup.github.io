> And what about the 13-limit zeus extensions? Xenharmonic Wiki lists tridecimal zeus (121/120, 176/175, 351/350) and tinia (66/65, 121/120, 176/175), while the temperament finder says 13-limit zeus is the one tempering out 66/65, 121/120, 176/175.

> 2) According to the Xenharmonic Wiki, 11-limit gorgo tempers out 36/35, 56/55, 1029/1024, while the temperament finder calls it spartan, and claims gorgo is the one tempering out 36/35, 45/44, 1323/1280.

This is an inconsistency in the wiki. 11-limit gorgo comes from the "proposed names for rank 2 temperaments" page.

> 3) Xenharmonic Wiki lists the 7-limit temperament that tempers out 81/80, 1029/1024 as mothra, while the temperament finder calls it cynder.

This is on of a list of collisions that I ignore. There are good reasons for cynder being valid that I can't remember now. It's a case where there's more than one valid name for a particular temperament class in a particular prime limit.
