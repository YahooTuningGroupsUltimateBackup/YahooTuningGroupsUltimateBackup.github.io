Actually Gene,

Now that I think of it, the Kronos Quartet did a recording of Terry Riley's "Salome Dances for Peace". The whole work fits on two CD's, the first being about 71 minutes long, encompassing the first two sections of the whole work. The second CD is about 48 minutes, and it is the last three sections. At the same time, I don't know how much "leaway" the performers are given in the performance, but the liner notes say this work is "three times longer than any of Beethoven's string quartets."

The CD came out in 1989.
