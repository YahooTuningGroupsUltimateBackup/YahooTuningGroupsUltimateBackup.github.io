I swear I posted this before, but I can't find it now. Either it didn't go
through, or I'm totally blind, but I'll post it again.

Check it out, from XH2 - 1974

http://launch.groups.yahoo.com/group/tuning/files/MikeBattaglia/ChalmersLinearCompressed.pdf

The three linear temperaments mentioned, if all the higher-limit extensions
are taken into account, correspond to

"Negative System":
http://x31eq.com/cgi-bin/rt.cgi?ets=19d_31&limit=13(13-limit Meantone)
"Positive System":
http://x31eq.com/cgi-bin/rt.cgi?ets=29_41&limit=13(13-limit Cassandra)
"Doubly Positive System" #1:
http://x31eq.com/cgi-bin/rt.cgi?ets=22p_27p&limit=13 (13-limit Suprapyth +
91/90)
"Doubly Positive System" #2:
http://x31eq.com/cgi-bin/rt.cgi?ets=22p_27e&limit=13 (13-limit Superpyth)

Note also that different least-squares tunings are given, corresponding to
different subgroups of the above temperaments, including things like 2.3.7
and 2.5.7 and so on.

-Mike
