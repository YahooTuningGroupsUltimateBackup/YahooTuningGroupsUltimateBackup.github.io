Because I use RI (= "rational intonation") for that,
and it's been adopted by several others here too.

... because it is possible to construct a tuning from ratios
which does not exhibit the audible characteristics of JI.

The Hammond Organ is an excellent example of this: a
rational tuning which is audibly indistinguishable from 12-EDO.

JI is a subset of RI.
