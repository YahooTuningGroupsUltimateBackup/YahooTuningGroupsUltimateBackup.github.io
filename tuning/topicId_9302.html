As I mentioned recently, my new disc "Other Worlds" is ready to
go...I had a couple more comments on the music I wanted to post. This CD
is all electronic guitar/synth music, and concerns my feelings about
Outer Space, and the potentials which may exist out there, somewhere. I
have always been fascinated by science fiction books/films...Ray
Bradbury's "Martian Chronicles," Robert Heinlein, Frank Herbert, and
George Adamski (anybody remember him? Pretty whacked); Forbidden Planet,
2001, and many "B" movies of the 1950's and '60's, plus great TV shows
such as Outer Limits and Twilight Zone. These influenced me greatly as a
kid, and stimulated my imagination in many ways...this music is my
tribute to those early influences. This disc is much more concerned with
images and landscapes then it is with "playing" the guitar, per se. I am
well pleased with the way it turned out...for more info on my 1st 2
CD's, please go to www.virtualchautauqua.org/haverstick. My address is
Box 150271 Lakewood Colo 80215..the discs go for $15, postage paid. (and
this will be my last ad...just want to make sure the tuning folks know
