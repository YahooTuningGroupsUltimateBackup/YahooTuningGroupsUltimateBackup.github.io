Thank you, I see you've been busy. Sorry about the sleepless nights. It looks like an interesting set of intervals. I count 20, but you can delete F# as the tritone (semioctave) shouldn't exist in 19-tet or any approximation to it, IMO. Alas, I don't have time at the moment to tune it up in Scala or Csound and listen to it.

An approach I would take is too keep the diatonic set pretty much the same, but split each of the chromatic semitones into two intervals and the diatonic semitones E-F and B-C. The goal is to create a tuning that sounds like 19-tet, but hopefully has better fifths and major thirds without damaging the excellent minor thirds.

--John
