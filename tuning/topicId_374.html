I have just uploaded this,
http://groups.yahoo.com/group/crazy_music/files/sillybass.mp3
emboldened by the recent live acoustic uploads. This is a free pitch
bass solo from a gig at the Boulderado in 1988. I have been thinking
about the free pitch approach over the last couple of days, and it
seems to me one can make interesting music without a fixed pitch set.
I bet Hendrix fans can sing the talking guitar outro to Rainy Day
Dreamaway, even though they would be hard pressed to write it out
without some thought.
