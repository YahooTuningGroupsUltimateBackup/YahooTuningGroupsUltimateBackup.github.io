7: 12/7 933.129 septimal major sixth
8: 120/67 1008.962

ado.mp3> :

1: 8/7 231.174 septimal whole tone

3: 10/7 617.488 Euler's tritone
4: 11/7 782.492 undecimal augmented fifth
5: 12/7 933.129 septimal major sixth
6: 13/7 1071.702 16/3-tone
7: 2/1 1200.000 octave
