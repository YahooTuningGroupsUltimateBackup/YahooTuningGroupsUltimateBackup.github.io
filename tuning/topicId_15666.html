For those who are in the area: I'll do a concert with the French guitarist
Caroline Delume on Thursday 23 November 7 pm in the Instituto Cervantes, 7,
rue Quentin Bauchart, 75008 Paris. The program is as follows:

Philippe Leroux - "Hommage à Wyschnegradsky" for two guitars, 24-tet
scordatura

Thierry Blondeau - "Non-lieu" for guitar, Just Intonation (harmonics only,
13-limit)

Joseba Torre - "Argia Eta Itzala" for guitar, 24-tet scordatura

Félix Ibarrondo - "Pakeruntz" for two guitars, 12-tet

Pascale Criton - "La Ritournelle et le Galop" for quartertone fretted
guitar, 96-tet scordatura

Francisco Luque - "Ma Martulî" for guitar, 24-tet scordatura

Francisco Luque - "L'Oiseau Rare" for two guitars, 24-tet scordatura

Admission is free.
