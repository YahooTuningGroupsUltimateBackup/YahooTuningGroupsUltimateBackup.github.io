Just wanted to thank Johnny Reinhard again for choosing to broadcast
one of the pieces that Ara Sarkissian and I composed and performed at
the Microthon. It wasn't the piece that my mother wanted to hear, or
the one most favorably received by the audience, plus I completely
botched an important part of the melody, but perhaps it made for
better programming (notice how it was in the same "key" as Marc
Jones' 32-tET piece that immediately followed it). In any case, I
just wanted to say I appreciate Johnny overlooking our many
differences of opinion on this list and finding my music of enough
interest to be broadcast.

P.S. Ara did co-compose the piece -- actually, it was more of a co-
improvisation on a co-composed set of chord changes -- so perhaps he
feels slighted that you named only me as the composer . . . or
perhaps he doesn't care . . . I think he should at least be given
acknowledgement in the future, though.
