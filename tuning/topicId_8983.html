IMO it has to do with the threads (usually
silk) that are placed at the region of
contact between the string and the bridge.

That and the sympathetic strings.

I can make my veena sound suspiciously like a
sitar by making those two changes. In
fact a Karnataka state-based veena player
added sympathetic strings to his veena to lend
it a sitaresque tone.
