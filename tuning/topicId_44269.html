I've been listening to a lot of circulating temperaments, and grail
continues to knock my socks off. However, we have two fifths 1/2 comma
flat in grail and two fifths 1/2 comma sharp in rms grail, so I
decided to take a linear combination of the two and equalize the
error. This is achieved by taking 4 parts of grail and five parts of
rms grail, or 44.1348% grail to be more exact than Jon would prefer.
This should probably be considered the canonical version of grail, the
"bad" fifths are nine cents sharp or flat, which is
tolerable--certainly a long way from wolfdom.
