Weekly Tuning Community Honor Awards
.. last Sunday of every week! ..

This week's winner:

Jon Szanto, for the MakeMicroMusic mailing list, which has
immeasurably furthered the cause of practical microtonal
music in the World today.

I personally have been introduced to dozens of artists
through the MMM list. There have been times on the list,
as I was remarking to a friend just yesterday, when it
was actually difficult to keep up with the onslaught of
music!

Make no mistake: microtonal tunings are rapidly gaining
in popularity. And Jon's list has played a big part in
this.

Stay Tuned for next week's installment, and Thanks again, Jon!
