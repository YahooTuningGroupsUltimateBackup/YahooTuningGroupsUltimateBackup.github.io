At around 1:00 AM PST this morning, I decided to try Proxomitron 4.0. It (appeared) to block all of the (in-message) Yahoo Ads (not sure now, since I did not subsequently disable the Proxomitron to retest without it). I figured - what are the chances that the (in-message) ads "just disappeared? Confident that while "Murphy" (of "Murphy's Law) has often plauged me (it seems), it was just *too* unlikely - so I didn't verify it ... Duh.

A couple hours hours later I noticed that Proxo 4 was "also" nixing all other Yahoo ads (something which I *know* to be true), so I made a second post to that effect.

After reading Joe Pehrson's post I disabled Proxo 4, and (of course) the (in-message) ads were not there to be foiled...!

The good news is that they have (for now) pulled their (in- message) ads (it seems). More good news is that Proxo 4 is blocking all other Yahoo ads, and if the (in-message) ads are reinstated, it *MAY* be that they, too, *will* be successfully blocked by Proxo 4, as well. Wish that I had been able to try Proxo 4 at a time prior to the (unannounced) rescinding of the (in-message) ads... Now I'm just not certain whether it would, and (once again) humbled by "Murphy" for "talkin" prior to "checkin".
