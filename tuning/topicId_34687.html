Thanks, Paul. I thought you might enjoy these tracks. I'm trying to get
permission from the producers to re-issue the complete "audiophile"
recording as a CD. It was the first of two LA Jazz Choir recordings
nominated for a Grammy.

I've found that singing to a piano accompaniment doesn't "get in the way"
that much as long as it doesn't play the same exact thing the choir is
singing. I instruct my pianists to comp only when we're singing lines and
only play lines while we're singing chords. The decay of the piano or guitar
sounds minimizes the conflict, I think.

I agree that this may not be a good source of "research data" but I wanted
to give you a taste of where my ears are coming from. I'm still working on
the CD transfer. If I come on any sustained a cappella chords that sound
"interesting" I'll zap them to you.
