Explore the Scala file archive with ScalaVista, free for Mac and Windows.

- includes Scala file archive (by permission from Manuel Op de Coul)
- listen to any scale by pitches or intervals
- sort scales by name, description, scale tones, or comments
- edit existing scales and create new scales
- keep your archive in sync with the online archive

DETAILS: <http://www.h-pi.com/ScalaVistasoftware.html>
DOWNLOAD: <http://www.h-pi.com/downloads.html>

Cheers,
Aaron
