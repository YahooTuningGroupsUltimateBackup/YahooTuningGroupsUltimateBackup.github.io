Hello Mohajeri Shahin!
Since the great writings of Persian Theory as well as it practice has bee in using ratios except for an exception or two, there is no reason not to keep to a Just Intonational system that allows for constant revision and adaptability.
To propose any one system to this great tradition would do nothing more than reduce its diversity into a homogeneous mess that when working against any type of drone, would sound incorrect. This tradition is also based on tetrachoral scales and to have a meantone second would be completely out of the language being proposed.
