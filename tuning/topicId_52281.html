They're great. When I saw them they gave a workshop on overtone singing
afterwards, and under their guidance I was able to make some neat sounds
(this one called something like "kargeel" where you get your chest under
your sternum to vibrate kinda like a kazoo. it was wild). Unfortunately, I
haven't been able to reproduce it since them (years ago this was). I even
jammed with them on guitar a bit. A nice bunch of fellows as well as cool
singers & players.
