Book Review: The Secret Power of Music - David Tame, ISBN 0-89281-056-4
Destiny Books, Rochester, Vermont, http:/www.gotoit.com

I haven't read it all yet but have had a good browse. This is an
amazing book that deals with many aspects of music that are not
generally dealt with including music from various cultures, effects
related to chakras etc, ancient and modern music, and the hidden power
of music. Highly recommended, especially to anyone that wants to deeply
understand *how* music affects people.
