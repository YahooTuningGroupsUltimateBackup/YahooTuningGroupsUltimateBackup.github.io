Tuning Box TBX1 default tunings are
divided into 3 categories of 32 tunings each:

(1) World Tunings
(2) Just Intonation
(3) Equal Divisions

TBX1 imports scala files.
Tunings can include any number of pitches,
expressed as ratios or cents as in the scala format.

See the Tuning Box here:
http://www.h-pi.com/TBX1intro.html

Please submit your votes for:

(1) World Tunings

When voting, please either give the scala filename
or include the tuning in the vote message.
