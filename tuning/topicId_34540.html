in case anyone needs some help in following that ...

2^(190/323) = 2^(10/17) = ~705.8823529 cents

2^(189/323) = ~702.1671827 cents

2^(188/323) = ~698.4520124 cents

2^(187/323) = 2^(11/19) = ~694.7368421 cents
