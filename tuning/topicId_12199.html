Joe Pass was a phenomenal musician who transcended his instrument to
create music of the highest level...he's one of my real heroes, and I
believe what he did will last the ages. Although his playing was
extremely deep emotionally, and complex intellectually, he still had
this to say ( in the August issue of GP mag)..."Keep things simple.
Don't fill your head with theory and numbers that have little to do with
music." I agree...Hstick
