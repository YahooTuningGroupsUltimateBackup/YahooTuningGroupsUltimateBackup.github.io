hi,

i'm no expert myself but you might want to check out:
http://kevindumpscore.com/docs/csound-manual/cent.html

i generally score using frequencies and have not used
this myself.

something else that may be of interest... BLUE by
Steven Yi (you can find it from the CSound page).
it is a multrack front end (and more) to CSound
written in Java and cross platform.
just a week or two ago he added the ability to use
SCALA files by applying a Scala scale to an
instrument's score.
