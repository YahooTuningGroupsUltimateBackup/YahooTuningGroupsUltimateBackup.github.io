- 480 => 10TET & 33/25

- 745.8 = 20/13
- 770 = 10TET close to Tibetan interval of 773
- 840 = 10TET, close to 13/8
- 897.5 => meantone of 22/13 & 19TET's 884.2

- 1080 = 10TET close to Tibetan 1086
- 1109.8 = 243/128
- 1231.8 = 8ve + 56/55 comma
