To those interested in Bohlen-Pierce, I'd like to point out an interesting development. Stephen Fox is a Canadian clarinet builder who's currently developing BP-clarinets in three different sizes. What instrument could better represent the acoustics of this particular scale?
For more information, you may want to visit http://www.sfoxclarinets.com/BP_sale.htm. There's also a trio of Canadian clarinetist that will focus on a program with BP pieces, once the instruments are finished.
