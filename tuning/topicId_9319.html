15-limit; these would simply be the 3-member subsets of the 8 otonal octads

subsets of the octads, as well as the "anomalous" ones: 12:14:18:21,
22:24:33:36, 24:26:36:39, 12:14:15:21, and 20:28:30:35. Do all of those
occur in the diamond? Find them.
