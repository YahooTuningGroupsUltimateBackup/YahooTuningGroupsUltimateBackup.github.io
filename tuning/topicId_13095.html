In my opinion Cool Edit is the finest general purpose audio processing program for PC. It will set you back about $70 for the low end version (the one I use) and $400 for the full studio version (essentially a complete multitrack recording studio for you computer). The $70 version has a time limited demo that may be able to do what you want. In any case get to http://www.syntrillium.com, and for $70 you will be able to turn your
mp3 files into wav files.
Alternatively, go to www.sonicspot.com for a complete listing (and downloads) of audio software, many reviewed.
