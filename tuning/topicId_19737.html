I think we're already ready for our FAQ "content providers" to step
forward.

Could everyone who has already written a FAQ please save it to the
FILES directory here at the Tuning List?

It can be written in a regular wordprocessor and saved as a .txt
file.

You can upload that, or you can even write the text right on screen,
if you want to, in the FILES section. Be careful, though, that the
filename of your entry has no spaces!

Directories can easily be created in the FILES area, just like any
computer file management process.

You don't even have to know anything about the DATABASE, where the
FAQ tree will eventually be organized.

In fact, you don't even have to know how to save your file to the
FILES area. If anybody is having problems, simply post your FAQ
again to the Tuning List with the instructions that you want somebody
to post it in the FILES area for you.

Somebody will, then, just copy the text and save it in the FILES area
for you...

Let's let the "FAQs" begin!!
