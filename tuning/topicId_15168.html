I just wanted to express my appreciation for all the amazing
multimedia moments on the Tuning List in the last couple of months or
so...

Some of my favorite highlights:

1) Joe Monzo's all night extravaganza where he calculated the
orbitary motion of the solar system planets and turned out with one
big glorious extended major chord!

2) Paul Erlich's amazing green pizzas for Harmonic Entropy (well this
is partially on the HE list) with sound files by David Finnamore.
What a long strange trip that is!

3) The recent rotating geometrical solids created by Dave Keenan and
Paul Erlich, with perhaps the most incredible SOUNDING VMRLs by
Robert Walker. I hope there is more of this to come.

3) And, suddenly, even sound enhancements of Margo Schulter's work by
Robert Walker!

You know, it occurs to me that we are participating in an amazing
"real time" art event here. It's a little like a INTERACTIVE museum
unfolding!

It seems like the list is presently more "multimedia" than ever!
