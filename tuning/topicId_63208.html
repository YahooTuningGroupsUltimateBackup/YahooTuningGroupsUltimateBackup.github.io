Is music theory a "zero-sum" activity or not?

synthesis/sampling (on the tuning accuracy front/axis/plane)

total re-evaluation of every aspect of music AND music theory.

And contrary to the tabula-rosa
fantasies of the 1950s
serialists, THIS IS THE REAL DEAL! :)
