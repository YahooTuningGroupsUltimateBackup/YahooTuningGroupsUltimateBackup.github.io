Hi,

I have a recording of Barry Anderson's music, which includes a ring
modulator effect which was new to me. I've heard plenty of stuff against
a single oscillator, but here in "Piano Piece no.2" (I think), Anderson
notated two piano parts - one played on a mic'ed grand and the other on
an electronic keyboard, both fed through either channel of a ring
modulator.

A short biography and catalog extract of Anderson's work is here:
<http://mac-texier.ircam.fr/textes/c00000166/index.html>
