Hello everyone.

Maybe someone of you here could help me. I'm trying to find two recordings
of the same piece of music, one played in 12-EDO and the other one in some
kind of meantone -- something in the range from 1/4 to 2/7-comma would be
just what I'm looking for. The matter is simply that a friend of mine has
asked me to demonstrate the differences between 12-EDO and something closer
to JI. The reason why I'd like to have recordings of acoustic instruments
playing this is that if I do this on my synth, some people may immediately
turn their attention to the fact that there are "not-too-high-quality
samples" being used there instead of listening to the intervals and chords.
I was also trying to find some kind of hardware sampler with more RAM that
could serve better but these are often unexpectedly difficult to tune into
something else than 12-EDO. That's why I finally went for the idea of using
recordings of whole pieces played on acoustic instruments. Any suggestions
for these?

Thanks a lot in advance.

Petr

Hi Petr,

This doesn't exactly meet your needs, necessarily, but it may be helpful: I wrote a little piece last year called "Sunday Afternoon", which I composed in 12-EDO, and invited people to render it in other tunings. Here's the page:

http://mysterybear.net/article/20/sunday-afternoon

There's MIDI for the original version, along with versions by JL Smith (JI), Gene Ward Smith (31-EDO) and Charles Lucy (LucyTuning). These are all rendered in software, but they do provide an opportunity to compare different tunings as applied to the same piece of music.

- Dave
