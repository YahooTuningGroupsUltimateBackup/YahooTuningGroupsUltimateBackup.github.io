Comma pumps are characteristic chord progressions for codimension-1
temperaments. Has anyone thought about characteristic chord
progressions for codimension-2 temperaments?

Obviously you could just pump through each comma separately, but
there's probably a better way to do it that treats the entire
multimonzo being tempered out as a cohesive whole, and hence creates
the illusion of the harmony being as tight-knit and densely packed
together as possible.
