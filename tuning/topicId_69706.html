You are using negative frequencies. What does that mean?

Also, you are creating quantities with the dimensions of frequency
squared. That is, if anything, even more meaningless. Frequency has
dimension 1/time, and you can't meaningfully compare two quantities
with different dimensions.

> Is there some standard musical, math explanation for this?

If you play with enough apparently random numbers, you will obtain
some apparent coincidences.

Why did you choose those particular values to start with?

Why don't you choose different starting values, then see what happens?

Better still, use algebra!
