As always when undertaking a big trip, I would like to
meet with as many microtonalists and Mahler/Schoenberg fans
as possible.

So I'm posting my itinerary here. If anyone who will be in
these cities and towns on these dates would be interested in
getting together, please contact me at this email address.

If enough people in a certain area feel merit in a public
response, for the possibility of a larger gathering, I suppose
it's OK to post it here. Note that my time in Bruges, Brussels,
Rome, Florence, Venice and Zoetermeer is extremely limited:
perhaps enough for a coffee or beer together. During my week
in Perugia, I'll be attending the conference during daytime hours
and will be free in the evenings.

Tu 09-04 leave San Diego, fly to Paris
W 09-05 arrive Paris AM, overnight Paris
Th 09-06 overnight Paris
F 09-07 leave Paris AM, train to Brussels then Bruges, overnight Bruges
Sat 09-08 leave Bruges AM, train to Charleroi, fly to Pisa,
train to Rome, overnight Rome
Sun 09-09 leave Rome PM, train to Perugia, overnight Perugia
M 09-10 Perugia: ISMA conference
Tu 09-11 Perugia: ISMA conference - my presentation at 9:20
W 09-12 Perugia: ISMA conference
Th 09-13 Perugia: ISMA conference
F 09-14 Perugia: ISMA conference
Sat 09-15 leave Perugia AM, train to Florence, overnight Florence
Sun 09-16 leave Florence AM, train to Venice, overnight Venice
M 09-17 leave Venice AM, fly to Charleroi, train to Brussels
then Amsterdam, overnight Amsterdam
Tu 09-18 overnight Amsterdam (or possibly train to Zoetermeer today)
W 09-19 train to Zoetermeer, overnight Zoetermeer: meeting with
Manuel Op de Coul, Graham Breed, Robert Walker
Th 09-20 overnight Zoetermeer
F 09-21 leave Den Haag AM, train to Paris:
meeting with Henry-Louis de La Grange; overnight Paris
Sat 09-22 overnight Paris
Sun 09-23 depart Paris AM, fly back to San Diego

love / peace / harmony ...
