John Link recently wrote about the isle of Anaphoria:

> Joe, how about travelling together to the next festival
> that is held on the island?

John, this is an excellent suggestion, but one must be in excellent
physical and, especially, mental health to voyage there. Many persons
never return, either through their inclination to permanently "defect" a
la Gauguin, or a particular orientation that seems to affect those
exposed to the place -- the inability to distinguish between their own
computer screens and totemic deities...

In any case, ONE person did return, by the name of Brian Harlan. He
actually DID voyage to Anaphoria and he returned with his mind curiously
intact... He lived to write about it. You can read about his successful
interview with Kraig Grady on the Harry Partch Corporeal Meadows
website:

http://www.corporeal.com/kgintrvu.html

But remember, he had a particular strength of mind and character to make
it back "through the looking glass!"

In the meantime, please visit the Erv Wilson archive on the Website... a
reasonable substitute for a virtual trip there... I'm "stuck" in the
archive at the moment. (Ahem...)
