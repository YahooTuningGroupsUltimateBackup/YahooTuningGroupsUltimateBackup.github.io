It has a 7 second fade-in and fade-out that Andy put in for the
concert. You can change this at Sheet1!E53. I think if you set it to 1
it will be instant.

If you set your screen resolution to 640x480 and choose Excel's
View/Full-Screen you can have it fill most of the screen without any
speed degradation. Ctrl-S and Ctrl-G work for Stop and Go.

There's another problem when running under Windows NT. It seems to
lose most of the keypresses or button-clicks when it is running. So
just keep bouncing up and down on that control-S (or "Stop" button)
until one gets thru and it stops (after the fade-out).

Not getting all the voices is a new one. Can you please give more
details. Is anyone else experiencing this.

For other folks that may be trying to run this thing for the first
time: You will need to enable macros. You may need to click the MIDI
out button and choose your MIDI device and close and save it, before
you can hear anything.
