I just wanted to drop a note of special thanks to Mary Beth Ackerley
for the gift of beautiful music she has provided on her site.

http://www.elucida.com/

There is such wonderful diversity of creative compositions with great
sound design and tuning concepts. Some pieces make very creative use
of "beats" (as in drum beat loops); an art-form in itself to be
respected. Mary also has a delightfully lovely singing voice - the
best microtonal instrument conceivable.

I also enjoyed the spoken word pieces on MP3.com, and just generally
loved all I heard.

Keep up the beautiful music Mary, and please let us all know as new
material becomes available. You've got my attention.

Peace,

Jacky Ligon
