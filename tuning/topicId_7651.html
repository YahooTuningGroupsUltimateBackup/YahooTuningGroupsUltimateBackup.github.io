ONDAR AND THE EAGLES OF TUVA WITH

Knitting Factory 74 Leonard St ( btwn Broadway and Church)

Ondar and the Eagles of Tuva practice the centuries-old Tuvan art of
throat-singing, in which a soloist can sing several notes simultaneously.
They also play traditional Tuvan instruments-the bowed igil and the
doshpuluur. The Tuvans combine their sounds with their long time associate
Tom Rutishauser on electric cello. Joe Pereira brings his array of
percussion instruments from the New York Philharmonic. The show will center
around the Tuvan music with a blend of free jazz and surprises. Ondar is
People's Throat-Singer of Tuva and is the star in the internationaly
acclaimed film "Genghis Blues"; Igor K�shkendey is the reigning world
champion of throat-singing (UNESCO Festival of Throat-singing, 1998), and
Yevgeny Saryglar is Ondar's star student at Ondar's School of Music in
Kyzyl,capital of Tuva.
