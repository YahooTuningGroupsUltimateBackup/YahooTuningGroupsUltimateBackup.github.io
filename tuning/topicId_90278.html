*Dear Freinds*

**

*Here are my 4 musics *for *"AEH Ethno2 microtonal demos competition"*.* I
wish they sounds good to you.*

*They can be found in http://www.96edo.com/My_Music.html or as pasted
bellow:*

**
*Micro890305-1 <http://www.96edo.com/music/Micro890305-1.mp3>* : A music
composed using "*Ethno 2 <http://www.motu.com/products/software/ethno>*" for
*"AEH Ethno2 microtonal demos competition".*Instrument used are:

Lira 2 KS Fiddle KS Celtic harp 1 Acoustic Bass 2
Cha Gu 1 Bass

*Tuning :Segah*

*Micro890305-2 <http://www.96edo.com/music/Micro890305-2.mp3>* : A music
composed using "*Ethno 2 <http://www.motu.com/products/software/ethno>*" for
*"AEH Ethno2 microtonal demos competition".*Instrument used are:

Kendbang Ageng-big Saron Congolese sanza 5 plate Balafon 2
clean Jaladarangam sus Taiko1 mic 1 Body bells

*Tuning :Shur*

*Micro890315 <http://www.96edo.com/music/Micro890315.mp3>* : A music
composed using "*Ethno 2 <http://www.motu.com/products/software/ethno>*" for
*"AEH Ethno2 microtonal demos competition".*Instrument used are:

Bambou sax KS Lira 1 KS Tunisian Lute trem Santur 1
Roots Koto KS Santur Hits Daires Aboriginal Perc
Rattles Darabuka

*Tuning : Homayun*

*Micro890318 <http://www.96edo.com/music/Micro890318.mp3>* : A music
composed using "*Ethno 2 <http://www.motu.com/products/software/ethno>*" for
*"AEH Ethno2 microtonal demos competition".*Instrument used are:

Lira 1 Sustain Lira 1 Plucked Lira 1 Tremolo Balkanish Double
bass Fiddle Sustain Cymbalum sus

*Tuning :12ED(900)*

*Shaahin mohajeri , Tombak player and microtonalist*
