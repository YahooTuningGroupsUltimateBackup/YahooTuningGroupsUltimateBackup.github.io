The South East Just Intonation has built now two functional tunable
microinterval synthesizers from donations to the old SEIJC and is currently
manufacturing a Lambdoma Matrix proto for Denny Genovese of the WOrld
Harmony Project. We would gladly build you a synth to whatever spec you
deemed necessary with most any interface you can desire. The matrix for
Pandit GEnovese is 12by 16 presently but it is only a proto.
inquiries can be sent to benchic@fdt.net----Robert Benchic our
astounding,innovative techlord. Or if you are wanting rapid info you may
email me back at anytime.
