4 pairs (which form the 72-tET basis):

# and b for 1/2-tones = 100 cents
^ and v for 1/4-tones = 50 cents
> and < for 1/6-tones = 33.1/3 cents
+ and - for 1/12-tones = 16.2/3 cents
>
