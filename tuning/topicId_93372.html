Hi music fans,

Visa has teamed up with indie music site Pitchfork on a contest that lets music fans flex their creative muscle using the new online shopping application Rightcliq to create the ultimate visual wishlists around music-related themes.

Each week the editors of P4k choose a different contest theme and put together their own collection. Think you can do better? The editors of Pitchfork will choose the most creative user-submitted collection and its creator will win what's in it. Check out this week's theme (Instrument Essentials) and contest details here: http://rightcliq.pitchfork.com/

This is your chance to show your creativity and have the opportunity to win some great stuff. Good luck!

Vince
