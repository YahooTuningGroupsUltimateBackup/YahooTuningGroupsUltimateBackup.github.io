Friends,

Much rending of breasts and gnashing of teeth lately. I side with the folks
that say we need to be one community of tuning interests that is adult
enough to accommodate all fancies and skill levels. If you can perform
calculus, you can be civil; if you can shred on a (place favorite ax'
here), you can be civil.

Civility is *easy*, people.

It was way worse in days gone by, and these are the bumps in the road that
make the freedom so worth the while. Keep calculating, keep composing, keep
improvising -- just stop being ill-mannered. I don't want to have to call
Johnny Cochran in here to settle things down...
