line space line

a weekly new and improvised music series

at Salvation Theatre

Monday February 23, 2003 at 8 pm

Kraig Grady, sole meta-slendro vibraphone
Laurie Buenafe, dance/movement

and

Susan Allen, harp
Nora Hoffmann, violin

- -

Instrument builder and micro-tonal composer Kraig Grady returns Monday to
line space line. As the U.S. Ambassador to the Island of Anaphoria, he has
dedicated himself to the presentation of it's rich artistic history to the
American public. He continues this tradition by performing a duet with
dancer Laurie Buenafe.

- -

- -

Susan Allen says of the Allen/Hoffmann duo:

"Nora and I have been working together for two years - one hour a week - to
develop our voice together...we are now ready to show this work to the world
and will do so at line space line...we will eventually release a recording
of our work together."

- -

For more information on the performers and upcoming performances at line
space line, please visit our website: www.linespaceline.org
