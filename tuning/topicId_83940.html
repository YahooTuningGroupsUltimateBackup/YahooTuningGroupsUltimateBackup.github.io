Rick,

CSE is mainly for use with the H-Pi hardware item the Tuning Box which retunes standard keyboards. It has further general uses, but that's the focus.
TPXE is for use with the H-Pi specialized keyboard the Tonal Plexus, of which I am a happy owner. It could be used by others as a virtual version of the keyboard but it is definitely all about the keyboard and its concept.
Scordatura is more general and for use by all sorts of people for all sorts of purposes and I have less experience with it and less understanding of its capabilities. It is in many ways a competitor to Max Magic Microtuner.

All three are distinct software programs.

Regards,
