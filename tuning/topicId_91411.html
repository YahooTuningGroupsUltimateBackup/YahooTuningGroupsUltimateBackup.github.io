Here is a rewrite of a piece i did in the 80's that was called Ascension.
It uses nothing but a single 7-9-11-15 hexany in different inversions/spacing.
Back then the resolution was not good and beats occurred due to the midi standard as opposed to the desired intervals. Once i realized this reconstruction and heard what i was going for i played with a bit and came up with this 'canon' on the original material.
a rather atypical piece of mine i think.
Dedicated to Brian McLaren~
