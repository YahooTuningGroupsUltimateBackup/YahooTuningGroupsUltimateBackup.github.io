I am proud to announce the premiere of my new piece
Exit for violin and live electronics.

Exit is written in 19TET and uses MAX/MSP for real-time processing.

It was performed in Burbach-Holzhausen on April 1st by violinist Peter
Stein.

For more information as well as the live recording in various formats please
