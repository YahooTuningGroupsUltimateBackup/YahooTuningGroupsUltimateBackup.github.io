The people of Anaphoria are pleased to announce the completion of
The Gleeson Memorial Chapel/Tritri <http://anaphoria.com/trichimes.html>adic Chimes. <http://anaphoria.com/trichimes.html>

In memory of the poet/painter James Gleeson, we are pleased to announce the opening of the Gleeson Memorial Chapel of Anaphoria and

We look forward to hearing the chimes' unique signal every hour.
