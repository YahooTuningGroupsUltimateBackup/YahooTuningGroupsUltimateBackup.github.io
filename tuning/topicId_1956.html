Hi Pat,

I just found out about your web pages from a recent Usenet post. Great job!
I liked the example WAV files at:

http://www.users.globalnet.co.uk/~patm/tunings.html

I was particularly surprised at how rough this one sounded:

Holes 3, 4 and 5 draw in Just Intonation, with 5 draw tempered

http://www.pacificnet.net/~nowitzky/justint/dom7.htm

I may have to rethink that. Anyway, thanks for making your info available
