Here are some variantions on ART OF NOISE'S, OPUS 4:

Opus four reversed:

Simply a revered in time version of Opus four, and this wasn't too hard to
acheive.

I managed to do this by tweaking a bolt on my friends casette disk player.

I actually enjoyed this almost as much as the origional, and this, in no way

esteem that I hold for OPUS 4.

This is primarily the theme in my proposed version on the CD, or trahsed up
album cover
for an AON fan tribute, and this is WAYS OF TAKING THE INFORMATION IN
HERANT AND AS REPRESENTATIVE OF OPUS 4, and INVERTING it.

The second variant does, in fact, involve inverting the information, but in
another way.

Sample depth is, for certain given in Sigma notation, thus:

n
E 2^n * (0, 1)
0

and this Greek "E" surely does describe a sample depth, but what I wanted to
do was
find:

n
E n^2 *(0, 1)
0

which is a sample depth reversed.

A little mental effort actually shows that this gives a sampling system all
right, but with
BLOOMING GREAT HOLES!!!!!!!!!!

There is another means to encrypt:

n
E n^2 * (0, 4, 2.25, 1.77777777777777, 1.5625, 1.44------>1)
0

which is a reverse sampleing system, without hoels, and map it back to 2^n,
and hey
presto, we have two more variants.

For now, as time permist, and I am hungry, I will mention, that there are

variants that a worthy of noteing.
