Alex,

As far as Varese's music is concerned, there's not much of it to find. In
fact, I'm pretty sure that the entire catalogue of his works would fit on 2
CDs. If I remember correctly, he burned all of his early pieces at one
time, so there's really not much left. (The fire may have been an
accident, I can't quite remember.) Anyway, there are a number of CDs that
have various combinations of his pieces. I would personally recommend
(pieces): Ameriques (very _Rite of Spring_-ish), Ionisation, Integrales,
Poem Electronique, and Hyperprism. Also, if you can find a recording of
Deserts that includes the tape-part, snatch it up. The only CD I know of
that has Deserts on it (conducted by Boulez) doesn't include the tape
parts.

Cheers,

Jon Southwood
