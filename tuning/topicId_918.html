Write to Mark Rankin...

PO Box 201
Alderpoint, CA
95511

...and request the information packet. It will tell you how to order. The
information packet is free, and Mark asks that you not request it until you
are serious. As of November the cost was $300 for instructions and enough
materials for 4 fretboards. Rankin can also provide computer-generated
fretboard templates for a wide variety of tunings. David Canright is the
last person I know of to buy one, and he was very satisfied.
