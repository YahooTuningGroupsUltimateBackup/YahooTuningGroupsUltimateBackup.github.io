Hi george,
These numbers are the number of shruti-s. Thus, for example, in the
first line, the distance between R and G is 2 shruti-s. In a recent
posting, shreeswifty has given us a good idea of the step sizes.

>>>> What do the letters mean? Indian solfege? >>>

Yes; rough, but practical equivalences are: [Taking the note C as the
Sa (S)]

Indian solfege:- Sa = S; Re = R; Ga = G; Ma = m; Pa = P; Dha = D; Ni
= N. All white keys on the keyboard. Note: m = F, M = F#

C = S; C# [= Db ]= r; D = R; D#/Eb = g; E = G; F = m; F# = M; G = P;
G#/Ab = d; A = D; A#/Bb = n; B = N.
