Whilst everyone else on the tuning list seems to be busy attempting to reconcile Turkish and Western tunings;
I have been busy teaching myself how to mix and arrange pop music.
This is a reworked song, using 12-string and electric LucyTuned guitars.
It is now at the stage to add and mix in the vocals; once I finalise the lyrics,
which are about the Hawaiian island Maui and Kamahameha's savage batttle of the 1790's.

The vocals will go where you hear the melody on the digital choir on this mix.

It is mixed in 5:1 surround, using all the latest Logic Pro 7.1 Tiger bells, whistles, tuning correction etc., but only mp3 tracks 1-2 here:

http://www.lucytune.com/mp3/MauiTimp17Jun05.mp3

My intent is to produce a microtuned standard for mainstream pop. I expect that 98% of the pop listeners won't even realise that it is entirely microtuned.
