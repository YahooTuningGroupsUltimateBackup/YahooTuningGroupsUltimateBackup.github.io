Additional release notes: The Tone Player tool

The Tone Player audio tool will allow to test the frequencies
appearing in the intonation table by means of the built-in General
MIDI instruments (with a certain degree of approximation). You
can open a series of satellite Tone Player windows for each
Microtuner window; selecting a row from the intonation table will
cause all the satellite Tone Players to play the corresponding
pitch, as long as their current frequency hasn't been protected
with the "Lock" checkbox. Tone Players are monophonic but all
the Tone Players linked to a Microtuner window can be "synced"
together in order to play chords, also when the selected GM
instruments have a fast attack transient, like in the case of a
piano (this can be achieved by means of both the "Lock" and
"Sync" checkboxes).

General description:

Max Magic Microtuner allows Max/MSP and Pluggo users to
generate their own alternate tunings external objects by
converting specially formatted text files into Max/MSP
MIDI-to-frequency externals. These tuning text files contain the
intonation table data and can be created and edited with Max
Magic Microtuner itself or with any other text editor: the program
will import data from both plain text files and "mttx" native format
files.
