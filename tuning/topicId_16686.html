I am pleased to announce the 10th annual broadcast of my WKCR 89.9 FM New York Microtonal Bach Christmas broadcast. It will run from 10 AM to 3 PM on Christmas Day (Dec. 25) as part of a 10-day all-Bach winter festival.

Since my recent visits to Wolfenbuttal, Halberstadt, Wernigerode, Quedlinburg, Nordhausen, and Magdeburg, I have many juicy stories pointing to the useage of Werckmeister's chromatic temperament for many composers, including J.S. Bach.

Johnny Reinhard

p.s. We haven't gotten the Microthon tapes in yet. We can proceed from there as to a product to be made available.
