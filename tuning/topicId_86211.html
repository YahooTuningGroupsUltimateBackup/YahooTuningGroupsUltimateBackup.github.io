Hi,

I have uploaded files concerning to dissonance analysis of 19-EDO pc-set classes as chords:

http://launch.groups.yahoo.com/group/tuning/files/HudsonLacerda/

Look for files with *19* in the name, and also read:

comments12.txt
results12.txt

The files *diss*.txt contain all pitch permutations and respective dissonance values. Most of the files end with a ranking of dissonance means (the ranking for 12-EDO is in results12.txt).

There are also files on 17-EDO and 22-EDO.

(I am also analysing 24-EDO and other EDO, but I will not upload more files to the list, because they are becoming too large for a shared space. Eventually I can upload the files -- including the computer programs and organized information -- to another site.)

Cheers,
Hudson
