Hi folks-
My new mp3.com page Kami's African Expedition is up with about
half of the 33 cuts approved:

http://artists.mp3s.com/artists/293/kamis_african_expedition.html
This is a page of pretty low quality but never before published field
recordings made by an acquaintance in the early 1950s during his
diamond prospecting days. Most of the musicians playing are "amateurs"
in the sense that they are ordinary people playing music for Kami as
he travelled through Africa. Some of the cuts are pretty interesting,
a couple have some really cool rhythms, a lot are "ehhh..." but take a
listen, and come back in a couple of days when the rest should be up.

John Starrett
