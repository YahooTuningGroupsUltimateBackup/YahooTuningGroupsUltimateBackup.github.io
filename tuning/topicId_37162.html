A Call For Entries

TENSILE - an Acoustic Guitar Compilation

Compositions no longer than 6 minutes. No limitation of style imposed.
The only rule: the sound source must be derived from acoustic guitar
(nylon, steel string,
amplified acoustic, are all acceptable). Whatever you chose to do as far
as processing
or rearranging the recorded sound is up to you. Unconventional
performance techniques
are acceptable. Straight up guitar tracks are also acceptable (single
track, or
multitrack).

Chosen artists will receive 1 copy of the compilation. On our part,
we will work to
get reviews and distribution. Deadline for submission is August 15
(postmarked).
The projected release date is sometime before the end of 2002.
