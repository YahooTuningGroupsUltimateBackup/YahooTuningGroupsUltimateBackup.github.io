Attachments

Hi friends,

Due to heavy floods and hail storms in our Var area we had severe damages of all kinds and many animals and human deaths. We are OK here in our village, but our garden is destroyed and we will have no internet at home for a while.
Thanks to not send new demos at my personal e-mail until it is restored. Use the dropbox instead if you want to share your demos. Ask Francois on this list if you need help.
Last sunday I "D-Jayed" 30 of the demos sent in an ambient festival (Ambiosonic 18-20 June Collobrieres France) and they were really well received. People even danced on some of them and it was a pleasure to hear it out in nature on big "boom-boom" speakers. It was a good sound test too and I can say that considering all was done with the package's sounds, everyone did a super fine job.

Other info is that because of technical and other problems several participants asked for extra time to finish their works.
Jerome Desigaud did not succeed on installing the software on his computer and passed his box to the percussionist Naomi O'Sullivan.
So if everyone agrees we propose a prolongation till the 18 th of July.
Special thanks for those who already fulfilled their contracts. We hope for everyone's comprehension.
We have no news from MOTU anyway about the website space that will welcome the selected demos.
So I guess until then every participant can complete his works -

Thanks for the good comments !

I will be back soon (I hope),
Take care,
- - - - - -
Jacques

I also asked the candidates to tell us the most important of their demos for them (this won't be published, but it would possibly help us us to cite, in case, the most relevant piece(s) for each composer).

Cheers,

Jacques
