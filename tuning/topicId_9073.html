The American Festival of Microtonal Music will present its 2nd Microthon - an
all-day event of microtonal concertizing - in November. The program is wide
open at this time and we welcome suggestions. If you might be in New York in
November, for example, and perform your own music, this could be the one.

At this time, we are still not secure with the location, but we have our
sights on a room rarely used for concerts, and yet superb.
