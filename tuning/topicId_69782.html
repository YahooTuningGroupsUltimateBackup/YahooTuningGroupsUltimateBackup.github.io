This is a one and only reminder that UK MicroFest 2 will take place at
the wonderful

Entry prices are even lower than last time in an attempt to encourage
delegates to come for the
weekend:
Students can attend all the events for 10 pounds only!

For those without transport, however, who want to stay in Walton we have

Walton (only 20

latest on 22.02.07. Full
booking details and the menu have also be added the website.

riverhouse is an 18th century barn-conversion with its own modern

free parking.
