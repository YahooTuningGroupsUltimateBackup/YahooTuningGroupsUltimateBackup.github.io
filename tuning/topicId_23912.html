I just uploaded a short collaboration to the files section that I did
with Ken Rubenstein for his CD "Is this my world that hovers?"

This CD also features fretless five string bassist Rick Van
Benschoten, electronic percussionist Jay Dittamo, mezzo soprano Elaine
Valby, traditional shloka vocalist Priya Doraswamy, and spoken word by
Julia Mueller.

Anyone interested in more info can contact Ken at his email address
which I'll spell out -- tellyrubes at aol dot com.

The tune I put up is called "Ayn Fist". It has a very simple ABA (plus
a tag) form.

Ken took the first solo and I took the middle and end solos.

Iron Fist was Danny Rand, Ayn Rand was Ayn Rand...
