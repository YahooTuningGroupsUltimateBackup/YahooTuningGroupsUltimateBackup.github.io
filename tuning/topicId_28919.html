a little algorithmic JI sketch --

www.neuron.net/~pfly/ear/mp3s/TrapezoidDream.mp3

a nine minute dream of trapezoidal waveforms through a
basic lowpass filter. each note enters randomly between
0.1 and 5 seconds after previous note.

pitches determined by randomly generating ratios between 1:13
and 13:1 (logrithmically tending towards 1:1). tonic = 330 hertz.
