I know only very little about logic audio, but I suspect it will depend on

regarding what should be "minimal requirements" for Music Device audio
units, which are basically plug-in midi synths. I have wanted to say
something about tunings, but have not yet composed my thoughts about what
should be said.

In any case this is perhaps a good opportunity for us to give input into the
process to helpe make sure that OS X supports alternate tunings in a more
universal as opposed to a piecemeal way.

I am going to try to clarify this and present it to the list. Others who
have things to say might want to join the CoreAudio list and give inputs.
It is primary a list to support developers, but it is an open forum, and
