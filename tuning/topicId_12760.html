Dear Dante
David and i have a similar aesthetic as it applies (it seems) to several
important things to me
1. just intonation and the exploration of higher primes as scale building
tools
2. "duration,duration, duration"......................hah a little real
estate joke there..
3. and what seems to be termed "Minimalism" as fathered by LY

I am applying these criteria to visual realizations
ATARAXYA----peace of mind----db's term (ancient greek)
represented by two rooms that intersect at a certain point
--db has his own comps
--me as well

they exist via the extenuations of and ornamentations of the, dare i say
"Young Gharana"
that is a style and influence as Shivkumar Sharma might exude a certain
shankar training in his musics
while this may be Direct in Hindu musics via the Guru/sishya traditions
it is INDIRECT in our western culture
I think it is valid to attempt these visuals with the premise that Art is
indeed collaborative in technological culture and
may exist eternally via some installation and serves to document a
concentric union or intersection of progress of a collaborative relationship

On a basic JI level it's 17 limit for the most part.

I hope i did not go too OT
cheers and thanks for the kind words

Pat Pagano, Director
