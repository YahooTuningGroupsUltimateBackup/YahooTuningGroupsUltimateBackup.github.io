Hello all.

Yesterday, Denny Genovese played for me Dave Hill's CD
of piano pieces tuned in JI and meantone.

My favorite ones were a JI piece by Dave about a cat,
and an improvisation he did exploiting the close approximations
to 7- and 11-limit harmonics in 1/4-comma meantone.

I was so intrigued by the latter that I made an interval matrix
and list of available intervals for a 12-tone version of
1/4-comma meantone:

http://www.ixpres.com/interval/dict/1-4cmt.htm

For those without web access who find it useful:

(if line-breaks have been added which shorten the lines,
copy these tables to a text editor and delete the line-breaks
to arrive at the correct format)
