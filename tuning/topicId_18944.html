These figures are also widely known and used in Occult/Alchemical
circles and referred to as Magic squares. Each is associated with a
particular planet. the two below being Mercury and Mars. I have tried in
the past to pull things out of them with lukewarm results. The most
interesting was to have players play them as durations, choosing a row
or diagonal freely with the result that everyone will still come down on
the same beat every 15 or 65 beats. Much to do was done with the line
figure that result when you draw a line to the numbers in their natural
order. In turn, everything from talismans and background patterns are
refer to these magic squares without the need to write the numbers
themselves!

Peter Maxwell Davies, via his interest in medieval mysticism and runic
esoterica of his native Orkney Islands, has used magic squares in his
compositions, often as replacement to tone row matrices, but for other
parameters too, I believe. There is a good book on his music by Paul
Griffiths that explains some of this. Also one by Mike Seabrook.
