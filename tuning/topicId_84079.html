Dear Aaron,

Thank you for this software -- a microtonal aural training program is an excellent idea.

I did not have the time yet to look at it further. Only some very quick feedback: in the Mac version, the help window (shown after startup and accessible via the menu help) seems to be empty, just a white page. I might have missed the obvious. However, is there some "query mode", where after playing a random interval (or whatever) the software would give no visual clue (e.g., no solution in notation nor by highlighting any button).
