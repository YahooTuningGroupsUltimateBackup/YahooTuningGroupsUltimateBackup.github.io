after a cable that worked at the sound check was replaced.

and 13 limit chords, I accidentally included a 30/16 and it
provided a new contrast, it might stay. I'm thinking of
expanding the piece to 17 limit.

A nice turn out, I was worried that the store would be too
small but was there was plenty of room.
