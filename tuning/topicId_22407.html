It's great how much musical sense this lattice makes to someone used to
12-tET notation and knows a little about 7-limit JI!!

72-tET notation will be 12-tET notation with the following new accidentals:

v = 1/4 tone flat
< = 1/6 tone flat
{ = 1/12 tone flat
} = 1/12 tone sharp
> = 1/6 tone sharp
^ = 1/4 tone sharp

So the blackjack scale is

note degree Semitones
C< 70 11&2/3
B{ 65 10&5/6
Bv 63 10&1/2
Bb< 58 9&2/3
A> 56 9&1/3
Av 51 8&1/2
Ab} 49 8&1/6
G> 44 7&1/3
G 42 7
Gb} 37 6&1/6
F#{ 35 5&5/6
F 30 5
F< 28 4&2/3
E{ 23 3&5/6
Ev 21 3&1/2
Eb< 16 2&2/3
D> 14 2&1/3
Dv 9 1&1/2
Db} 7 1&1/6
C> 2 0&1/3
C 0 0
