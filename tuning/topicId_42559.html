Sounds and looks like
the tuning is for a Db
Major triad kind of tuning.
If you can, tune your guitar
to a D Major tuning first.
Then drop all of your strings
flat.

Your standard guitar should
already have the 5th and 4th
strings tuned to A and D.
Lower your 6th string a
whole step. Lower your 3rd
string a half step. Lower
your 2nd string a whole step.
Then lower your 1st string a
whole step.
That will give you-

6 string- D
5 string- A
4 string- D
3 string- F#
2 string- A
1 string- D

THEN..lower ALL strings
a half step!

Your guitar is now tuned:

6 string- Db
5 string- Ab
4 string- Db
3 string- F
2 string- Ab
1 string- Db

Joel
