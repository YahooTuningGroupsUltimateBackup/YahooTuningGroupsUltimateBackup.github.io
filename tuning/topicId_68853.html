Anyone heard this CD?

<http://www.amazon.com/Corigliano-Fantasy-Fantasia-Ostinato-Chiaroscuro/dp/B000EUMKP4>

The final piece, "Chiaroscuro", is for two pianos tuned apart, so say
the liner notes, by a quarter tone. The difference between them
sounds to me much smaller, perhaps as if the tuner took "quarter tone"
to mean "quarter of a semitone" - i.e. eighth tones. I bet you can't
hear from the Amazon sound samples because it takes a while for him to
get into it. It sounds way subtle.

I wonder what Corigliano wanted.

Reminds me also of 'Fantango' for harpsichord by Jukka Tiensuu, with
the two manuals a quartertone apart, but for which I have a recording
in nothing but plain ol' 12TET. Booooooriiiiing, and a more
open-and-shut case of subversion of intentions than with JSBach...
