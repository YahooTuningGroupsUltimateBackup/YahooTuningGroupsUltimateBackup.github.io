   Note: this tuning is designed to use a whole lot of 7-limit the the 7/6, 12/7...7/5 and 10/7.  My Infinity tuning has that weak point...it's focused more around either meantone or Mohajira-type intervals with a little 7-limit...this scale is designed to have a lot of 7-limit and some meantone-type intervals with a few Mohajira-style ones.

  The result is a scale that, hopefully, will sound very xenharmonic to a whole lot of people, but doesn't have many weird fifths (there are only two Mohajira-style 22/15 diminished fifths) or 9 and 11 limit
intervals...plus the only "non just/off" intervals include one near the 12TET major 7th and Minor 12TET minor 7th IE "wrong, but familiar" intervals.  So it should hopefully sound oddly stable to a lot of people.  Any general thoughts/impressions/ideas/suggestions for this scale?
