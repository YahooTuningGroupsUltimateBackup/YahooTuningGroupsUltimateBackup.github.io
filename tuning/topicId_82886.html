Thanks guys. Chris, for as long as the video is up you can point to it,
especially if broadcasts announce the impending May 2nd performance of Samson
and Delilah in NYC, along with my new just intonation quintet for bassoon
and string quartet titled The New Administration, and my polymicrotonal Cosmic
Rays string quartet...and more.

The two Saturday nights beginning on April 25 in NYC are an opportunity to
focus on my compositional interests. They will also spotlight some great
playing talent and uncover some rare items.

Dan, the third dimension of theater particularly in this piece is
intentional on my part for all the obvious reasons, and some which are not. I have
always believed duos of all kinds are jinxed by being only two-dimensional,
in the sense that when one voice drops out everything seems to sputter to a
stop. Three or more voices makes for a three dimensional musical product
which consistently stands up more easily. By using theater as a third
dimension, aided no doubt by circular breathing, we hopefully circumvented my
typical duo fears. Interestly, the 4th, time dimension, is implicit in any piece
of music.

all best, Johnny Reinhard
