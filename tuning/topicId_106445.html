ZynAddSubFX has high precision tuning and will probably work on Macintosh. Timidity has high precision tuning and will work on Macintosh, at least from the command line. Csound has high precision tuning, will certainly work on Macintosh, and will work with Hz directly, but you need to program it yourself. It isn't a simple way of starting up.

Cents are a good deal better than nothing.

Pitch bends can potentially be used for high precision tuning. Not all synths respond to this precision, though. There are also problems with polyphony.
