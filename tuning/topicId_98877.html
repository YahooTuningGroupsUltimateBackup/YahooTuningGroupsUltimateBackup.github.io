Apropos several recent discussions, here is an excerpt from "Annette of the Plapes" which demonstrates Wuerschmidt temperament.

http://soundcloud.com/cameron-bobro/annettexcerpt-cbobro

The first 20 seconds of the excerpt demonstrates the primary or defining "comma pump" of the temperament, as it goes from "I" to "i" using augmented triads to run through all eight 5/4s, arriving at the pure 6:1.

Notice that the 7th generator gives you a 24:5, so it is "natural" to go from I to i (simply drop in the starting tone under the last two generators).
