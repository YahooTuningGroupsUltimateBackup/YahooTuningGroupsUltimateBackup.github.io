My server was apparently having problems for a few days,
but it's OK now and I've finally been able to upload several
new files to my site:

- The 'high 3rd' experiment (based on Jerry Eskelin's), with
a plain vibrato-less harmonium patch, and with the 4/3 'sus'
chord resolving to the 14:18:21 'high 3rd major triad' at
the end, in a MIDI-file:
http://www.ixpres.com/interval/td/monzo/high3rd.mid

- Thanks to prompting from Dave Canright, I've completely
redone my Woolhouse page - it looks much better now:
http://www.ixpres.com/interval/monzo/woolhouse/essay.htm

- Thanks to being inspired by Jerry's comments, a newly-updated
version of my _Invisible Haircut_ page (see my other posting
from today):
http://www.ixpres.com/interval/monzo/haircut/haircut.htm

- Thanks to more info submitted by Paul Erlich, an expanded
and correcterd 'Table of ET advocates' in my 'equal-temperament'
Dictionary entry:
http://www.ixpres.com/interval/dict/eqtemp.htm

Thanks to Dave Canright, I now know that some of my more
recent 'quickie' webpages, where I simply copied TD postings
into a webpage without using HTML code, look fine in Microsoft
Internet Explorer but ridiculous in Netscape Navigator. The
Woolhouse page was one offender that's been fixed; others that
haven't include my pages on Ives's '1/8-tones' and Aristoxenus
(and probably many others). I'll get to them when I can.
Thanks, Dave!
