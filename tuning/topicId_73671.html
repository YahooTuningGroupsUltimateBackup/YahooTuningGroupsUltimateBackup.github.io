Testing lower semiaugmented tetrachord and grave tetrachord as I use these terms for 96-EDO interval and tetrachord naming.
96-edo is a good equal tempered framework for persian usage of interval naming as 24-edo which is now used is very rigid.i can have 3 kind of for example neutral third of 337.5 350 362.5 and may be 325 is also good.
