I have constructed an online tool (using PHP and LISP) that will convert a
score you give it, written in any tuning system, into a Standard MIDI File.

The syntax for the score, and etc. are text-based and pretty simple.

This isn't as sophisticated as SCALA, of course. But it's online, which
means anybody anyhwere on any computer can use it. It's also easy-to-use
and so it could be good for coding up examples to post on the lists at a
moment's notice.

Here it is:

http://music.columbia.edu/~chris/micromidi/main.html

There are tutorials online as well.

The recently posted "Piece for Lou Harrison" was made with this tool.
It's score is linked to in the tutorial, as an example.

Enjoy,

C Bailey
