This one was promised to Gene Ward Smith on the tuning list who devised the Bohpier 25 tuning for me.

! bohpier25.scl
Bohpier[25] in 41-et tuning
25

To pull this off I had to tune my guitar to all A's starting with the lowest A on the piano. This was done via midi by having each string on its own midi channel and adjusting the transpose function for the track as needed. Then I loaded Bophier 25 into piano tech and played at half the speed you hear. (truth in Advertising statement).

http://notonlymusic.com/board/download/file.php?id=449

Since I had this guitar set up I also played around a bit in 12 equal where I had 6 octaves in a simple full barre across the fret board.

http://notonlymusic.com/board/download/file.php?id=448

Tuning details and online play are here
