We're looking for someone that is able to teach Music Theory from a psychoacoustic
perspective. This includes basics of modes and tuning systems.

This class covers the fundamentals of music theory with an emphasis on how each aspect
affects human beings on all levels. The course uses creative techniques to help students
acquire a deeper understanding of the nuances of intervals and musical composition. The
course uses the harmonic structure as the basis for the exploration of the underlying
meaning of musical intervals.

This class will teach the fundamentals of rhythm and music notation: ABCs of notes, how
notes combine to create intervals, scales and modes and how these combine in turn into
voice lines, chords and harmonic motion. Throughout the factual material we will use our
senses to perceive experientially.to explore the energies behind intervals and certain
types of motion and rhythms. We will see, and experience, how harmony relates to the
overtone series as well. The goal is to be able to navigate through the matrix of sound
fundamentals with confidence, creating as you go. And to experience energetically what
is learned intellectually.
