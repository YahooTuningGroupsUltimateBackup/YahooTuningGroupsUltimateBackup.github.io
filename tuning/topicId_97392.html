Hi John,

Thanks for watching/listening.

Methinks that 23-edo can sound great on the kalimba largely b/c it has a gentle spectrum (a bit of inharmonicity for some milliseconds followed by what is essentially a decaying sine tone)...I think the story might be different if it were a bright sawtooth wave with organ-like sustain!

I also think one can culturally or however else, accustom oneself to just about anything after a while, too, and lose the sense of "this is harsh or strange".

Anyway, I think any discussion of consonance and dissonance is senseless without considering cultural or learned factors, plus taking a look at spectral considerations a la Bill Sethares.

Best,
AKJ
