I want to announce that with the synthesizer "z3ta+" of
"RGC audio" now the first virtual instrument will have implemented
Hermode Tuning.

For more informations see
www.rgcaudio.com

For all who didn't follow the discussions within the
last weeks:
Hermode Tuning is a dynamic self-correcting tuning system,
controlling the fifth and third intervals of the performed music
according (or near) to the ratios of just intonation.
Additionally, if desired, the diminished septh in
minor sept chords to "natural septs".
It is a real time program, combining best frequency ratios
by almost inaudible retuning steps (indeed, the natural septh
is a problem) and by frequency positions as near as possible to ET.
