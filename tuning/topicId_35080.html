quite simply,

they are not at all lattices of the ets they refer to (while james
mccartney - style 'bingo cards' would fulfill exactly that purpose).

rather, they are lattices of ji, expressed in terms of a pitch
quantization given by the et in question.

thus they can be useful if you're working in ji, but they're much
less directly applicable if you're working in the et in question.
