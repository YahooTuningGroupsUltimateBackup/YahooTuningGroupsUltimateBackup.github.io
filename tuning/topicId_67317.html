This is juvenalia, from shortly after my first visit with Erv Wilson in
Spring of '78. It's for piano and ensemble, in the "double hexany"
tuning. I've spared you the rest of the piece, which is basically more
of the same, but the opening horn chords over the pizzicato contrabass
is still pretty cool for a sixteen-year-old.

http://home.snafu.de/djwolf/Con78.ogg

This is a quick and dirty rendering of the first two of three
sections, the second is a hocket, perhaps a bit over the top. The
rendering of the violins and piano is not wonderful, but at leasts
it's suggestive of what the piece could have been.

djw
