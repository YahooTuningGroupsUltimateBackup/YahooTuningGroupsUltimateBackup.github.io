names, I propose the following: one name goes to the rank-two
temperament (and by inclusion, the rank-one no twos temperament)
tempering out 3125/3087, plus more in higher limits, and the other
goes to the 37&41 temperament. The latter has [14 11 9 -6 -7] as a
generator mapping/OE part of wedgie. It can be construed as having a
generator of 13/11 in the 13-limit, for which 25/21 has to make do in
the 7-limit.

and since that is divisible by 13, the BP scale and other BP
manifestations have an interpretation in 41-et, and this would be an
example.
