I was remarking on Danny Wier's tumblr blog that an interesting alternative to Kleismic[19], especially if you are going to tune it in 72et, is Unimarv[19]. It still has tons of 5-limit triads, but makes much better use of the 7 and 11 limit possibilities of 72et. It terms of 72, it goes 4354343452543434534. For greater accuracy, in 197et it's [11 8 14 11 8 11 8 11 14 5 14 11 8 11 8 11 14 8 11]. Here it is in its POTE tuning. Lots of harmonic possibilities!
