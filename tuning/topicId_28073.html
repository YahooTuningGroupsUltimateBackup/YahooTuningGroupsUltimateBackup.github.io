Words cannot adequately describe my feelings during these strange days. As a
transplanted New Yorker (now in NH) with many friends and family still
living in the city, I feel truly blessed that my family and friends are
alive (so far as I know at present). I feel great compassion for everyone
whose families and friends didn't survive.

During the last two days of teaching music, preparing for upcoming gigs this
weekend (performing and teaching at a fiddle camp and playing klezmer for a
UU Rosh Hoshana service) and talking with everyone I know about these
events, I feel deeply committed to a vision of constant striving to be a
source of love and light with my music and actions in these times of
darkness.
