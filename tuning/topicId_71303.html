There's been so much talk about 13 lately that I've last track of who
has said what or on what thread. It doesn't bother me to hear
criticism of 13 or any tuning. I realise that perceptions of music
vary widely. Your observations are appreciated. For me, part of the
appeal of microtonal music is the insight it provides into musical
perception and culture. I think microtonal music can help us to
understand each other better.

I personally find some dissonant chords in 12edo to be among the most
unpleasant sounds around. I even have trouble with 19edo at times. I
enjoy some things about 13edo, but can well appreciate how many would
find it to be unsettling.
