Greetings,

I have been working on something that may
be of some interest to this group.
I've written some software that I've been
using to create microtonal compositions that are based
on recursive iterations of the golden section and which
link color and music.
The golden sections are displayed in a 3D graph and from
there can be selected and sent to a composition window.
Of particular interest is the fact that the
just ratios are also generated and show up as a subset of
those iterations. If you're curious there
are compositions and some information about what is behind
their creation at
http://synesthesiaproject.com/index.html
