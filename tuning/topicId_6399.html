There is now a Windows version of the free Midi file player
Megamid. Under DOS this was a superior one and yesterday I tried
the Windows version. It's really splendid to play microtonal
MIDI files with, because it shows everything on the screen.
Normal notes are little vertical lines and what I really like
is that pitch-bended notes are little curved lines.
Controller settings are also shown. It can also decode XG and
GS sysex messages. The Megamid homepage is:
http://megamid.cjb.net
