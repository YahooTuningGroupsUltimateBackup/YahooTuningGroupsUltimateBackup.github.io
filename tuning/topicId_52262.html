Hello,

I can't answer the first question.

But your second question has an answer: basically, no. Johnston's
notation is not based on notated intervals having fixed sizes, so his
added symbols are not fixed numbers of units away from a basic set of
intervals. Unless you are really fixed on Johnston, it's better to
use the notation that Joe Monzo calles HEWM in his dictionary. If
you use HEWM, then you can set up your tuning as a special key
signature in Finale and playback through any synth with full-keyboard
tuning option set to your complete scale.
