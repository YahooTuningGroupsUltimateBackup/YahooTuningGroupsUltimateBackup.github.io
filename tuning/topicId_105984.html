In Jorgensen’s other book TUNING THE HISTORICAL TEMPERAMENTS BY EAR, he has
a tuning called Owen Jorgensen “Five and Seven” Temperament. Both books
have HIS tuning methods in them; some are mathematically correct and some
are equal beating; some temperament have both. I have both books, if
any one needs information from them, I could copy it for you.
