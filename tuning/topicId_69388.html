Yes, this number 192 is correct but actually quite misleading, because the c-thru
duplicates MIDI notes as you hint here. On the other hand, only 6 of the 211 keys per
octave are duplicates in the default JND tuning of the Tonal Plexus, and in fact even these
are not MIDI duplicates. For a closer look, see http://www.h-pi.com/TPX28keyboard.html
