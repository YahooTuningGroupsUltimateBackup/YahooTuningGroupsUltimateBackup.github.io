For what it's worth... When I'm searching for something out-of-print, I go
to http://www.abebooks.com/ and set up a "want list" -- a free service.
Eventually, a copy will turn up at one of the zillions of used book dealers
in their network.

I waited a year or so to find a copy of Colin McPhee's "Music in Bali".
Barbour's "Tuning and Temperament" turned up in less than a fortnight.
