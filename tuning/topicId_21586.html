Alison,

The chapter 1 etudes use loscil.
In the earlier version of Csound, loscil handled loop points
a bit differently. This is why the orcs worked when I edited
the book and at the last minute, these same samples
caused errors.

I have put together another set of samples for chapter 1
that have loop points in them and work correctly with the
version of Csound distributed with the first printing of the
Csound Book. (The second printing is coming. New CDROMS.
with many updates... and this is no longer a problem.)

For now. You might install a newer version of Csound.
or get new samples from this url: http://csounds.com/catalog/index.html

You will find the samples in the "support" section of this page.

Hope this helps.

Dr.B.
