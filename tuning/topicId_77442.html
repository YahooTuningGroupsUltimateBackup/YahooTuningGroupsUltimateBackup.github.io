On July, 2nd, in Leipzig a workshop about mathematics and music will be
held (in German).

On this workshop the tuning software Mutabor will be presented. Mutabor
enables any MIDI complient hard- and software to use any static or
mutable tunings or tuning logics.

Links:
http://www.hmt-leipzig.de/index.php?id=662,0,0,1,0,0
http://www.math.tu-dresden.de/~mutabor/
