Franck Franck Jedrzejewski has put up my English
translation of his present-tense biography
of Ivan Wyschnegradsky (original in French):
<http://music.dartmouth.edu/~franck/iw/bio-en.htm>.

AFAIK, this is the only English biography of
Wyschnegradsky currently available on the internet.

The picture at the bottom is a gathering of several
well-known microtonalists, and should carry the legend:
"From left to right: Melle (= mademoiselle?) Carrillo,
A. Haba, A. Fokker, J. Carrillo, Ivan Wyschnegradsky"
(the woman next to Fokker is apparently Mrs. Fokker...?).

Franck's main page:
<http://music.dartmouth.edu/~franck/wys.htm>,
has links to other info about Wyschnegradsky.
