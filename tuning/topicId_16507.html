Yo Franz...when I was a youngster, I lived in a very funky part of St.
Louis, and there weren't a lot of white folks around...one picks up a
lot of slang that way. Also, I've played blues/jazz and country in a lot
of fairly slimy locations, and that adds to the stew. I enjoy slang,
feeling it is quite expressive...I'd use more of it, but I don't want to
scare all the nice college educated folks on this list (sense of humor,
please). And, I first saw the phrase "bad motorcycle" on an Albert
Collins album a long time ago "The Cool Sounds of...) And, indeed, Mr.
Collins was a very bad feller....Hstick
BTW. read Miles Davis's autobiography for the ultimate in slang talk...
