(( living between partials)) is based on different intervalorhythmic
concepts :

1- microtonal scale , using the intervallic relations between partials
in sound spectrum of E-tuned tombak to make a 7- tone scale which its
degrees are NEARLY equal to some degrees of 144-EDO

2-two microrhythmic scales with different time intervals ( based on my
theory)

Living in such mysterious and virtual worlds is a dream .... As a
personal point of view , Microtonality and microrhythm are the soul ,
self and nature of music.

From E( in cent):

0 152 409 603 712 925 993 1195

Which with a good estimation correlate with these digrees of 144-edo :

0 18 49 72 85 111 119 143

Its octave is 5 cent compressed.the compressin factor is .9958 . the
scale before compression would be (in cent):

0 153 411 605 715 929 997 1200

Which with a good estimation correlate with these digrees of 150-edo :

0 19 51 76 89 116 125 150

See when compression occures , the amount of change is more when going
to upper degrees of scale.
