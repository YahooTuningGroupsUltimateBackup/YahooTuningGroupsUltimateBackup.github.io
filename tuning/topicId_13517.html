As you probably already know from that helpful little automaton with the
surprizingly good grammar, I have just uploaded my first piece of microtonal
music, "Chromatic Prelude in 5." It's not very long (just 8 bars of 4/4),
and it's very simple, with only two voices and the only intervals being
5-limit consonances (in a microtonal analog of the first species of
counterpoint), but it modulates a little and has some interesting chromatic
melodies. I hope you all can derive some pleasure from listening.

Let me know what you think,
