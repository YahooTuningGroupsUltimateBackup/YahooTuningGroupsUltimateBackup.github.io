A circulating 22 note scale with integer brats

Gene Ward Smith <gwsmith@svpal.org>

9/8/2005 7:35:10 PM

This is a mildly detempered 22-equal, with 20 brats of 4 and one 3,
and one a touch over 3. The mode is chosen so that the sweetest major
triad is over 1/1. I'm calling it cpor1 because I want to do at least
one more of these, this one with some pure major thirds in it.

! cpor1.scl
A circulating 22 note porcupine-based scale with 20 brats of 4 and one
of 3
