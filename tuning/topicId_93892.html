Today I received my AXiS 49 midi controller from C-Thru Music. It is excellent and has a groovy "selfless" mode that gives one 98 individual notes Â– more than a standard piano. I couldn't resist trying my hand at an improvisation.

The music is in 17 notes per octave. Since the AXiS 49 does not have a sustain pedal port I used my M-Audio 88es to provide a sustain pedal by putting both software synths into OMNI mode to accept MIDI input from any source. The two synths I chose were pianoteq and Z3TA+. The midi performance was recorded live in Sonar 8.5 Â– there is not editing since that would defeat the point of the demonstration.
