I should add

The piece was realized by first improvising the piano + percussion by
layering virtual instruments. Then I added fretless guitar by matching
pitches by ear. This required a few takes to learn where the pitches were.
Lastly I recorded the voice - but since that is really non-pitched it was
pretty easy to do. Here is the original piano + percussion track

http://micro.soonlabel.com/Semimarvelous_dwarf/daily20101119-IF-piano-jazz-kit-and-asian-percussion.mp3
