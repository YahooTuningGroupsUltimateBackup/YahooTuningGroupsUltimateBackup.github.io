Since the best fifth in 16-tET falls below Rasch's limits of
recognizability (between 4/7 and 3/5 of an octave) one might choose
a notation without it. A possibility is then taking every three out
of 48-tET:
C C#\ D\\ D/ D# E\ E// F/ F# G\ G// G#/ A A#\ B\\ B/ C
D#=Eb, etc.
