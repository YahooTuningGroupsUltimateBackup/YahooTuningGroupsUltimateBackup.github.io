They've automatically encoded the email addresses as links, so that
clicking on them brings up a mailto: form. The colors (unless you've
altered your default browser color setting, I believe that would be
blue, *purple*, and black) are just like any links:

blue: hasn't been visited (you haven't clicked on it)
purple: has been visited (you have clicked on it)
black: this email is hidden from view (by choice of member or use of
a non-standard address), so it is actually not a link, and is text
just like the rest of the page.

I can only assume at some point you've clicked on your own email
link, which must mean you are sending mail to yourself. Shame!
