and drafts which raise important questions very helpful in writing my FAQ

By the way, Dan, our drafts may touch on some common points: bass movement
by a third is a routine feature of Renaissance style, both in conventional
diatonic and radical chromatic settings, and also fits the frequently
important role of the third degree of a mode both in traditional modal
theory and in its modernized version offered by Zarlino (1558 and later).
