Hi Joel,

Thanks for this link. It's bound to be controversial (and probably
misunderstood as well), but the bit about being 'alchemically
transformed by the context' is absolutely golden and really hits the
nail on the head as far as I'm concerned: That being that the
difference between consonance-dissonance and concordance-discordance
is a colossal one, and that this difference hinges on music--dare I
say art!
