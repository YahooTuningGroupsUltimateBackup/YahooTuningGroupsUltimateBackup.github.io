While possibilities of transposition of maqamat are somewhat limited compared to, say, 41 or 53-tET, this is by no means detrimental to serious music-making.

For optimal keyboard mapping In SCALA, choose Equal 45. You have to imagine nerm segah, buselik, nerm evdj and mahur as raised white keys instead of black. I suppose such a keyboard design is feasible for an acoustic piano.
