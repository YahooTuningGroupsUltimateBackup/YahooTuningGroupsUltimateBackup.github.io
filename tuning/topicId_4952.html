I've used some alternate mappings for seven note 34e scales with two
step sizes (L&s) in the past. Six large and one small where L=5 & s=4
was one:

0, (141) 176, (318) 353, (494) 529, (671) 706, (847) 882, (1024) 1059,
1200

0 5 10 15 20 25 30 34
0 5 10 15 20 25 29 34
0 5 10 15 20 24 29 34
0 5 10 15 19 24 29 34
0 5 10 14 19 24 29 34
0 5 9 14 19 24 29 34
0 4 9 14 19 24 29 34
0 5 10 15 20 25 30 34
