The instrument you refer to is called a "Blaster Beam". It was made &
performed by Craig Huxley (who, ironically, played the boy Tommy Starnes in
one of the original [& worst] Star Trek episodes "And the Children Shall
Lead").
The Blaster Beam is both a metallic percussion & long string instrument.
It has sonorities/timbres similar to both a large gong & an monster sized
electric guitar/bass/cello/etc..
It was used in the movie _Star Trek: The Motion Picture_ in scenes with
V'ger. It is probably the most "alien" music Star Trek has come up with so
far,
IMHO. (It was also used in a few short scenes in - I think - one other ST
movie).
