MicroFest conference attendees,

First, remember that rooms at the conference hotel, the Claremont Inn, will
only be held through tomorrow, March 5. If you plan to attend the
conference and stay at the hotel, please make reservations now! After March
5, room cannot be guaranteed. For Claremont Inn reservations, call (800)
854-5733 or (909) 626-2411.

Second, if you plan on attending the conference, please register with me so
that we can get an accurate count and have packets and name tags ready. To
register, please send to me your name and contact information, even if
you've already sent it with a previous submission. Also indicate whether
you plan on attending the conference dinner.

The conference dinner should be a lot of fun. If you plan on attending,
remember that you need to send a check for $20 per person made out to
Harvey Mudd College by March 15. You can send it to me at the address
below. Spouses and others are of course welcome.

Updated information should be available via the website soon. It is looking
like it will be a very exciting, even historic, event. Thanks, and I'll be
seeing you soon!
