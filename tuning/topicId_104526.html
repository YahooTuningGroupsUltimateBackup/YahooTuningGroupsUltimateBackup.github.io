Not sure how valuable this may be. But this 2D temperament looks capable of approximating many recognizable factors with a scale of no more than 9 tones -- I used 12 tones to make more transpositions possible. If I then add the overdriving effect to the whole thing, it becomes even more powerful.

So, the period is 400 cents, the generator is ~182.458 cents.
