Hi all,

The tables below represent some basic harmonic facts.
I'm sure there's nothing particularly new in this for
most of you. Still, every now and then I find it worth
my while to review basics.

I worked them out yesterday because I wanted to tune
up the Roland MIDI keyoard in JI. The only thing that
I found surprising was that (a good approximation to)
the major sixth doesn't appear in the harmonic series
until the 27th partial. However, something similar to
the tritones F# and Gb, and the minor sixths Ab and G#,
do appear much earlier in the overtone series.

Best viewed in a monospace font; dots added to align
the columns.

Regards,
Yahya
