I'm hoping to include a short report of some of my activities in
Russia, and I will post bits pertaining to microtonality on the list.
Through the wonderful organizing skills of Anton Rovner, our first
Russian Tuning List poster (I believe!) I was able to have a total of
14 different pieces played! Two pieces (two piano piece, solo flute)
were performed in the outlying city of Bryansk, 2 at an "arts club"
in Moscow, (violin and piano, solo cello) and 10 more, including
microtonal electronic ones, at the "Dom," a performance space in
Moscow... (solo bass flute, solo piano, solo flute, recorder and
speaker, toy piano, viola and electronics, 3 solo electronic pieces).
More (but not too much!) on this later...
