Every 3 months on a Friday night, the South Park
neighborhood in San Diego (which is where
Jonathan Glasier's Sonic Arts Gallery is located)
hosts what they call a "walkabout" -- the local
shops stay open until 9pm, and there's a little
mini-bus that makes a loop between the 3 main
intersections where the stores are located.

Friday, July 22 is the time of the next walkabout.
I'll be giving public demos of Tonescape, and in
between the demos we'll be doing improvisations with
Jonathan and Brink on keyboards and me playing the
Tonescape Lattice live. Open invitation for anyone
who's interested and able to attend ... the
festivities begin at 6pm.

Sonic Arts Gallery
2961 Beech St
San Diego, CA 92102
