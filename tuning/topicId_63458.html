This scale is in 271-ADO (arithematic divisions of octave):

Ratio of Each degree is 1/271 greater than the previous degree. We can't
find any relation between fifth and fourth as 1200-fifth = fourth in
ados with prime cardinality so for such systems like 271-ado fourth
related to 271-fifth

is in 406-ADO or 203-Ado as 542/406 or 271/203 or in 542-EDL(equal
divisions of length). In 271-edl we havn't any octave so such systems
are non-octavic.
