Carl et al. I don't recall at the moment whether the stellated 20-any
has 70 or 80 tones, but I do know that the formula in my and Erv's 1981
ICMC paper is wrong. If I have time today, I'll run a quick computation
and post it.

Brute Force seems to safest way to compute stellated CPS -- write down
all the component N-ads and add sufficient new tones to complete the
generator (or it's inversion) at each "facet."

By this means, one can stellate objects of lower symmetry such as 2)5
and 3)5 10-anies.
