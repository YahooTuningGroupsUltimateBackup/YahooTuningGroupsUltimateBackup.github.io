Hi,
my idea is to follow the melody. I've got a website about this:
http://www.comp.nus.edu.sg/~nguyenhu

Our purpose is to make the melody become not the creation of an
individual but that of a community of songwriters. Collaboration and
participation are much appreciated. It's a chance for you to get to
know more about the music of other cultures, countries.

You can download the melody over there. If you want to follow it,
send your composition to us, then we will append it to the melody.
Our concept is clearly shown in our website. Pls come and join.
http://www.comp.nus.edu.sg/~nguyenhu
