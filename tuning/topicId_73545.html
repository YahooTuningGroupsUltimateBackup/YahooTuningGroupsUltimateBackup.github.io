After 44 steps of fourths or fifths LucyTuning arrives at approx. +/-
1.7¢ from 600 cents.

i.e from A by fifths at (B6#) B followed by six sharps = 601.6905 cents.

A by fourths at (D6b)D followed by six flats = 598.3095 cents

I can see no reason why it should ever arrive at exactly 600¢.

Hence LucyTuning can be approximated by 88 edo.

It gets even closer with 1420 edo, so check what happens at 710 steps;-)

integers a and b such that 1200a + 600b + 300b/pi = 600. Since pi is
