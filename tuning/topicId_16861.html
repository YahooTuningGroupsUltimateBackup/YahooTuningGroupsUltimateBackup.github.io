Blackwood records for Cedille Records
(http://www.cedillerecords.org/blackwood.html). He was, and I think still
is, a Professor Emeritus at the University of Chicago Music Department
(http://music.uchicago.edu/). The bad news is that his book is apparently
out of print. By the way, he is the son of the Easley Blackwood that was a
famous contract bridge player. So if you do a web search, make sure you
filter out contract bridge or you may get lots of hits about the elder
Easley Blackwood.
