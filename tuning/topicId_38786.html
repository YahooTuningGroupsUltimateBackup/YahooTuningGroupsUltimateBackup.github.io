Visit our new "streaming" files from our Composers Concordance
website:

http://www.composersconcordance.org/

Just click on "Listen to the Music!"

Several microtonal works are among the selections.

Actually, I just listened again to my _Stringing_, which I thought
was of a somewhat conventional bent, but was surprised to find how
much microtonality there was actually in it! A repeated upwards
violin glissando becomes extended and goes slowly downward near the
conclusion...

Enjoy!
