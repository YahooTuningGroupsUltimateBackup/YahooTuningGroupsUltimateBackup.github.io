Greetings and Harmonic Salutations!

We'd like to share with you the news of a 4-part series of Harmonic
Awareness events and teachings in Vancouver, from Saturday to Wenesday
evening.

David Hykes and the Harmonic Choir will be performing in Vancouver, B.C.
this Sunday evening at the Sacred World Music Festival. This performance
will feature the full U.S.
Harmonic Choir (David Hykes, Timothy Hill, Seth Markel and Joel Bluestein)
and includes the world premiere of "The Silent Ground." David will also be
teaching an experiential workshop, lecturing at the Vancouver Public
Library, and making a presenation at Banyen Books.
Hope to see you there. Here's the schedule of events:
