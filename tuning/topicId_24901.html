A new piece for 22 tet guitar, Satyrical, based on approximations of
Archytas' enharmonic and the Al-Farabi tetrachords has been uploaded
at:-
http://homepages.which.net/~alison.monteith3/satyrical.mp3.bin

Satyrical, for 22 tet guitar and dancer, is the Satyr's ritualised
erotic song danced out in front of the Faun, or loved one. The dance
moves between lyricism and rhythm as the Satyr persuades and pouts time
about. The companion piece, to go up soon , tells of the Faun's reply.

I hope to get this choreographed and performed this summer.

Best Wishes.
