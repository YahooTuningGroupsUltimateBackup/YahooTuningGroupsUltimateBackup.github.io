I think Wendy Carlos is at peace with herself, who she is, and what she has done as an artist...and what she may yet do as well. I think tunings are important to her, but I also think that music, as a whole, is more important to her than any one tuning concept. And, that is as it should be. Tuning is a tool, as is the knowledge of compositional techniques, arranging skills, orchestration principles, and most important of all, some sort of deep and profound feeling that is communicated by a piece of music....Hstick
