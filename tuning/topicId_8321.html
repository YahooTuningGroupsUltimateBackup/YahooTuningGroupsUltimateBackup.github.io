I have posted four of Carl Lumma's just piano improvisations at
www.mp3.com/thetuningpunks.
I have downloaded Robert Walker's Fractal Tune Smithy from
http://www.robertwalker.f9.co.uk/fts_download.htm#download, and this is
one you Windows users should definitely get. It is $10 shareware that uses
fractals to generate sequences of pitches that can be tuned to 21
different pitch sets, almost all of which are microtonal.
JDL, in the quest for the retuning grail, don't forget that there
are those of us here (at least me myself and I) who actually _like_ the
comma pump and the other startling effects. When the commercial version is
released :?) it would be cool to have the option to implement some of the
old fashioned weirdness that caused such a stir here months ago.
