MicroFest 2001 Conference info

Bill Alves <ALVES@ORION.AC.HMC.EDU>

2/8/2001 9:24:11 AM

Information about the MicroFest 2001 Conference, including a preliminary
schedule, abstracts of papers, hotel accommodations, and travel info is now
available at the conference web site at:

http://www2.hmc.edu/~alves/microfest2001.html

Please note that the schedule is preliminary and that concert programs have
not yet been finalized.

For those of you planning to attend, please note that we have booked a
special rate at the nearby Claremont Inn for conference attendees, but
these rooms are only blocked off through MARCH 5. If you want to make a
reservation, please do so by that date. After that date, space cannot be
guaranteed.

If you have any other questions, please respond to me. I apologize to those
receiving multiple copies of this update. If you would like to be removed
from this list of recepients of MicroFest conference information, please
let me know and I'll be happy to do so.

Bill
