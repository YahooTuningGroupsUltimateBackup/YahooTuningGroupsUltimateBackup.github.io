This idea of 'fuzzy' dynamic tuning can be used to characterize
my Beethoven experiment. Even tho I'm using a fixed set of
pitches, it's a set that's rather large to be considered a
'scale', and it includes twin and triplet sets of pitches for
what Beethoven wrote as one pitch[-_gestalt_].

RE: spelling nit

Paul Hahn is correct. The proper spelling, if it matters,
is _a cappella_ (I prefer to italicize/underscore-for-ASCII
because it really is an Italian and not an English term),
and is derived from the banishment of instruments from church
services in medieval times, _cappella_ being the word for chapel.
