BTW, it's also been pointed out to me by mathematicians
that what we musicians are calling 'lattices' do not
necessarily fit the mathematical definition of the term.

There are different meanings for lattice, and the
way we use it, it refers to a way of modelling the
relationships between collections of objects
(similar to the way chemists use it) that does not
necessarily include the strict relationships required
by mathematics (a least upper bound and a greatest lower bound),.

My intention is certainly to try to model my lattices
according to the mathematical definition if at all possible.
(I need to learn much more about them)
