Kami Rousseau deserves our kudos for creating two 31-tone midi files
and putting them in his directory of microtonal example midi files.
http://www.interlinx.qc.ca/~kami/music/

It is "Preludium chromaticum" by Adriaan Fokker in the 8-tone
genus [357] (genus sextum), file: imp31.mid. The tempo of this one
is too quick for the indicated largo.
The other is "Improvisando" by Jan van Dijk in the 6-tone genus [557]
(genus septimum), file: pre31.mid.

These and other little pieces serve as musical examples to their
both article "Expe'riences musicales avec les genres musicaux de
Leonhard Euler contenant la septie`me harmonique", 1949.
