> 3} Any additional internet resources for contemporary music (other than Ircam,
> which I already know about.

I don't know much about chamber ensembles. Below are some resources for
electronic music.

The IPEM in Gent, Belgium:
The WWW homepage is http://next.rug.ac.be/
IPEM stands for "Instituut voor Psychoacustica en Elektronische
Muziek". There are descriptions of activities and compositions,
articles, short biographies of composers, soundfiles, etc. It's best
to browse during office hours (GMT+0100) since some pages might
otherwise be inaccessible.
Other links I've collected:

CCRMA computer music:
ftp ccrma-ftp.stanford.edu /pub
CERL Sound Group:
http://datura.cerl.uiuc.edu
Computer Music Journal:
file://mitpress.mit.edu/pub/Computer-Music-Journal/CMJ.html
file://ccrma-ftp.stanford.edu/pub/Publications/cmj/CMJ.html
Fractal music:
http://www-ks.rus.uni-stuttgart.de/people/schulz/fmusic
ICMA:
dartmouth.edu pub/ICMA-Library
http://coos.dartmouth.edu/~rsn/icma/
Music Research Digest
music-research-request@prg.oxford.ac.uk
archive-management@prg.oxford.ac.uk
ftp.comlab.ox.ac.uk: /Music-Research
Music Theory Online mail server
listserv@husc.harvard.edu http://fas-www.harvard.edu/smt/Smt.html
