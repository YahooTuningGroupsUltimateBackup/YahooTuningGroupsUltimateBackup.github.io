There is certainly Dutch choral music in 31, including some by Henk
Badings (1907-87) which is recorded, although perhaps only on LP.
(Manuel, any CDs that you know of?) I'm not aware that Badings ever wrote
in 19.

In addition to the short piece Manuel kindly mentioned, I wrote 6 a
cappella songs in 31. In performance they come across as a (modified)
7-limit JI. Not that I am an expert in how these songs will always
sound--despite having been published, as far as I know only 2 have been
sung, and only 1 of them in 31. The songs are still in print, in 31- and
12-tET versions. (End of plug!)
