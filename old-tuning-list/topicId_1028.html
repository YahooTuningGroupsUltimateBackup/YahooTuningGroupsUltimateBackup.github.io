I just received a flyer from "the North American Embassy of Anaphoria
Island" announcing "the only L.A. Performance of TEN BLACK EYE, A Modern
Anaphorian Shadow Play assembled and performed by Kraig Grady, Roger
Mexico, B. Ed. Smith, Yelena Tokman. Music performed on the unique
musical instruments of Kraig Grady. Stella Adler Theatre, 6773
Hollywood Blvd, Sun Oct. 27, 8:00 PM, free Admission."

Grady works with Ervin Wilson and tunes his mixture of acoustic and
electronic instruments to CPS and other non-12 scales. Don't miss this
performance if you are in the LA area.

The postal address of the North American Embassy of Anaphoria Island is
2645 Crestmoore Pl. LA. CA 90056. Kraig has also recently released
a CD of Anaphorian music.
