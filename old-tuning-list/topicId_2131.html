The Wendy Carlos web site has files with information on the Tibetan
tuning and other preset intonation tables in the Kurzweil
1000/K2000/K2500 series. The file "Alternate Tuning Guide" explains how

for the scale tunings. They are located at:
http://www.apocalypse.org/pub/u/wendy/resources.html
