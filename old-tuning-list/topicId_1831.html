The discussion about whether or not our ears can hear certain
frequencies, and the implications thereof, is interesting to me. Simply,
just because we cannot see or hear certain vibrations does not
necessarily mean that they have no effect on us...I believe that we are
most likely affected by all sorts of things which we may not currently
understand, perhaps on deep spiritual levels. I think as the human race
progresses (whenever that may be), we will discover many new things about
the zillions of wavelengths around us. Unfortunately, many people seem to
think if you can't see or hear something, it has no effect on us...I
don't believe this for a moment. We just don't KNOW the effect
yet...Hstick
