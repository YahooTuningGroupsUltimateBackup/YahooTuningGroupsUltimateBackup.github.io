It's 3:30 am. The short report:

Both Sasha and Johnny Reinhard are very light on their feet.

Sasha's 1/2 hour Atom Turning in the Sun of Eternity exceded
my expectations - 20 tone 7 limit JI.

John Schneider's performance of Lou Harrison's Suite #2 - can you go
wrong here???!!!

Virgil Morfield's (Slight Return) - 48tet, glisses

and Johnny Reinhard's Adam and Eve - another theater/polymicrotonal
like last year's Odysseus. As good or even better.
Johnny and Christina Coppola are naturals with fig leafs.


Hopefully folks can still make Friday night's show...5/8/98
8pm, Columbia U., St. Paul's Chapel

>Is it possible to set up Just Intonation in the MIDI section of Logic

I'm familiar with Logic and Logic Audio, but not with Logic Audio
Discovery. I've been off the past year and a half learning the saxophone
