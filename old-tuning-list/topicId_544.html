I don't hear Harrison's _La Koro Sutro_ as being in any kind of noticable
JI, due to the homophonic texture, etc. What is really pleasing choral
JI music (to my ears) is any good performance of Gesualdo, Perotin, Byrd,
etc. It's only a vocal quartet, but don't exclude Toby Twining's group
for pop-vocal JI. They use overtone singing and extended techniques for
unique textures.

I sure do love that _Koro Sutro_, though.
