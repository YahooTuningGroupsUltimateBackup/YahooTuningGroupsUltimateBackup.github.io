PARTCH on the Radio

ðŸ”—Didier ASCHOUR <daschour@teaser.fr>

9/13/1997 12:19:49 AM
I recorded for the French National Radio (France-Musique) some pieces of
Harry Partch. The program will be broadcasted Sunday 14th around 23h30.
* Four "numbers" of BARSTOW (original version for voice and adapted guitar)
* Two lyrics of Li-Po (An Encounter in the field & The Intruder)
* "Come Away, Death" from December 42
Vincent Bouchot, Voice / Didier Aschour, adapted guitar.

The radio program will announce our next concert in Paris which will also
include contemporary composers microtonal works:
Pascale Criton: 'La ritournelle et le galop' for 1/16 of tone guitar
Salvatore Sciarrino: 'Canzona di ringraziamento' for solo flute
Bruce Mather: 'Tempranillo' for 1/6 of tone guitar (world premiere)
Patrice Granadel: 'U Lamentu' for flute and 1/4 of tone guitar
