All-
At the first two Microstocks we (the Neil
Haverstick Trio) performed 19tet pop songs of mine, the reggae tune
Sometimes its Right to Kill a Man and the heavy metal bluegrass Citified
Notions. Neil, Ernie and I are currently rehearsing my other 19tet pop
tunes for a CD, and I expect I will have at least one pop tune on the
upcoming tuning@eartha.mills.edu compilation. I'm sure there are more
micro-popsters out there. This stuff is just starting to find a popular
foothold, and I wouldn't be surprised if someday soon "alternative" comes
to imply micro.
