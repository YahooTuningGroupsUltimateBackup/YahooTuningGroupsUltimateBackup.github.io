New Computer Opera by Paul Lansky at CCM

A concert of music created on the computer by visiting composer Paul Lansky
of Princeton University will be heard Sat., Oct. 19, 8:00 pm in Watson Hall at the
College-Conservatory of Music, University of Cincinnati. Lansky uses the
computer as an aural camera to transformed sounds of the ordinary world
into something magical. The program will include movements from a new
computer opera, "Things She Carried", and works based on folk songs.
He will talk about his music and how he created it in this presentation,
and Sunday, Oct. 20 at Mount Adams. Admission is free.
Call 556-0807 for more information.
