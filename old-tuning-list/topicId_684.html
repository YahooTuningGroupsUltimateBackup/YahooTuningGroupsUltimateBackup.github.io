A new issue of 1/1, The Journal of the Just Intonation Network,
is ready to go to the printer. The new issue, Volume 9, Number 3,
features articles by Warren Burt, on some novel applications of
Ervin Wilson's Combination-Product Sets, Harold Waage, on a game
of keyboard expansion/contraction, and Adam Silverman, on the
music of Ben Johnston; plus a review of Heidi Von Gunden's new
book, *The Music of Lou Harrison*, a catalog of transcriptions of
the music of Harry Partch, and a remembrance of the late Kirana
vocal master, Pandit Pran Nath.

A limited number of free copies of this issue are being offered
to those who are not former or current members of the Just
Intonation Network, and have not received a copy of 1/1 through a
previous free offer.
