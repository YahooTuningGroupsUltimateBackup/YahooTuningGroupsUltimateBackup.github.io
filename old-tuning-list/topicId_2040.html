The products PitchPerfectr and PianoPerfect are tuning and harmonic
analysis software for Windows. The home page
http://www.itsnet.com/~hometech/ is worth a glance.

As I understand it, the resolution is only .5 to 2.5 cents (depending on
the soundcard and the microphone), but the basic concept of the products
seems quite sound. _PianoPerfect_ features an option which ''uses the
harmonic data to calculate an ideal tuning to compensate for string
inharmonicity''.
