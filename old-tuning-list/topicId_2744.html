I'm new to the list, and I've never posted to a mail list before. I hope
this works.

I'm a steel guitarist. The steel guitar is usually tuned in JI. Somehow
the tuning works well in country music, even though there are often 12tet
guitars and keyboards in the bands. I'm at a loss to explain this, except
to perhaps agree with the gentleman who made a statement about the "limit of
human perception".

For anyone with an interest in my instrument, I'd like to call your
attention to my web page entitled "Just Intonation of the E9th Pedal Steel"
at http://wco.com/~quasar/articles/just_e9.html

I'm glad I've joined this list. I'm a big fan of the music of Harry Partch,
Lou Harrison and Terry Riley. I look forward to learning more about
alternate tunings. Thanks for your attention.
