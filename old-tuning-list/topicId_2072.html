>Does anybody
>know which tunings were used for organs between 1680-1760 in France?

You may find an anser in these articles:

Asselin, Pierre-Yves. "Le tempe'rament en France au XVIIIe`me sie`cle",
_L'orgue a` notre e'poque_, pp. 45-69.

Bourgeret, Ge'rard. "Correction du tempe'rament de l'orgue de Lorris: Essai de
ge'ne'ralisation", _Revue de musicologie_ vol. 75 no. 1, 1989, pp. 5-24.

Legros, Henry. "Le tempe'rament des orgues en France aux 17e`me et 18e`me
sie`cles", in _Visitatio Organorum_ dl. 2. Feestbundel voor Maarten
Albert Vente ter gelegenheid van zijn 65e verjaardag, Albert Dunning (ed.),
Frits Knuf, Buren, 1980, pp. 419-438.
