I have made a short MIDI file with the first page of Nicola Vicentino's
madrigal "Madonna il poco dolce". Vicentino wrote this madrigal as an
example of a composition that employs the three modes diatonic,
chromatic and enharmonic. The microintervals of the enharmonic mode
are minor dieses. I have rendered it in 5-limit just intonation with
minor diesis of 128/125. Thanks to Kami Rousseau for putting it in his
website. Download http://www.interlinx.qc.ca/~kami/music/madonna.mid or
go to his music page http://www.interlinx.qc.ca/~kami/ and follow the
link "MIDI files". No special equipment needed.

Manuel Op de Coul coul@ezh.nl
