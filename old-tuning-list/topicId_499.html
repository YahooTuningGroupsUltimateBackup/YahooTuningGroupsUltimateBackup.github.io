This is not tuning-related strictly speaking, but I suspect that many of you
might find it interesting anyway. I saw a catalog yesterday for a company
called "Lark in the Morning". They mail-order a wide variety of unusual musical

almost impossibly big variety, from straight fanfare trumpets, to sitars and
tamburas, to clavichord kits.

have no information, positive nor negative, about the quality of their
merchandise or service.
