Mayumi Reinhard's tuning we call Harmonic 13 and she has written several
pieces in this tuning. There are 14 notes culled from Partch's Genesis
of a Music (pp. 461-463). Tune it up and hear it for yourself.
Attributes: great melodies and harmonies, sound beautiful, and quite
playable.

1/1 169/168 105/104 14/13 13/12 16/13 13/10 18/13 13/9 20/13
0 10 17 128 139 360 454 563 637 746


13/8 22/13 13/7 208/105
841 911 1072 1183


BTW, Paul and I can now communicate confidently that "it is impossible
sing the logarithmic middle of large, unfamiliar interval (e.g. a 7/5)
without learning it by rote (and practice)".
