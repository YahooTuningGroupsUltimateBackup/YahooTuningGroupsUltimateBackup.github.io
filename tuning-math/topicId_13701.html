Fooling around with NOT tunings, I found the following curiosity:

(1) 193-et is extremely close to the NOT tuning for hanson, the
15625/15552 5-limit temperament

(2) 193-et is essentially identical to the NOT tuning for hemifamity,
the 5120/5103 planar temperament

(3) 193-et is essentially identical to the NOT tuning for the
"countercata", the 5120/5103&15625/15552 temperament, with wedgie
<<6 5 -31 -6 -66 -86||

(4) 193-et is essentially identical to the NOT tuning for kwai, the
<<1 33 27 50 40 -30|| temperament

(5) The 7-limit TM basis for 193 is {5120/5103, 15625/15552, 16875/16807}
