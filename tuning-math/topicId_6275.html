The rationale for this subgroup is that while the 11-th partial is a
little awkwardly placed, between 10 and 12, the 13-th partial can
substitute for the 12; so that we have 8:10:13 as a variant on 8:10:12.

Clearly the standout system for this sort of thing is kleismic, but
orwell and (various forms of) meantone are possibilities, and
especially so if 7 is added to the mix.
