I was contemplating how easy it would be to catalog Fokker blocks for
11-limit scales of 22 notes. It seems to me at minimum I would beed to
look at the following comma bases, each of which is likely to lead to
more than one scale.

[121/120, 64/63, 55/54, 50/49], [100/99, 99/98, 64/63, 55/54],
[100/99, 64/63, 55/54, 50/49], [121/120, 100/99, 99/98, 64/63],
[121/120, 100/99, 64/63, 50/49], [121/120, 99/98, 64/63, 55/54],
[121/120, 99/98, 64/63, 50/49], [99/98, 64/63, 55/54, 50/49]

All of these look excellent, and I'd hardly think a catalog was very
good if I did less.
