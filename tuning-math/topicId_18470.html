IIRC, somebody found a web page from which one could derive systematic mathematical names for Wilson's CPSs. The hexany corresponds to the octahedron and the eikosany to some sort of -choron, but I don't recall the name or the naming procedure. As I recall, even the less symmetrical structures such as the dekanies could be named by this technique. Does anyone recall the name?

I did a Google search this AM and found several pages with polytopes, polychora, etc., but didn't see anything corresponding to the eikosany. Also, I'm not certain that this is an accepted nomenclature for these higher-dimensional structures.
