Tenney height:

[1, 56/55, 36/35, 26/25, 22/21, 16/15, 14/13, 12/11, 11/10, 10/9,

The smallest interval here is between 7/5 and 45/32; this could no doubt be smoothed out more than this, but it seems clear that we can get something decent in 58 tones, 13-limit JI which is epimorphic.
