I thought I'd try to see if I could get a decent magic
well-temperament on 22 notes; the following may be too extreme to
count, in that some of the fifths in remote keys are pretty sharp, but
it might have a use. Anyway it seems clear it is possible to make a
circulating temperament in more than 12 notes, and not necessary to
stick to something quite as easy as hanson on 19.

This has 17 major thirds of 380.5 cents and 5 pure major thirds. It
has 13 nice 702.5 cent fifths, two not-so-nice 708.3 cent fifths and
then even sharper ones up to a single 731.6 cent howlin' wolf.
