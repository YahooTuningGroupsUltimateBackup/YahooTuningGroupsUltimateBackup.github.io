Here are some 5-limit comma pairs which might be good for producing
Fokker blocks which marvel-temper into something with a lot of 7 and
11 limit harmony. I give the comma pair, the val they generate, and
the angle between the first comma, second comma, and product of the
commas and |-16 5 3>, which is the 5-limit interval equivalent under
marvel to 14/11. 14/11, the product of 7/8 and 16/11, is in the
direction we want the Fokker block to stretch.

[250/243, 32805/32768] [29, 46, 67] 105.200014 32.395685 3.498207
