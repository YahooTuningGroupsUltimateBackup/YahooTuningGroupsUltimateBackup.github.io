Aim : The site TurkZeka.Com was established with a purpose of
helping puzzle fans improve their ability of imaging, thinking,

CONTENTS :

Puzzle Competitions :

The first puzzle competition which had 50 questions was held in
2004, during all the year.

TurkZeka 2005/1 Puzzle Competition will start on 27 or 28 February
2005.
This year, each competition (total 4 times in a year) is going to
have 10 puzzles and these puzzle competitions will be open to all
country.
Competitions will be published in only Turkish and English.
You will have exactly 7 days to submit the answers of the puzzles
via "Answer" form page, beginning from the puzzle's publishing date.

You should read "How to Answer" page before aswering. Each correct
answer is 100 points and additionally there are two types of bonus
points.
One of the bonus points is given to the first 3 competitors who give
the correct answer (30, 20 and 10 point) and the other bonus is
given depending on answering day, beginning from current puzzle's
publishing date (30, 25, 20, 15, 10 and 5 point).

Correct answers and solutions will be given when the next puzzle is
published. Your score will be updated when you post your answer. You
can see whether your answer is correct or not on the "Score Anlayze
Table". To compete, you have to sign up to the site and log in.

We want the competitors from abroad participate in these
competitions which have great numbers of participants from Turkey
and that pleasent rivalry adds joy to the competitions.
In this respect, you should tell friends who are interested in
puzzles about these competitions and the site.
Our web address is : http://www.turkzeka.com

Thank you.

Good Luck...
