> What is this?

A mistake? It would take quite a stretch of my imagination to consider
the generator of this scale as a 4:7. It has a 32 cent error. If it
has a 7-limit mapping at all, it must be [2, -6, 1] in which case the
generator can be considered to be (the inversion of) half the pelogic
fifth. That temperament would be better represented in 37-ET, but 11
notes would no longer be MOS there. Even with the optimum generator
(259.64 cents) it has a max abs error of 28.5 cents.
