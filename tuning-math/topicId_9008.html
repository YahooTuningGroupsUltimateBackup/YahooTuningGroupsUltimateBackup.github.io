I'm not familiar with this terminology, but the keyboard is clearly based
on a generator of 5 steps of 23-ET, while the generator of pelogic
temperament is 10 steps of 23. In other words, this is the scale I've been
calling "superpelog", with the basic 9-note MOS subset used as the basis
for a system of notation.

I'll add a reference to this paper on my superpelog page:

http://www.io.com/~hmiller/music/superpelog.html
