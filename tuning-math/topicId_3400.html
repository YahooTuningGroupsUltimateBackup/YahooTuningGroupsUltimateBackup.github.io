If you wedge together 100/99^225/224^385/384 or 100/99^225/224^540/539 or 100/99^385/384^540/539 you get the
11-limit version of magic, but there doesn't seem to be any reason to prefer the 63 version over the 41. However, it extends to a different and more accurate 13-limit version of magic in the 63 form.
