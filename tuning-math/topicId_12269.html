These are isosceles triangles in the breed plane, with b(n) = 2n^2+2n+1
notes, b(n-1) otonal tetrads, b(n-2) utonal tetrads, for a total of
4n^2-8n+6 tetrads, so in the limit there are twice as many tetrads as
notes.
