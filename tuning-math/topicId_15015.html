It's a useful chord to have around in any equal temperament divisible
by five, such as 15, 25, 50, 55, or 80. Since 50 and 55 are both good
meantone systems, you can even smuggle it into meantone (in different
spellings.) It would allow for exotic modulation into very remote keys
in a generally common-practice/meantone style, a fact I very much
dount anyone has ever taken advantage of. However, I wouldn't say
similar JI chords in other tunings or JI are no good. Stacking one 9/8
, two 8/7s and two 7/6s leads to an octave, for starters.
12:14:16:18:21 for example can be either a pentatonic scale or a chord.

If anyone wants to try this meantone idea, 30 steps of 50-et or 33 of
55-et is a 49/32.
