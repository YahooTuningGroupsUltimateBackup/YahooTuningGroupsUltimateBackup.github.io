Godzilla/Semaphore/Hemifourths is the 7-limit temperament which splits
a sharp fourth in half, getting a combination 8/7 and 7/6 which it
uses as a generator. Even numbers of generator steps give 5-limit
meantone, so we tend to get a good 5-limit and a somewhat cheezy
7-limit. Not only is 19 7 and 9 limit poptimal, so is 81, so we can
use an excellent 5-limit meantone tuning. It can also be called 19&24,
and has {49/48, 81/80} as a comma basis.

If we stretch a secor by a little over a factor of two (or a ratio of
6:13 to be precise about it) we get a corresponding godzilla
generator. Along the lines of the meantone notation for 36-et, this
gives a correspondence between miralcle and godzilla, and hence to a
subset of miracle with even numbers of generator steps and meantone.
Notations designed for miracle can be used for godzilla, and vice
versa. This doesn't do much for the 7-limit aspect of godzilla, but of
course the 5-limit part is meantone and moves nicely over to miracle.
