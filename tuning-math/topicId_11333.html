There are twelve scales which result from taking a 3x2 rectangle of
tetrads within the cubic lattice of tetrads. One of them, reca1b2, or
glumma, is given by Scala to be permutation epimophic with respect to
the glumma val, <12 19 27 34|. In fact another of these scales,
reca1c2, has this property also. I give it below.
