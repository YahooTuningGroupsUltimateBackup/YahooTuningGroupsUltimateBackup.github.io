I obtained this scale by multiplying the 7-limit diamond by the
41-limit diamond, reducing to the octave, sorting according to 311,
and then choosing the smallest Tenney height. This left me with
nothing to represent a single step, but going to the 9-limit diamond
times the 41 limit diamond does that, giving 280/279. Below I give a
temperament based on going around by steps of 181 on this scale,
giving a fifth a little flat from pure. Then the full 311 note scale.
