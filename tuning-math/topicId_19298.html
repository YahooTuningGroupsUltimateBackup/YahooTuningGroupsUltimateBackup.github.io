Given that the end of the world didn't come (on the exact 7,000th anniversary of Noah's Flood, which was chosen because a day to God
is like 1,000 years or something...)

I thought, how about the Elect Comma (Using 144,000). Well this is
a pretty ordinary number 2^7 * 3^2 * 5^3, reducing to 1125.

I couldn't find any nice commas, except for one with a p=53, kinda
high, really, for such an ordinary number. The best I found is this

2^15 * 3^2 * 5^3 / 7^5 * 13^3 = 36,864,000 / 36,924,979

which is about 2.86 cents. It multiples the Elect by 256, which
means each tribe now gets 256,000 preachers instead of 1,000.
