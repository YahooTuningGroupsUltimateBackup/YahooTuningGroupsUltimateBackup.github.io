A generator is "copoptimal if" it is poptimal for more than one odd
limit. For instance, for a 7-limit temperament we might wish for one
poptimal in both the 7 and 9 odd limits; if one exists it would be 7&9
copoptimal, or simply copoptimal if the meaning is clear by context.
