Below is a list of 7-limit commas, followed by an integer. The integer
denotes the corresponding 7=limit standard val, and is distinguished
as coming the closest to the minimax tuning, in the sense that the
maximum error is least among all standard vals having the comma in
question as a comma. Using the given et would be one way of tuning the
planar temperament.
