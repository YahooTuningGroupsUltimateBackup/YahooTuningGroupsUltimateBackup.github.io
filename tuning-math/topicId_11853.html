9,5,7,11, and 13 forming symmetrical A5 sublattice, we have a hole
with center |* 7/6 7/12 7/12 7/12 7/12> and radius sqrt(29/12),
containing 20 pitch classes:

[105, 165, 195, 231, 273, 315, 385, 429, 455, 495, 585, 693, 715, 819,
1001, 1155, 1287, 1365, 2145, 3003]

[1, 78/77, 12/11, 39/35, 8/7, 13/11, 6/5, 26/21, 13/10, 4/3, 104/77,
78/55, 16/11, 52/35, 52/33, 8/5, 12/7, 26/15, 13/7, 104/55]

[-39, -37, -31, -29, -28, -26, -25, -23, -19, -17, -16, -14, -13, -11,
