More scales with all-consonance interval classes:

Does the 686/675 planar temperament
http://x31eq.com/cgi-bin/rt.cgi?ets=27_19_10&limit=7
have a name? I've been calling "sengic" but Mike said he hated that name for some reason. It has a good 13-limit extension too:
http://x31eq.com/cgi-bin/rt.cgi?ets=8d_9_19p&limit=2.3.5.7.13
(no 11)

Anyway it has quite a few good scales, for example this one in which "thirds" are {7/6,16/13,9/7} and "fourths" are {5/4,4/3,7/5}:
