I don't know that there are any attempts to keep a canonical list of 11-
limit temperaments other than mine, for what mine is worth. I have this
listed as heptadec:

<<5 3 7 4 -7 -3 -11 8 -1 -13||
Commas: 36/35, 56/55, 77/75
poptimal generator: 2/17

George and Hermann may want to take another look. The 17-et val for it
is <17 27 40 48 59|, which involves trying to use 423.529 as a major
third, which of course it isn't. But people who like sharp fifths and
very sharp thirds, (and you know you're out there) might love it.
