These are 7-connected scales in the Magic temperament
[5 1 12 25 -5 -10]. They are presented in terms of the 41-et, but are generic 7-limit Magic scales. It is striking that in both the 7 and 10 note cases, there is a non-MOS scale with the same connectivity as the MOS scale.
