Here is a list of 7-limit commas, under 50 cents in size and within a
Hahn radius of 7 of unity, in order of TOP error of the corresponding
temperament times the fourth power of the Hahn radius. There are five
limit commas on this list, but they are being evaluated from a seven
limit point of view, and hence do not show well. The first two on the
list vanish in ennealimmal, 50/49 and 49/48 are identified, and
1029/1024 and 225/224 are identified. The schisma shows up as
important in the five limit. Hence, asking for symbols for 49/48 or
50/49, 36/35, 126/125, and 225/224 or 1029/1024 seems justified in
terms of their importance, if you believe this analysis.
