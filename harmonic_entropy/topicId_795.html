> Can harmonic entropy help us predict the perceived

Well, it can tell you how salient each potential fundamental pitch
is, since each potential fundamental pitch implies a particular ratio-
interpretation for the heard partials, but as to the precise tuning
of each of these fundamental, I guess it's silent.

> What other models exist for doing this?
