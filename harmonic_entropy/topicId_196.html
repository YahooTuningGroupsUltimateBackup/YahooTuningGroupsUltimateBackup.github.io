OK -- a chord has three intervals, right? So the corner nearest us
(bottom-left on the 2-d graph) is where all three intervals are zero. Now
the distance from the left edge is the size of the upper interval; the
distance from the bottom-right edge is the size of the lower interval, and
the distance from the upper-right edge is one octave minus the size of the
outer interval. Look at the chords and see if you can see this pattern. Is
it starting to come together?
