Be aware that there's an error on that CD -- one of the pieces that is
said to be in 17-equal was measured by a tuning list member, and in
fact all of the intervals turned out to me exact multiples of 50 cents -
- hence, it's really in 24-equal.

Other than that, it's really a matter of taste. But I'd much more
heartily recommend _Beauty In The Beast_ by Wendy Carlos, which was
just re-released on CD, as a very musical introduction to various
tuning systems.
