Paul,

Please don't be hasty to "phase out" the tuning-math list.
I really think that if we keep it alive, the regular tuning
list will have much less math on it and will be the kind of
list that so many people there have been complaining that
they want.

As I've said, I think tuning, tuning-math, and spiritual_tuning
pretty much covers it. I even forsee that *this* list will
gradually disappear as the proliferation of different
tuning lists settles down and everyone sorts it all out
and subscribes to only those lists they like.

So the tuning and tuning-math separation is one that I really
think is a beneficial one. All the other lists can go,
except that one that's "out there...", for me.
