For anyone who might be interested I've just uploaded three chamber
pieces for two horns and electric guitar from the late 80s. These are
not for the squeamish though as these sit somewhere between The Music
Improvisation Company and LOUD guitar--ELECTRIC guitar (there's even
some microinflecting,but nothing out of the ordinary there). I'm
really quite happy with these pieces especially considering that I
forgot they even existed until I just found the recording!
