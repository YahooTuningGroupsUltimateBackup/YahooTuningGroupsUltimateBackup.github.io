BTW, I've read a lot in the last 2 weeks, newspapers, mags and such,
and seen a number of religions mentioned in the context of the recent
attacks...these include Judaism, Christianity, Islam, Hinduism, and
Buddhism. Nowhere have I seen anyone mention the Native American
beliefs...since we're living on their ancestral lands, perhaps a look
into their beliefs and prophecies wouldn't be a bad idea right
now...there's a lot of depth and wisdom there, I believe. The Book of
the Hopi would be a good place to start, as would Black Elk Speaks. The
teachings therein have great relevance to our current times...Hstick
