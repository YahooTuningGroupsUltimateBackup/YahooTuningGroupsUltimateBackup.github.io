they also invented a camera that was a big sphere with a mirror in the
center that could spin at very high speeds. A strip of film was pasted
inside the sphere as a circumference, so the light enters the sphere through
a lens, hits the mirror and is projected onto the strip of film so that the
one revolution of the mirror would throw the light in a tiny fraction of a
second. This way they could photograph the inital few milliseconds of the
detonation. The pics are very weird.
