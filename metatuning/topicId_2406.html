A find of Bashkir scientists contraries to traditional
notions of human history: stone stabs which is 120
million years covered with the relief map of Ural
Region.

This seems to be impossible. Scientists of Bashkir
State University have found indisputable proofs of an
ancient highly developed civilization¹s existence. The
question is about a great plate found in 1999, with
picture of the region done according to an unknown
technology. This is a real relief map. Today¹s military
has almost similar maps. The map contains civil
engineering works: a system of channels with a length
of about 12,000 km, weirs, powerful dams. Not far from
the channels, diamond-shaped grounds are shown, whose
destination is unknown. The map also contains some
inscriptions. Even numerous inscriptions. At first, the
scientists thought that was Old Chinese language.
Though, it turned out that the subscriptions were done
in a hieroglyphic-syllabic language of unknown origin.

while Ufa Canyon is the main point of our proofs,
because we carried out geological studies and found its

Geological structure of the stab was determined: it
