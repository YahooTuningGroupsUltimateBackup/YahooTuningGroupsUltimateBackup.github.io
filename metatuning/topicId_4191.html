It's probably not news to folks here (especially to Monz, our resident
Schoenberg scholar), but it was to me, that the Arnold Schoenberg Center
website has scans of his manuscripts!:

http://www.schoenberg.at/6_archiv/music/works/op/compositions_op47_sources_e
.htm#

It takes awhile to download the scans, but since I did I zipped them into
two files and uploaded them to the files section of this group for easier
access:

/metatuning/files/

in case anyone is innarested. It is very enjoyable to listen to the
recording following the manuscript. Amazingly, he apparently composed the
violin part first, and then "added" the piano part! An interesting
procedure, to say the least.
