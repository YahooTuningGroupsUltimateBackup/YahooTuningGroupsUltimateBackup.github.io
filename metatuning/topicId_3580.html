Here's the background on thimerosal: it's a preservative used in childhood
and other vaccines that has a high level of mercury. Been used for a long
time but in the last few years the number of required childhood vaccines
exploded. Finally someone looked into it and calculated that a vaccinated
baby in the US gets Mercury injected straight into his veins that is
hundreds of times greater than the maximum mercury exposure considered safe
by other researchers. Since there is tons of evidence (and has been for some
time) that mercury exposure causes brain damage and neurological problems,
vaccine companies finally cut back (after this all was made public). Of
course the fact remains that the companies knew how much mercury was in the
vaccines all these years, knew how many vaccines kids were getting, and knew
that mercury caused these problems. They just didn't care. You might think
they'd get sued, now that kids who are overvaccinated are having lots of
neurological problems...
