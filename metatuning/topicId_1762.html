vibrato, the width of it is not as great as what you
hear nowadays.

can someone p l e a s e find a recording of
the incredible Mary Garden, and post that?

i had read about Garden's amazing voice for years
until finally i heard her one day on the radio.
it was just gorgeous ... her vibrato was beautiful,
the width of it increasing sparingly only in the most
effective places, with a clarity of both pitch and
diction that i've n e v e r heard in any other
opera singer.

but in general, to my ears, the older recordings attest
that singers back then used narrower vibrato than they
do now.
