Metatunors,

Since a good number of you are in NY or nearby, I just wanted to
share this with you: last Sunday my wife, with the help of a
wonderful local soprano (and myself) produced a concert that centered
around Faure's "Requiem". A 15 piece orchestra (drawn from the SD
Symphony), a 16-voice choir consisting of many of SD's best
vocalists, and a stellar organist presented this Memorial/Benefit
concert at a modest Lutheran church. Done with just free publicity
and word-of-mouth in a very short amount of time, the concert was a
beautiful experience, the church was filled, and we raised over $3000
during the 'collection' period for the American Red Cross.

Doesn't end all our woes, but just wanted you to know we're trying to
back you up out here...

Cheers,
Jon
