For anyone who might be interested, I've uploaded a demo of my piece
"My Heart's got an Atonal Tongue"

http://briefcase.yahoo.com/daniel_anthony_stearns

It's 12-tone jazz for guitar, bass and drum trio--for the demo I
played all the parts. As far as motif development and harmonic
language goes, the approach is very similar to the solo guitar pieces.
But the trio is not quite as bop, and the overall effect is a whole
heck of a lot rawer too.
