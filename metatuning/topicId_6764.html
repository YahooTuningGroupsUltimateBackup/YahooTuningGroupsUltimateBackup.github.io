Thought Monz and others might be interested . . .

My friend Manisha sings in Chorus pro Musica; their
repertoire consists of choral music from the Renaissance to the
present, with
emphasis on new and rarely performed choral music, as well as major
works in
collaboration with other music organizations. Every year, CpM also
presents a
concert opera performance; in the spring of 2004, it will be Arrigo
Boito's
Mefistofele. Below is information on performances this month:

MAHLER Symphony No. 2 "The Resurrection"

Chorus pro Musica appears with the Boston Philharmonic (Benjamin
Zander,
conductor)

Wed Feb 18, 7:30PM, Symphony Hall, Boston
Sun Feb 22, 3:00PM, Symphony Hall, Boston
Sun Feb 29 8pm Carnegie Hall, New York

To learn more about Gustav Mahler, visit:

http://www.netaxs.com/~jgreshes/mahler/

http://w3.rz-berlin.mpg.de/cmp/mahler.html

To buy tickets for the Symphony dates, please call Symphony Hall: 617-
266-1200.

For Carnegie Hall tickets, please call 212-247-7800.

For more info visit www.choruspromusica.org or www.bostonphil.org.
