Nothing that I know about 15, but Z recorded probably the 'breakthrough' album of the band early on, and the album was titled "Heavy Weather". IIRC, the lineup was Z on keys, Jaco: bass, Wayne Shorter: sax/winds, Alex Acuna and Manolo Badrena: perc. Beyond smoking...

One thing I *had* read in a couple of places is that one of the kbds Z used to use (can't remember which one) had one of those functions that would remap the keys backwards, with the high pitches to the left and low to the right. He said he would riff like that and it would give him new ideas and break out of ruts.
