--- In metatuning@y..., "Dante Rosati" <dante.interport@r...> wrote:

> do you have access to the original typescripts? I didn't realize the
> published version was edited. Too bad about your website: I would have been
> interested to see that.

A year ago a version edited by Bill Thetford surfaced, with a lot of extra material. I am the titular head of something called the Course in Miracles Society, which published it after an intellectual property attorney we retained told us it appeared to be in the public domain. This put CIMS into a win-win situation--either we would not be sued, in which case we could publish this version, or we would be, in which case we would have a legal right to examine and copy the original transcripts, and Helen Schucman's notebooks, which had copies held in the US copyright office.

It did not however give us the legal right to make them publically available. However, the New Christian Church of Full Endeavor, which had obtained, and subsequently leaked, the Bill Thetford version, also got a copy by the same method, after hearing of our plan. By a strange coincidence (which I put in that way since nothing is proven) this was leaked also, by someone or other.

Various Yahoo groups might interest you re this--such as Experience_ACIM_Urtext_Epistemology (don't blame me for that name!), or JCIM, or CIMWORKS.
