I saw a rerun of an old "Saturday Night Live" the other night.
It opened with Dana Carvey doing an imitation of George Bush,
speaking about the details of the "New World Order" that would
be put in place if he were elected president.

Well... I didn't realize until much later that this episode was
made in 1987, while Reagan was president! I thought at first that
Carvey was imitating George *Dubya* Bush from last year, while
Clinton was still president. It wasn't until nearly the end of
the sketch, upon mentioning that he'd allow himself to take other
wives in addition to Barbara, that I realized that Bush *senior*
was the object of ridicule.

But the *really* scary thing, and the reason why I'm posting
about it here, is how incredibly relevant Carvey's satire was
to what's actually happening now. Does anyone else remember
that episode of SNL, or did anyone else happen to catch the rerun?
