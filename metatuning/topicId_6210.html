My wife and I just finished watching the movie "Conspiracy" with
Kenneth Branagh and Stanley Tucci this evening. This is a great movie,
based on transcripts of the meeting of top Nazis in which the German's
"final solution" for the Jews was put into effect. It is a masterful
study of interpersonal dynamics, power and control. I urge anyone
interested in politics and human behavior to see this film.
