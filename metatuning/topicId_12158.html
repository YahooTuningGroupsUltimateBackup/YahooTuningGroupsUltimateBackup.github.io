In the interest of forthcomingness, I thought I might post a link or
two to a gig I did recently.

like this onstage, and actually, it's the FIRST time I've ever done

years old, and I just did my first "jazz" gig, hee! Anyway...

Yeah, sorry, that's right. Not as a nominal jazz dude!
I still wish I could get a straight-ahead jazz gig,
sometimes, as much as I tried to train for it. But I
was serious about bein' a fusion tycoon 30 years ago,
hee! I was gigging as a rock guy all the time until I
left LA in '93. And I did some fairly regular stints
in the Bay Area until I left there a year ago. 10
years ago in SF I was playing classic honky-tonk
country music, in a gigging band. We played GAMH and
even the Fillmore, as an opening act. I picked that
clip because it was the weirdest, and to me the most
interesting style/composition. I'm trying to do, like,
death-metal Miles, a-suhn-in...hee! This IS only my
second gig in a year, and I'm feeling REALLY rusty...

But I got a little session work here and there and
I've been teaching, too.

The last gig I did, I played fretless bass with a
great gyil player here in Philly, and we had some of
the best drummers in the area on that one, traps and
African and Afro-Cuban percussion. For that, I retuned
the bass and did my best to play the intervals on the
gyil. Mucho Fun!

Thanks!
