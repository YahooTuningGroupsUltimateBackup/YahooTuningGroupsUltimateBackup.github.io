hey all you Californicators,

i just saw a fantastic show last night at the
"Global Peace Dance" at San Diego's World Beat Center.
the headlining act was Cubensis, which is billed
as California's greatest Grateful Dead tribute band.
these guys are fantastic!

just as with a real Dead show, no two shows
are ever the same, because there's a lot of
improvisation. they travel all over the state
and sometimes venture outside, and also play
regular weekly shows in Santa Monica and
Huntington Beach. i highly recommend catching them.
