Hi Paul,

That prob. means you need to go to
Internet Explorer | Tools | Internet Options
| Connections | Settings,
tick Use a proxy server, Advanced,
and in first line enter 127.0.0.1 as the
address and 8000 as the port.

Then in the little menu you have to tick to
block ads.

If you untick the proxy server (or can't use it for
some reason) then you get what you say, both the
advert and the word Advertisement.
