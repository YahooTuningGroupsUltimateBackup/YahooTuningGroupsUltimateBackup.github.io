For a long time now I've been finding that people on the Tuning List
don't have exactly my priorities, or write about exactly the things I
want them to write about. In particular they seem to live in other
places. A lot of them are in America, even those in the UK don't live
very near Clevedon.

So, I've decided to fix this problem by setting up my own mailing list
for microtonalists in the Clevedon area. The web page is at

<http://www.microtonal.co.uk/clevedon/>

or you can subscribe by sending e-mail to
<mailto:clevedon-subscribe@...>. Messages can be sent to
the list at <mailto:clevedon@...>.

I hope this will resolve the "other people" problem of the Tuning List,
and in some small way bring about the time when everybody can have a
tuning list all of their own.

Graham
