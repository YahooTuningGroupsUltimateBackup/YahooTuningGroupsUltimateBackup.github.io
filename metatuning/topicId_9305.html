Imagine the creative spirit that was able to address one of societies
greatest wrongs, and to have brought to life a series of ten plays,
one for each decade of the last millenium, that examine the fallout
from that wrong in all its difficult and beautiful complexity. I've
only seen 3 of the plays, but I sincerely hope to see a good number
more of them before I go.

A wonderful creator, gone too soon.
