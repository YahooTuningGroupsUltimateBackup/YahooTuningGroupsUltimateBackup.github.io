can and wants to play my GM file using better sounds, feel
free. I'll upload the MIDI to the files section too.

This little ditty has a special place in my heart. I just
wrote to John deLaubenfels about it this morning, and I
suppose what I wrote him is worth quoting here, at the very
least to acknowledge his contribution to what you're hearing:

> I thought-up this piece while working in the kitchen of a
> convalescent home in Los Angeles back in the summer of 1981
> (my first abortive attempt to live in California), and
> wrote a sketch of it (just melody and chord-letters) down
> on manuscript paper.
>
> But in the two decades since then, I never recorded,
> sequenced, or even *played* it once! Except for that sketchy
> lead-sheet (which we both know well is *not* a very accurate
> representation of the piece), the only place it ever really
> existed all that time was in my head!
>
> It was only just a couple of months ago that I finally
> decided to draw the notes into my computer screen and create
> the MIDI-file. And the 12-EDO version did *not* sound like
> the one in my head! Your 7-limit adaptive JI version does!
> So you definitely deserve a pat on the back for a job well done!
