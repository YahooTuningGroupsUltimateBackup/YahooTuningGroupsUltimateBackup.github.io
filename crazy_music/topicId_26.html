No, mp2 is fine.

Many people think its that but it ain't. mp2 and mp3 are
two versions of the mpeg audio compression standard.
mp3 is best at higher rates of compression. But if you
are trying to go for 'hifi' quality, then for the same
size file, mp2 does a better job. All programs that
support mp3 do mp2 as well. Its is only because of
the publicity of sites like mp3.com and so forth that
people think mp3 is always the way to go.

Its a common misperception that mp2 is the old obsolete
standard and mp3 is the new fancy better one. But that's
not true.
