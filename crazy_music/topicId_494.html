Since we have so much fun on this list, I thought it might be a good
time for us to "wallow in our sorrow" with a true Russian lint.

The piece in question is by composer/violist Lev Zhurbin from
Juilliard.... It's basically a "pop" piece as part of his musical,
which was never produced (another reason to wallow...)

Zhurbin is *very* interested in microtonality and, in fact, is going
to be playing my VIOLAHEXY in the future, so, given this fact, I
thought I might be excused by including a link to this piece in 12-
tET.

For the "composing set" on this list... note the "long line." Nadia
Boulanger really was right about this.

Are you crying yet?? Good.... "Enjoy"
