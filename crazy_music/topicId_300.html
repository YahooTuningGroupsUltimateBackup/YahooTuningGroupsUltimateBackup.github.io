Hello all,

I've just uploaded a lo-fi version of my latest piece,
a computer realization of a choral piece which John deLaubenfels
ran thru his adaptive tuning program.

It's supposed to be 11-limit, but I don't hear any intervals
that sound like 11-limit, and John is not sure if his program
is doing 11-limit correctly yet. So I decided not to upload
a hi-fi version yet. (When I did upload it at first, my
computer barfed trying to download it anyway). Also, the
lo-fi version will load much faster for those with low
bandwidth.

I was inspired to write this after hearing Margo's piece
about the scale tree. It was originally supposed to have
been Byzantine-chant style, perhaps with a tinge of Neo-Gothic,
but didn't turn out that way at all, sounding mostly like
the Bulgarian Women's Choir. I love Bulgarian music, so
I guess it just took over...
