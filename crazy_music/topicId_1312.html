I have not gotten any of the described spam. Also, it's not showing up in
the messages section of the website, and messages sent via the group start
with "[cm]" and not "cm:", so it's not coming through the list.

I have seen that there are spammers who join yahoo groups in order to post
spam, but that hasn't been a problem here yet. This group requires approval
to join, so that shouldn't be as much a problem as it is with an open list.

The spams I get on the address I use for this group are of two types - one
is the 'get ur prescription drugs 4 cheep' type, the other is 'get photoshop
and winders xp for $5, garanteed orishinul", both which are very common
spams.

I have never gotten any spanish spam.
