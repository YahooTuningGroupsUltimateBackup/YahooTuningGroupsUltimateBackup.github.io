Hey all,

Here's a neat gizmo for OS X that will be of interest to the experimentally
minded composers and performers here, as well as those who want a cheap way
to make some really awesome controllers:

http://www.steim.org/steim/junxion.html

It allows you to generate any sort of MIDI events in response to gestures
coming from any sort of USB game controller, of which there are tons
available. You can actually have 8 separate USB devices running at once,
each with its own MIDI data processor.

This is something that will be really awesome used with softsynths.

Could also be great for interactive sound installations in parks and other
site installations.

Fun!
