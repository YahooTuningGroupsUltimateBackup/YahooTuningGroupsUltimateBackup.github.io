Hi there,

I've just done a new piece in j.i. cross set to try out the
new root control, and as an adaptive tuning puzzle.

http://members.tripod.com/~robertinventor/tunes/tunes.htm

Puzzle is to find the optimal tuning without turning it into
a comma pump, given that the high Ab / G# is presented
with no preparation to give any clues about whether it is
a 25/16, 8/5, or 405/128, and one has to look ahead several
bars to find out which it is supposed to be.

Also has melodic diesis shifts at the end for fun,
a la Margo Schulter / Vincentino.
